ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकोनविंशम् दशकम्
ekonaviMsham dashakam

The Nineteenth group of Ten shlokas

प्रचेतसाम्  कथा
prachetasAm kathA

The Story of Prachetas

पृथोस्तु नप्ता पृथुधर्मकर्मठ:
प्राचीनबर्हिर्युवतौ शतद्रुतौ ।
प्रचेतसो नाम सुचेतस: सुतान्
अजीजनत्त्वत्करुणाङ्कुरानिव ॥१॥
pRthostu naptA pRthudharmakarmaThaH
prAchInabarhiryuvatau shatadrutau 
prachetaso nAma suchetasaH sutAn
ajIjanattvatkaruNAnkurAniva 1

पृथोस्तु नप्ता पृथुधर्मकर्मठ:
Prithu's great grand son, who performed many righteous deeds,
प्राचीनबर्हिर्युवतौ शतद्रुतौ ।
by the name of Prachinabarhi, along with a young wife named Shatadruti,
प्रचेतसो नाम सुचेतस: सुतान्
pure minded Sons, by the names of Prachetas
अजीजनत्त्वत्करुणाङ्कुरानिव ॥१॥
gave birth to ten of them, as if they are offshoots of your grace!

पितु: सिसृक्षानिरतस्य शासनाद्
भवत्तपस्याभिरता दशापि ते
पयोनिधिं पश्चिममेत्य तत्तटे
सरोवरं सन्ददृशुर्मनोहरम् ॥२॥
pituH sisRkShAniratasya shAsanAd
bhavattapasyAbhiratA dashApi te
payonidhiM pashchimametya tattaTe
sarovaraM sandadRshurmanoharam 2

पितु: सिसृक्षानिरतस्य शासनाद्
By the command of their father, who was intent on furthering creation,
भवत्तपस्याभिरता दशापि ते
in their desire to perform your penance, all Ten of them,
पयोनिधिं पश्चिममेत्य तत्तटे
reached the ocean to the west, and on its banks,
सरोवरं सन्ददृशुर्मनोहरम् ॥२॥
saw a beautiful lake!

तदा भवत्तीर्थमिदं समागतो
भवो भवत्सेवकदर्शनादृत: ।
प्रकाशमासाद्य पुर: प्रचेतसाम्
उपादिशत् भक्ततमस्तव स्तवम् ॥३॥
tadA bhavattIrthamidaM samAgato
bhavo bhavatsevakadarshanAdRtaH 
prakAshamAsAdya puraH prachetasAm
upAdishat bhaktatamastava stavam 3

तदा भवत्तीर्थमिदं समागतो
As they reached this holy lake, it was visited by
भवो भवत्सेवकदर्शनादृत: ।
Shiva himself, who was desirous of seeing your devotees!
प्रकाशमासाद्य पुर: प्रचेतसाम्
He manifested himself in front of the Ten Prachetas
उपादिशत् भक्ततमस्तव स्तवम् ॥३॥
and that best of your devotees, taught them a great hymn of yours!

स्तवं जपन्तस्तममी जलान्तरे
भवन्तमासेविषतायुतं समा: ।
भवत्सुखास्वादरसादमीष्वियान्
बभूव कालो ध्रुववन्न शीघ्रता ॥४॥
stavaM japantastamamI jalAntare
bhavantamAseviShatAyutaM samAH 
bhavatsukhAsvAdarasAdamIShviyAn
babhUva kAlo dhruvavanna shIghratA 4

स्तवं जपन्तस्तममी जलान्तरे
Chanting that hymn, they, within those waters,
भवन्तमासेविषतायुतं समा: ।
meditated upon you for ten thousand years, called as an ayuta!
भवत्सुखास्वादरसादमीष्वियान्
Immersed in relishing the essence of your bliss, for them,
बभूव कालो ध्रुववन्न शीघ्रता ॥४॥
it took such a long time, and not quickly as it did for Dhruva!

तपोभिरेषामतिमात्रवर्धिभि:
स यज्ञहिंसानिरतोऽपि पावित: ।
पिताऽपि तेषां गृहयातनारद-
प्रदर्शितात्मा भवदात्मतां ययौ ॥५॥
tapobhireShAmatimAtravardhibhiH
sa yajnahiMsAniratopi pAvitaH 
pitApi teShAM gRhayAtanArada-
pradarshitAtmA bhavadAtmatAM yayau 5

तपोभिरेषामतिमात्रवर्धिभि:
Due to their penance which was growing in intensity,
स यज्ञहिंसानिरतोऽपि पावित: ।
despite performing many cruelties in yajnas, being purified,
पिताऽपि तेषां गृहयातनारद-
their father, whose residence was visited by Narada,
प्रदर्शितात्मा भवदात्मतां ययौ ॥५॥
was granted self-realization, and attained to your supreme self!

कृपाबलेनैव पुर: प्रचेतसां
प्रकाशमागा: पतगेन्द्रवाहन: ।
विराजि चक्रादिवरायुधांशुभिः
भुजाभिरष्टाभिरुदञ्चितद्युति: ॥६॥
kRpAbalenaiva puraH prachetasAM
prakAshamAgAH patagendravAhanaH 
virAji chakrAdivarAyudhAMshubhiH
bhujAbhiraShTAbhirudanchitadyutiH 6

कृपाबलेनैव पुर: प्रचेतसां
It is only by the power of your compassion, in front of those Prachetas,
प्रकाशमागा: पतगेन्द्रवाहन: ।
you manifested yourself, riding on the king of birds,
विराजि चक्रादिवरायुधांशुभिः
shining with the splendour of your chakra and other best of weapons,
भुजाभिरष्टाभिरुदञ्चितद्युति: ॥६॥
spreading your lustre with eight glorious arms!

प्रचेतसां तावदयाचतामपि
त्वमेव कारुण्यभराद्वरानदा: ।
भवद्विचिन्ताऽपि शिवाय देहिनां
भवत्वसौ रुद्रनुतिश्च कामदा ॥७॥
prachetasAM tAvadayAchatAmapi
tvameva kAruNyabharAdvarAnadAH 
bhavadvichintApi shivAya dehinAM
bhavatvasau rudranutishcha kAmadA 7

प्रचेतसां तावदयाचतामपि
To those Prachetas, even without them asking,
त्वमेव कारुण्यभराद्वरानदा: ।
you yourself, out of your compassion, granted boons!
भवद्विचिन्ताऽपि शिवाय देहिनां
Even the mere thought of you, may it cause well-being to creatures,
भवत्वसौ रुद्रनुतिश्च कामदा ॥७॥
and may that hymn of rudra ever grant one's desires!

अवाप्य कान्तां तनयां महीरुहां
तया रमध्वं दशलक्षवत्सरीम् ।
सुतोऽस्तु दक्षो ननु तत्क्षणाच्च मां
प्रयास्यथेति न्यगदो मुदैव तान् ॥८॥
avApya kAntAM tanayAM mahIruhAM
tayA ramadhvaM dashalakShavatsarIm 
sutostu dakSho nanu tatkShaNAchcha mAM
prayAsyatheti nyagado mudaiva tAn 8

अवाप्य कान्तां तनयां महीरुहां
"Begetting as a wife, the daughter of trees,
तया रमध्वं दशलक्षवत्सरीम् ।
rejoice with her, for ten lakh years!
सुतोऽस्तु दक्षो ननु तत्क्षणाच्च मां
May there be a son by the name of Daksha, and from that instant, me
प्रयास्यथेति न्यगदो मुदैव तान् ॥८॥
you shall all attain!" thus you spoke to them in joy!

ततश्च ते भूतलरोधिनस्तरून्
क्रुधा दहन्तो द्रुहिणेन वारिता: ।
द्रुमैश्च दत्तां तनयामवाप्य तां
त्वदुक्तकालं सुखिनोऽभिरेमिरे ॥९॥
tatashcha te bhUtalarodhinastarUn
krudhA dahanto druhiNena vAritAH 
drumaishcha dattAM tanayAmavApya tAM
tvaduktakAlaM sukhinobhiremire 9

ततश्च ते भूतलरोधिनस्तरून्
They then looked at the trees which prevented earth from seeing the sun,
क्रुधा दहन्तो द्रुहिणेन वारिता: ।
and with anger, started burning them, but were then stopped by Brahma.
द्रुमैश्च दत्तां तनयामवाप्य तां
Begetting a daughter granted to them by the trees,
त्वदुक्तकालं सुखिनोऽभिरेमिरे ॥९॥
for the time mentioned by you, they lived in happiness!

अवाप्य दक्षं च सुतं कृताध्वरा:
प्रचेतसो नारदलब्धया धिया ।
अवापुरानन्दपदं तथाविधः
त्वमीश वातालयनाथ पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
avApya dakShaM cha sutaM kRtAdhvarAH
prachetaso nAradalabdhayA dhiyA 
avApurAnandapadaM tathAvidhaH
tvamIsha vAtAlayanAtha pAhi mAm 10

अवाप्य दक्षं च सुतं कृताध्वरा:
Begetting a son named Daksha, performing the yajna,
प्रचेतसो नारदलब्धया धिया ।
those Prachetas, by knowledge granted by Narada,
अवापुरानन्दपदं तथाविधः
obtained the state of bliss, as planned!
त्वमीश वातालयनाथ पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
You, O Lord of the Abode of Wind, protect me!
