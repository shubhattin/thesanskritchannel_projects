अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्ततितमम् दशकम्
saptatitamam dashakam

The Seventieth group of Ten shlokas

सुदर्शन शाप मोक्ष: शङ्खचूड वध: अरिष्ट वध: च
sudarshana shApa mokShaH shankhachUDa vadhaH ariShTa vadhaH cha

Lifting Sudarshana's Curse
Killing Shanka Chooda and Arishta

इति त्वयि रसाकुलं रमितवल्लभे वल्लवा:
कदापि पुरमम्बिकाकमितुरम्बिकाकानने ।
समेत्य भवता समं निशि निषेव्य दिव्योत्सवं
सुखं सुषुपुरग्रसीद्व्रजपमुग्रनागस्तदा ॥१॥
iti tvayi rasAkulaM ramitavallabhe vallavAH
kadApi puramambikAkamiturambikAkAnane 
sametya bhavatA samaM nishi niShevya divyotsavaM
sukhaM suShupuragrasIdvrajapamugranAgastadA 1

इति त्वयि रसाकुलं रमितवल्लभे वल्लवा:
Thus as you were conducting the plays of Raasa, and the Gopikas were immersed in ecstacies, the Gopas
कदापि पुरमम्बिकाकमितुरम्बिकाकानने ।
once went to a temple of Lord Shiva, the beloved of Ambika, in the Ambika forest.
समेत्य भवता समं निशि निषेव्य दिव्योत्सवं
Having gone along with you, celebrating a grand festival, in the night,
सुखं सुषुपुरग्रसीद्व्रजपमुग्रनागस्तदा ॥१॥
as they were sleeping happily, a fierce snake grabbed hold of Nanda, the chief of Gopas!

समुन्मुखमथोल्मुकैरभिहतेऽपि तस्मिन् बलात्
अमुञ्चति भवत्पदे न्यपति पाहि पाहीति तै: ।
तदा खलु पदा भवान् समुपगम्य पस्पर्श तं
बभौ स च निजां तनुं समुपसाद्य वैद्याधरीम् ॥२॥
samunmukhamatholmukairabhihatepi tasmin balAt
amunchati bhavatpade nyapati pAhi pAhIti taiH 
tadA khalu padA bhavAn samupagamya pasparsha taM
babhau sa cha nijAM tanuM samupasAdya vaidyAdharIm 2

समुन्मुखमथोल्मुकैरभिहतेऽपि तस्मिन् बलात्
Looking upwards, despite being hit with burning logs of wood, with force,
अमुञ्चति भवत्पदे न्यपति पाहि पाहीति तै: ।
as it did not let go of Nanda, as the Gopas quickly fell at your feet crying for help,
तदा खलु पदा भवान् समुपगम्य पस्पर्श तं
didn't you then, approach it and touch it with your foot!
बभौ स च निजां तनुं समुपसाद्य वैद्याधरीम् ॥२॥
Upon which that snake regained his original form of a Vidyadhara!

सुदर्शनधर प्रभो ननु सुदर्शनाख्योऽस्म्यहं
मुनीन् क्वचिदपाहसं त इह मां व्यधुर्वाहसम् ।
भवत्पदसमर्पणादमलतां गतोऽस्मीत्यसौ
स्तुवन् निजपदं ययौ व्रजपदं च गोपा मुदा ॥३॥
sudarshanadhara prabho nanu sudarshanAkhyosmyahaM
munIn kvachidapAhasaM ta iha mAM vyadhurvAhasam 
bhavatpadasamarpaNAdamalatAM gatosmItyasau
stuvan nijapadaM yayau vrajapadaM cha gopA mudA 3

सुदर्शनधर प्रभो ननु सुदर्शनाख्योऽस्म्यहं
"O Lord who wields the Sudarshana Chakra! I am indeed also named as Sudarshana!
मुनीन् क्वचिदपाहसं त इह मां व्यधुर्वाहसम् ।
I once made fun of sages who then cursed me to turn into a python!
भवत्पदसमर्पणादमलतां गतोऽस्मीत्यसौ
By the touch of your foot, I have now been purified!" thus
स्तुवन् निजपदं ययौ व्रजपदं च गोपा मुदा ॥३॥
praising you, he went back to his original abodes, and Gopas too returned to Vraja.

कदापि खलु सीरिणा विहरति त्वयि स्त्रीजनै:
जहार धनदानुग: स किल शङ्खचूडोऽबला: ।
अतिद्रुतमनुद्रुतस्त्वमथ मुक्तनारीजनं
रुरोजिथ शिरोमणिं हलभृते च तस्याददा: ॥४॥
kadApi khalu sIriNA viharati tvayi strIjanaiH
jahAra dhanadAnugaH sa kila shankhachUDobalAH 
atidrutamanudrutastvamatha muktanArIjanaM
rurojitha shiromaNiM halabhRte cha tasyAdadAH 4

कदापि खलु सीरिणा विहरति त्वयि स्त्रीजनै:
One day as you were taking a stroll with Balarama, accompanied by the women folk,
जहार धनदानुग: स किल शङ्खचूडोऽबला: ।
didn't the follower of Kubera, named Shankhachooda kidnap those women!
अतिद्रुतमनुद्रुतस्त्वमथ मुक्तनारीजनं
Quickly going after him, releasing the women from his grip,
रुरोजिथ शिरोमणिं हलभृते च तस्याददा: ॥४॥
slaying him, you offered his crest jewel to Balarama!

दिनेषु च सुहृज्जनैस्सह वनेषु लीलापरं
मनोभवमनोहरं रसितवेणुनादामृतम् ।
भवन्तममरीदृशाममृतपारणादायिनं
विचिन्त्य किमु नालपन् विरहतापिता गोपिका: ॥५॥
dineShu cha suhRjjanaissaha vaneShu lIlAparaM
manobhavamanoharaM rasitaveNunAdAmRtam 
bhavantamamarIdRshAmamRtapAraNAdAyinaM
vichintya kimu nAlapan virahatApitA gopikAH 5

दिनेषु च सुहृज्जनैस्सह वनेषु लीलापरं
Immersed in sporting with your friends during the day in forests,
मनोभवमनोहरं रसितवेणुनादामृतम् ।
playing nectarine tunes on your flute that enchant manmatha himself,
भवन्तममरीदृशाममृतपारणादायिनं
your sight which quenches the sights of even the celestial maidens like drinking Amrita,
विचिन्त्य किमु नालपन् विरहतापिता गोपिका: ॥५॥
thinking of these, suffering their separation from you, in how many ways did they not lament, those Gopikas!

भोजराजभृतकस्त्वथ कश्चित् कष्टदुष्टपथदृष्टिररिष्ट: ।
निष्ठुराकृतिरपष्ठुनिनादस्तिष्ठते स्म भवते वृषरूपी ॥६॥
bhojarAjabhRtakastvatha kashchit kaShTaduShTapathadRShTirariShTaH 
niShThurAkRtirapaShThuninAdastiShThate sma bhavate vRSharUpI 6

भोजराजभृतकस्त्वथ कश्चित् कष्टदुष्टपथदृष्टिररिष्ट: ।
A follower of Kamsa, the king of Bhoja, by the name of Arishta, who was always looking to inflict cruelty and harm,
निष्ठुराकृतिरपष्ठुनिनादस्तिष्ठते स्म भवते वृषरूपी ॥६॥
bellowing loudly, in the terrifying form of a bull once appeared before you!

शाक्वरोऽथ जगतीधृतिहारी मूर्तिमेष बृहतीं प्रदधान: ।
पङ्क्तिमाशु परिघूर्ण्य पशूनां छन्दसां निधिमवाप भवन्तम् ॥७॥
shAkvarotha jagatIdhRtihArI mUrtimeSha bRhatIM pradadhAnaH 
panktimAshu parighUrNya pashUnAM ChandasAM nidhimavApa bhavantam 7

शाक्वरोऽथ जगतीधृतिहारी मूर्तिमेष बृहतीं प्रदधान: ।
Then, that bull, the remover of peace on earth, assuming that humongous form,
पङ्क्तिमाशु परिघूर्ण्य पशूनां छन्दसां निधिमवाप भवन्तम् ॥७॥
quickly entering the groups of cattle, approached closer to you, who is the treasure of bliss!

तुङ्गशृङ्गमुखमाश्वभियन्तं संगृहय्य रभसादभियं तम् ।
भद्ररूपमपि दैत्यमभद्रं मर्दयन्नमदय: सुरलोकम् ॥८॥
tungashRngamukhamAshvabhiyantaM saMgRhayya rabhasAdabhiyaM tam 
bhadrarUpamapi daityamabhadraM mardayannamadayaH suralokam 8

तुङ्गशृङ्गमुखमाश्वभियन्तं संगृहय्य रभसादभियं तम् ।
Swinging his head and thrusting his horns, as he rushed towards you, catching hold of him swiftly and fearlessly,
भद्ररूपमपि दैत्यमभद्रं मर्दयन्नमदय: सुरलोकम् ॥८॥
in your auspicious form, slaying that inauspicious Daitya, you delighted the groups of Devas!

चित्रमद्य भगवन् वृषघातात् सुस्थिराऽजनि वृषस्थितिरुर्व्याम् ।
वर्धते च वृषचेतसि भूयान् मोद इत्यभिनुतोऽसि सुरैस्त्वम् ॥९॥
chitramadya bhagavan vRShaghAtAt susthirAjani vRShasthitirurvyAm 
vardhate cha vRShachetasi bhUyAn moda ityabhinutosi suraistvam 9

चित्रमद्य भगवन् वृषघातात् सुस्थिराऽजनि वृषस्थितिरुर्व्याम् ।
How surprising O Lord! That by slaying that one Bull:vRSha, righteousness:vRShasthiti got firmly established on earth! 
वर्धते च वृषचेतसि भूयान् मोद इत्यभिनुतोऽसि सुरैस्त्वम् ॥९॥
"It also enhances the elation in the mind of Indra:vRSha!" thus you were worshipped by the Suras!

औक्षकाणि परिधावत दूरं वीक्ष्यतामयमिहोक्षविभेदी ।
इत्थमात्तहसितै: सह गोपैर्गेहगस्त्वमव वातपुरेश ॥१०॥
aukShakANi paridhAvata dUraM vIkShyatAmayamihokShavibhedI 
itthamAttahasitaiH saha gopairgehagastvamava vAtapuresha 10

औक्षकाणि परिधावत दूरं वीक्ष्यतामयमिहोक्षविभेदी ।
"O Bulls! Run Run far! See here, the Bull slayer is coming!"
इत्थमात्तहसितै: सह गोपैर्गेहगस्त्वमव वातपुरेश ॥१०॥
thus cracking jokes, along with the Gopas, you returned home, O Lord of the City of Wind! Protect me!
