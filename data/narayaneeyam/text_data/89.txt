अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकोनवतितमम् दशकम्
ekonanavatitamam dashakam

The Eighty-ninth group of Ten shlokas

वृकासुर कथा भृगु परीक्षणं च
vRkAsura kathA bhRgu parIkShaNam cha

The Story of Vrkasura and Sage Bhrugu's Test

रमाजाने जाने यदिह तव भक्तेषु विभवो
न सद्यस्सम्पद्यस्तदिह मदकृत्त्वादशमिनाम् ।
प्रशान्तिं कृत्वैव प्रदिशसि तत: काममखिलं
प्रशान्तेषु क्षिप्रं न खलु भवदीये च्युतिकथा ॥१॥
ramAjAne jAne yadiha tava bhakteShu vibhavo
na sadyassampadyastadiha madakRttvAdashaminAm 
prashAntiM kRtvaiva pradishasi tataH kAmamakhilaM
prashAnteShu kShipraM na khalu bhavadIye chyutikathA 1

रमाजाने जाने यदिह तव भक्तेषु विभवो
O Beloved of Ramaa, I know that among your devotees, prosperity
न सद्यस्सम्पद्यस्तदिह मदकृत्त्वादशमिनाम् ।
is not bestowed upon immediately, since it generates pride among the impatient!
प्रशान्तिं कृत्वैव प्रदिशसि तत: काममखिलं
Only after bestowing peace, do you bestow upon them all desires,
प्रशान्तेषु क्षिप्रं न खलु भवदीये च्युतिकथा ॥१॥
among those who are peaceful, there are no instaces where you failed them!

सद्य: प्रसादरुषितान् विधिशङ्करादीन्
केचिद्विभो निजगुणानुगुणं भजन्त: ।
भ्रष्टा भवन्ति बत कष्टमदीर्घदृष्ट्या
स्पष्टं वृकासुर उदाहरणं किलास्मिन् ॥२॥
sadyaH prasAdaruShitAn vidhishankarAdIn
kechidvibho nijaguNAnuguNaM bhajantaH 
bhraShTA bhavanti bata kaShTamadIrghadRShTyA
spaShTaM vRkAsura udAharaNaM kilAsmin 2

सद्य: प्रसादरुषितान् विधिशङ्करादीन्
Gods who are quickly pleased or angered, like Brahma and Shankara,
केचिद्विभो निजगुणानुगुणं भजन्त: ।
some people worship according to their tendencies, O Vibhu!
भ्रष्टा भवन्ति बत कष्टमदीर्घदृष्ट्या
They fall and suffer so many hardships due to this shortsightedness! Say,
स्पष्टं वृकासुर उदाहरणं किलास्मिन् ॥२॥
isn't the example of Vrikasura very clear in this regard!

शकुनिज: स तु नारदमेकदा
त्वरिततोषमपृच्छदधीश्वरम् ।
स च दिदेश गिरीशमुपासितुं
न तु भवन्तमबन्धुमसाधुषु ॥३॥
shakunijaH sa tu nAradamekadA
tvaritatoShamapRchChadadhIshvaram 
sa cha didesha girIshamupAsituM
na tu bhavantamabandhumasAdhuShu 3

शकुनिज: स तु नारदमेकदा
That son of Shakuni, Vrikasura, once to Narada,
त्वरिततोषमपृच्छदधीश्वरम् ।
posed the question, as to who is the God, who is quickly pleased!
स च दिदेश गिरीशमुपासितुं
He then instructed him to worship Shiva,
न तु भवन्तमबन्धुमसाधुषु ॥३॥
and not you, who is not favourable towards the wicked ones!

तपस्तप्त्वा घोरं स खलु कुपित: सप्तमदिने
शिर: छित्वा सद्य: पुरहरमुपस्थाप्य पुरत: ।
अतिक्षुद्रं रौद्रं शिरसि करदानेन निधनं
जगन्नाथाद्वव्रे भवति विमुखानां क्व शुभधी: ॥४॥
tapastaptvA ghoraM sa khalu kupitaH saptamadine
shiraH ChitvA sadyaH puraharamupasthApya purataH 
atikShudraM raudraM shirasi karadAnena nidhanaM
jagannAthAdvavre bhavati vimukhAnAM kva shubhadhIH 4

तपस्तप्त्वा घोरं स खलु कुपित: सप्तमदिने
Performing a severe penance, didn't he get angered on the seventh day!
शिर: छित्वा सद्य: पुरहरमुपस्थाप्य पुरत: ।
Severing his own head at once, he made Lord Shiva appear in front of him,
अतिक्षुद्रं रौद्रं शिरसि करदानेन निधनं
and asked for an extremely savage and brutal boon to be able to kill by placing his hand on someone's head,
जगन्नाथाद्वव्रे भवति विमुखानां क्व शुभधी: ॥४॥
from that lord of the world! Where are there auspicious thoughts in those averse to you!

मोक्तारं बन्धमुक्तो हरिणपतिरिव प्राद्रवत्सोऽथ रुद्रं
दैत्यात् भीत्या स्म देवो दिशि दिशि वलते पृष्ठतो दत्तदृष्टि: ।
तूष्णीके सर्वलोके तव पदमधिरोक्ष्यन्तमुद्वीक्ष्य शर्वं
दूरादेवाग्रतस्त्वं पटुवटुवपुषा तस्थिषे दानवाय ॥५॥
moktAraM bandhamukto hariNapatiriva prAdravatsotha rudraM
daityAt bhItyA sma devo dishi dishi valate pRShThato dattadRShTiH 
tUShNIke sarvaloke tava padamadhirokShyantamudvIkShya sharvaM
dUrAdevAgratastvaM paTuvaTuvapuShA tasthiShe dAnavAya 5

मोक्तारं बन्धमुक्तो हरिणपतिरिव प्राद्रवत्सोऽथ रुद्रं
As a Lion would pounce upon someone who liberated it, he then rushed towards Rudra!
दैत्यात् भीत्या स्म देवो दिशि दिशि वलते पृष्ठतो दत्तदृष्टि: ।
Afraid of that Daitya, that lord fled in all the directions as he kept looking backwards!
तूष्णीके सर्वलोके तव पदमधिरोक्ष्यन्तमुद्वीक्ष्य शर्वं
As all the worlds remained silent, and as he approached your abode, looking at that Sharva
दूरादेवाग्रतस्त्वं पटुवटुवपुषा तस्थिषे दानवाय ॥५॥
from afar, you took on the form of a skilled Brahmin boy and waited for the Danava!

भद्रं ते शाकुनेय भ्रमसि किमधुना त्वं पिशाचस्य वाचा
सन्देहश्चेन्मदुक्तौ तव किमु न करोष्यङ्गुलीमङ्गमौलौ ।
इत्थं त्वद्वाक्यमूढ: शिरसि कृतकर: सोऽपतच्छिन्नपातं
भ्रंशो ह्येवं परोपासितुरपि च गति: शूलिनोऽपि त्वमेव ॥६॥
bhadraM te shAkuneya bhramasi kimadhunA tvaM pishAchasya vAchA
sandehashchenmaduktau tava kimu na karoShyangulImangamaulau 
itthaM tvadvAkyamUDhaH shirasi kRtakaraH sopatachChinnapAtaM
bhraMsho hyevaM paropAsiturapi cha gatiH shUlinopi tvameva 6

भद्रं ते शाकुनेय भ्रमसि किमधुना त्वं पिशाचस्य वाचा
"Best wishes to you, O Son of Shakuni! Why are you unnecessarily running around based on the words of that ghost!
सन्देहश्चेन्मदुक्तौ तव किमु न करोष्यङ्गुलीमङ्गमौलौ ।
If you doubt my words, why don't you test his boon by placing the hand on your head!"
इत्थं त्वद्वाक्यमूढ: शिरसि कृतकर: सोऽपतच्छिन्नपातं
Thus deluded by your words, placing his hand on his own head, he fell like an uprooted tree!
भ्रंशो ह्येवं परोपासितुरपि च गति: शूलिनोऽपि त्वमेव ॥६॥
Thus fall those who seek other deities of worship! O Lord, you are the refuge for even the lord who wields the trident!

भृगुं किल सरस्वतीनिकटवासिनस्तापसा:
त्रिमूर्तिषु समादिशन्नधिकसत्त्वतां वेदितुम् ।
अयं पुनरनादरादुदितरुद्धरोषे विधौ
हरेऽपि च जिहिंसिषौ गिरिजया धृते त्वामगात् ॥७॥
bhRguM kila sarasvatInikaTavAsinastApasAH
trimUrtiShu samAdishannadhikasattvatAM veditum 
ayaM punaranAdarAduditaruddharoShe vidhau
harepi cha jihiMsiShau girijayA dhRte tvAmagAt 7

भृगुं किल सरस्वतीनिकटवासिनस्तापसा:
Didn't the sages who live on the banks of River Saraswati, once employ Sage Bhrigu,
त्रिमूर्तिषु समादिशन्नधिकसत्त्वतां वेदितुम् ।
on the task of finding out who among the trinity is most Sattvic!
अयं पुनरनादरादुदितरुद्धरोषे विधौ
He then, with utter disrespect enraged Brahma, who forcibly controlled his anger!
हरेऽपि च जिहिंसिषौ गिरिजया धृते त्वामगात् ॥७॥
Lord Shiva, when enraged was about to kill Sage Bhrgu, but was stopped by Goddess Parvati. He then, approached you!

सुप्तं रमाङ्कभुवि पङ्कजलोचनं त्वां
विप्रे विनिघ्नति पदेन मुदोत्थितस्त्वम् ।
सर्वं क्षमस्व मुनिवर्य भवेत् सदा मे
त्वत्पादचिह्नमिह भूषणमित्यवादी: ॥८॥
suptaM ramAnkabhuvi pankajalochanaM tvAM
vipre vinighnati padena mudotthitastvam 
sarvaM kShamasva munivarya bhavet sadA me
tvatpAdachihnamiha bhUShaNamityavAdIH 8

सुप्तं रमाङ्कभुवि पङ्कजलोचनं त्वां
As you with your lotus like eyes were lying asleep in the lap of Goddess Lakshmi,
विप्रे विनिघ्नति पदेन मुदोत्थितस्त्वम् ।
as that sage kicked you with his feet, you, waking up cheerfully,
सर्वं क्षमस्व मुनिवर्य भवेत् सदा मे
"Excuse all my doings, O Great Sage! The mark of your foot
त्वत्पादचिह्नमिह भूषणमित्यवादी: ॥८॥
shall always remain as an ornament on my chest!", thus you spoke!

निश्चित्य ते च सुदृढं त्वयि बद्धभावा:
सारस्वता मुनिवरा दधिरे विमोक्षम् ।
त्वामेवमच्युत पुनश्च्युतिदोषहीनं
सत्त्वोच्चयैकतनुमेव वयं भजाम: ॥९॥
nishchitya te cha sudRDhaM tvayi baddhabhAvAH
sArasvatA munivarA dadhire vimokSham 
tvAmevamachyuta punashchyutidoShahInaM
sattvochchayaikatanumeva vayaM bhajAmaH 9

निश्चित्य ते च सुदृढं त्वयि बद्धभावा:
Having arrived at a firm conclusion, bound in their devotion to you,
सारस्वता मुनिवरा दधिरे विमोक्षम् ।
the residents by the banks of river Saraswati, attained to liberation!
त्वामेवमच्युत पुनश्च्युतिदोषहीनं
O Achyuta, thus, you, who is free from the fault of abandonment,
सत्त्वोच्चयैकतनुमेव वयं भजाम: ॥९॥
who is the embodiment of utmost Sattva, we worship you alone!

जगत्सृष्ट्यादौ त्वां निगमनिवहैर्वन्दिभिरिव
स्तुतं विष्णो सच्चित्परमरसनिर्द्वैतवपुषम् ।
परात्मानं भूमन् पशुपवनिताभाग्यनिवहं
परीतापश्रान्त्यै पवनपुरवासिन् परिभजे ॥१०॥
jagatsRShTyAdau tvAM nigamanivahairvandibhiriva
stutaM viShNo sachchitparamarasanirdvaitavapuSham 
parAtmAnaM bhUman pashupavanitAbhAgyanivahaM
parItApashrAntyai pavanapuravAsin paribhaje 10

जगत्सृष्ट्यादौ त्वां निगमनिवहैर्वन्दिभिरिव
At the beginning of creation, the Vedas sang your praise, like the minstrels in a King's court,
स्तुतं विष्णो सच्चित्परमरसनिर्द्वैतवपुषम् ।
O Vishnu! you, who are of the form of the non-dual essence of sat and chit!
परात्मानं भूमन् पशुपवनिताभाग्यनिवहं
O Abundant One! You, who is the supreme self, who is the bundle of fortune of the cowherd women,
परीतापश्रान्त्यै पवनपुरवासिन् परिभजे ॥१०॥
for the pacification of my sufferings, O One who Resides in the City of Wind, I Worship you!
