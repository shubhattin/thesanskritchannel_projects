अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

त्र्यशीतितमम् दशकम्
tryashItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-third group of Ten shlokas

पौण्ड्रक वध: विविध वध: च
pauNDraka vadhaH vivdha vadhaH cha

Slaying of Paundraka and Vividha

रामेऽथ गोकुलगते प्रमदाप्रसक्ते
हूतानुपेतयमुनादमने मदान्धे ।
स्वैरं समारमति सेवकवादमूढो
दूतं न्ययुङ्क्त तव पौण्ड्रकवासुदेव: ॥१॥
rAmetha gokulagate pramadAprasakte
hUtAnupetayamunAdamane madAndhe 
svairaM samAramati sevakavAdamUDho
dUtaM nyayunkta tava pauNDrakavAsudevaH 1

रामेऽथ गोकुलगते प्रमदाप्रसक्ते
Once, as Balarama went to Gokula and was sporting with the women,
हूतानुपेतयमुनादमने मदान्धे ।
and blinded by intoxication, as he was busy diverting Yamuna, who did not arrive despite invitation,
स्वैरं समारमति सेवकवादमूढो
at that time, someone who was deluded by his follower's words,
दूतं न्ययुङ्क्त तव पौण्ड्रकवासुदेव: ॥१॥
by the name of Paundraka Vasudeva, sent forth a messenger to you!

नारायणोऽहमवतीर्ण इहास्मि भूमौ
धत्से किल त्वमपि मामकलक्षणानि ।
उत्सृज्य तानि शरणं व्रज मामिति त्वां
दूतो जगाद सकलैर्हसित: सभायाम् ॥२॥
nArAyaNohamavatIrNa ihAsmi bhUmau
dhatse kila tvamapi mAmakalakShaNAni 
utsRjya tAni sharaNaM vraja mAmiti tvAM
dUto jagAda sakalairhasitaH sabhAyAm 2

नारायणोऽहमवतीर्ण इहास्मि भूमौ
"I am Narayana who has now been incarnate in this world!
धत्से किल त्वमपि मामकलक्षणानि ।
You too seem to be wearing my signs and attributes, don't you!
उत्सृज्य तानि शरणं व्रज मामिति त्वां
Abandon all of them, and take refuge in me!" thus
दूतो जगाद सकलैर्हसित: सभायाम् ॥२॥
as his messenger conveyed his words, everyone in the court laughed at him!

दूतेऽथ यातवति यादवसैनिकैस्त्वं
यातो ददर्शिथ वपु: किल पौण्ड्रकीयम् ।
तापेन वक्षसि कृताङ्कमनल्पमूल्य-
श्रीकौस्तुभं मकरकुण्डलपीतचेलम् ॥३॥
dUtetha yAtavati yAdavasainikaistvaM
yAto dadarshitha vapuH kila pauNDrakIyam 
tApena vakShasi kRtAnkamanalpamUlya-
shrIkaustubhaM makarakuNDalapItachelam 3

दूतेऽथ यातवति यादवसैनिकैस्त्वं
Then, as the messenger went away, you, along with the Yadava soldiers
यातो ददर्शिथ वपु: किल पौण्ड्रकीयम् ।
didn't you go and see that form of Paundraka, with
तापेन वक्षसि कृताङ्कमनल्पमूल्य-
a mark made by heat on his chest, which was supposed to be Shrivatsa, a valuable jem stone
श्रीकौस्तुभं मकरकुण्डलपीतचेलम् ॥३॥
which is supposed to be Kaustubha, and wearing curved earrings and yellow silken garments!

कालायसं निजसुदर्शनमस्यतोऽस्य
कालानलोत्करकिरेण सुदर्शनेन ।
शीर्षं चकर्तिथ ममर्दिथ चास्य सेनां
तन्मित्रकाशिपशिरोऽपि चकर्थ काश्याम् ॥४॥
kAlAyasaM nijasudarshanamasyatosya
kAlAnalotkarakireNa sudarshanena 
shIrShaM chakartitha mamarditha chAsya senAM
tanmitrakAshipashiropi chakartha kAshyAm 4

कालायसं निजसुदर्शनमस्यतोऽस्य
As he threw his sudarshana made of black cast iron at you,
कालानलोत्करकिरेण सुदर्शनेन ।
you, with your Sudarshana, emanating the very fire of Kala,
शीर्षं चकर्तिथ ममर्दिथ चास्य सेनां
cut off his head, crushed his army,
तन्मित्रकाशिपशिरोऽपि चकर्थ काश्याम् ॥४॥
and also lopped off the head of his friend Kashi, which fell all the way in the land of Kashi!

जाल्येन बालकगिराऽपि किलाहमेव
श्रीवासुदेव इति रूढमतिश्चिरं स: ।
सायुज्यमेव भवदैक्यधिया गतोऽभूत्
को नाम कस्य सुकृतं कथमित्यवेयात् ॥५॥
jAlyena bAlakagirApi kilAhameva
shrIvAsudeva iti rUDhamatishchiraM saH 
sAyujyameva bhavadaikyadhiyA gatobhUt
ko nAma kasya sukRtaM kathamityaveyAt 5

जाल्येन बालकगिराऽपि किलाहमेव
Either due to stupidity, or through childish words, I myself
श्रीवासुदेव इति रूढमतिश्चिरं स: ।
am Vaasudeva, thinking thus for a long time, firmly, he
सायुज्यमेव भवदैक्यधिया गतोऽभूत्
attained to unity with you, with a focused mind!
को नाम कस्य सुकृतं कथमित्यवेयात् ॥५॥
Who knows about the what and how of virtues of anyone!

काशीश्वरस्य तनयोऽथ सुदक्षिणाख्य:
शर्वं प्रपूज्य भवते विहिताभिचार: ।
कृत्यानलं कमपि बाणरणातिभीतै:
भूतै: कथञ्चन वृतै: सममभ्यमुञ्चत् ॥६॥
kAshIshvarasya tanayotha sudakShiNAkhyaH
sharvaM prapUjya bhavate vihitAbhichAraH 
kRtyAnalaM kamapi bANaraNAtibhItaiH
bhUtaiH kathanchana vRtaiH samamabhyamunchat 6

काशीश्वरस्य तनयोऽथ सुदक्षिणाख्य:
Then, the son of the King of Kashi, by the name of Sudakshina,
शर्वं प्रपूज्य भवते विहिताभिचार: ।
worshipping Shiva, performing occult practices on you,
कृत्यानलं कमपि बाणरणातिभीतै:
released the fire of some kind of Kritya, and the ones extremely frightened in the battle with Bana,
भूतै: कथञ्चन वृतै: सममभ्यमुञ्चत् ॥६॥
somehow he caught those Bhutas too, and let them loose on you!

तालप्रमाणचरणामखिलं दहन्तीं
कृत्यां विलोक्य चकितै: कथितोऽपि पौरै: ।
द्यूतोत्सवे किमपि नो चलितो विभो त्वं
पार्श्वस्थमाशु विससर्जिथ कालचक्रम् ॥७॥
tAlapramANacharaNAmakhilaM dahantIM
kRtyAM vilokya chakitaiH kathitopi pauraiH 
dyUtotsave kimapi no chalito vibho tvaM
pArshvasthamAshu visasarjitha kAlachakram 7

तालप्रमाणचरणामखिलं दहन्तीं
With palm tree like legs, burning everything along the way,
कृत्यां विलोक्य चकितै: कथितोऽपि पौरै: ।
looking at that Kritya, as the bewildered citizens informed you, 
द्यूतोत्सवे किमपि नो चलितो विभो त्वं
immersed in the play of dice, not moving a bit, you O Vibhu,
पार्श्वस्थमाशु विससर्जिथ कालचक्रम् ॥७॥
let out the Chakra which was lying beside you!

अभ्यापतत्यमितधाम्नि भवन्महास्त्रे
हा हेति विद्रुतवती खलु घोरकृत्या।
रोषात् सुदक्षिणमदक्षिणचेष्टितं तं
पुप्लोष चक्रमपि काशिपुरीमधाक्षीत् ॥८॥
abhyApatatyamitadhAmni bhavanmahAstre
hA heti vidrutavatI khalu ghorakRtyA
roShAt sudakShiNamadakShiNacheShTitaM taM
puploSha chakramapi kAshipurImadhAkShIt 8

अभ्यापतत्यमितधाम्नि भवन्महास्त्रे
As that great astra of yours with utmost brilliance fell upon her,
हा हेति विद्रुतवती खलु घोरकृत्या।
didn't that ferocious Kritya, run away crying in terror!
रोषात् सुदक्षिणमदक्षिणचेष्टितं तं
Enraged, that Sudakshina who engaged in improper conduct,
पुप्लोष चक्रमपि काशिपुरीमधाक्षीत् ॥८॥
that Chakra burnt down and in the process burnt down the entire city of Kashi!

स खलु विविदो रक्षोघाते कृतोपकृति: पुरा
तव तु कलया मृत्युं प्राप्तुं तदा खलतां गत: ।
नरकसचिवो देशक्लेशं सृजन् नगरान्तिके
झटिति हलिना युध्यन्नद्धा पपात तलाहत: ॥९॥
sa khalu vivido rakShoghAte kRtopakRtiH purA
tava tu kalayA mRtyuM prAptuM tadA khalatAM gataH 
narakasachivo deshakleshaM sRjan nagarAntike
jhaTiti halinA yudhyannaddhA papAta talAhataH 9

स खलु विविदो रक्षोघाते कृतोपकृति: पुरा
Didn't that vanara, Vivida, who once helped you in destroying Rakshasas,
तव तु कलया मृत्युं प्राप्तुं तदा खलतां गत: ।
with a desire to find death by an aspect of you, take to wicked ways!
नरकसचिवो देशक्लेशं सृजन् नगरान्तिके
Being the minister of Narakasura, creating trouble to the people in the city of Dwaraka,
झटिति हलिना युध्यन्नद्धा पपात तलाहत: ॥९॥
was quickly felled by the blow of Balarama in a battle!

साम्बं कौरव्यपुत्रीहरणनियमितं सान्त्वनार्थी कुरूणां
यातस्तद्वाक्यरोषोद्धृतकरिनगरो मोचयामास राम: ।
ते घात्या: पाण्डवेयैरिति यदुपृतनां नामुचस्त्वं तदानीं
तं त्वां दुर्बोधलीलं पवनपुरपते तापशान्त्यै निषेवे ॥१०॥
sAmbaM kauravyaputrIharaNaniyamitaM sAntvanArthI kurUNAM
yAtastadvAkyaroShoddhRtakarinagaro mochayAmAsa rAmaH 
te ghAtyAH pANDaveyairiti yadupRtanAM nAmuchastvaM tadAnIM
taM tvAM durbodhalIlaM pavanapurapate tApashAntyai niSheve 10

साम्बं कौरव्यपुत्रीहरणनियमितं सान्त्वनार्थी कुरूणां
As Samba was captured by the Kurus when he went to abduct their daughter, in order to pacify the Kurus
यातस्तद्वाक्यरोषोद्धृतकरिनगरो मोचयामास राम: ।
Balarama went there, but enraged by their words, lifted up Hastinapura, and released Samba!
ते घात्या: पाण्डवेयैरिति यदुपृतनां नामुचस्त्वं तदानीं
Saying that they must be killed by the Pandavas, you did not set forth the army of Yadavas then!
तं त्वां दुर्बोधलीलं पवनपुरपते तापशान्त्यै निषेवे ॥१०॥
To that you, who is of unfathomable playful acts, O Lord of the City of Wind, I offer my prayers in order to pacify my suffering!
