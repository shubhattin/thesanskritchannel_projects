ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

त्रयस्त्रिंशम् दशकम्
trayastriMsham dashakam

The Thirty-third group of Ten shlokas

अम्बरीष चरितम्
ambarISha charitam

The Story of Ambarisha

वैवस्वताख्यमनुपुत्रनभागजात-
नाभागनामकनरेन्द्रसुतोऽम्बरीष: ।
सप्तार्णवावृतमहीदयितोऽपि रेमे
त्वत्सङ्गिषु त्वयि च मग्नमनास्सदैव ॥१॥
vaivasvatAkhyamanuputranabhAgajAta-
nAbhAganAmakanarendrasutombarIShaH 
saptArNavAvRtamahIdayitopi reme
tvatsangiShu tvayi cha magnamanAssadaiva 1

वैवस्वताख्यमनुपुत्रनभागजात-
The Manu named Vaivasvata, had a son named Nabhaaga,
नाभागनामकनरेन्द्रसुतोऽम्बरीष: ।
whose son, king Naabhaaga had a son, named Ambarisha!
सप्तार्णवावृतमहीदयितोऽपि रेमे
Despite being the king of earth surrounded by seven seas, he rejoiced
त्वत्सङ्गिषु त्वयि च मग्नमनास्सदैव ॥१॥
in the company of your devotees and with his mind always immersed in you!

त्वत्प्रीतये सकलमेव वितन्वतोऽस्य
भक्त्यैव देव नचिरादभृथा: प्रसादम् ।
येनास्य याचनमृतेऽप्यभिरक्षणार्थं
चक्रं भवान् प्रविततार सहस्रधारम् ॥२॥
tvatprItaye sakalameva vitanvatosya
bhaktyaiva deva nachirAdabhRthAH prasAdam 
yenAsya yAchanamRtepyabhirakShaNArthaM
chakraM bhavAn pravitatAra sahasradhAram 2

त्वत्प्रीतये सकलमेव वितन्वतोऽस्य
As he kept performing all activity to please you,
भक्त्यैव देव नचिरादभृथा: प्रसादम् ।
with devotion, O Lord! In no time he gained your grace!
येनास्य याचनमृतेऽप्यभिरक्षणार्थं
Due to this, despite him asking, for his protection,
चक्रं भवान् प्रविततार सहस्रधारम् ॥२॥
you engaged the chakra of a thousand spikes!

स द्वादशीव्रतमथो भवदर्चनार्थं
वर्षं दधौ मधुवने यमुनोपकण्ठे ।
पत्न्या समं सुमनसा महतीं वितन्वन्
पूजां द्विजेषु विसृजन् पशुषष्टिकोटिम् ॥३॥
sa dvAdashIvratamatho bhavadarchanArthaM
varShaM dadhau madhuvane yamunopakaNThe 
patnyA samaM sumanasA mahatIM vitanvan
pUjAM dvijeShu visRjan pashuShaShTikoTim 3

स द्वादशीव्रतमथो भवदर्चनार्थं
He then followed the Dvadashi Vrata for your worship
वर्षं दधौ मधुवने यमुनोपकण्ठे ।
for a period of an year, in Madhuvana by the banks of Yamuna.
पत्न्या समं सुमनसा महतीं वितन्वन्
Along with his loving wife, performing the great
पूजां द्विजेषु विसृजन् पशुषष्टिकोटिम् ॥३॥
Puja, he donated sixty crores of cattle to the Dvijas!

तत्राथ पारणदिने भवदर्चनान्ते
दुर्वाससाऽस्य मुनिना भवनं प्रपेदे ।
भोक्तुं वृतश्चस नृपेण परार्तिशीलो
मन्दं जगाम यमुनां नियमान्विधास्यन् ॥४॥
tatrAtha pAraNadine bhavadarchanAnte
durvAsasAsya muninA bhavanaM prapede 
bhoktuM vRtashchasa nRpeNa parArtishIlo
mandaM jagAma yamunAM niyamAnvidhAsyan 4

तत्राथ पारणदिने भवदर्चनान्ते
And then, on the day of culmination, at the end of your worship,
दुर्वाससाऽस्य मुनिना भवनं प्रपेदे ।
he returned to his mansion along with Sage Durvasa.
भोक्तुं वृतश्चस नृपेण परार्तिशीलो
Invited by the king for a feast, that sage who was infamous for troubling others,
मन्दं जगाम यमुनां नियमान्विधास्यन् ॥४॥
slowly went to River Yamuna to perform his rituals.

राज्ञाऽथ पारणमुहूर्तसमाप्तिखेदाद्
वारैव पारणमकारि भवत्परेण ।
प्राप्तो मुनिस्तदथ दिव्यदृशा विजानन्
क्षिप्यन् क्रुधोद्धृतजटो विततान कृत्याम् ॥५॥
rAjnAtha pAraNamuhUrtasamAptikhedAd
vAraiva pAraNamakAri bhavatpareNa 
prApto munistadatha divyadRshA vijAnan
kShipyan krudhoddhRtajaTo vitatAna kRtyAm 5

राज्ञाऽथ पारणमुहूर्तसमाप्तिखेदाद्
Then the King, worried, as the auspicious time to end the Vrata was coming to a close,
वारैव पारणमकारि भवत्परेण ।
with his devotion to you, culminated the fast with water itself!
प्राप्तो मुनिस्तदथ दिव्यदृशा विजानन्
The sage then arriving there and with his divine insight realizing what had happened,
क्षिप्यन् क्रुधोद्धृतजटो विततान कृत्याम् ॥५॥
in anger, threw a lock of his matted hair on the ground, giving rise to Kritya!

कृत्यां च तामसिधरां भुवनं दहन्तीम्
अग्रेऽभिवीक्ष्यनृपतिर्न पदाच्चकम्पे ।
त्वद्भक्तबाधमभिवीक्ष्य सुदर्शनं ते
कृत्यानलं शलभयन् मुनिमन्वधावीत् ॥६॥
kRtyAM cha tAmasidharAM bhuvanaM dahantIm
agrebhivIkShyanRpatirna padAchchakampe 
tvadbhaktabAdhamabhivIkShya sudarshanaM te
kRtyAnalaM shalabhayan munimanvadhAvIt 6

कृत्यां च तामसिधरां भुवनं दहन्तीम्
That Kritya, wielding a great sword, and scorching the very earth on which she stood,
अग्रेऽभिवीक्ष्यनृपतिर्न पदाच्चकम्पे ।
looking at her in front of him, the king did not waver from where he stood!
त्वद्भक्तबाधमभिवीक्ष्य सुदर्शनं ते
Sudarshana Chakra looking at your devotee in trouble,
कृत्यानलं शलभयन् मुनिमन्वधावीत् ॥६॥
put out the fire of Kritya and started chasing the sage!

धावन्नशेषभुवनेषु भिया स पश्यन्
विश्वत्र चक्रमपि ते गतवान् विरिञ्चम् ।
क: कालचक्रमतिलङ्घयतीत्यपास्त:
शर्वं ययौ स च भवन्तमवन्दतैव ॥७॥
dhAvannasheShabhuvaneShu bhiyA sa pashyan
vishvatra chakramapi te gatavAn virincham 
kaH kAlachakramatilanghayatItyapAstaH
sharvaM yayau sa cha bhavantamavandataiva 7

धावन्नशेषभुवनेषु भिया स पश्यन्
Running across all the worlds in fright, looking at
विश्वत्र चक्रमपि ते गतवान् विरिञ्चम् ।
the chakra following him everywhere, he went to Brahma!
क: कालचक्रमतिलङ्घयतीत्यपास्त:
As he dismissed him saying, "Who can overturn the wheel of Time!",
शर्वं ययौ स च भवन्तमवन्दतैव ॥७॥
he then went to Shiva, who too just bowed down to you!

भूयो भवन्निलयमेत्य मुनिं नमन्तं
प्रोचे भवानहमृषे ननु भक्तदास: ।
ज्ञानं तपश्च विनयान्वितमेव मान्यं
याह्यम्बरीषपदमेव भजेति भूमन् ॥८॥
bhUyo bhavannilayametya muniM namantaM
proche bhavAnahamRShe nanu bhaktadAsaH 
jnAnaM tapashcha vinayAnvitameva mAnyaM
yAhyambarIShapadameva bhajeti bhUman 8

भूयो भवन्निलयमेत्य मुनिं नमन्तं
Once again, as the sage reached your abode and bowed before you,
प्रोचे भवानहमृषे ननु भक्तदास: ।
you spoke, "O Sage! I am but a slave of my devotees!
ज्ञानं तपश्च विनयान्वितमेव मान्यं
Knowledge and Penance are only respected when combined with modesty!
याह्यम्बरीषपदमेव भजेति भूमन् ॥८॥
Go back to Ambarisha's abode and pray!" you spoke thus O Abundant One!

तावत्समेत्य मुनिना स गृहीतपादो
राजाऽपसृत्य भवदस्त्रमसावनौषीत् ।
चक्रे गते मुनिरदादखिलाशिषोऽस्मै
त्वद्भक्तिमागसि कृतेऽपि कृपां च शंसन् ॥९॥
tAvatsametya muninA sa gRhItapAdo
rAjApasRtya bhavadastramasAvanauShIt 
chakre gate muniradAdakhilAshiShosmai
tvadbhaktimAgasi kRtepi kRpAM cha shaMsan 9

तावत्समेत्य मुनिना स गृहीतपादो
As he was then approached by the sage and his feet were clasped,
राजाऽपसृत्य भवदस्त्रमसावनौषीत् ।
the king moved aside and sang the praise of your weapon!
चक्रे गते मुनिरदादखिलाशिषोऽस्मै
As the chakra left, the sage granted him all his kinds of blessings!
त्वद्भक्तिमागसि कृतेऽपि कृपां च शंसन् ॥९॥
Despite being troubled, he praised the king's devotion and compassion!

राजा प्रतीक्ष्य मुनिमेकसमामनाश्वान्
सम्भोज्य साधु तमृषिं विसृजन् प्रसन्नम् ।
भुक्त्वा स्वयं त्वयि ततोऽपि दृढं रतोऽभूत्
सायुज्यमाप च स मां पवनेश पाया: ॥१०॥
rAjA pratIkShya munimekasamAmanAshvAn
sambhojya sAdhu tamRShiM visRjan prasannam 
bhuktvA svayaM tvayi tatopi dRDhaM ratobhUt
sAyujyamApa cha sa mAM pavanesha pAyAH 10

राजा प्रतीक्ष्य मुनिमेकसमामनाश्वान्
The king then, waited for the sage for an entire year without eating,
सम्भोज्य साधु तमृषिं विसृजन् प्रसन्नम् ।
then after feeding him well and sending him back pleased,
भुक्त्वा स्वयं त्वयि ततोऽपि दृढं रतोऽभूत्
he himself ate, getting even firmer in his devotion to you!
सायुज्यमाप च स मां पवनेश पाया: ॥१०॥
He then attained unity with your own self! That you, O Lord, the Ruler of Wind! Protect me!
