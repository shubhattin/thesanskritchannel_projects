ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

द्विचत्वारिंशम् दशकम्
dvichatvAriMsham dashakam

The Forty-second group of Ten shlokas

शकट असुर निग्रह:
shakaTa asura nighraH

Shakatasura's Defeat

कदापि जन्मर्क्षदिने तव प्रभो निमन्त्रितज्ञातिवधूमहीसुरा ।
महानसस्त्वां सविधे निधाय सा महानसादौ ववृते व्रजेश्वरी ॥१॥
kadApi janmarkShadine tava prabho nimantritajnAtivadhUmahIsurA 
mahAnasastvAM savidhe nidhAya sA mahAnasAdau vavRte vrajeshvarI 1

कदापि जन्मर्क्षदिने तव प्रभो निमन्त्रितज्ञातिवधूमहीसुरा ।
One day, on the occasion of your birthday, O Lord! Inviting all relatives, their families and Brahmins,
महानसस्त्वां सविधे निधाय सा महानसादौ ववृते व्रजेश्वरी ॥१॥
placing you by the shade of a huge cart, the Queen of the Vrajas Yashoda, was busy in the kitchens.

ततो भवत्त्राणनियुक्तबालकप्रभीतिसङ्क्रन्दनसङ्कुलारवै: ।
विमिश्रमश्रावि भवत्समीपत: परिस्फुटद्दारुचटच्चटारव: ॥२॥
tato bhavattrANaniyuktabAlakaprabhItisankrandanasankulAravaiH 
vimishramashrAvi bhavatsamIpataH parisphuTaddAruchaTachchaTAravaH 2

ततो भवत्त्राणनियुक्तबालकप्रभीतिसङ्क्रन्दनसङ्कुलारवै: ।
Then, along with the terrified cries of the children placed to look after you,
विमिश्रमश्रावि भवत्समीपत: परिस्फुटद्दारुचटच्चटारव: ॥२॥
was heard a ruckus from near you, mixed with the sounds of the creaking and shattering of wood!

ततस्तदाकर्णनसम्भ्रमश्रमप्रकम्पिवक्षोजभरा व्रजाङ्गना: ।
भवन्तमन्तर्ददृशुस्समन्ततो विनिष्पतद्दारुणदारुमध्यगम् ॥३॥
tatastadAkarNanasambhramashramaprakampivakShojabharA vrajAnganAH 
bhavantamantardadRshussamantato viniShpataddAruNadArumadhyagam 3

ततस्तदाकर्णनसम्भ्रमश्रमप्रकम्पिवक्षोजभरा व्रजाङ्गना: ।
Then, the women of Vraja upon hearing that ruckus, terrified with their heavy breasts heaving in anguish
भवन्तमन्तर्ददृशुस्समन्ततो विनिष्पतद्दारुणदारुमध्यगम् ॥३॥
looked at you in the middle, with large cogs of wood shattered all around you!

शिशोरहो किं किमभूदिति द्रुतं प्रधाव्य नन्द: पशुपाश्च भूसुरा: ।
भवन्तमालोक्य यशोदया धृतं समाश्वसन्नश्रुजलार्द्रलोचना: ॥४॥
shishoraho kiM kimabhUditi drutaM pradhAvya nandaH pashupAshcha bhUsurAH 
bhavantamAlokya yashodayA dhRtaM samAshvasannashrujalArdralochanAH 4

शिशोरहो किं किमभूदिति द्रुतं प्रधाव्य नन्द: पशुपाश्च भूसुरा: ।
"O What happened to the child! What Happened!" thus quickly gathering, Nanda, the cowherds and other Brahmins
भवन्तमालोक्य यशोदया धृतं समाश्वसन्नश्रुजलार्द्रलोचना: ॥४॥
looking at you held by Yashoda, felt relieved with their eyes wet with tears!

कस्को नु कौतस्कुत एष विस्मयो विशङ्कटं यच्छकटं विपाटितम् ।
न कारणं किञ्चिदिहेति ते स्थिता: स्वनासिकादत्तकरास्त्वदीक्षका: ॥५॥
kasko nu kautaskuta eSha vismayo vishankaTaM yachChakaTaM vipATitam 
na kAraNaM kinchidiheti te sthitAH svanAsikAdattakarAstvadIkShakAH 5

कस्को नु कौतस्कुत एष विस्मयो विशङ्कटं यच्छकटं विपाटितम् ।
"What! How! Where! Who did it! How shocking that this huge cart got shattered 
न कारणं किञ्चिदिहेति ते स्थिता: स्वनासिकादत्तकरास्त्वदीक्षका: ॥५॥
with no apparent reason!" thus wondering they stood with their hands on their noses, looking at you!

कुमारकस्यास्य पयोधरार्थिन: प्ररोदने लोलपदाम्बुजाहतम् ।
मया मया दृष्टमनो विपर्यगादितीश ते पालकबालका जगु: ॥६॥
kumArakasyAsya payodharArthinaH prarodane lolapadAmbujAhatam 
mayA mayA dRShTamano viparyagAditIsha te pAlakabAlakA jaguH 6

कुमारकस्यास्य पयोधरार्थिन: प्ररोदने लोलपदाम्बुजाहतम् ।
"This boy as he was crying for milk, kicking his lotus like legs, they struck that cart!
मया मया दृष्टमनो विपर्यगादितीश ते पालकबालका जगु: ॥६॥
I saw! I saw! That cart then got overturned!" thus O Lord! Those boys, your guardians spoke!

भिया तदा किञ्चिदजानतामिदं कुमारकाणामतिदुर्घटं वच: ।
भवत्प्रभावाविदुरैरितीरितं मनागिवाशङ्क्यत दृष्टपूतनै: ॥७॥
bhiyA tadA kinchidajAnatAmidaM kumArakANAmatidurghaTaM vachaH 
bhavatprabhAvAvidurairitIritaM manAgivAshankyata dRShTapUtanaiH 7

भिया तदा किञ्चिदजानतामिदं कुमारकाणामतिदुर्घटं वच: ।
These words of the frightened children, spoken in ignorance, was highly improbable,
भवत्प्रभावाविदुरैरितीरितं मनागिवाशङ्क्यत दृष्टपूतनै: ॥७॥
may be for those who are unaware of your prowess! But those who witnessed what happened with Putana, they started to wonder just a little!

प्रवालताम्रं किमिदं पदं क्षतं सरोजरम्यौ नु करौ विरोजितौ।
इति प्रसर्पत्करुणातरङ्गितास्त्वदङ्गमापस्पृशुरङ्गनाजना: ॥८॥
pravAlatAmraM kimidaM padaM kShataM sarojaramyau nu karau virojitau
iti prasarpatkaruNAtarangitAstvadangamApaspRshuranganAjanAH 8

प्रवालताम्रं किमिदं पदं क्षतं सरोजरम्यौ नु करौ विरोजितौ।
"Tender leaves like feet, have they been hurt! Charming lotus like hands have they been bruised!"
इति प्रसर्पत्करुणातरङ्गितास्त्वदङ्गमापस्पृशुरङ्गनाजना: ॥८॥
thus flowing with waves of love and care, those maidens started caressing your hands and feet!

अये सुतं देहि जगत्पते: कृपातरङ्गपातात्परिपातमद्य मे ।
इति स्म सङ्गृह्य पिता त्वदङ्गकं मुहुर्मुहु: श्लिष्यति जातकण्टक: ॥९॥
aye sutaM dehi jagatpateH kRpAtarangapAtAtparipAtamadya me 
iti sma sangRhya pitA tvadangakaM muhurmuhuH shliShyati jAtakaNTakaH 9

अये सुतं देहि जगत्पते: कृपातरङ्गपातात्परिपातमद्य मे ।
"Hey! Give my son to me! He was protected today by the showers of the lord's compassion!"
इति स्म सङ्गृह्य पिता त्वदङ्गकं मुहुर्मुहु: श्लिष्यति जातकण्टक: ॥९॥
thus your father taking you into his arms, embraced you again and again in ecstacy!

अनोनिलीन: किल हन्तुमागत: सुरारिरेवं भवता विहिंसित: ।
रजोऽपि नो दृष्टममुष्य तत्कथं स शुद्धसत्त्वे त्वयि लीनवान् ध्रुवम् ॥१०॥
anonilInaH kila hantumAgataH surArirevaM bhavatA vihiMsitaH 
rajopi no dRShTamamuShya tatkathaM sa shuddhasattve tvayi lInavAn dhruvam 10

अनोनिलीन: किल हन्तुमागत: सुरारिरेवं भवता विहिंसित: ।
Didn't the asura who came to kill you disguised as a cart, in turn get killed this way!
रजोऽपि नो दृष्टममुष्य तत्कथं स शुद्धसत्त्वे त्वयि लीनवान् ध्रुवम् ॥१०॥
Even a trace of his activity was not to be seen! How could it be! He must surely have dissolved into the pure being that is you!

प्रपूजितैस्तत्र ततो द्विजातिभिर्विशेषतो लम्भितमङ्गलाशिष: ।
व्रजं निजैर्बाल्यरसैर्विमोहयन् मरुत्पुराधीश रुजां जहीहि मे ॥११॥
prapUjitaistatra tato dvijAtibhirvisheShato lambhitamangalAshiShaH 
vrajaM nijairbAlyarasairvimohayan marutpurAdhIsha rujAM jahIhi me 11

प्रपूजितैस्तत्र ततो द्विजातिभिर्विशेषतो लम्भितमङ्गलाशिष: ।
Everybody gathered there were honoured well, and the Brahmins especially extended their auspicious blessings!
व्रजं निजैर्बाल्यरसैर्विमोहयन् मरुत्पुराधीश रुजां जहीहि मे ॥११॥
Enchanting the entire land of Vraja with the plays of your child like form, O Ruler of the City of Wind, drive my suffering away!
