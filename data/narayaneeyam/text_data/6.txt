ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

षष्ठम् दशकम्
ShaShTham dashakam

The Sixth group of Ten shlokas

विराट्  स्वरूप वर्णनम्
virAT svarUpa varNanam

Description of The Body of The Creator

एवं चतुर्दशजगन्मयतां गतस्य
पातालमीश तव पादतलं वदन्ति ।
पादोर्ध्वदेशमपि देव रसातलं ते
गुल्फद्वयं खलु महातलमद्भुतात्मन् ॥१॥
evaM chaturdashajaganmayatAM gatasya
pAtAlamIsha tava pAdatalaM vadanti 
pAdordhvadeshamapi deva rasAtalaM te
gulphadvayaM khalu mahAtalamadbhutAtman 1

एवं चतुर्दशजगन्मयतां गतस्य
Thus, of that which has been manifested in Fourteen different worlds,
पातालमीश तव पादतलं वदन्ति ।
O Lord, pAtAla is said to be the soles of your feet!
पादोर्ध्वदेशमपि देव रसातलं ते
The upper part of your feet, O Luminant One! is called as rasAtala,
गुल्फद्वयं खलु महातलमद्भुतात्मन् ॥१॥
and aren't your pair of ankles said to be mahAtala, O One of A Wondrous Self!

जङ्घे तलातलमथो सुतलं च जानू
किञ्चोरुभागयुगलं वितलातले द्वे ।
क्षोणीतलं जघनमम्बरमङ्ग नाभिः
वक्षश्च शक्रनिलयस्तव चक्रपाणे ॥२॥
janghe talAtalamatho sutalaM cha jAnU
kinchorubhAgayugalaM vitalAtale dve 
kShoNItalaM jaghanamambaramanga nAbhiH
vakShashcha shakranilayastava chakrapANe 2

जङ्घे तलातलमथो सुतलं च जानू
Both the shins are said to be talAtala and the two knees are sutala.
किञ्चोरुभागयुगलं वितलातले द्वे ।
And, the pair of your thighs are the two worlds of vitala and atala.
क्षोणीतलं जघनमम्बरमङ्ग नाभिः
Your hip is kShoNItala(earth), and your navel, the sky!
वक्षश्च शक्रनिलयस्तव चक्रपाणे ॥२॥
Your chest is said to be the abode of indra(svarga), O One Who Wields the Chakra!

ग्रीवा महस्तव मुखं च जनस्तपस्तु
फालं शिरस्तव समस्तमयस्य सत्यम् ।
एवं जगन्मयतनो जगदाश्रितैर-
प्यन्यैर्निबद्धवपुषे भगवन्नमस्ते ॥३॥
grIvA mahastava mukhaM cha janastapastu
phAlaM shirastava samastamayasya satyam 
evaM jaganmayatano jagadAshritaira-
pyanyairnibaddhavapuShe bhagavannamaste 3

ग्रीवा महस्तव मुखं च जनस्तपस्तु
Your neck is maharloka, and your face, is janaloka. tapaloka is
फालं शिरस्तव समस्तमयस्य सत्यम् ।
your forehead. The head of You, who is everything, is satyaloka.
एवं जगन्मयतनो जगदाश्रितैर-
Thus O Lord whose body is the entire universe! Everything that constitutes the entire creation,
प्यन्यैर्निबद्धवपुषे भगवन्नमस्ते ॥३॥
O One who has all of it as your body! O Radiant One! I bow down to You!

त्वद्ब्रह्मरन्ध्रपदमीश्वर विश्वकन्द
छन्दांसि केशव घनास्तव केशपाशा: ।
उल्लासिचिल्लियुगलं द्रुहिणस्य गेहं
पक्ष्माणि रात्रिदिवसौ सविता च नेत्रे ॥४॥
tvadbrahmarandhrapadamIshvara vishvakanda
ChandAMsi keshava ghanAstava keshapAshAH 
ullAsichilliyugalaM druhiNasya gehaM
pakShmANi rAtridivasau savitA cha netre 4

त्वद्ब्रह्मरन्ध्रपदमीश्वर विश्वकन्द
O Lord, the brahmarandhra on the crown of your head is the source of the universe!
छन्दांसि केशव घनास्तव केशपाशा: ।
O Keshava! The Vedas are your luxurious locks of hair!
उल्लासिचिल्लियुगलं द्रुहिणस्य गेहं
Your two delightful eyebrows are the abode of brahmA,
पक्ष्माणि रात्रिदिवसौ सविता च नेत्रै ॥४॥
your eyelashes are night and day times, and your eyes are the Sun!

निश्शेषविश्वरचना च कटाक्षमोक्ष:
कर्णौ दिशोऽश्वियुगलं तव नासिके द्वे ।
लोभत्रपे च भगवन्नधरोत्तरोष्ठौ
तारागणाश्च दशना: शमनश्च दंष्ट्रा ॥५॥
nishsheShavishvarachanA cha kaTAkShamokShaH
karNau dishoshviyugalaM tava nAsike dve 
lobhatrape cha bhagavannadharottaroShThau
tArAgaNAshcha dashanAH shamanashcha daMShTrA 5

निश्शेषविश्वरचना च कटाक्षमोक्ष:
The creation of the entire universe is set forth by your side glance!
कर्णौ दिशोऽश्वियुगलं तव नासिके द्वे ।
Both your ears are the directions and the ashvinIdevatas are your two nostrils!
लोभत्रपे च भगवन्नधरोत्तरोष्ठौ
Greed and Modesty, O Lord are your lower and upper lips,
तारागणाश्च दशना: शमनश्च दंष्ट्रा ॥५॥
the clusters of stars are your teeth, and the God of death is your terrible tusks!

माया विलासहसितं श्वसितं समीरो
जिह्वा जलं वचनमीश शकुन्तपङ्क्ति: ।
सिद्धादय: स्वरगणा मुखरन्ध्रमग्निः
देवा भुजा: स्तनयुगं तव धर्मदेव: ॥६॥
mAyA vilAsahasitaM shvasitaM samIro
jihvA jalaM vachanamIsha shakuntapanktiH 
siddhAdayaH svaragaNA mukharandhramagniH
devA bhujAH stanayugaM tava dharmadevaH 6

माया विलासहसितं श्वसितं समीरो
Illusory power of creation-mAyA is your charming smile, your breath is the wind!
जिह्वा जलं वचनमीश शकुन्तपङ्क्ति: ।
Your tongue is water and your speech O Lord! is the chirping of birds!
सिद्धादय: स्वरगणा मुखरन्ध्रमग्निः
The Siddhas and other accomplished beings are your vocal cords and Fire your vocal cavity!
देवा भुजा: स्तनयुगं तव धर्मदेव: ॥६॥
Devas are your arms, the pair of your breasts is DharmaDeva!

पृष्ठं त्वधर्म इह देव मन: सुधांशुः
अव्यक्तमेव हृदयांबुजमम्बुजाक्ष ।
कुक्षि: समुद्रनिवहा वसनं तु सन्ध्ये
शेफ: प्रजापतिरसौ वृषणौ च मित्र: ॥७॥
pRShThaM tvadharma iha deva manaH sudhAMshuH
avyaktameva hRdayAMbujamambujAkSha 
kukShiH samudranivahA vasanaM tu sandhye
shephaH prajApatirasau vRShaNau cha mitraH 7

पृष्ठं त्वधर्म इह देव मन: सुधांशुः
Your back is unrighteousness and O Lord! Your mind is the moon!
अव्यक्तमेव हृदयांबुजमम्बुजाक्ष ।
All that is unmanifest is the lotus of your heart, O Lotus-eyed One!
कुक्षि: समुद्रनिवहा वसनं तु सन्ध्ये
Your abdomen is the ocean, your garments are the twilight periods!
शेफ: प्रजापतिरसौ वृषणौ च मित्र: ॥७॥
Your genital organ is the lord of procreation - prajApati, and your testes are lord mitra!

श्रोणीस्थलं मृगगणा: पदयोर्नखास्ते
हस्त्युष्ट्रसैन्धवमुखा गमनं तु काल: ।
विप्रादिवर्णभवनं वदनाब्जबाहु-
चारूरुयुग्मचरणं करुणांबुधे ते ॥८॥
shroNIsthalaM mRgagaNAH padayornakhAste
hastyuShTrasaindhavamukhA gamanaM tu kAlaH 
viprAdivarNabhavanaM vadanAbjabAhu-
chArUruyugmacharaNaM karuNAMbudhe te 8

श्रोणीस्थलं मृगगणा: पदयोर्नखास्ते
Your lower back is the groups of animals, the nails of your feet
हस्त्युष्ट्रसैन्धवमुखा गमनं तु काल: ।
are elephants, camels, and horses, and your movement is Time!
विप्रादिवर्णभवनं वदनाब्जबाहु-
brAhmaNas and the other varNas are of your lotus-like body, the arms
चारूरुयुग्मचरणं करुणांबुधे ते ॥८॥
beautiful pair of thighs and feet, O Ocean of Compassion!

संसारचक्रमयि चक्रधर क्रियास्ते
वीर्यं महासुरगणोऽस्थिकुलानि शैला: ।
नाड्यस्सरित्समुदयस्तरवश्च रोम
जीयादिदं वपुरनिर्वचनीयमीश ॥९॥
saMsArachakramayi chakradhara kriyAste
vIryaM mahAsuragaNosthikulAni shailAH 
nADyassaritsamudayastaravashcha roma
jIyAdidaM vapuranirvachanIyamIsha 9

संसारचक्रमयि चक्रधर क्रियास्ते
The wheel of creation, O One who Wields the Chakra, is your activity,
वीर्यं महासुरगणोऽस्थिकुलानि शैला: ।
Your virility is the group of great asuras, and your skeletal system is the mountains!
नाड्यस्सरित्समुदयस्तरवश्च रोम
Your energy channels are the groups of rivers, and the trees are your body hair!
जीयादिदं वपुरनिर्वचनीयमीश ॥९॥
May it ever be resplendent, this indescribable form of yours, O Lord!

ईदृग्जगन्मयवपुस्तव कर्मभाजां
कर्मावसानसमये स्मरणीयमाहु: ।
तस्यान्तरात्मवपुषे विमलात्मने ते
वातालयाधिप नमोऽस्तु निरुन्धि रोगान् ॥१०॥
IdRgjaganmayavapustava karmabhAjAM
karmAvasAnasamaye smaraNIyamAhuH 
tasyAntarAtmavapuShe vimalAtmane te
vAtAlayAdhipa namostu nirundhi rogAn 10

ईदृग्जगन्मयवपुस्तव कर्मभाजां
This kind of a cosmic body of yours, by all creatures of activity
कर्मावसानसमये स्मरणीयमाहु: ।
during their end times, it is said that it must be remembered!
तस्यान्तरात्मवपुषे विमलात्मने ते
To the one who resides within this cosmic body, to your pure self,
वातालयाधिप नमोऽस्तु निरुन्धि रोगान् ॥१०॥
O Ruler of the Abode of Wind! I bow down to you! Destroy my diseases!
