ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अष्टत्रिंशम् दशकम्
aShTatriMsham dashakam

The Thirty-eighth group of Ten shlokas

श्रीकृष्ण अवतार:
shrIkRShNa avatAraH

Incarnation as Lord Krishna

आनन्दरूप भगवन्नयि तेऽवतारे
प्राप्ते प्रदीप्तभवदङ्गनिरीयमाणै: ।
कान्तिव्रजैरिव घनाघनमण्डलैर्द्याम्
आवृण्वती विरुरुचे किल वर्षवेला ॥१॥
AnandarUpa bhagavannayi tevatAre
prApte pradIptabhavadanganirIyamANaiH 
kAntivrajairiva ghanAghanamaNDalairdyAm
AvRNvatI viruruche kila varShavelA 1

आनन्दरूप भगवन्नयि तेऽवतारे
O Embodiment of Bliss! O Lord, as your incarnation
प्राप्ते प्रदीप्तभवदङ्गनिरीयमाणै: ।
happened, emanating from your resplendent body,
कान्तिव्रजैरिव घनाघनमण्डलैर्द्याम्
rays of light as if from thick rain clouds
आवृण्वती विरुरुचे किल वर्षवेला ॥१॥
that have covered the sky, didn't the rainy season itself rejoice!

आशासु शीतलतरासु पयोदतोयै:
आशासिताप्तिविवशेषु च सज्जनेषु ।
नैशाकरोदयविधौ निशि मध्यमायां
क्लेशापहस्त्रिजगतां त्वमिहाविरासी: ॥२॥
AshAsu shItalatarAsu payodatoyaiH
AshAsitAptivivasheShu cha sajjaneShu 
naishAkarodayavidhau nishi madhyamAyAM
kleshApahastrijagatAM tvamihAvirAsIH 2

आशासु शीतलतरासु पयोदतोयै:
Cooling down all the directions with showers of rain,
आशासिताप्तिविवशेषु च सज्जनेषु ।
as the pious were overwhelmed with their desires fulfilled,
नैशाकरोदयविधौ निशि मध्यमायां
like a moon rise in the middle of the night,
क्लेशापहस्त्रिजगतां त्वमिहाविरासी: ॥२॥
O Remover of the afflictions of the three worlds! You appeared! 

बाल्यस्पृशाऽपि वपुषा दधुषा विभूती:
उद्यत्किरीटकटकाङ्गदहारभासा ।
शङ्खारिवारिजगदापरिभासितेन
मेघासितेन परिलेसिथ सूतिगेहे ॥३॥
bAlyaspRshApi vapuShA dadhuShA vibhUtIH
udyatkirITakaTakAngadahArabhAsA 
shankhArivArijagadAparibhAsitena
meghAsitena parilesitha sUtigehe 3

बाल्यस्पृशाऽपि वपुषा दधुषा विभूती:
Despite being in an infant form, displaying exceptional powers,
उद्यत्किरीटकटकाङ्गदहारभासा ।
shining with resplendent crown, bracelets, armlets and garlands,
शङ्खारिवारिजगदापरिभासितेन
weilding the conch, discus, lotus and mace, beautiful 
मेघासितेन परिलेसिथ सूतिगेहे ॥३॥
with the darkness of a rain cloud, you shined in the delivery chamber!

वक्ष:स्थलीसुखनिलीनविलासिलक्ष्मी-
मन्दाक्षलक्षितकटाक्षविमोक्षभेदै: ।
तन्मन्दिरस्य खलकंसकृतामलक्ष्मीम्
उन्मार्जयन्निव विरेजिथ वासुदेव ॥४॥
vakShaHsthalIsukhanilInavilAsilakShmI-
mandAkShalakShitakaTAkShavimokShabhedaiH 
tanmandirasya khalakaMsakRtAmalakShmIm
unmArjayanniva virejitha vAsudeva 4

वक्ष:स्थलीसुखनिलीनविलासिलक्ष्मी-
Residing on your chest, immersed in bliss, the resplendent Goddess Lakshmi,
मन्दाक्षलक्षितकटाक्षविमोक्षभेदै: ।
shedding her gentle glances with her eyes all around
तन्मन्दिरस्य खलकंसकृतामलक्ष्मीम्
in that hall, the inauspiciousness brought about by the cruel Kamsa
उन्मार्जयन्निव विरेजिथ वासुदेव ॥४॥
as if uprooting it, you shined, O Son of Vasudeva!

शौरिस्तु धीरमुनिमण्डलचेतसोऽपि
दूरस्थितं वपुरुदीक्ष्य निजेक्षणाभ्याम् ॥
आनन्दवाष्पपुलकोद्गमगद्गदार्द्र:
तुष्टाव दृष्टिमकरन्दरसं भवन्तम् ॥५॥
shauristu dhIramunimaNDalachetasopi
dUrasthitaM vapurudIkShya nijekShaNAbhyAm 
AnandavAShpapulakodgamagadgadArdraH
tuShTAva dRShTimakarandarasaM bhavantam 5

शौरिस्तु धीरमुनिमण्डलचेतसोऽपि
Vasudeva on the other hand, for even the minds of the most devoted groups of sages,
दूरस्थितं वपुरुदीक्ष्य निजेक्षणाभ्याम् ॥
that which is hard to obtain, your form, gazing upon it with his own eyes,
आनन्दवाष्पपुलकोद्गमगद्गदार्द्र:
shedding tears of joy, with his throat choking up in that ecstacy,
तुष्टाव दृष्टिमकरन्दरसं भवन्तम् ॥५॥
started praising you, who is the essence of nectar for one's eyes!

देव प्रसीद परपूरुष तापवल्ली-
निर्लूनदात्रसमनेत्रकलाविलासिन् ।
खेदानपाकुरु कृपागुरुभि: कटाक्षै:
इत्यादि तेन मुदितेन चिरं नुतोऽभू: ॥६॥
deva prasIda parapUruSha tApavallI-
nirlUnadAtrasamanetrakalAvilAsin 
khedAnapAkuru kRpAgurubhiH kaTAkShaiH
ityAdi tena muditena chiraM nutobhUH 6

देव प्रसीद परपूरुष तापवल्ली-
"Be pleased, O Lord! O Supreme Being! The vines of my suffering,
निर्लूनदात्रसमनेत्रकलाविलासिन् ।
O One who has playful eyes which are like a sword that cuts them all down!
खेदानपाकुरु कृपागुरुभि: कटाक्षै:
Remove my sorrows with your glances filled with great compassion!"
इत्यादि तेन मुदितेन चिरं नुतोऽभू: ॥६॥
with such kinds of words from that delighted one, you were praised for long!

मात्रा च नेत्रसलिलास्तृतगात्रवल्या
स्तोत्रैरभिष्टुतगुण: करुणालयस्त्वम् ।
प्राचीनजन्मयुगलं प्रतिबोध्य ताभ्यां
मातुर्गिरा दधिथ मानुषबालवेषम् ॥७॥
mAtrA cha netrasalilAstRtagAtravalyA
stotrairabhiShTutaguNaH karuNAlayastvam 
prAchInajanmayugalaM pratibodhya tAbhyAM
mAturgirA dadhitha mAnuShabAlaveSham 7

मात्रा च नेत्रसलिलास्तृतगात्रवल्या
And by the mother, who was like a creeper drenched by waters from her eyes,
स्तोत्रैरभिष्टुतगुण: करुणालयस्त्वम् ।
as all your attributes were being praised, O Abode of Compassion, you
प्राचीनजन्मयुगलं प्रतिबोध्य ताभ्यां
making them realize two previous lifetimes of theirs
मातुर्गिरा दधिथ मानुषबालवेषम् ॥७॥
upon your mother's words took on the appearance of a human child!

त्वत्प्रेरितस्तदनु नन्दतनूजया ते
व्यत्यासमारचयितुं स हि शूरसूनु: ।
त्वां हस्तयोरधृत चित्तविधार्यमार्यै:
अम्भोरुहस्थकलहंसकिशोररम्यम् ॥८॥
tvatpreritastadanu nandatanUjayA te
vyatyAsamArachayituM sa hi shUrasUnuH 
tvAM hastayoradhRta chittavidhAryamAryaiH
ambhoruhasthakalahaMsakishoraramyam 8

त्वत्प्रेरितस्तदनु नन्दतनूजया ते
Then, impelled by you, with the daughter of Nanda,
व्यत्यासमारचयितुं स हि शूरसूनु: ।
in order to swap you, that son of Shurasena,
त्वां हस्तयोरधृत चित्तविधार्यमार्यै:
took You into his arms, which is realized in the minds of the pious,
अम्भोरुहस्थकलहंसकिशोररम्यम् ॥८॥
as grasping the beautiful baby swan in the lotus of one's heart!

जाता तदा पशुपसद्मनि योगनिद्रा ।
निद्राविमुद्रितमथाकृत पौरलोकम् ।
त्वत्प्रेरणात् किमिव चित्रमचेतनैर्यत्
द्वारै: स्वयं व्यघटि सङ्घटितै: सुगाढम् ॥९॥
jAtA tadA pashupasadmani yoganidrA 
nidrAvimudritamathAkRta pauralokam 
tvatpreraNAt kimiva chitramachetanairyat
dvAraiH svayaM vyaghaTi sanghaTitaiH sugADham 9

जाता तदा पशुपसद्मनि योगनिद्रा ।
Then was born in the house of the lord of cowherds, Yoga Nidra herself,
निद्राविमुद्रितमथाकृत पौरलोकम् ।
who made everyone in the city fall into a deep slumber!
त्वत्प्रेरणात् किमिव चित्रमचेतनैर्यत्
With your impelling, O What Wonder, that the inanimate
द्वारै: स्वयं व्यघटि सङ्घटितै: सुगाढम् ॥९॥
doors flung themselves open despite being deeply shut!

शेषेण भूरिफणवारितवारिणाऽथ
स्वैरं प्रदर्शितपथो मणिदीपितेन ।
त्वां धारयन् स खलु धन्यतम: प्रतस्थे
सोऽयं त्वमीश मम नाशय रोगवेगान् ॥१०॥
sheSheNa bhUriphaNavAritavAriNAtha
svairaM pradarshitapatho maNidIpitena 
tvAM dhArayan sa khalu dhanyatamaH pratasthe
soyaM tvamIsha mama nAshaya rogavegAn 10

शेषेण भूरिफणवारितवारिणाऽथ
And then, as Adishesha with his many hoods protected against the pouring rain,
स्वैरं प्रदर्शितपथो मणिदीपितेन ।
unobstructed, with the path shone by the glowing jewel on his hood,
त्वां धारयन् स खलु धन्यतम: प्रतस्थे
holding you, didn't that highly fortunate one set out!
सोऽयं त्वमीश मम नाशय रोगवेगान् ॥१०॥
That you, O Lord, destroy the exertions of my disease!
