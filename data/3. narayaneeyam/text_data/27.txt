ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्तविंशम् दशकम्
saptaviMsham dashakam

The Twenty-seventh group of Ten shlokas

क्षीराब्धि मथनं कूर्मावतार: च
narasiMha avatAraH

Churning the Ocean of Milk and Incarnation as a Tortoise

दुर्वासास्सुरवनिताप्तदिव्यमाल्यं
शक्राय स्वयमुपदाय तत्र भूय: ।
नागेन्द्रप्रतिमृदिते शशाप शक्रं
का क्षान्तिस्त्वदितरदेवतांशजानाम् ॥१॥
durvAsAssuravanitAptadivyamAlyaM
shakrAya svayamupadAya tatra bhUyaH 
nAgendrapratimRdite shashApa shakraM
kA kShAntistvaditaradevatAMshajAnAm 1

दुर्वासास्सुरवनिताप्तदिव्यमाल्यं
Sage Durvasa, having obtained a celestial garland from divine maidens,
शक्राय स्वयमुपदाय तत्र भूय: ।
he in turn conferred it upon indra, and then
नागेन्द्रप्रतिमृदिते शशाप शक्रं
having been trampled by his celestial elephant, airAvata, cursed indra!
का क्षान्तिस्त्वदितरदेवतांशजानाम् ॥१॥
Where indeed is there patience in those who are born as the aspects of other deities!

शापेन प्रथितजरेऽथ निर्जरेन्द्रे
देवेष्वप्यसुरजितेषु निष्प्रभेषु ।
शर्वाद्या: कमलजमेत्य सर्वदेवा
निर्वाणप्रभव समं भवन्तमापु: ॥२॥
shApena prathitajaretha nirjarendre
deveShvapyasurajiteShu niShprabheShu 
sharvAdyAH kamalajametya sarvadevA
nirvANaprabhava samaM bhavantamApuH 2

शापेन प्रथितजरेऽथ निर्जरेन्द्रे
As the ageless Indra, fell to old age due to the curse,
देवेष्वप्यसुरजितेषु निष्प्रभेषु ।
as the Devas who lost their glory, were now being defeated by Asuras,  
शर्वाद्या: कमलजमेत्य सर्वदेवा
Lord Shiva and others, went to lotus-born Brahma, and all the Devas, 
निर्वाणप्रभव समं भवन्तमापु: ॥२॥
approached you, O One Adept at Granting Liberation!

ब्रह्माद्यै: स्तुतमहिमा चिरं तदानीं
प्रादुष्षन् वरद पुर: परेण धाम्ना ।
हे देवा दितिजकुलैर्विधाय सन्धिं
पीयूषं परिमथतेति पर्यशास्त्वम् ॥३॥
brahmAdyaiH stutamahimA chiraM tadAnIM
prAduShShan varada puraH pareNa dhAmnA 
he devA ditijakulairvidhAya sandhiM
pIyUShaM parimathateti paryashAstvam 3

ब्रह्माद्यै: स्तुतमहिमा चिरं तदानीं
As Brahma and others kept singing the praise of your glory,
प्रादुष्षन् वरद पुर: परेण धाम्ना ।
appearing before them, O Varada, from your supreme abode!
हे देवा दितिजकुलैर्विधाय सन्धिं
"O Devas! Make an arrangement with the groups of Asuras,
पीयूषं परिमथतेति पर्यशास्त्वम् ॥३॥
and work together to churn out Amrita!", thus you commanded them!

सन्धानं कृतवति दानवै: सुरौघे
मन्थानं नयति मदेन मन्दराद्रिम् ।
भ्रष्टेऽस्मिन् बदरमिवोद्वहन् खगेन्द्रे
सद्यस्त्वं विनिहितवान् पय:पयोधौ ॥४॥
sandhAnaM kRtavati dAnavaiH suraughe
manthAnaM nayati madena mandarAdrim 
bhraShTesmin badaramivodvahan khagendre
sadyastvaM vinihitavAn payaHpayodhau 4

सन्धानं कृतवति दानवै: सुरौघे
An arrangement having been made between the asuras and suras,
मन्थानं नयति मदेन मन्दराद्रिम् ।
with great pride, as they carried the Mandara mountain to be used as the churn,
भ्रष्टेऽस्मिन् बदरमिवोद्वहन् खगेन्द्रे
as it fell along the way, picking it up as easily as a berry, riding on the king of birds, garuDa,
सद्यस्त्वं विनिहितवान् पय:पयोधौ ॥४॥
you quickly placed it in the ocean of milk!

आधाय द्रुतमथ वासुकिं वरत्रां
पाथोधौ विनिहितसर्वबीजजाले ।
प्रारब्धे मथनविधौ सुरासुरैस्तैः
व्याजात्त्वं भुजगमुखेऽकरोस्सुरारीन् ॥५॥
AdhAya drutamatha vAsukiM varatrAM
pAthodhau vinihitasarvabIjajAle 
prArabdhe mathanavidhau surAsuraistaiH
vyAjAttvaM bhujagamukhekarossurArIn 5

आधाय द्रुतमथ वासुकिं वरत्रां
Thus after placing it quickly, using the serpent Vasuki, as the great rope,
पाथोधौ विनिहितसर्वबीजजाले ।
placing all assortments of seeds and herbs into the ocean of milk,
प्रारब्धे मथनविधौ सुरासुरैस्तैः
starting the process of churning by those suras and asuras,
व्याजात्त्वं भुजगमुखेऽकरोस्सुरारीन् ॥५॥
trickily you placed the enemies of suras towards the mouth of the serpent!

क्षुब्धाद्रौ क्षुभितजलोदरे तदानीं
दुग्धाब्धौ गुरुतरभारतो निमग्ने ।
देवेषु व्यथिततमेषु तत्प्रियैषी
प्राणैषी: कमठतनुं कठोरपृष्ठाम् ॥६॥
kShubdhAdrau kShubhitajalodare tadAnIM
dugdhAbdhau gurutarabhArato nimagne 
deveShu vyathitatameShu tatpriyaiShI
prANaiShIH kamaThatanuM kaThorapRShThAm 6

क्षुब्धाद्रौ क्षुभितजलोदरे तदानीं
As the waters started getting churned using that great mountain,
दुग्धाब्धौ गुरुतरभारतो निमग्ने ।
due to its immense weight, as it started to sink into the ocean of milk,
देवेषु व्यथिततमेषु तत्प्रियैषी
as the Devas started getting worried, their well-wisher,
प्राणैषी: कमठतनुं कठोरपृष्ठाम् ॥६॥
you, assumed the form of a giant tortoise with a hardened back!

वज्रातिस्थिरतरकर्परेण विष्णो
विस्तारात्परिगतलक्षयोजनेन ।
अम्भोधे: कुहरगतेन वर्ष्मणा त्वं
निर्मग्नं क्षितिधरनाथमुन्निनेथ ॥७॥
vajrAtisthiratarakarpareNa viShNo
vistArAtparigatalakShayojanena 
ambhodheH kuharagatena varShmaNA tvaM
nirmagnaM kShitidharanAthamunninetha 7

वज्रातिस्थिरतरकर्परेण विष्णो
O Vishnu, with a shell harder than a diamond,
विस्तारात्परिगतलक्षयोजनेन ।
spreading yourself to a width of one lakh yojanas,
अम्भोधे: कुहरगतेन वर्ष्मणा त्वं
with that form, that which had sunk into the depths of the ocean, 
निर्मग्नं क्षितिधरनाथमुन्निनेथ ॥७॥
the king of earth, that great mountain, you lifted back up!

उन्मग्ने झटिति तदा धराधरेन्द्रे
निर्मेथुर्दृढमिह सम्मदेन सर्वे ।
आविश्य द्वितयगणेऽपि सर्पराजे
वैवश्यं परिशमयन्नवीवृधस्तान् ॥८॥
unmagne jhaTiti tadA dharAdharendre
nirmethurdRDhamiha sammadena sarve 
Avishya dvitayagaNepi sarparAje
vaivashyaM parishamayannavIvRdhastAn 8

उन्मग्ने झटिति तदा धराधरेन्द्रे
As the mountain, the king of earth, came back up quickly,
निर्मेथुर्दृढमिह सम्मदेन सर्वे ।
everyone now started churning even more firmly!
आविश्य द्वितयगणेऽपि सर्पराजे
Entering both the groups, as well as the king of serpents,
वैवश्यं परिशमयन्नवीवृधस्तान् ॥८॥
you removed their fatigue and invigorated them!

उद्दामभ्रमणजवोन्नमद्गिरीन्द्र-
न्यस्तैकस्थिरतरहस्तपङ्कजं त्वाम् ।
अभ्रान्ते विधिगिरिशादय: प्रमोदाद्
उद्भ्रान्ता नुनुवुरुपात्तपुष्पवर्षा: ॥९॥
uddAmabhramaNajavonnamadgirIndra-
nyastaikasthiratarahastapankajaM tvAm 
abhrAnte vidhigirishAdayaH pramodAd
udbhrAntA nunuvurupAttapuShpavarShAH 9

उद्दामभ्रमणजवोन्नमद्गिरीन्द्र-
With the speed of their churning, as the mountain started to rise up,
न्यस्तैकस्थिरतरहस्तपङ्कजं त्वाम् ।
as you placed your lotus-like hand firmly on top of it,
अभ्रान्ते विधिगिरिशादय: प्रमोदाद्
In the skies, Brahma, Shiva and others, with utmost joy
उद्भ्रान्ता नुनुवुरुपात्तपुष्पवर्षा: ॥९॥
and wonder, started praising you showering a torrent of flowers!

दैत्यौघे भुजगमुखानिलेन तप्ते
तेनैव त्रिदशकुलेऽपि किञ्चिदार्ते ।
कारुण्यात्तव किल देव वारिवाहा:
प्रावर्षन्नमरगणान्न दैत्यसङ्घान् ॥१०॥
daityaughe bhujagamukhAnilena tapte
tenaiva tridashakulepi kinchidArte 
kAruNyAttava kila deva vArivAhAH
prAvarShannamaragaNAnna daityasanghAn 10

दैत्यौघे भुजगमुखानिलेन तप्ते
As the group of asuras were burning up due to the fires from the serpent's mouth,
तेनैव त्रिदशकुलेऽपि किञ्चिदार्ते ।
and due to the same thing, as the group of suras too started to suffer a little,
कारुण्यात्तव किल देव वारिवाहा:
Isn't it out of your compassion, O Lord, that clouds
प्रावर्षन्नमरगणान्न दैत्यसङ्घान् ॥१०॥
showered rain upon the suras but not on the asuras!

उद्भ्राम्यद्बहुतिमिनक्रचक्रवाले
तत्राब्धौ चिरमथितेऽपि निर्विकारे ।
एकस्त्वं करयुगकृष्टसर्पराज:
संराजन् पवनपुरेश पाहि रोगात् ॥११॥
udbhrAmyadbahutiminakrachakravAle
tatrAbdhau chiramathitepi nirvikAre 
ekastvaM karayugakRShTasarparAjaH
saMrAjan pavanapuresha pAhi rogAt 11

उद्भ्राम्यद्बहुतिमिनक्रचक्रवाले
Throwing out all kinds of fishes, crocodiles and aquatic creatures
तत्राब्धौ चिरमथितेऽपि निर्विकारे ।
despite being churned for so long, as the ocean remained unchanged,
एकस्त्वं करयुगकृष्टसर्पराज:
you yourself, with both your hands, tugged at the serpent king,
संराजन् पवनपुरेश पाहि रोगात् ॥११॥
gloriously, O Lord of the City of Wind! Protect me from my diseases!
