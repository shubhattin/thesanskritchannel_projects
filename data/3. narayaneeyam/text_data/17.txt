And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्तदशम् दशकम्
saptadasham dashakam

The Seventeenth group of Ten shlokas

ध्रुव चरितम्
dhruva charitram

The Story of Dhruva

उत्तानपादनृपतेर्मनुनन्दनस्य
जाया बभूव सुरुचिर्नितरामभीष्टा ।
अन्या सुनीतिरिति भर्तुरनादृता सा
त्वामेव नित्यमगति: शरणं गताऽभूत् ॥१॥
uttAnapAdanRpatermanunandanasya
jAyA babhUva suruchirnitarAmabhIShTA 
anyA sunItiriti bharturanAdRtA sA
tvAmeva nityamagatiH sharaNaM gatAbhUt 1

उत्तानपादनृपतेर्मनुनन्दनस्य
Of king Uttanapada, who is the son of Manu,
जाया बभूव सुरुचिर्नितरामभीष्टा ।
there was an extremely dear wife, by the name of Suruchi.
अन्या सुनीतिरिति भर्तुरनादृता सा
There was also another wife who wasn't given the same regard, by the name of Suniti.
त्वामेव नित्यमगति: शरणं गताऽभूत् ॥१॥
Being helpless, she always sought refuge in You!

अङ्के पितु: सुरुचिपुत्रकमुत्तमं तं
दृष्ट्वा ध्रुव: किल सुनीतिसुतोऽधिरोक्ष्यन् ।
आचिक्षिपे किल शिशु: सुतरां सुरुच्या
दुस्सन्त्यजा खलु भवद्विमुखैरसूया ॥२॥
anke pituH suruchiputrakamuttamaM taM
dRShTvA dhruvaH kila sunItisutodhirokShyan 
AchikShipe kila shishuH sutarAM suruchyA
dussantyajA khalu bhavadvimukhairasUyA 2

अङ्के पितु: सुरुचिपुत्रकमुत्तमं तं
In the lap of his father, that son of Suruchi, Uttama
दृष्ट्वा ध्रुव: किल सुनीतिसुतोऽधिरोक्ष्यन् ।
looking at him, hasn't Dhruva, the son of Suniti, too climbed up,
आचिक्षिपे किल शिशु: सुतरां सुरुच्या
and was immediately thrown down, that child, by Suruchi's son!
दुस्सन्त्यजा खलु भवद्विमुखैरसूया ॥२॥
Isn't it really hard to relinquish jealousy by those who are averse to You!

त्वन्मोहिते पितरि पश्यति दारवश्ये
दूरं दुरुक्तिनिहत: स गतो निजाम्बाम् ।
साऽपि स्वकर्मगतिसन्तरणाय पुंसां
त्वत्पादमेव शरणं शिशवे शशंस ॥३॥
tvanmohite pitari pashyati dAravashye
dUraM duruktinihataH sa gato nijAmbAm 
sApi svakarmagatisantaraNAya puMsAM
tvatpAdameva sharaNaM shishave shashaMsa 3

त्वन्मोहिते पितरि पश्यति दारवश्ये
Looking at the father who was deluded and under the influence of his wife,
दूरं दुरुक्तिनिहत: स गतो निजाम्बाम् ।
having been abused, he went away to his own mother!
साऽपि स्वकर्मगतिसन्तरणाय पुंसां
She too "To get over one's own karma, for all human beings,
त्वत्पादमेव शरणं शिशवे शशंस ॥३॥
Your feet are the only refuge", thus she advised the child!

आकर्ण्य सोऽपि भवदर्चननिश्चितात्मा
मानी निरेत्य नगरात् किल पञ्चवर्ष: ।
सन्दृष्टनारदनिवेदितमन्त्रमार्ग:
त्वामारराध तपसा मधुकाननान्ते ॥४॥
AkarNya sopi bhavadarchananishchitAtmA
mAnI niretya nagarAt kila panchavarShaH 
sandRShTanAradaniveditamantramArgaH
tvAmArarAdha tapasA madhukAnanAnte 4

आकर्ण्य सोऽपि भवदर्चननिश्चितात्मा
Having heard this, he then with his mind resolute in your worship,
मानी निरेत्य नगरात् किल पञ्चवर्ष: ।
dignified, didn't he, the five year old then leave the city!
सन्दृष्टनारदनिवेदितमन्त्रमार्ग:
Having seen Narada, and been provided the path of Mantra,
त्वामारराध तपसा मधुकाननान्ते ॥४॥
he started worshipping you with penance in the middle of the Madhu forest!

ताते विषण्णहृदये नगरीं गतेन
श्रीनारदेन परिसान्त्वितचित्तवृत्तौ ।
बालस्त्वदर्पितमना: क्रमवर्धितेन
निन्ये कठोरतपसा किल पञ्चमासान् ॥५॥
tAte viShaNNahRdaye nagarIM gatena
shrInAradena parisAntvitachittavRttau 
bAlastvadarpitamanAH kramavardhitena
ninye kaThoratapasA kila panchamAsAn 5

ताते विषण्णहृदये नगरीं गतेन
As his father's heart broke, the town was visited
श्रीनारदेन परिसान्त्वितचित्तवृत्तौ ।
by Narada, and as his dejected mind was pacified,
बालस्त्वदर्पितमना: क्रमवर्धितेन
as the child, with his mind immersed in you, gradually increased
निन्ये कठोरतपसा किल पञ्चमासान् ॥५॥
the intensity of his severe penance, hadn't five months gone by!

तावत्तपोबलनिरुच्छ्वसिते दिगन्ते
देवार्थितस्त्वमुदयत्करुणार्द्रचेता: ।
त्वद्रूपचिद्रसनिलीनमते: पुरस्ताद्
आविर्बभूविथ विभो गरुडाधिरूढ: ॥६॥
tAvattapobalaniruchChvasite digante
devArthitastvamudayatkaruNArdrachetAH 
tvadrUpachidrasanilInamateH purastAd
AvirbabhUvitha vibho garuDAdhirUDhaH 6

तावत्तपोबलनिरुच्छ्वसिते दिगन्ते
With the intensity of that kind of penance, the ends of the world started to suffocate!
देवार्थितस्त्वमुदयत्करुणार्द्रचेता: ।
As Devas sought you, as compassion arose, melting your heart,
त्वद्रूपचिद्रसनिलीनमते: पुरस्ताद्
Your form, in front of the one whose mind was absorbed in the essence of consciousness,
आविर्बभूविथ विभो गरुडाधिरूढ: ॥६॥
you revealed, O Vibhu! riding on top of Garuda!

त्वद्दर्शनप्रमदभारतरङ्गितं तं
दृग्भ्यां निमग्नमिव रूपरसायने ते ।
तुष्टूषमाणमवगम्य कपोलदेशे
संस्पृष्टवानसि दरेण तथाऽऽदरेण ॥७॥
tvaddarshanapramadabhAratarangitaM taM
dRgbhyAM nimagnamiva rUparasAyane te 
tuShTUShamANamavagamya kapoladeshe
saMspRShTavAnasi dareNa tathAdareNa 7

त्वद्दर्शनप्रमदभारतरङ्गितं तं
At your sight, under the weight of intense waves of bliss, to him,
दृग्भ्यां निमग्नमिव रूपरसायने ते ।
who with his eyes seemed to be drowning in the essence of your form,
तुष्टूषमाणमवगम्य कपोलदेशे
who wished to sing your praise, understanding his intent, at his cheeks,
संस्पृष्टवानसि दरेण तथाऽऽदरेण ॥७॥
you touched him with your conch, with immense affection!

तावद्विबोधविमलं प्रणुवन्तमेनम्
आभाषथास्त्वमवगम्य तदीयभावम् ।
राज्यं चिरं समनुभूय भजस्व भूय:
सर्वोत्तरं ध्रुव पदं विनिवृत्तिहीनम् ॥८॥
tAvadvibodhavimalaM praNuvantamenam
AbhAShathAstvamavagamya tadIyabhAvam 
rAjyaM chiraM samanubhUya bhajasva bhUyaH
sarvottaraM dhruva padaM vinivRttihInam 8

तावद्विबोधविमलं प्रणुवन्तमेनम्
Then to him who was purified through realization, who was praising You,
आभाषथास्त्वमवगम्य तदीयभावम् ।
you spoke to him, understanding his mind's intent,
राज्यं चिरं समनुभूय भजस्व भूय:
"Enjoy a kingly abode for a long time, and thereafter,
सर्वोत्तरं ध्रुव पदं विनिवृत्तिहीनम् ॥८॥
attain to the highest abode of Dhruva, from which there is no falling back!"

इत्यूचिषि त्वयि गते नृपनन्दनोऽसौ
आनन्दिताखिलजनो नगरीमुपेत: ।
रेमे चिरं भवदनुग्रहपूर्णकाम:
ताते गते च वनमादृतराज्यभार: ॥९॥
ityUchiShi tvayi gate nRpanandanosau
AnanditAkhilajano nagarImupetaH 
reme chiraM bhavadanugrahapUrNakAmaH
tAte gate cha vanamAdRtarAjyabhAraH 9

इत्यूचिषि त्वयि गते नृपनन्दनोऽसौ
As you spoke thus and left, the prince then
आनन्दिताखिलजनो नगरीमुपेत: ।
pleasing everybody, entered the city once again!
रेमे चिरं भवदनुग्रहपूर्णकाम:
He enjoyed living there for long, his desires fulfilled by your grace,
ताते गते च वनमादृतराज्यभार: ॥९॥
and when his father left for vAnaprastha, he took up rulership!

यक्षेण देव निहते पुनरुत्तमेऽस्मिन्
यक्षै: स युद्धनिरतो विरतो मनूक्त्या ।
शान्त्या प्रसन्नहृदयाद्धनदादुपेतात्
त्वद्भक्तिमेव सुदृढामवृणोन्महात्मा ॥१०॥
yakSheNa deva nihate punaruttamesmin
yakShaiH sa yuddhanirato virato manUktyA 
shAntyA prasannahRdayAddhanadAdupetAt
tvadbhaktimeva sudRDhAmavRNonmahAtmA 10

यक्षेण देव निहते पुनरुत्तमेऽस्मिन्
By Yaksha, O Lord, when once Uttama was killed,
यक्षै: स युद्धनिरतो विरतो मनूक्त्या ।
he started a war with all the Yakshas, but withdrew only upon Manu's counsel.
शान्त्या प्रसन्नहृदयाद्धनदादुपेतात्
Having been pacified, he asked from the King of Yaksha's, Kubera,
त्वद्भक्तिमेव सुदृढामवृणोन्महात्मा ॥१०॥
devotion in you alone, which is firm, as a boon, that great being!

अन्ते भवत्पुरुषनीतविमानयातो
मात्रा समं ध्रुवपदे मुदितोऽयमास्ते ।
एवं स्वभृत्यजनपालनलोलधीस्त्वं
वातालयाधिप निरुन्धि ममामयौघान् ॥११॥
ante bhavatpuruShanItavimAnayAto
mAtrA samaM dhruvapade muditoyamAste 
evaM svabhRtyajanapAlanaloladhIstvaM
vAtAlayAdhipa nirundhi mamAmayaughAn 11

अन्ते भवत्पुरुषनीतविमानयातो
At the end, carried by your own attendants in a vimAna,
मात्रा समं ध्रुवपदे मुदितोऽयमास्ते ।
along with his mother, in the abode of Dhruva, he lived happily.
एवं स्वभृत्यजनपालनलोलधीस्त्वं
Thus, you are adept at taking care of your own devotees!
वातालयाधिप निरुन्धि ममामयौघान् ॥११॥
O Lord of the Abode of Wind! Remove my heaps of ailments!
