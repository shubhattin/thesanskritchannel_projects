ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अष्टाविंशम् दशकम्
aShTAviMsham dashakam

The Twenty-eighth group of Ten shlokas

लक्ष्मी स्वयंवर: अमृत प्रादुर्भाव: च
lakShmI svayaMvaraH amRta prAdurbhAvaH cha

Lakshmi Choosing Her Husband and Emergence of Amrita

गरलं तरलानलं पुरस्तात्
जलधेरुद्विजगाल कालकूटम् ।
अमरस्तुतिवादमोदनिघ्नो
गिरिशस्तन्निपपौ भवत्प्रियार्थम् ॥१॥
garalaM taralAnalaM purastAt
jaladherudvijagAla kAlakUTam 
amarastutivAdamodanighno
girishastannipapau bhavatpriyArtham 1

गरलं तरलानलं पुरस्तात्
Poison, like molten fire came first
जलधेरुद्विजगाल कालकूटम् ।
spewing forth from that ocean, it was called as Kalakutam.
अमरस्तुतिवादमोदनिघ्नो
As the Devas sang words of his praise, 
गिरिशस्तन्निपपौ भवत्प्रियार्थम् ॥१॥
Shiva drank it up in order to please you!

विमथत्सु सुरासुरेषु जाता
सुरभिस्तामृषिषु न्यधास्त्रिधामन् ।
हयरत्नमभूदथेभरत्नं
द्युतरुश्चाप्सरस: सुरेषु तानि ॥२॥
vimathatsu surAsureShu jAtA
surabhistAmRShiShu nyadhAstridhAman 
hayaratnamabhUdathebharatnaM
dyutarushchApsarasaH sureShu tAni 2

विमथत्सु सुरासुरेषु जाता
As the Suras and Asuras continued churning, next was born
सुरभिस्तामृषिषु न्यधास्त्रिधामन् ।
Kamadhenu, who was given to the Sages by You, O Lord of the Three Worlds!  
हयरत्नमभूदथेभरत्नं
Next came Ucchaishravas and Airavata, the best of horses and the best of elephants,
द्युतरुश्चाप्सरस: सुरेषु तानि ॥२॥
next came the celestial tree, Kalpavriksha and celestial maidens, Apsaras, who were given to the Devas!

जगदीश भवत्परा तदानीं
कमनीया कमला बभूव देवी ।
अमलामवलोक्य यां विलोल:
सकलोऽपि स्पृहयाम्बभूव लोक: ॥३॥
jagadIsha bhavatparA tadAnIM
kamanIyA kamalA babhUva devI 
amalAmavalokya yAM vilolaH
sakalopi spRhayAmbabhUva lokaH 3

जगदीश भवत्परा तदानीं
O Lord of Creation, the one devoted to you, then
कमनीया कमला बभूव देवी ।
came the enchating Goddess Kamala,
अमलामवलोक्य यां विलोल:
looking at whose pure form, utterly fascinated,
सकलोऽपि स्पृहयाम्बभूव लोक: ॥३॥
everyone in the entire world lost their senses!

त्वयि दत्तहृदे तदैव देव्यै
त्रिदशेन्द्रो मणिपीठिकां व्यतारीत् ।
सकलोपहृताभिषेचनीयै:
ऋषयस्तां श्रुतिगीर्भिरभ्यषिञ्चन् ॥४॥
tvayi dattahRde tadaiva devyai
tridashendro maNipIThikAM vyatArIt 
sakalopahRtAbhiShechanIyaiH
RShayastAM shrutigIrbhirabhyaShinchan 4

त्वयि दत्तहृदे तदैव देव्यै
To that Goddess whose heart was set on You,
त्रिदशेन्द्रो मणिपीठिकां व्यतारीत् ।
Indra, offered her a throne of jewels!
सकलोपहृताभिषेचनीयै:
Gathering all substances of abhiSheka from everywhere,
ऋषयस्तां श्रुतिगीर्भिरभ्यषिञ्चन् ॥४॥
the sages, with hymns of the vedas, performed her abhiSheka!

अभिषेकजलानुपातिमुग्ध-
त्वदपाङ्गैरवभूषिताङ्गवल्लीम् ।
मणिकुण्डलपीतचेलहार-
प्रमुखैस्ताममरादयोऽन्वभूषन् ॥५॥
abhiShekajalAnupAtimugdha-
tvadapAngairavabhUShitAngavallIm 
maNikuNDalapItachelahAra-
pramukhaistAmamarAdayonvabhUShan 5

अभिषेकजलानुपातिमुग्ध-
Dazzling with the streaks of waters from that abhiSheka,
त्वदपाङ्गैरवभूषिताङ्गवल्लीम् ।
and ornamented with your side glances, her creeper like form,
मणिकुण्डलपीतचेलहार-
with gem-studded earrings, and with golden garments,
प्रमुखैस्ताममरादयोऽन्वभूषन् ॥५॥
the foremost of suras thus decorated her further!

वरणस्रजमात्तभृङ्गनादां
दधती सा कुचकुम्भमन्दयाना ।
पदशिञ्जितमञ्जुनूपुरा त्वां
कलितव्रीलविलासमाससाद ॥६॥
varaNasrajamAttabhRnganAdAM
dadhatI sA kuchakumbhamandayAnA 
padashinjitamanjunUpurA tvAM
kalitavrIlavilAsamAsasAda 6

वरणस्रजमात्तभृङ्गनादां
A wedding garland humming with bees trying to drink from it,
दधती सा कुचकुम्भमन्दयाना ।
holding it, she, of a gentle gait due to the heaviness of her bosom,
पदशिञ्जितमञ्जुनूपुरा त्वां
with the tinkling sounds of her beautiful anklets, you,
कलितव्रीलविलासमाससाद ॥६॥
who had a playful smile on your face, she approached!

गिरिशद्रुहिणादिसर्वदेवान्
गुणभाजोऽप्यविमुक्तदोषलेशान् ।
अवमृश्य सदैव सर्वरम्ये
निहिता त्वय्यनयाऽपि दिव्यमाला ॥७॥
girishadruhiNAdisarvadevAn
guNabhAjopyavimuktadoShaleshAn 
avamRshya sadaiva sarvaramye
nihitA tvayyanayApi divyamAlA 7

गिरिशद्रुहिणादिसर्वदेवान्
Shiva, Brahma and all other Devas,
गुणभाजोऽप्यविमुक्तदोषलेशान् ।
despite being of great virtues, not being free from very minor faults,
अवमृश्य सदैव सर्वरम्ये
she reflecting on all those, on the all-enchating form,
निहिता त्वय्यनयाऽपि दिव्यमाला ॥७॥
she then placed the divine garland, on You!

उरसा तरसा ममानिथैनां
भुवनानां जननीमनन्यभावाम् ।
त्वदुरोविलसत्तदीक्षणश्री-
परिवृष्ट्या परिपुष्टमास विश्वम् ॥८॥
urasA tarasA mamAnithainAM
bhuvanAnAM jananImananyabhAvAm 
tvadurovilasattadIkShaNashrI-
parivRShTyA paripuShTamAsa vishvam 8

उरसा तरसा ममानिथैनां
You honoured her at once by taking her to your bosom,
भुवनानां जननीमनन्यभावाम् ।
she, who is the mother of all worlds, who is devoted to no one but you!
त्वदुरोविलसत्तदीक्षणश्री-
Thus, with the auspicious glances from your resplendent chest,
परिवृष्ट्या परिपुष्टमास विश्वम् ॥८॥
showering riches, the entire universe became prosperous!

अतिमोहनविभ्रमा तदानीं
मदयन्ती खलु वारुणी निरागात् ।
तमस: पदवीमदास्त्वमेनाम्
अतिसम्माननया महासुरेभ्य: ॥९॥
atimohanavibhramA tadAnIM
madayantI khalu vAruNI nirAgAt 
tamasaH padavImadAstvamenAm
atisammAnanayA mahAsurebhyaH 9

अतिमोहनविभ्रमा तदानीं
Then, the utterly bewitching and bewildering
मदयन्ती खलु वारुणी निरागात् ।
intoxicating drink, Varuni, came forth!
तमस: पदवीमदास्त्वमेनाम्
You gave her the abode of Tamas,
अतिसम्माननया महासुरेभ्य: ॥९॥
and with great reverence offered her to the great asuras!

तरुणाम्बुदसुन्दरस्तदा त्वं
ननु धन्वन्तरिरुत्थितोऽम्बुराशे: ।
अमृतं कलशे वहन् कराभ्याम्
अखिलार्तिं हर मारुतालयेश ॥१०॥
taruNAmbudasundarastadA tvaM
nanu dhanvantarirutthitomburAsheH 
amRtaM kalashe vahan karAbhyAm
akhilArtiM hara mArutAlayesha 10

तरुणाम्बुदसुन्दरस्तदा त्वं
Then, you, beautiful like a fresh rain-cloud,
ननु धन्वन्तरिरुत्थितोऽम्बुराशे: ।
didn't you rise up from the ocean in the form of Dhanvantari,
अमृतं कलशे वहन् कराभ्याम्
carrying the vessel of Amrita in both your hands!
अखिलार्तिं हर मारुतालयेश ॥१०॥
Destroy all suffering, O Lord of the Abode of Wind!
