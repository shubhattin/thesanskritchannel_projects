ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

षट्चत्वारिंशम् दशकम्
ShaTchatvAriMsham dashakam

The Forty-sixth group of Ten shlokas

श्री कृष्णस्य वदने यशोदया लोकानां दर्शनम्
shrI kRShNasya vadane yashodayA lokAnAM darshanam

Yashoda Perceives All of Creation in Krishna's Mouth

अयि देव पुरा किल त्वयि स्वयमुत्तानशये स्तनन्धये ।
परिजृम्भणतो व्यपावृते वदने विश्वमचष्ट वल्लवी ॥१॥
ayi deva purA kila tvayi svayamuttAnashaye stanandhaye 
parijRmbhaNato vyapAvRte vadane vishvamachaShTa vallavI 1

अयि देव पुरा किल त्वयि स्वयमुत्तानशये स्तनन्धये ।
O Lord! Didn't once upon a time, as you lay on your back and were suckling at your mother's breasts,
परिजृम्भणतो व्यपावृते वदने विश्वमचष्ट वल्लवी ॥१॥
as you yawned, in your open mouth, didn't the Queen of the Cowherds see the entire creation!

पुनरप्यथ बालकै: समं त्वयि लीलानिरते जगत्पते ।
फलसञ्चयवञ्चनक्रुधा तव मृद्भोजनमूचुरर्भका: ॥२॥
punarapyatha bAlakaiH samaM tvayi lIlAnirate jagatpate 
phalasanchayavanchanakrudhA tava mRdbhojanamUchurarbhakAH 2

पुनरप्यथ बालकै: समं त्वयि लीलानिरते जगत्पते ।
Once again, O Lord of creation, when you were immersed in your play along with children,
फलसञ्चयवञ्चनक्रुधा तव मृद्भोजनमूचुरर्भका: ॥२॥
angered that you stole their fruits, those rascals told on you that you ate mud!

अयि ते प्रलयावधौ विभो क्षितितोयादिसमस्तभक्षिण: ।
मृदुपाशनतो रुजा भवेदिति भीता जननी चुकोप सा ॥३॥
ayi te pralayAvadhau vibho kShititoyAdisamastabhakShiNaH 
mRdupAshanato rujA bhavediti bhItA jananI chukopa sA 3

अयि ते प्रलयावधौ विभो क्षितितोयादिसमस्तभक्षिण: ।
O Lord! At the time of pralaya, you, who devours all of land, water and everything else,
मृदुपाशनतो रुजा भवेदिति भीता जननी चुकोप सा ॥३॥
by eating a little mud, afraid that you will fall sick, that mother got angry!

अयि दुर्विनयात्मक त्वया किमु मृत्सा बत वत्स भक्षिता ।
इति मातृगिरं चिरं विभो वितथां त्वं प्रतिजज्ञिषे हसन् ॥४॥
ayi durvinayAtmaka tvayA kimu mRtsA bata vatsa bhakShitA 
iti mAtRgiraM chiraM vibho vitathAM tvaM pratijajniShe hasan 4

अयि दुर्विनयात्मक त्वया किमु मृत्सा बत वत्स भक्षिता ।
"O Unruly Child! Tell me, did you really eat the mud, son?"
इति मातृगिरं चिरं विभो वितथां त्वं प्रतिजज्ञिषे हसन् ॥४॥
thus as your mother asked you, O Vibhu! You kept denying it for a long time, smiling!

अयि ते सकलैर्विनिश्चिते विमतिश्चेद्वदनं विदार्यताम् ।
इति मातृविभर्त्सितो मुखं विकसत्पद्मनिभं व्यदारय: ॥५॥
ayi te sakalairvinishchite vimatishchedvadanaM vidAryatAm 
iti mAtRvibhartsito mukhaM vikasatpadmanibhaM vyadArayaH 5

अयि ते सकलैर्विनिश्चिते विमतिश्चेद्वदनं विदार्यताम् ।
"Oh! If you deny what everyone had told me, open your mouth then!"
इति मातृविभर्त्सितो मुखं विकसत्पद्मनिभं व्यदारय: ॥५॥
thus admonished by your mother, like the blossoming of a lotus, you opened your mouth!

अपि मृल्लवदर्शनोत्सुकां जननीं तां बहु तर्पयन्निव ।
पृथिवीं निखिलां न केवलं भुवनान्यप्यखिलान्यदीदृश: ॥६॥
api mRllavadarshanotsukAM jananIM tAM bahu tarpayanniva 
pRthivIM nikhilAM na kevalaM bhuvanAnyapyakhilAnyadIdRshaH 6

अपि मृल्लवदर्शनोत्सुकां जननीं तां बहु तर्पयन्निव ।
And then, as if satisfying the mother's curiosity to spot some mud in your mouth,
पृथिवीं निखिलां न केवलं भुवनान्यप्यखिलान्यदीदृश: ॥६॥
not just the entire earth, but you showed her all the worlds and the entire cosmos!

कुहचिद्वनमम्बुधि: क्वचित् क्वचिदभ्रं कुहचिद्रसातलम् ।
मनुजा दनुजा: क्वचित् सुरा ददृशे किं न तदा त्वदानने ॥७॥
kuhachidvanamambudhiH kvachit kvachidabhraM kuhachidrasAtalam 
manujA danujAH kvachit surA dadRshe kiM na tadA tvadAnane 7

कुहचिद्वनमम्बुधि: क्वचित् क्वचिदभ्रं कुहचिद्रसातलम् ।
Somewhere in there were forests, somewhere oceans, sky, and rasAtala!
मनुजा दनुजा: क्वचित् सुरा ददृशे किं न तदा त्वदानने ॥७॥
Somewhere in there were also Asuras and Suras! What did she not see in your mouth!

कलशाम्बुधिशायिनं पुन: परवैकुण्ठपदाधिवासिनम् ।
स्वपुरश्च निजार्भकात्मकं कतिधा त्वां न ददर्श सा मुखे ॥८॥
kalashAmbudhishAyinaM punaH paravaikuNThapadAdhivAsinam 
svapurashcha nijArbhakAtmakaM katidhA tvAM na dadarsha sA mukhe 8

कलशाम्बुधिशायिनं पुन: परवैकुण्ठपदाधिवासिनम् ।
As the one who reclines in the ocean of milk! As the one who resides in the supreme abode of Vaikuntha!
स्वपुरश्च निजार्भकात्मकं कतिधा त्वां न ददर्श सा मुखे ॥८॥
Right in front of her, as her own wee child, in how many ways did she not see you in your own mouth!

विकसद्भुवने मुखोदरे ननु भूयोऽपि तथाविधानन: ।
अनया स्फुटमीक्षितो भवाननवस्थां जगतां बतातनोत् ॥९॥
vikasadbhuvane mukhodare nanu bhUyopi tathAvidhAnanaH 
anayA sphuTamIkShito bhavAnanavasthAM jagatAM batAtanot 9

विकसद्भुवने मुखोदरे ननु भूयोऽपि तथाविधानन: ।
Revealing all the worlds in your mouth, didn't you once again with that same mouth,
अनया स्फुटमीक्षितो भवाननवस्थां जगतां बतातनोत् ॥९॥
showing those worlds to her clearly, tell her even of the unmanifest aspects of creation! 

धृततत्त्वधियं तदा क्षणं जननीं तां प्रणयेन मोहयन् ।
स्तनमम्ब दिशेत्युपासजन् भगवन्नद्भुतबाल पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
dhRtatattvadhiyaM tadA kShaNaM jananIM tAM praNayena mohayan 
stanamamba dishetyupAsajan bhagavannadbhutabAla pAhi mAm 10

धृततत्त्वधियं तदा क्षणं जननीं तां प्रणयेन मोहयन् ।
As your mother stood there for a moment, in complete realization of truth, once again enchanting her with your love,
स्तनमम्ब दिशेत्युपासजन् भगवन्नद्भुतबाल पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
approaching her seeking her breasts, O Lord! O Wondrous Child! Protect me!
