अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्तपञ्चाशम् दशकम्
saptapanchAsham dashakam

The Fifty-seventh group of Ten shlokas

प्रलंबासुर वध:
pralambAsura vadhaH

Slaying of Pralambasura

रामसख: क्वापि दिने कामद भगवन् गतो भवान् विपिनम् ।
सूनुभिरपि गोपानां धेनुभिरभिसंवृतो लसद्वेष: ॥१॥
rAmasakhaH kvApi dine kAmada bhagavan gato bhavAn vipinam 
sUnubhirapi gopAnAM dhenubhirabhisaMvRto lasadveShaH 1

रामसख: क्वापि दिने कामद भगवन् गतो भवान् विपिनम् ।
Along with your dear Balarama, O Bestower of Desires, One Day, you went to the forest,
सूनुभिरपि गोपानां धेनुभिरभिसंवृतो लसद्वेष: ॥१॥
accompanied by the sons of the cowherds, surrounded by the cows, in a well decorated attire!

सन्दर्शयन् बलाय स्वैरं वृन्दावनश्रियं विमलाम् ।
काण्डीरै: सह बालैर्भाण्डीरकमागमो वटं क्रीडन् ॥२॥
sandarshayan balAya svairaM vRndAvanashriyaM vimalAm 
kANDIraiH saha bAlairbhANDIrakamAgamo vaTaM krIDan 2

सन्दर्शयन् बलाय स्वैरं वृन्दावनश्रियं विमलाम् ।
Showing around the pristine natural beauty of Vrindavana to Balarama, joyfully,
काण्डीरै: सह बालैर्भाण्डीरकमागमो वटं क्रीडन् ॥२॥
playing around with sticks and staffs, you reached the Bhandiraka tree along with all the children.

तावत्तावकनिधनस्पृहयालुर्गोपमूर्तिरदयालु: ।
दैत्य: प्रलम्बनामा प्रलम्बबाहुं भवन्तमापेदे ॥३॥
tAvattAvakanidhanaspRhayAlurgopamUrtiradayAluH 
daityaH pralambanAmA pralambabAhuM bhavantamApede 3

तावत्तावकनिधनस्पृहयालुर्गोपमूर्तिरदयालु: ।
Then, desiring to kill you, in the form of a cowherd boy, the merciless
दैत्य: प्रलम्बनामा प्रलम्बबाहुं भवन्तमापेदे ॥३॥
asura by the name of Pralamba, with long reaching arms approached you!

जानन्नप्यविजानन्निव तेन समं निबद्धसौहार्द: ।
वटनिकटे पटुपशुपव्याबद्धं द्वन्द्वयुद्धमारब्धा: ॥४॥
jAnannapyavijAnanniva tena samaM nibaddhasauhArdaH 
vaTanikaTe paTupashupavyAbaddhaM dvandvayuddhamArabdhAH 4

जानन्नप्यविजानन्निव तेन समं निबद्धसौहार्द: ।
Despite knowing this, pretending to not know, being friendly with him,
वटनिकटे पटुपशुपव्याबद्धं द्वन्द्वयुद्धमारब्धा: ॥४॥
near the Banyan tree, you started having a wrestling match with all the skilled cowherd boys!

गोपान् विभज्य तन्वन् सङ्घं बलभद्रकं भवत्कमपि ।
त्वद्बलभीरुं दैत्यं त्वद्बलगतमन्वमन्यथा भगवन् ॥५॥
gopAn vibhajya tanvan sanghaM balabhadrakaM bhavatkamapi 
tvadbalabhIruM daityaM tvadbalagatamanvamanyathA bhagavan 5

गोपान् विभज्य तन्वन् सङ्घं बलभद्रकं भवत्कमपि ।
Dividing the Gopas into two teams, one yours and one Balarama's,
त्वद्बलभीरुं दैत्यं त्वद्बलगतमन्वमन्यथा भगवन् ॥५॥
the asura who was afraid of your strength, you agreed to have him on your team, O Lord!

कल्पितविजेतृवहने समरे परयूथगं स्वदयिततरम् ।
श्रीदामानमधत्था: पराजितो भक्तदासतां प्रथयन् ॥६॥
kalpitavijetRvahane samare parayUthagaM svadayitataram 
shrIdAmAnamadhatthAH parAjito bhaktadAsatAM prathayan 6

कल्पितविजेतृवहने समरे परयूथगं स्वदयिततरम् ।
Creating the rule of the victor being carried by the defeated, in the opposite team, your dearest friend
श्रीदामानमधत्था: पराजितो भक्तदासतां प्रथयन् ॥६॥
by the name of Shridama, being defeated by him, you demonstrated your service to your devotees!

एवं बहुषु विभूमन् बालेषु वहत्सु वाह्यमानेषु ।
रामविजित: प्रलम्बो जहार तं दूरतो भवद्भीत्या ॥७॥
evaM bahuShu vibhUman bAleShu vahatsu vAhyamAneShu 
rAmavijitaH pralambo jahAra taM dUrato bhavadbhItyA 7

एवं बहुषु विभूमन् बालेषु वहत्सु वाह्यमानेषु ।
Like this, O Multifaceted One! As many children were being carried, and carrying each other in the game,
रामविजित: प्रलम्बो जहार तं दूरतो भवद्भीत्या ॥७॥
Pralamba, defeated by Balarama, carried him and took him far away, being afraid of you!

त्वद्दूरं गमयन्तं तं दृष्ट्वा हलिनि विहितगरिमभरे ।
दैत्य: स्वरूपमागाद्यद्रूपात् स हि बलोऽपि चकितोऽभूत् ॥८॥
tvaddUraM gamayantaM taM dRShTvA halini vihitagarimabhare 
daityaH svarUpamAgAdyadrUpAt sa hi balopi chakitobhUt 8

त्वद्दूरं गमयन्तं तं दृष्ट्वा हलिनि विहितगरिमभरे ।
Seeing that he was being carried far away from you, as Balarama increased to enormous weight,
दैत्य: स्वरूपमागाद्यद्रूपात् स हि बलोऽपि चकितोऽभूत् ॥८॥
the asura came back to his original form, looking at which even Balarama was shocked!

उच्चतया दैत्यतनोस्त्वन्मुखमालोक्य दूरतो राम: ।
विगतभयो दृढमुष्ट्या भृशदुष्टं सपदि पिष्टवानेनम् ॥९॥
uchchatayA daityatanostvanmukhamAlokya dUrato rAmaH 
vigatabhayo dRDhamuShTyA bhRshaduShTaM sapadi piShTavAnenam 9

उच्चतया दैत्यतनोस्त्वन्मुखमालोक्य दूरतो राम: ।
Due to the height of the asura's body, Balarama now able to see your face from afar,
विगतभयो दृढमुष्ट्या भृशदुष्टं सपदि पिष्टवानेनम् ॥९॥
devoid of fear, with a firm fist, immediately thrashed that wicked asura!

हत्वा दानववीरं प्राप्तं बलमालिलिङ्गिथ प्रेम्णा ।
तावन्मिलतोर्युवयो: शिरसि कृता पुष्पवृष्टिरमरगणै: ॥१०॥
hatvA dAnavavIraM prAptaM balamAlilingitha premNA 
tAvanmilatoryuvayoH shirasi kRtA puShpavRShTiramaragaNaiH 10

हत्वा दानववीरं प्राप्तं बलमालिलिङ्गिथ प्रेम्णा ।
Killing that powerful asura, as Balarama came back, embracing him lovingly,
तावन्मिलतोर्युवयो: शिरसि कृता पुष्पवृष्टिरमरगणै: ॥१०॥
as you were united once again, a torrent of flowers were showered over your heads by the Devas!

आलम्बो भुवनानां प्रालम्बं निधनमेवमारचयन् ।
कालं विहाय सद्यो लोलम्बरुचे हरे हरे: क्लेशान् ॥११॥
Alambo bhuvanAnAM prAlambaM nidhanamevamArachayan 
kAlaM vihAya sadyo lolambaruche hare hareH kleshAn 11

आलम्बो भुवनानां प्रालम्बं निधनमेवमारचयन् ।
O Support of All the Worlds! Thus conducting the end of Pralambasura,
कालं विहाय सद्यो लोलम्बरुचे हरे हरे: क्लेशान् ॥११॥
without any delay, O One Resplendant like a Bumblebee, remove my afflictions!
