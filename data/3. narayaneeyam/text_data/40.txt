ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

चत्वारिंशम् दशकम्
chatvAriMsham dashakam

The Fortieth group of Ten shlokas

पूतना मोक्ष:
pUtanA mokShaH

Putana's Liberation

तदनु नन्दममन्दशुभास्पदं नृपपुरीं करदानकृते गतम्।
समवलोक्य जगाद भवत्पिता विदितकंससहायजनोद्यम: ॥१॥
tadanu nandamamandashubhAspadaM nRpapurIM karadAnakRte gatam
samavalokya jagAda bhavatpitA viditakaMsasahAyajanodyamaH 1

तदनु नन्दममन्दशुभास्पदं नृपपुरीं करदानकृते गतम्।
And then, when the immensely virtuous Nanda went to the Capital to pay his taxes,
समवलोक्य जगाद भवत्पिता विदितकंससहायजनोद्यम: ॥१॥
looking at him, your father Vasudeva told him of the efforts of Kamsa and his supporters!

अयि सखे तव बालकजन्म मां सुखयतेऽद्य निजात्मजजन्मवत् ।
इति भवत्पितृतां व्रजनायके समधिरोप्य शशंस तमादरात् ॥२॥
ayi sakhe tava bAlakajanma mAM sukhayatedya nijAtmajajanmavat 
iti bhavatpitRtAM vrajanAyake samadhiropya shashaMsa tamAdarAt 2

अयि सखे तव बालकजन्म मां सुखयतेऽद्य निजात्मजजन्मवत् ।
"O friend! The birth of your son rejoices me as if I had a son myself!"
इति भवत्पितृतां व्रजनायके समधिरोप्य शशंस तमादरात् ॥२॥
thus his fatherhood of you he cleverly conveyed to Nanda, and praised him with his regards!

इह च सन्त्यनिमित्तशतानि ते कटकसीम्नि ततो लघु गम्यताम् ।
इति च तद्वचसा व्रजनायको भवदपायभिया द्रुतमाययौ ॥३॥
iha cha santyanimittashatAni te kaTakasImni tato laghu gamyatAm 
iti cha tadvachasA vrajanAyako bhavadapAyabhiyA drutamAyayau 3

इह च सन्त्यनिमित्तशतानि ते कटकसीम्नि ततो लघु गम्यताम् ।
"There are hundreds of bad omens lurking in this place. You should go back to your place quickly!"
इति च तद्वचसा व्रजनायको भवदपायभिया द्रुतमाययौ ॥३॥
and with these words, the leader of Vrajas, fearing danger to you, went back quickly!

अवसरे खलु तत्र च काचन व्रजपदे मधुराकृतिरङ्गना ।
तरलषट्पदलालितकुन्तला कपटपोतक ते निकटं गता ॥४॥
avasare khalu tatra cha kAchana vrajapade madhurAkRtiranganA 
taralaShaTpadalAlitakuntalA kapaTapotaka te nikaTaM gatA 4

अवसरे खलु तत्र च काचन व्रजपदे मधुराकृतिरङ्गना ।
At that very moment, there in the land of Vrajas appeared a woman of an enchanting form!
तरलषट्पदलालितकुन्तला कपटपोतक ते निकटं गता ॥४॥
With her braids of hair beautified by hovering bumblebees, O One Who is in the false disguise of a Child, she approached you!

सपदि सा हृतबालकचेतना निशिचरान्वयजा किल पूतना ।
व्रजवधूष्विह केयमिति क्षणं विमृशतीषु भवन्तमुपाददे ॥५॥
sapadi sA hRtabAlakachetanA nishicharAnvayajA kila pUtanA 
vrajavadhUShviha keyamiti kShaNaM vimRshatIShu bhavantamupAdade 5

सपदि सा हृतबालकचेतना निशिचरान्वयजा किल पूतना ।
At once, she who took the lives of many children, she who was born in the clan of rakshasas, didn't that Putana
व्रजवधूष्विह केयमिति क्षणं विमृशतीषु भवन्तमुपाददे ॥५॥
as the women of Vraja were still wondering as to who she was, she quickly lifted you up!

ललितभावविलासहृतात्मभिर्युवतिभि: प्रतिरोद्धुमपारिता ।
स्तनमसौ भवनान्तनिषेदुषी प्रददुषी भवते कपटात्मने ॥६॥
lalitabhAvavilAsahRtAtmabhiryuvatibhiH pratiroddhumapAritA 
stanamasau bhavanAntaniSheduShI pradaduShI bhavate kapaTAtmane 6

ललितभावविलासहृतात्मभिर्युवतिभि: प्रतिरोद्धुमपारिता ।
Those women who were charmed by her graceful features and actions, could not come to stop her from doing so!
स्तनमसौ भवनान्तनिषेदुषी प्रददुषी भवते कपटात्मने ॥५॥
Sitting inside the house, she started nursing you, the illusory child, at her breasts!

समधिरुह्य तदङ्कमशङ्कितस्त्वमथ बालकलोपनरोषित: ।
महदिवाम्रफलं कुचमण्डलं प्रतिचुचूषिथ दुर्विषदूषितम् ॥७॥
samadhiruhya tadankamashankitastvamatha bAlakalopanaroShitaH 
mahadivAmraphalaM kuchamaNDalaM pratichuchUShitha durviShadUShitam 7

समधिरुह्य तदङ्कमशङ्कितस्त्वमथ बालकलोपनरोषित: ।
Climbing into her lap without hesitation, angered by her killing all those children,
महदिवाम्रफलं कुचमण्डलं प्रतिचुचूषिथ दुर्विषदूषितम् ॥७॥
as if it were a huge mango, you started suckling on her poison smeared bosom!

असुभिरेव समं धयति त्वयि स्तनमसौ स्तनितोपमनिस्वना ।
निरपतद्भयदायि निजं वपु: प्रतिगता प्रविसार्य भुजावुभौ ॥८॥
asubhireva samaM dhayati tvayi stanamasau stanitopamanisvanA 
nirapatadbhayadAyi nijaM vapuH pratigatA pravisArya bhujAvubhau 8

असुभिरेव समं धयति त्वयि स्तनमसौ स्तनितोपमनिस्वना ।
As you sucked out her very life along with the breast milk, with a roar like thunder,
निरपतद्भयदायि निजं वपु: प्रतिगता प्रविसार्य भुजावुभौ ॥८॥
her real ferocious form fell to the ground, with both her arms outstrectched!

भयदघोषणभीषणविग्रहश्रवणदर्शनमोहितवल्लवे ।
व्रजपदे तदुर:स्थलखेलनं ननु भवन्तमगृह्णत गोपिका: ।।९॥
bhayadaghoShaNabhIShaNavigrahashravaNadarshanamohitavallave 
vrajapade taduraHsthalakhelanaM nanu bhavantamagRhNata gopikAH 9

भयदघोषणभीषणविग्रहश्रवणदर्शनमोहितवल्लवे ।
Hearing that utterly terrifying sound, and looking at her ferocious form, as the Gopas who were stunned
व्रजपदे तदुर:स्थलखेलनं ननु भवन्तमगृह्णत गोपिका: ।।९॥
in the land of the vrajas, the gopikas picked you up as you were innocently playing on her chest!

भुवनमङ्गलनामभिरेव ते युवतिभिर्बहुधा कृतरक्षण: ।
त्वमयि वातनिकेतननाथ मामगदयन् कुरु तावकसेवकम् ॥१०॥
bhuvanamangalanAmabhireva te yuvatibhirbahudhA kRtarakShaNaH 
tvamayi vAtaniketananAtha mAmagadayan kuru tAvakasevakam 10

भुवनमङ्गलनामभिरेव ते युवतिभिर्बहुधा कृतरक्षण: ।
O One Who Bestows Auspiciousness to the entire world! You are the one who with your names protected these women in various different ways!
त्वमयि वातनिकेतननाथ मामगदयन् कुरु तावकसेवकम् ॥१०॥
That you, O Lord of the Abode of Wind, freeing me of my ailments, make me a devotee in your service!
