ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

तृतीयम् दशकम्
tRtIyam dashakam

The Third group of Ten shlokas

भक्ति प्रार्थना
bhakti prArthanA

Prayer of Devotion

पठन्तो नामानि प्रमदभरसिन्धौ निपतिता:
स्मरन्तो रूपं ते वरद कथयन्तो गुणकथा: ।
चरन्तो ये भक्तास्त्वयि खलु रमन्ते परममून्
अहं धन्यान् मन्ये समधिगतसर्वाभिलषितान् ॥१॥
paThanto nAmAni pramadabharasindhau nipatitAH
smaranto rUpaM te varada kathayanto guNakathAH 
charanto ye bhaktAstvayi khalu ramante paramamUn
ahaM dhanyAn manye samadhigatasarvAbhilaShitAn 1

पठन्तो नामानि प्रमदभरसिन्धौ निपतिता:
Chanting your names, they fall into the ocean of intoxication,
स्मरन्तो रूपं ते वरद कथयन्तो गुणकथा: ।
remembering your form, O Bestower of Boons, and narrating your stories!
चरन्तो ये भक्तास्त्वयि खलु रमन्ते परममून्
Thus wandering about, don't your devotees ever rejoice in you! But
अहं धन्यान् मन्ये समधिगतसर्वाभिलषितान् ॥१॥
I consider them as being truly fortunate, and as having fulfilled all their desires!

गदक्लिष्टं कष्टं तव चरणसेवारसभरेऽ-
प्यनासक्तं चित्तं भवति बत विष्णो कुरु दयाम् ।
भवत्पादाम्भोजस्मरणरसिको नामनिवहान्
अहं गायं गायं कुहचन विवत्स्यामि विजने ॥२॥
gadakliShTaM kaShTaM tava charaNasevArasabhare-
pyanAsaktaM chittaM bhavati bata viShNo kuru dayAm 
bhavatpAdAmbhojasmaraNarasiko nAmanivahAn
ahaM gAyaM gAyaM kuhachana vivatsyAmi vijane 2

गदक्लिष्टं कष्टं तव चरणसेवारसभरेऽ-
Entangled by disease, and troubled in relishing the service of your feet
प्यनासक्तं चित्तं भवति बत विष्णो कुरु दयाम् ।
my mind is unable to find interest, say O Vishnu! please have mercy!
भवत्पादाम्भोजस्मरणरसिको नामनिवहान्
Rejoicing in the memory of your lotus like feet, your series of names
अहं गायं गायं कुहचन विवत्स्यामि विजने ॥२॥
I shall continue to sing and go reside somewhere in a secluded place!

कृपा ते जाता चेत्किमिव न हि लभ्यं तनुभृतां
मदीयक्लेशौघप्रशमनदशा नाम कियती ।
न के के लोकेऽस्मिन्ननिशमयि शोकाभिरहिता
भवद्भक्ता मुक्ता: सुखगतिमसक्ता विदधते ॥३॥
kRpA te jAtA chetkimiva na hi labhyaM tanubhRtAM
madIyakleshaughaprashamanadashA nAma kiyatI 
na ke ke lokesminnanishamayi shokAbhirahitA
bhavadbhaktA muktAH sukhagatimasaktA vidadhate 3

कृपा ते जाता चेत्किमिव न हि लभ्यं तनुभृतां
If your compassion has been obtained, what here is unattainable to physical creatures!
मदीयक्लेशौघप्रशमनदशा नाम कियती ।
My heaps of troubles, pacifying them is what big task!
न के के लोकेऽस्मिन्ननिशमयि शोकाभिरहिता
Aren't there so many people in this world who are eternally free from sorrow!
भवद्भक्ता मुक्ता: सुखगतिमसक्ता विदधते ॥३॥
Your devotees, the liberated ones, uninterested in worldly pleasures, who are truly free!

मुनिप्रौढा रूढा जगति खलु गूढात्मगतयो
भवत्पादाम्भोजस्मरणविरुजो नारदमुखा: ।
चरन्तीश स्वैरं सततपरिनिर्भातपरचि -
त्सदानन्दाद्वैतप्रसरपरिमग्ना: किमपरम् ॥४॥
muniprauDhA rUDhA jagati khalu gUDhAtmagatayo
bhavatpAdAmbhojasmaraNavirujo nAradamukhAH 
charantIsha svairaM satataparinirbhAtaparachi -
tsadAnandAdvaitaprasaraparimagnAH kimaparam 4

मुनिप्रौढा रूढा जगति खलु गूढात्मगतयो
Accomplished sages who are well versed with the world, aren't they the ones who move around unnoticed!
भवत्पादाम्भोजस्मरणविरुजो नारदमुखा: ।
The ones who are free of their sorrows through meditating upon your lotus like feet, the ones like nArada and others,
चरन्तीश स्वैरं सततपरिनिर्भातपरचि -
wander about, O Lord, as they wish, always indulged in spreading the light
त्सदानन्दाद्वैतप्रसरपरिमग्ना: किमपरम् ॥४॥
of consciousness that leads to the bliss of the non-dual realization! What else is there beyond that!

भवद्भक्ति: स्फीता भवतु मम सैव प्रशमयेद्
अशेषक्लेशौघं न खलु हृदि सन्देहकणिका ।
न चेद्व्यासस्योक्तिस्तव च वचनं नैगमवचो
भवेन्मिथ्या रथ्यापुरुषवचनप्रायमखिलम् ॥५॥
bhavadbhaktiH sphItA bhavatu mama saiva prashamayed
asheShakleshaughaM na khalu hRdi sandehakaNikA 
na chedvyAsasyoktistava cha vachanaM naigamavacho
bhavenmithyA rathyApuruShavachanaprAyamakhilam 5

भवद्भक्ति: स्फीता भवतु मम सैव प्रशमयेद्
My devotion to you, may it find full-fledged expression! That alone may pacify my
अशेषक्लेशौघं न खलु हृदि सन्देहकणिका ।
endless heaps of obstacles, there is absolutely no speck of doubt in my heart about that!
न चेद्व्यासस्योक्तिस्तव च वचनं नैगमवचो
Otherwise Vyasa's words, your own words, and of the Vedas
भवेन्मिथ्या रथ्यापुरुषवचनप्रायमखिलम् ॥५॥
would become myths! Otherwise, they would become wayward ramblings of the street rogues!

भवद्भक्तिस्तावत् प्रमुखमधुरा त्वत् गुणरसात्
किमप्यारूढा चेदखिलपरितापप्रशमनी ।
पुनश्चान्ते स्वान्ते विमलपरिबोधोदयमिलन्
महानन्दाद्वैतं दिशति किमत: प्रार्थ्यमपरम् ॥६॥
bhavadbhaktistAvat pramukhamadhurA tvat guNarasAt
kimapyArUDhA chedakhilaparitApaprashamanI 
punashchAnte svAnte vimalaparibodhodayamilan
mahAnandAdvaitaM dishati kimataH prArthyamaparam 6

भवद्भक्तिस्तावत् प्रमुखमधुरा त्वत् गुणरसात्
Devotion to you is indeed of exceeding sweetness because of the nature of your own attributes,
किमप्यारूढा चेदखिलपरितापप्रशमनी ।
Even if begotten a little, it pacifies all kinds of suffering.
पुनश्चान्ते स्वान्ते विमलपरिबोधोदयमिलन्
Again towards the end, at the end of one's self, it unites one with the dawning of the pure knowledge
महानन्दाद्वैतं दिशति किमत: प्रार्थ्यमपरम् ॥६॥
of the blissful non-duality of existence! So what else is there to pray for!

विधूय क्लेशान्मे कुरु चरणयुग्मं धृतरसं
भवत्क्षेत्रप्राप्तौ करमपि च ते पूजनविधौ ।
भवन्मूर्त्यालोके नयनमथ ते पादतुलसी-
परिघ्राणे घ्राणं श्रवणमपि ते चारुचरिते ॥७॥
vidhUya kleshAnme kuru charaNayugmaM dhRtarasaM
bhavatkShetraprAptau karamapi cha te pUjanavidhau 
bhavanmUrtyAloke nayanamatha te pAdatulasI-
parighrANe ghrANaM shravaNamapi te chArucharite 7

विधूय क्लेशान्मे कुरु चरणयुग्मं धृतरसं
Remove my obstacles, make my pair of feet firmly rejoice
भवत्क्षेत्रप्राप्तौ करमपि च ते पूजनविधौ ।
in reaching your abode, and my hands rejoice in your prayer!
भवन्मूर्त्यालोके नयनमथ ते पादतुलसी-
In looking at your form, may my eyes rejoice, and the tulasI at your feet,
परिघ्राणे घ्राणं श्रवणमपि ते चारुचरिते ॥७॥
by smelling that may my nose rejoice, and may my ears rejoice when they hear of your beautiful acts!

प्रभूताधिव्याधिप्रसभचलिते मामकहृदि
त्वदीयं तद्रूपं परमसुखचिद्रूपमुदियात् ।
उदञ्चद्रोमाञ्चो गलितबहुहर्षाश्रुनिवहो
यथा विस्मर्यासं दुरुपशमपीडापरिभवान् ॥८॥
prabhUtAdhivyAdhiprasabhachalite mAmakahRdi
tvadIyaM tadrUpaM paramasukhachidrUpamudiyAt 
udanchadromAncho galitabahuharShAshrunivaho
yathA vismaryAsaM durupashamapIDAparibhavAn 8

प्रभूताधिव्याधिप्रसभचलिते मामकहृदि
Having been afflicted by the onslaught of chronic ailments, in my heart
त्वदीयं तद्रूपं परमसुखचिद्रूपमुदियात् ।
that form of yours which is of the nature of conscious bliss, may it arise,
उदञ्चद्रोमाञ्चो गलितबहुहर्षाश्रुनिवहो
inciting a hair-raising experience, and causing a flood of happy tears,
यथा विस्मर्यासं दुरुपशमपीडापरिभवान् ॥८॥
so that I may forget the suffering brought forth by incurable afflictions!

मरुद्गेहाधीश त्वयि खलु पराञ्चोऽपि सुखिनो
भवत्स्नेही सोऽहं सुबहु परितप्ये च किमिदम् ।
अकीर्तिस्ते मा भूद्वरद गदभारं प्रशमयन्
भवत् भक्तोत्तंसं झटिति कुरु मां कंसदमन ॥९॥
marudgehAdhIsha tvayi khalu parAnchopi sukhino
bhavatsnehI sohaM subahu paritapye cha kimidam 
akIrtiste mA bhUdvarada gadabhAraM prashamayan
bhavat bhaktottaMsaM jhaTiti kuru mAM kaMsadamana 9

मरुद्गेहाधीश त्वयि खलु पराञ्चोऽपि सुखिनो
O Ruler of the Abode of the Wind! Isn't it within you that even the non-believers exist happily!
भवत्स्नेही सोऽहं सुबहु परितप्ये च किमिदम् ।
I am affectionate towards you, but I am going through enormous suffering! What is this!
अकीर्तिस्ते मा भूद्वरद गदभारं प्रशमयन्
May there be no ill reputation for you, O Bestower of Boons! Pacifying the burden of my illness,
भवत् भक्तोत्तंसं झटिति कुरु मां कंसदमन ॥९॥
quickly make me the best of your devotees, O Vanquisher of Kamsa!

किमुक्तैर्भूयोभिस्तव हि करुणा यावदुदियाद्
अहं तावद्देव प्रहितविविधार्तप्रलपितः ।
पुरः क्लृप्ते पादे वरद तव नेष्यामि दिवसान्
यथाशक्ति व्यक्तं नतिनुतिनिषेवा विरचयन् ॥१०॥
kimuktairbhUyobhistava hi karuNA yAvadudiyAd
ahaM tAvaddeva prahitavividhArtapralapitaH 
puraH klRpte pAde varada tava neShyAmi divasAn
yathAshakti vyaktaM natinutiniShevA virachayan 10

किमुक्तैर्भूयोभिस्तव हि करुणा यावदुदियाद्
What's the use of all these words repeatedly! Until your compassion arises
अहं तावद्देव प्रहितविविधार्तप्रलपितः ।
I, until then, O Lord, am going to keep submitting the rant of all my sufferings!
पुरः क्लृप्ते पादे वरद तव नेष्यामि दिवसान्
In short, in your feet that are in front of me, I shall spend my days
यथाशक्ति व्यक्तं नतिनुतिनिषेवा विरचयन् ॥१०॥
certainly to the best of my abilities, performing your prostrations, praise, and prayer!
