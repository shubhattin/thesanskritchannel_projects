अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अष्टाशीतितमम् दशकम्
aShTAshItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-eighth group of Ten shlokas

श्री सन्तान गोपाल:
shrI santAna gopAlaH

Santhana Gopala

प्रागेवाचार्यपुत्राहृतिनिशमनया स्वीयषट्सूनुवीक्षां
काङ्क्षन्त्या मातुरुक्त्या सुतलभुवि बलिं प्राप्य तेनार्चितस्त्वम् ।
धातु: शापाद्धिरण्यान्वितकशिपुभवान् शौरिजान् कंसभग्नान्
आनीयैनान् प्रदर्श्य स्वपदमनयथा: पूर्वपुत्रान् मरीचे: ॥१॥
prAgevAchAryaputrAhRtinishamanayA svIyaShaTsUnuvIkShAM
kAnkShantyA mAturuktyA sutalabhuvi baliM prApya tenArchitastvam 
dhAtuH shApAddhiraNyAnvitakashipubhavAn shaurijAn kaMsabhagnAn
AnIyainAn pradarshya svapadamanayathAH pUrvaputrAn marIcheH 1

प्रागेवाचार्यपुत्राहृतिनिशमनया स्वीयषट्सूनुवीक्षां
Having heard that you once brought back your Guru's son from death, to see her own six sons,
काङ्क्षन्त्या मातुरुक्त्या सुतलभुवि बलिं प्राप्य तेनार्चितस्त्वम् ।
as your mother expressed her wish, you went to King Bali in land of Sutala and were worshipped by him!
धातु: शापाद्धिरण्यान्वितकशिपुभवान् शौरिजान् कंसभग्नान्
They, who, upon Brahma's curse, were first Marichi's sons, then Hiranyakashipu's and then Vasudeva's, when they were killed by Kamsa,
आनीयैनान् प्रदर्श्य स्वपदमनयथा: पूर्वपुत्रान् मरीचे: ॥१॥
bringing them back, having your mother see them, you sent them back to their abodes!

श्रुतदेव इति श्रुतं द्विजेन्द्रं
बहुलाश्वं नृपतिं च भक्तिपूर्णम् ।
युगपत्त्वमनुग्रहीतुकामो
मिथिलां प्रापिथ तापसै: समेत: ॥२॥
shrutadeva iti shrutaM dvijendraM
bahulAshvaM nRpatiM cha bhaktipUrNam 
yugapattvamanugrahItukAmo
mithilAM prApitha tApasaiH sametaH 2

श्रुतदेव इति श्रुतं द्विजेन्द्रं
A great Dvija by the name of Shrutadeva,
बहुलाश्वं नृपतिं च भक्तिपूर्णम् ।
and a highly devoted king by the name of Bahulashva,
युगपत्त्वमनुग्रहीतुकामो
with the desire to bless them both,
मिथिलां प्रापिथ तापसै: समेत: ॥२॥
you went to the land of Mithila along with the sages!

गच्छन् द्विमूर्तिरुभयोर्युगपन्निकेतम्
एकेन भूरिविभवैर्विहितोपचार: ।
अन्येन तद्दिनभृतैश्च फलौदनाद्यै:
तुल्यं प्रसेदिथ ददाथ च मुक्तिमाभ्याम् ॥३॥
gachChan dvimUrtirubhayoryugapanniketam
ekena bhUrivibhavairvihitopachAraH 
anyena taddinabhRtaishcha phalaudanAdyaiH
tulyaM praseditha dadAtha cha muktimAbhyAm 3

गच्छन् द्विमूर्तिरुभयोर्युगपन्निकेतम्
Going to both their houses at once, taking on two different forms,
एकेन भूरिविभवैर्विहितोपचार: ।
by one you were honoured with bountiful offerings,
अन्येन तद्दिनभृतैश्च फलौदनाद्यै:
and by the other with fruits and other alms gotten through begging that day!
तुल्यं प्रसेदिथ ददाथ च मुक्तिमाभ्याम् ॥३॥
Equally pleased with both of them, you granted Mukti to them both!

भूयोऽथ द्वारवत्यां द्विजतनयमृतिं तत्प्रलापानपि त्वम्
को वा दैवं निरुन्ध्यादिति किल कथयन् विश्ववोढाप्यसोढा: ।
जिष्णोर्गर्वं विनेतुं त्वयि मनुजधिया कुण्ठितां चास्य बुद्धिं
तत्त्वारूढां विधातुं परमतमपदप्रेक्षणेनेति मन्ये ॥४॥
bhUyotha dvAravatyAM dvijatanayamRtiM tatpralApAnapi tvam
ko vA daivaM nirundhyAditi kila kathayan vishvavoDhApyasoDhAH 
jiShNorgarvaM vinetuM tvayi manujadhiyA kuNThitAM chAsya buddhiM
tattvArUDhAM vidhAtuM paramatamapadaprekShaNeneti manye 4

भूयोऽथ द्वारवत्यां द्विजतनयमृतिं तत्प्रलापानपि त्वम्
Then, once again in Dwaraka, hearing the cries of a Brahmin, upon the deaths of his children, you
को वा दैवं निरुन्ध्यादिति किल कथयन् विश्ववोढाप्यसोढा: ।
saying, "Who may overcome destiny!" didn't you even bear the disbelief of everyone!
जिष्णोर्गर्वं विनेतुं त्वयि मनुजधिया कुण्ठितां चास्य बुद्धिं
In order to pacify Arjuna's pride, to correct his twisted understanding that you are just another human,
तत्त्वारूढां विधातुं परमतमपदप्रेक्षणेनेति मन्ये ॥४॥
to give him an understanding of the truth, and a glimpse of your highest abode, I believe you may have said so!

नष्टा अष्टास्य पुत्रा: पुनरपि तव तूपेक्षया कष्टवाद:
स्पष्टो जातो जनानामथ तदवसरे द्वारकामाप पार्थ: ।
मैत्र्या तत्रोषितोऽसौ नवमसुतमृतौ विप्रवर्यप्ररोदं
श्रुत्वा चक्रे प्रतिज्ञामनुपहृतसुत: सन्निवेक्ष्ये कृशानुम् ॥५॥
naShTA aShTAsya putrAH punarapi tava tUpekShayA kaShTavAdaH
spaShTo jAto janAnAmatha tadavasare dvArakAmApa pArthaH 
maitryA tatroShitosau navamasutamRtau vipravaryaprarodaM
shrutvA chakre pratijnAmanupahRtasutaH sannivekShye kRshAnum 5

नष्टा अष्टास्य पुत्रा: पुनरपि तव तूपेक्षया कष्टवाद:
Eight of his sons died, and due to your neglect you started attracting blame!
स्पष्टो जातो जनानामथ तदवसरे द्वारकामाप पार्थ: ।
This blame got more and more evident among the people, and during that time, Arjuna came to Dwaraka!
मैत्र्या तत्रोषितोऽसौ नवमसुतमृतौ विप्रवर्यप्ररोदं
As he was residing there cordially, upon hearing the cries of the Brahmin when the ninth child died,
श्रुत्वा चक्रे प्रतिज्ञामनुपहृतसुत: सन्निवेक्ष्ये कृशानुम् ॥५॥
he made a pledge saying, "I shall bring back your dead child, or I myself shall enter the fire!"

मानी स त्वामपृष्ट्वा द्विजनिलयगतो बाणजालैर्महास्त्रैः
रुन्धान: सूतिगेहं पुनरपि सहसा दृष्टनष्टे कुमारे ।
याम्यामैन्द्रीं तथाऽन्या: सुरवरनगरीर्विद्ययाऽऽसाद्य सद्यो
मोघोद्योग: पतिष्यन् हुतभुजि भवता सस्मितं वारितोऽभूत् ॥६॥
mAnI sa tvAmapRShTvA dvijanilayagato bANajAlairmahAstraiH
rundhAnaH sUtigehaM punarapi sahasA dRShTanaShTe kumAre 
yAmyAmaindrIM tathAnyAH suravaranagarIrvidyayAsAdya sadyo
moghodyogaH patiShyan hutabhuji bhavatA sasmitaM vAritobhUt 6

मानी स त्वामपृष्ट्वा द्विजनिलयगतो बाणजालैर्महास्त्रैळ्
That proud one, without asking you, went to the Brahmin's house with his arrows and great astras!
रुन्धान: सूतिगेहं पुनरपि सहसा दृष्टनष्टे कुमारे ।
He stood guard at the birthing chambers, and once again upon seeing that this son too died,
याम्यामैन्द्रीं तथाऽन्या: सुरवरनगरीर्विद्ययाऽऽसाद्य सद्यो
he quickly went to the abode of Yama, Indra and other great suras through his yogic powers!
मोघोद्योग: पतिष्यन् हुतभुजि भवता सस्मितं वारितोऽभूत् ॥६॥
His efforts in wain, as he was about to jump into the fire, he was stopped by you!

सार्धं तेन प्रतीचीं दिशमतिजविना स्यन्दनेनाभियातो
लोकालोकं व्यतीतस्तिमिरभरमथो चक्रधाम्ना निरुन्धन् ।
चक्रांशुक्लिष्टदृष्टिं स्थितमथ विजयं पश्य पश्येति वारां
पारे त्वं प्राददर्श: किमपि हि तमसां दूरदूरं पदं ते ॥७॥
sArdhaM tena pratIchIM dishamatijavinA syandanenAbhiyAto
lokAlokaM vyatItastimirabharamatho chakradhAmnA nirundhan 
chakrAMshukliShTadRShTiM sthitamatha vijayaM pashya pashyeti vArAM
pAre tvaM prAdadarshaH kimapi hi tamasAM dUradUraM padaM te 7

सार्धं तेन प्रतीचीं दिशमतिजविना स्यन्दनेनाभियातो
Along with him, as you headed west on the extremely swift chariot,
लोकालोकं व्यतीतस्तिमिरभरमथो चक्रधाम्ना निरुन्धन् ।
you crossed the circle of mountains and seas destroying the darkness with the splendour of your chakra!
चक्रांशुक्लिष्टदृष्टिं स्थितमथ विजयं पश्य पश्येति वारां
Asking Arjuna, whose vision was being blinded by the lights of the chakra, to see beyond those waters,
पारे त्वं प्राददर्श: किमपि हि तमसां दूरदूरं पदं ते ॥७॥
you showed him some kind of an abode of yours which lay far far away beyond the region of Tamas!

तत्रासीनं भुजङ्गाधिपशयनतले दिव्यभूषायुधाद्यै:
आवीतं पीतचेलं प्रतिनवजलदश्यामलं श्रीमदङ्गम् ।
मूर्तीनामीशितारं परमिह तिसृणामेकमर्थं श्रुतीनां
त्वामेव त्वं परात्मन् प्रियसखसहितो नेमिथ क्षेमरूपम् ॥८॥
tatrAsInaM bhujangAdhipashayanatale divyabhUShAyudhAdyaiH
AvItaM pItachelaM pratinavajaladashyAmalaM shrImadangam 
mUrtInAmIshitAraM paramiha tisRNAmekamarthaM shrutInAM
tvAmeva tvaM parAtman priyasakhasahito nemitha kShemarUpam 8

तत्रासीनं भुजङ्गाधिपशयनतले दिव्यभूषायुधाद्यै:
Reclining there on the surface of the bed of the king of serpents, adorned in celestial ornaments and weapons,
आवीतं पीतचेलं प्रतिनवजलदश्यामलं श्रीमदङ्गम् ।
draped in yellow silken garments, resplendent in the hue of a fresh lotus, accompanied by Goddess Lakshmi,
मूर्तीनामीशितारं परमिह तिसृणामेकमर्थं श्रुतीनां
the supreme lord of the trinity, who is the sole essence of the Vedas,
त्वामेव त्वं परात्मन् प्रियसखसहितो नेमिथ क्षेमरूपम् ॥८॥
O Lord!, you, along with your dear friend, prostrated to you alone, who is the embodiment of well-being!

युवां मामेव द्वावधिकविवृतान्तर्हिततया
विभिन्नौ सन्द्रष्टुं स्वयमहमहार्षं द्विजसुतान् ।
नयेतं द्रागेतानिति खलु वितीर्णान् पुनरमून्
द्विजायादायादा: प्रणुतमहिमा पाण्डुजनुषा ॥९॥
yuvAM mAmeva dvAvadhikavivRtAntarhitatayA
vibhinnau sandraShTuM svayamahamahArShaM dvijasutAn 
nayetaM drAgetAniti khalu vitIrNAn punaramUn
dvijAyAdAyAdAH praNutamahimA pANDujanuShA 9

युवां मामेव द्वावधिकविवृतान्तर्हिततया
"The both of you are in fact aspects of I alone, it is only by the nature of realizations that
विभिन्नौ सन्द्रष्टुं स्वयमहमहार्षं द्विजसुतान् ।
you two are different. In order to see you both it was I who took away the Brahmin boys!
नयेतं द्रागेतानिति खलु वितीर्णान् पुनरमून्
Take them back now!" didn't you thus giving them back,
द्विजायादायादा: प्रणुतमहिमा पाण्डुजनुषा ॥९॥
then you yourself, bestowed them back to the Dvija, while Arjuna kept singing the praises of your glory!

एवं नानाविहारैर्जगदभिरमयन् वृष्णिवंशं प्रपुष्णन्
ईजानो यज्ञभेदैरतुलविहृतिभि: प्रीणयन्नेणनेत्रा: ।
भूभारक्षेपदम्भात् पदकमलजुषां मोक्षणायावतीर्ण:
पूर्णं ब्रह्मैव साक्षाद्यदुषु मनुजतारूषितस्त्वं व्यलासी: ॥१०॥
evaM nAnAvihArairjagadabhiramayan vRShNivaMshaM prapuShNan
IjAno yajnabhedairatulavihRtibhiH prINayanneNanetrAH 
bhUbhArakShepadambhAt padakamalajuShAM mokShaNAyAvatIrNaH
pUrNaM brahmaiva sAkShAdyaduShu manujatArUShitastvaM vyalAsIH 10

एवं नानाविहारैर्जगदभिरमयन् वृष्णिवंशं प्रपुष्णन्
Thus, with various kinds of plays, delighting the world, nourishing the lineage of Vrshnis,
ईजानो यज्ञभेदैरतुलविहृतिभि: प्रीणयन्नेणनेत्रा: ।
conducting various kinds of Yajnas, and unparalleled deeds, enchanting the doe eyed ones,
भूभारक्षेपदम्भात् पदकमलजुषां मोक्षणायावतीर्ण:
under the guise of reducing the weight on earth, in order to grant liberation to those devoted to your feet, you were incarnate!
पूर्णं ब्रह्मैव साक्षाद्यदुषु मनुजतारूषितस्त्वं व्यलासी: ॥१०॥
You are indeed the complete embodiment of the creator, resplendent in the form of a human, born into the Yadu clan!

प्रायेण द्वारवत्यामवृतदयि तदा नारदस्त्वद्रसार्द्र:
तस्माल्लेभे कदाचित्खलु सुकृतनिधिस्त्वत्पिता तत्त्वबोधम् ।
भक्तानामग्रयायी स च खलु मतिमानुद्धवस्त्वत्त एव
प्राप्तो विज्ञानसारं स किल जनहितायाधुनाऽऽस्ते बदर्याम् ॥११॥
prAyeNa dvAravatyAmavRtadayi tadA nAradastvadrasArdraH
tasmAllebhe kadAchitkhalu sukRtanidhistvatpitA tattvabodham 
bhaktAnAmagrayAyI sa cha khalu matimAnuddhavastvatta eva
prApto vijnAnasAraM sa kila janahitAyAdhunAste badaryAm 11

प्रायेण द्वारवत्यामवृतदयि तदा नारदस्त्वद्रसार्द्र:
O Lord! Narada used to mostly reside in Dwaraka those days, immersed in your essence!
तस्माल्लेभे कदाचित्खलु सुकृतनिधिस्त्वत्पिता तत्त्वबोधम् ।
From him, didn't once your virtuous father obtain the knowledge of true realization!
भक्तानामग्रयायी स च खलु मतिमानुद्धवस्त्वत्त एव
Also, didn't that foremost among devotees, Uddhava, from you yourself,
प्राप्तो विज्ञानसारं स किल जनहितायाधुनाऽऽस्ते बदर्याम् ॥११॥
obtain the essence of all knowledge, and doesn't he still reside until this day, in Badari!

सोऽयं कृष्णावतारो जयति तव विभो यत्र सौहार्दभीति-
स्नेहद्वेषानुरागप्रभृतिभिरतुलैरश्रमैर्योगभेदै: ।
आर्तिं तीर्त्वा समस्ताममृतपदमगुस्सर्वत: सर्वलोका:
स त्वं विश्वार्तिशान्त्यै पवनपुरपते भक्तिपूर्त्यै च भूया: ॥१२॥
soyaM kRShNAvatAro jayati tava vibho yatra sauhArdabhIti-
snehadveShAnurAgaprabhRtibhiratulairashramairyogabhedaiH 
ArtiM tIrtvA samastAmamRtapadamagussarvataH sarvalokAH
sa tvaM vishvArtishAntyai pavanapurapate bhaktipUrtyai cha bhUyAH 12

सोऽयं कृष्णावतारो जयति तव विभो यत्र सौहार्दभीति-
This incarnation of yours as Krishna, is ever victorious, O Vibhu, where through friendship, fright,
स्नेहद्वेषानुरागप्रभृतिभिरतुलैरश्रमैर्योगभेदै: ।
affection, aversion, love and other such unparalleled, effortless means of attainment,
आर्तिं तीर्त्वा समस्ताममृतपदमगुस्सर्वत: सर्वलोका:
you, removing all the sorrows of everyone of all worlds, bestowed upon them the immortal abode!
स त्वं विश्वार्तिशान्त्यै पवनपुरपते भक्तिपूर्त्यै च भूया: ॥१२॥
That you, O Lord of the City of Wind, for the pacification of everyone's sorrows, and to fill them with devotion, may you be!
