त्रिनवतितमम् दशकम्

The Ninety-third group of Ten shlokas

चतुर्विंशति: गुरव:

The Twenty Four Gurus

बन्धुस्नेहं विजह्यां तव हि करुणया त्वय्युपावेशितात्मा
सर्वं त्यक्त्वा चरेयं सकलमपि जगद्वीक्ष्य मायाविलासम् ।
नानात्वाद्भ्रान्तिजन्यात् सति खलु गुणदोषावबोधे विधिर्वा
व्यासेधो वा कथं तौ त्वयि निहितमतेर्वीतवैषम्यबुद्धे: ॥१॥
bandhusnehaM vijahyAM tava hi karuNayA tvayyupAveshitAtmA
sarvaM tyaktvA chareyaM sakalamapi jagadvIkShya mAyAvilAsam 
nAnAtvAdbhrAntijanyAt sati khalu guNadoShAvabodhe vidhirvA
vyAsedho vA kathaM tau tvayi nihitamatervItavaiShamyabuddheH 1

बन्धुस्नेहं विजह्यां तव हि करुणया त्वय्युपावेशितात्मा
I shall conquer affection towards my own people over others through your own compassion, and with my self immersed in you!
सर्वं त्यक्त्वा चरेयं सकलमपि जगद्वीक्ष्य मायाविलासम् ।
Abandoning everything, I shall wander about, seeing the world under the influence of your illusory play!
नानात्वाद्भ्रान्तिजन्यात् सति खलु गुणदोषावबोधे विधिर्वा
Isn't it only because of the idea of separateness born out of delusion, and the consequent understanding of Virtue and Vice that there are either prescriptions or
व्यासेधो वा कथं तौ त्वयि निहितमतेर्वीतवैषम्यबुद्धे: ॥१॥
prohibitions! How can they exist for someone who has their mind immersed in you, whose understanding has transcended duality! 

क्षुत्तृष्णालोपमात्रे सततकृतधियो जन्तव: सन्त्यनन्ता:
तेभ्यो विज्ञानवत्त्वात् पुरुष इह वरस्तज्जनिर्दुर्लभैव ।
तत्राप्यात्मात्मन: स्यात्सुहृदपि च रिपुर्यस्त्वयि न्यस्तचेता:
तापोच्छित्तेरुपायं स्मरति स हि सुहृत् स्वात्मवैरी ततोऽन्य: ॥२॥
kShuttRShNAlopamAtre satatakRtadhiyo jantavaH santyanantAH
tebhyo vijnAnavattvAt puruSha iha varastajjanirdurlabhaiva 
tatrApyAtmAtmanaH syAtsuhRdapi cha ripuryastvayi nyastachetAH
tApochChitterupAyaM smarati sa hi suhRt svAtmavairI tatonyaH 2

क्षुत्तृष्णालोपमात्रे सततकृतधियो जन्तव: सन्त्यनन्ता:
There are countless animals in the world whose minds are constantly in the pursuit of fulfilling the vacancies of Hunger and Thirst!
तेभ्यो विज्ञानवत्त्वात् पुरुष इह वरस्तज्जनिर्दुर्लभैव ।
Among them, the human birth that is hard to obtain is highest because of having a sense of discrimination.
तत्राप्यात्मात्मन: स्यात्सुहृदपि च रिपुर्यस्त्वयि न्यस्तचेता:
And even in that, between two different senses of self, there may be feelings of friendship and enemity! But the one whose mind is firmly placed in you,
तापोच्छित्तेरुपायं स्मरति स हि सुहृत् स्वात्मवैरी ततोऽन्य: ॥२॥
thinks of ways of alleviating suffering, and he is the friend to his own self, and the other is his own enemy!

त्वत्कारुण्ये प्रवृत्ते क इव नहि गुरुर्लोकवृत्तेऽपि भूमन्
सर्वाक्रान्तापि भूमिर्नहि चलति ततस्सत्क्षमां शिक्षयेयम् ।
गृह्णीयामीश तत्तद्विषयपरिचयेऽप्यप्रसक्तिं समीरात्
व्याप्तत्वञ्चात्मनो मे गगनगुरुवशाद्भातु निर्लेपता च ॥३॥
tvatkAruNye pravRtte ka iva nahi gururlokavRttepi bhUman
sarvAkrAntApi bhUmirnahi chalati tatassatkShamAM shikShayeyam 
gRhNIyAmIsha tattadviShayaparichayepyaprasaktiM samIrAt
vyAptatvanchAtmano me gaganaguruvashAdbhAtu nirlepatA cha 3

त्वत्कारुण्ये प्रवृत्ते क इव नहि गुरुर्लोकवृत्तेऽपि भूमन्
If your compassion acts forth, who in this world does not become a Guru, O Abundant One!
सर्वाक्रान्तापि भूमिर्नहि चलति ततस्सत्क्षमां शिक्षयेयम् ।
Despite being stepped on from all directions, the earth doesn't waver, from that, may I learn endurance;
गृह्णीयामीश तत्तद्विषयपरिचयेऽप्यप्रसक्तिं समीरात्
Despite being in contact with various smells all the time, air remains untainted, from that may I learn detachment;
व्याप्तत्वञ्चात्मनो मे गगनगुरुवशाद्भातु निर्लेपता च ॥३
The all-pervasive nature of my self, and its unaffectedness, may I learn from the Guru which is the sky!

स्वच्छ: स्यां पावनोऽहं मधुर उदकवद्वह्निवन्मा स्म गृह्णां
सर्वान्नीनोऽपि दोषं तरुषु तमिव मां सर्वभूतेष्ववेयाम् ।
पुष्टिर्नष्टि: कलानां शशिन इव तनोर्नात्मनोऽस्तीति विद्यां
तोयादिव्यस्तमार्ताण्डवदपि च तनुष्वेकतां त्वत्प्रसादात् ॥४॥
svachChaH syAM pAvanohaM madhura udakavadvahnivanmA sma gRhNAM
sarvAnnInopi doShaM taruShu tamiva mAM sarvabhUteShvaveyAm 
puShTirnaShTiH kalAnAM shashina iva tanornAtmanostIti vidyAM
toyAdivyastamArtANDavadapi cha tanuShvekatAM tvatprasAdAt 4

स्वच्छ: स्यां पावनोऽहं मधुर उदकवद्वह्निवन्मा स्म गृह्णां
May I be pure and sweet like water! Like fire, may I be unaffected 
सर्वान्नीनोऽपि दोषं तरुषु तमिव मां सर्वभूतेष्ववेयाम् ।
despite taking all sorts of inputs! And as it is inherent in all the trees, may I realize me in all creatures!
पुष्टिर्नष्टि: कलानां शशिन इव तनोर्नात्मनोऽस्तीति विद्यां
'Like the waxing and waning phases of the moon, there is growth and decay for the body, but not for the soul!' May I realize this,
तोयादिव्यस्तमार्ताण्डवदपि च तनुष्वेकतां त्वत्प्रसादात् ॥४॥
and also that 'Despite the Sun being reflected as many in water bodies and others, it is only One, very like the self that dwells as many in different bodies, but is One!' May I realize all this through your grace!

स्नेहाद्व्याधात्तपुत्रव्यसनमृतकपोतायितो मा स्म भूवं
प्राप्तं प्राश्नन् सहेय क्षुधमपि शयुवत् सिन्धुवत्स्यामगाध: ।
मा पप्तं योषिदादौ शिखिनि शलभवत् भृङ्गवत्सारभागी
भूयासं किन्तु तद्वद्धनचयनवशान्माहमीश प्रणेशम् ॥५॥
snehAdvyAdhAttaputravyasanamRtakapotAyito mA sma bhUvaM
prAptaM prAshnan saheya kShudhamapi shayuvat sindhuvatsyAmagAdhaH 
mA paptaM yoShidAdau shikhini shalabhavat bhRngavatsArabhAgI
bhUyAsaM kintu tadvaddhanachayanavashAnmAhamIsha praNesham 5

स्नेहाद्व्याधात्तपुत्रव्यसनमृतकपोतायितो मा स्म भूवं
May I not be like the dove, who, for the love of its children, was killed by the hunter, along with them.
प्राप्तं प्राश्नन् सहेय क्षुधमपि शयुवत् सिन्धुवत्स्यामगाध: ।
May I eat whatever has been obtained, but also be able to tolerate hunger, like a python! May I be profound like the ocean!
मा पप्तं योषिदादौ शिखिनि शलभवत् भृङ्गवत्सारभागी
May I not fall prey to lust towards women and others, like a moth to fire! Like a honey bee that grasps the essence of something,
भूयासं किन्तु तद्वद्धनचयनवशान्माहमीश प्रणेशम् ॥५॥
may I be O Lord, but like it, may I not be lost in the pursuit of accumulating it endlessly!

मा बद्ध्यासं तरुण्या गज इव वशया नार्जयेयं धनौघं
हर्तान्यस्तं हि माध्वीहर इव मृगवन्मा मुहं ग्राम्यगीतै: ।
नात्यासज्जेय भोज्ये झष इव बलिशे पिङ्गलावन्निराश:
सुप्यां भर्तव्ययोगात् कुरर इव विभो सामिषोऽन्यैर्न हन्यै ॥६॥
mA baddhyAsaM taruNyA gaja iva vashayA nArjayeyaM dhanaughaM
hartAnyastaM hi mAdhvIhara iva mRgavanmA muhaM grAmyagItaiH 
nAtyAsajjeya bhojye jhaSha iva balishe pingalAvannirAshaH
supyAM bhartavyayogAt kurara iva vibho sAmiShonyairna hanyai 6

मा बद्ध्यासं तरुण्या गज इव वशया नार्जयेयं धनौघं
May I not be bound by a woman like a female elephant in captivity! May I not accumulate heaps of wealth,
हर्तान्यस्तं हि माध्वीहर इव मृगवन्मा मुहं ग्राम्यगीतै: ।
which will eventually be taken away by someone else, like honeybees accumulating honey! May I not be deluded by pleasing folk tunes like a deer!
नात्यासज्जेय भोज्ये झष इव बलिशे पिङ्गलावन्निराश:
May I not be too attached to food, like a fish that gets hooked to its bait! Like a tawny owl, without a care,
सुप्यां भर्तव्ययोगात् कुरर इव विभो सामिषोऽन्यैर्न हन्यै ॥६॥
may I sleep! O Vibhu! May I not fall to death like an eagle immersed in carrying meat for its nourishment!

वर्तेय त्यक्तमान: सुखमतिशिशुवन्निस्सहायश्चरेयं
कन्याया एकशेषो वलय इव विभो वर्जितान्योन्यघोष: ।
त्वच्चित्तो नावबुध्यै परमिषुकृदिव क्ष्माभृदायानघोषं
गेहेष्वन्यप्रणीतेष्वहिरिव निवसान्युन्दुरोर्मन्दिरेषु ॥७॥
varteya tyaktamAnaH sukhamatishishuvannissahAyashchareyaM
kanyAyA ekasheSho valaya iva vibho varjitAnyonyaghoShaH 
tvachchitto nAvabudhyai paramiShukRdiva kShmAbhRdAyAnaghoShaM
geheShvanyapraNIteShvahiriva nivasAnyundurormandireShu 7

वर्तेय त्यक्तमान: सुखमतिशिशुवन्निस्सहायश्चरेयं
Abandoning the sense of pride and shame, I shall wander about in joy like a hapless child!
कन्याया एकशेषो वलय इव विभो वर्जितान्योन्यघोष: ।
Like a single bangle on a maiden's wrist, O Vibhu, devoid of the ruckus by hitting against other bangles!
त्वच्चित्तो नावबुध्यै परमिषुकृदिव क्ष्माभृदायानघोषं
May my mind not be immersed in anything else but you, like an arrow-maker doesn't even hear the announcement of the king's arrival!
गेहेष्वन्यप्रणीतेष्वहिरिव निवसान्युन्दुरोर्मन्दिरेषु ॥७॥
May I live in the houses built by others, but not be attached to any, like a snake that dwells in the burrows of rats!

त्वय्येव त्वत्कृतं त्वं क्षपयसि जगदित्यूर्णनाभात् प्रतीयां
त्वच्चिन्ता त्वत्स्वरूपं कुरुत इति दृढं शिक्षये पेशकारात् ।
विड्भस्मात्मा च देहो भवति गुरुवरो यो विवेकं विरक्तिं
धत्ते सञ्चिन्त्यमानो मम तु बहुरुजापीडितोऽयं विशेषात् ॥८॥
tvayyeva tvatkRtaM tvaM kShapayasi jagadityUrNanAbhAt pratIyAM
tvachchintA tvatsvarUpaM kuruta iti dRDhaM shikShaye peshakArAt 
viDbhasmAtmA cha deho bhavati guruvaro yo vivekaM viraktiM
dhatte sanchintyamAno mama tu bahurujApIDitoyaM visheShAt 8

त्वय्येव त्वत्कृतं त्वं क्षपयसि जगदित्यूर्णनाभात् प्रतीयां
You dissolve this creation, created by you into your own self! May I realize this by looking at the spider!
त्वच्चिन्ता त्वत्स्वरूपं कुरुत इति दृढं शिक्षये पेशकारात् ।
Thoughts of you grant me your form, may I learn this lesson firmly from the beetle!
विड्भस्मात्मा च देहो भवति गुरुवरो यो विवेकं विरक्तिं
Even this body of the nature of filth and ash becomes a great Guru, when knowledge and detachment
धत्ते सञ्चिन्त्यमानो मम तु बहुरुजापीडितोऽयं विशेषात् ॥८॥
are bestowed by it upon me, when it falls to various kinds of diseases and discomfort!

ही ही मे देहमोहं त्यज पवनपुराधीश यत्प्रेमहेतो:
गेहे वित्ते कलत्रादिषु च विवशितास्त्वत्पदं विस्मरन्ति ।
सोऽयं वह्नेश्शुनो वा परमिह परत: साम्प्रतञ्चाक्षिकर्ण-
त्वग्जिह्वाद्या विकर्षन्त्यवशमत इत: कोऽपि न त्वत्पदाब्जे ॥९॥
hI hI me dehamohaM tyaja pavanapurAdhIsha yatpremahetoH
gehe vitte kalatrAdiShu cha vivashitAstvatpadaM vismaranti 
soyaM vahneshshuno vA paramiha parataH sAmpratanchAkShikarNa-
tvagjihvAdyA vikarShantyavashamata itaH kopi na tvatpadAbje 9

ही ही मे देहमोहं त्यज पवनपुराधीश यत्प्रेमहेतो:
Alas! Rid me of my infatuation with this body, O Lord of the City of Wind! Due to this love for the body,
गेहे वित्ते कलत्रादिषु च विवशितास्त्वत्पदं विस्मरन्ति ।
becoming invested in a house, in wealth and the spouse, people forget your abode!
सोऽयं वह्नेश्शुनो वा परमिह परत: साम्प्रतञ्चाक्षिकर्ण-
That body, finally, O Supreme one, is only for the fire, or to the dogs! And even now, eyes, ears,
त्वग्जिह्वाद्या विकर्षन्त्यवशमत इत: कोऽपि न त्वत्पदाब्जे ॥९॥
tongue, and other organs drag me helplessly all over the place, but none of them take me towards your lotus feet!

दुर्वारो देहमोहो यदि पुनरधुना तर्हि निश्शेषरोगान्
हृत्वा भक्तिं द्रढिष्ठां कुरु तव पदपङ्केरुहे पङ्कजाक्ष ।
नूनं नानाभवान्ते समधिगतममुं मुक्तिदं विप्रदेहं
क्षुद्रे हा हन्त मा मा क्षिप विषयरसे पाहि मां मारुतेश ॥१०॥
durvAro dehamoho yadi punaradhunA tarhi nishsheSharogAn
hRtvA bhaktiM draDhiShThAM kuru tava padapankeruhe pankajAkSha 
nUnaM nAnAbhavAnte samadhigatamamuM muktidaM vipradehaM
kShudre hA hanta mA mA kShipa viShayarase pAhi mAM mArutesha 10

दुर्वारो देहमोहो यदि पुनरधुना तर्हि निश्शेषरोगान्
Even if infatuation towards the body is hard to set aside, if you now remove all my diseases,
हृत्वा भक्तिं द्रढिष्ठां कुरु तव पदपङ्केरुहे पङ्कजाक्ष ।
and establish firm devotion in me, towards your lotus feet, O Lotus-eyed One,
नूनं नानाभवान्ते समधिगतममुं मुक्तिदं विप्रदेहं
this body of a Brahmin which has definitely been attained after multiple lifetimes, which is capable of granting mukti,
क्षुद्रे हा हन्त मा मा क्षिप विषयरसे पाहि मां मारुतेश ॥१०॥
O Alas, Don't again throw it into worldly sensory pleasures! Protect me, O Lord of Wind!
