ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

दशमम् दशकम्
dashamam dashakam

The Tenth group of Ten shlokas

सृष्टि भेदाः
sRShTi bhedAH

Various Forms of Creation

वैकुण्ठ वर्धितबलोऽथ भवत्प्रसादात्
अम्भोजयोनिरसृजत् किल जीवदेहान् ।
स्थास्नूनि भूरुहमयानि तथा तिरश्चां
जातीर्मनुष्यनिवहानपि देवभेदान् ॥१॥
vaikuNTha vardhitabalotha bhavatprasAdAt
ambhojayonirasRjat kila jIvadehAn 
sthAsnUni bhUruhamayAni tathA tirashchAM
jAtIrmanuShyanivahAnapi devabhedAn 1

वैकुण्ठ वर्धितबलोऽथ भवत्प्रसादात्
O Lord of Vaikuntha! With his strength enhanced by your grace,
अम्भोजयोनिरसृजत् किल जीवदेहान् ।
the lotus-born, hasn't he then created the bodies of all living beings!
स्थास्नूनि भूरुहमयानि तथा तिरश्चां
He created immobile entities like trees, earth-dwelling creatures, and horizontal creatures like animals and birds!
जातीर्मनुष्यनिवहानपि देवभेदान् ॥१॥
He also created humankind and various groups of devas!

मिथ्याग्रहास्मिमतिरागविकोपभीतिः
अज्ञानवृत्तिमिति पञ्चविधां स सृष्ट्वा ।
उद्दामतामसपदार्थविधानदूनः
तेने त्वदीयचरणस्मरणं विशुद्ध्यै ॥२॥
mithyAgrahAsmimatirAgavikopabhItiH
ajnAnavRttimiti panchavidhAM sa sRShTvA 
uddAmatAmasapadArthavidhAnadUnaH
tene tvadIyacharaNasmaraNaM vishuddhyai 2

मिथ्याग्रहास्मिमतिरागविकोपभीतिः
Confusion, egoism, attachment, anger, and fear,
अज्ञानवृत्तिमिति पञ्चविधां स सृष्ट्वा ।
the activities born out of ignorance, these five, he created,
उद्दामतामसपदार्थविधानदूनः
and repenting for having created these extremely tAmasic entities,
तेने त्वदीयचरणस्मरणं विशुद्ध्यै ॥२॥
he resorted to the remembrance of Your feet for his own purification!

तावत् ससर्ज मनसा सनकं सनन्दं
भूय: सनातनमुनिं च सनत्कुमारम् ।
ते सृष्टिकर्मणि तु तेन नियुज्यमानाः
त्वत्पादभक्तिरसिका जगृहुर्न वाणीम् ॥३॥
tAvat sasarja manasA sanakaM sanandaM
bhUyaH sanAtanamuniM cha sanatkumAram 
te sRShTikarmaNi tu tena niyujyamAnAH
tvatpAdabhaktirasikA jagRhurna vANIm 3

तावत् ससर्ज मनसा सनकं सनन्दं
And then he created out of his mind sages sanaka and sananda,
भूय: सनातनमुनिं च सनत्कुमारम् ।
and then sages sanAtana and sanatkumAra!
ते सृष्टिकर्मणि तु तेन नियुज्यमानाः
These four when employed in the activity of procreation by him,
त्वत्पादभक्तिरसिका जगृहुर्न वाणीम् ॥३॥
being only interested in devotion to your feet, they did not heed his words!

तावत् प्रकोपमुदितं प्रतिरुन्धतोऽस्य
भ्रूमध्यतोऽजनि मृडो भवदेकदेश: ।
नामानि मे कुरु पदानि च हा विरिञ्चे-
त्यादौ रुरोद किल तेन स रुद्रनामा ॥४॥
tAvat prakopamuditaM pratirundhatosya
bhrUmadhyatojani mRDo bhavadekadeshaH 
nAmAni me kuru padAni cha hA virinche-
tyAdau ruroda kila tena sa rudranAmA 4

तावत् प्रकोपमुदितं प्रतिरुन्धतोऽस्य
Then as anger arose within him and as he tried to suppress it,
भ्रूमध्यतोऽजनि मृडो भवदेकदेश: ।
from the middle of his eyebrows mRDa was born, who is yet another aspect of You!
नामानि मे कुरु पदानि च हा विरिञ्चे-
"Provide me with Names and Abodes, O Creator!"
त्यादौ रुरोद किल तेन स रुद्रनामा ॥४॥
Thus, he was the first to roar, and from there came the names of Rudra!

एकादशाह्वयतया च विभिन्नरूपं
रुद्रं विधाय दयिता वनिताश्च दत्वा ।
तावन्त्यदत्त च पदानि भवत्प्रणुन्न:
प्राह प्रजाविरचनाय च सादरं तम् ॥५॥
ekAdashAhvayatayA cha vibhinnarUpaM
rudraM vidhAya dayitA vanitAshcha datvA 
tAvantyadatta cha padAni bhavatpraNunnaH
prAha prajAvirachanAya cha sAdaraM tam 5

एकादशाह्वयतया च विभिन्नरूपं
Creating eleven different names and various kinds of forms,
रुद्रं विधाय दयिता वनिताश्च दत्वा ।
and bestowing these to rudra along with their beloved feminine counterparts,
तावन्त्यदत्त च पदानि भवत्प्रणुन्न:
then giving them their respective abodes, impelled by You,
प्राह प्रजाविरचनाय च सादरं तम् ॥५॥
brahmA requested him respectfully to begin the process of procreation!

रुद्राभिसृष्टभयदाकृतिरुद्रसंघ-
सम्पूर्यमाणभुवनत्रयभीतचेता: ।
मा मा प्रजा: सृज तपश्चर मङ्गलाये-
त्याचष्ट तं कमलभूर्भवदीरितात्मा ॥६॥
rudrAbhisRShTabhayadAkRtirudrasaMgha-
sampUryamANabhuvanatrayabhItachetAH 
mA mA prajAH sRja tapashchara mangalAye-
tyAchaShTa taM kamalabhUrbhavadIritAtmA 6

रुद्राभिसृष्टभयदाकृतिरुद्रसंघ-
rudra's creation of fierce-looking groups of more rudras in his own image
सम्पूर्यमाणभुवनत्रयभीतचेता: ।
started filling up the three worlds and started causing terror!
मा मा प्रजा: सृज तपश्चर मङ्गलाये-
"No! No! Do not procreate anymore! Perform penance for welfare"
त्याचष्ट तं कमलभूर्भवदीरितात्मा ॥६॥
Thus spoke to him the lotus-born, once again being prompted by You from within!

तस्याथ सर्गरसिकस्य मरीचिरत्रिः
तत्राङिगरा: क्रतुमुनि: पुलह: पुलस्त्य: ।
अङ्गादजायत भृगुश्च वसिष्ठदक्षौ
श्रीनारदश्च भगवन् भवदंघ्रिदास: ॥७॥
tasyAtha sargarasikasya marIchiratriH
tatrAnigarAH kratumuniH pulahaH pulastyaH 
angAdajAyata bhRgushcha vasiShThadakShau
shrInAradashcha bhagavan bhavadaMghridAsaH 7

तस्याथ सर्गरसिकस्य मरीचिरत्रिः
And then, of him who was intent on the process of creation, marIchi, atri,
तत्राङिगरा: क्रतुमुनि: पुलह: पुलस्त्य: ।
angiras, kratu, pulaha, and pulastya
अङ्गादजायत भृगुश्च वसिष्ठदक्षौ
were born from his own body, along with bhRgu, vasiShTha, dakSha,
श्रीनारदश्च भगवन् भवदंघ्रिदास: ॥७॥
and the auspicious nArada, O Lord!, the devotee of your feet!

धर्मादिकानभिसृजन्नथ कर्दमं च
वाणीं विधाय विधिरङ्गजसंकुलोऽभूत् ।
त्वद्बोधितैस्सनकदक्षमुखैस्तनूजैः
उद्बोधितश्च विरराम तमो विमुञ्चन् ॥८॥
dharmAdikAnabhisRjannatha kardamaM cha
vANIM vidhAya vidhirangajasaMkulobhUt 
tvadbodhitaissanakadakShamukhaistanUjaiH
udbodhitashcha virarAma tamo vimunchan 8

धर्मादिकानभिसृजन्नथ कर्दमं च
And then, having created dharma-devas and others, kardama,
वाणीं विधाय विधिरङ्गजसंकुलोऽभूत् ।
and sarasvatI, the creator started getting enamoured with his own creation!
त्वद्बोधितैस्सनकदक्षमुखैस्तनूजैः
Prompted by You, by his own sons like sanaka, dakSha, and others 
उद्बोधितश्च विरराम तमो विमुञ्चन् ॥८॥
he was instructed once again, and then took a break, letting go of tamas!

वेदान् पुराणनिवहानपि सर्वविद्या:
कुर्वन् निजाननगणाच्चतुराननोऽसौ ।
पुत्रेषु तेषु विनिधाय स सर्गवृद्धिम्
अप्राप्नुवंस्तव पदाम्बुजमाश्रितोभूत् ॥९॥
vedAn purANanivahAnapi sarvavidyAH
kurvan nijAnanagaNAchchaturAnanosau 
putreShu teShu vinidhAya sa sargavRddhim
aprApnuvaMstava padAmbujamAshritobhUt 9

वेदान् पुराणनिवहानपि सर्वविद्या:
Vedas, the groups of Puranas, and all systems of knowledge,
कुर्वन् निजाननगणाच्चतुराननोऽसौ ।
he created from his own faces; and that four-faced one 
पुत्रेषु तेषु विनिधाय स सर्गवृद्धिम्
distributing them among those sons of his, the process of creation,
अप्राप्नुवंस्तव पदाम्बुजमाश्रितोभूत् ॥९॥
stopping himself from doing it further, he then took refuge in your lotus feet!

जानन्नुपायमथ देहमजो विभज्य
स्त्रीपुंसभावमभजन्मनुतद्वधूभ्याम् ।
ताभ्यां च मानुषकुलानि विवर्धयंस्त्वं
गोविन्द मारुतपुराधिप रुन्धि रोगान् ॥१०॥
jAnannupAyamatha dehamajo vibhajya
strIpuMsabhAvamabhajanmanutadvadhUbhyAm 
tAbhyAM cha mAnuShakulAni vivardhayaMstvaM
govinda mArutapurAdhipa rundhi rogAn 10

जानन्नुपायमथ देहमजो विभज्य
Realizing the technique, the birthless one then dividing his own body into two,
स्त्रीपुंसभावमभजन्मनुतद्वधूभ्याम् ।
assumed the forms of female and male, as manu and his consort(shatarUpA)!
ताभ्यां च मानुषकुलानि विवर्धयंस्त्वं
Through both of them, You have started propagating the race of human beings!
गोविन्द मारुतपुराधिप रुन्धि रोगान् ॥१०॥
O Govinda! O Lord of the City of Wind! Eradicate my ailments!
