ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अष्टादशम् दशकम्
aShTAdasham dashakam

The Eighteenth group of Ten shlokas

पृथु चरितम्
pRthu charitam

The Story of Prithu

जातस्य ध्रुवकुल एव तुङ्गकीर्तेः
अङ्गस्य व्यजनि सुत: स वेननामा ।
यद्दोषव्यथितमति: स राजवर्यः
त्वत्पादे निहितमना वनं गतोऽभूत् ॥१॥
jAtasya dhruvakula eva tungakIrteH
angasya vyajani sutaH sa venanAmA 
yaddoShavyathitamatiH sa rAjavaryaH
tvatpAde nihitamanA vanaM gatobhUt 1

जातस्य ध्रुवकुल एव तुङ्गकीर्तेः
Born in the lineage of Dhruva, of great repute,
अङ्गस्य व्यजनि सुत: स वेननामा ।
Anga, gave birth to a son by the name of Vena.
यद्दोषव्यथितमति: स राजवर्यः
His mind troubled by his son's misdeeds, King Anga,
त्वत्पादे निहितमना वनं गतोऽभूत् ॥१॥
in your feet, he immersed his mind and left to the forest!

पापोऽपि क्षितितलपालनाय वेन:
पौराद्यैरुपनिहित: कठोरवीर्य: ।
सर्वेभ्यो निजबलमेव सम्प्रशंसन्
भूचक्रे तव यजनान्ययं न्यरौत्सीत् ॥२॥
pApopi kShititalapAlanAya venaH
paurAdyairupanihitaH kaThoravIryaH 
sarvebhyo nijabalameva samprashaMsan
bhUchakre tava yajanAnyayaM nyarautsIt 2

पापोऽपि क्षितितलपालनाय वेन:
Despite being evil, in order to rule the kingdom, Vena,
पौराद्यैरुपनिहित: कठोरवीर्य: ।
was appointed as the King, despite being of cruel power!
सर्वेभ्यो निजबलमेव सम्प्रशंसन्
To everyone, proclaiming and boasting about his own might,
भूचक्रे तव यजनान्ययं न्यरौत्सीत् ॥२॥
in the entire world, all your yajnas, he prohibited!

सम्प्राप्ते हितकथनाय तापसौघे
मत्तोऽन्यो भुवनपतिर्न कश्चनेति ।
त्वन्निन्दावचनपरो मुनीश्वरैस्तै:
शापाग्नौ शलभदशामनायि वेन: ॥३॥
samprApte hitakathanAya tApasaughe
mattonyo bhuvanapatirna kashchaneti 
tvannindAvachanaparo munIshvaraistaiH
shApAgnau shalabhadashAmanAyi venaH 3

सम्प्राप्ते हितकथनाय तापसौघे
When groups of sages approached him for wise counsel,
मत्तोऽन्यो भुवनपतिर्न कश्चनेति ।
"Apart from me, there is no other ruler of this world!" thus
त्वन्निन्दावचनपरो मुनीश्वरैस्तै:
using words of insult aimed at you, by the sages'
शापाग्नौ शलभदशामनायि वेन: ॥३॥
fiery curses, Vena was turned into a mere grasshopper!

तन्नाशात् खलजनभीरुकैर्मुनीन्द्रैः
तन्मात्रा चिरपरिरक्षिते तदङ्गे ।
त्यक्ताघे परिमथितादथोरुदण्डाद्
दोर्दण्डे परिमथिते त्वमाविरासी: ॥४॥
tannAshAt khalajanabhIrukairmunIndraiH
tanmAtrA chiraparirakShite tadange 
tyaktAghe parimathitAdathorudaNDAd
dordaNDe parimathite tvamAvirAsIH 4

तन्नाशात् खलजनभीरुकैर्मुनीन्द्रैः
With his downfall, as the sages were afraid of an evil uprising, 
तन्मात्रा चिरपरिरक्षिते तदङ्गे ।
the body which was safeguarded by his mother,
त्यक्ताघे परिमथितादथोरुदण्डाद्
which was devoid of its sins now as it kept rubbing its thighs together,
दोर्दण्डे परिमथिते त्वमाविरासी: ॥४॥
when it rubbed its hands as if in prayer, You manifested yourself!

विख्यात: पृथुरिति तापसोपदिष्टै:
सूताद्यै: परिणुतभाविभूरिवीर्य: ।
वेनार्त्या कबलितसम्पदं धरित्रीम्
आक्रान्तां निजधनुषा समामकार्षी: ॥५॥
vikhyAtaH pRthuriti tApasopadiShTaiH
sUtAdyaiH pariNutabhAvibhUrivIryaH 
venArtyA kabalitasampadaM dharitrIm
AkrAntAM nijadhanuShA samAmakArShIH 5

विख्यात: पृथुरिति तापसोपदिष्टै:
Renowned by the name of Prithu, as advised by the sages,
सूताद्यै: परिणुतभाविभूरिवीर्य: ।
as the minstrels and others praised your mighty potential,
वेनार्त्या कबलितसम्पदं धरित्रीम्
earth, who was stripped of all her riches due to Vena's torture,
आक्रान्तां निजधनुषा समामकार्षी: ॥५॥
you struck her with your bow, and enriched her once more!

भूयस्तां निजकुलमुख्यवत्सयुक्त्यैः
देवाद्यै: समुचितचारुभाजनेषु ।
अन्नादीन्यभिलषितानि यानि तानि
स्वच्छन्दं सुरभितनूमदूदुहस्त्वम् ॥६॥
bhUyastAM nijakulamukhyavatsayuktyaiH
devAdyaiH samuchitachArubhAjaneShu 
annAdInyabhilaShitAni yAni tAni
svachChandaM surabhitanUmadUduhastvam 6

भूयस्तां निजकुलमुख्यवत्सयुक्त्यैः
Once again, by making the leaders of all the groups into calves,
देवाद्यै: समुचितचारुभाजनेषु ।
like devas and others, in appropriate beautiful vessels, 
अन्नादीन्यभिलषितानि यानि तानि
food and other objects, whichever were desired,
स्वच्छन्दं सुरभितनूमदूदुहस्त्वम् ॥६॥
you yourself, making the earth into a celestial cow, started milking it!

आत्मानं यजति मखैस्त्वयि त्रिधामन्
आरब्धे शततमवाजिमेधयागे ।
स्पर्धालु: शतमख एत्य नीचवेषो
हृत्वाऽश्वं तव तनयात् पराजितोऽभूत् ॥७॥
AtmAnaM yajati makhaistvayi tridhAman
Arabdhe shatatamavAjimedhayAge 
spardhAluH shatamakha etya nIchaveSho
hRtvAshvaM tava tanayAt parAjitobhUt 7

आत्मानं यजति मखैस्त्वयि त्रिधामन्
As Prithu, as you were performing Yajnas for your own self, O Lord of the Three Worlds,
आरब्धे शततमवाजिमेधयागे ।
when you started the hundredth Ashvamedha Yajna,
स्पर्धालु: शतमख एत्य नीचवेषो
to counter you, Indra, the one of a hundred yajnas, came in the form of a barbarian,
हृत्वाऽश्वं तव तनयात् पराजितोऽभूत् ॥७॥
and stole your horse, but was defeated by your son!

देवेन्द्रं मुहुरिति वाजिनं हरन्तं
वह्नौ तं मुनिवरमण्डले जुहूषौ ।
रुन्धाने कमलभवे क्रतो: समाप्तौ
साक्षात्त्वं मधुरिपुमैक्षथा: स्वयं स्वम् ॥८॥
devendraM muhuriti vAjinaM harantaM
vahnau taM munivaramaNDale juhUShau 
rundhAne kamalabhave kratoH samAptau
sAkShAttvaM madhuripumaikShathAH svayaM svam 8

देवेन्द्रं मुहुरिति वाजिनं हरन्तं
As Devendra was once again stealing the horse,
वह्नौ तं मुनिवरमण्डले जुहूषौ ।
as you caught him and were about to throw him into the fire of Yajna in the middle of the sages,
रुन्धाने कमलभवे क्रतो: समाप्तौ
as Brahma stopped you, and as the yajna was completed successfully,
साक्षात्त्वं मधुरिपुमैक्षथा: स्वयं स्वम् ॥८॥
you, as King Prithu witnessed yourself as the Vanquisher of Madhu!

तद्दत्तं वरमुपलभ्य भक्तिमेकां
गङ्गान्ते विहितपद: कदापि देव ।
सत्रस्थं मुनिनिवहं हितानि शंसन्
ऐक्षिष्ठा: सनकमुखान् मुनीन् पुरस्तात् ॥९॥
taddattaM varamupalabhya bhaktimekAM
gangAnte vihitapadaH kadApi deva 
satrasthaM muninivahaM hitAni shaMsan
aikShiShThAH sanakamukhAn munIn purastAt 9

तद्दत्तं वरमुपलभ्य भक्तिमेकां
Having obtained the boons of the yajna with firm devotion,
गङ्गान्ते विहितपद: कदापि देव ।
establishing a residence at the banks of Ganga, O Lord,
सत्रस्थं मुनिनिवहं हितानि शंसन्
to the groups of sages performing yagnas there, preaching them wellbeing,
ऐक्षिष्ठा: सनकमुखान् मुनीन् पुरस्तात् ॥९॥
you saw sages Sanaka and others in front!

विज्ञानं सनकमुखोदितं दधान:
स्वात्मानं स्वयमगमो वनान्तसेवी ।
तत्तादृक्पृथुवपुरीश सत्वरं मे
रोगौघं प्रशमय वातगेहवासिन् ॥१०॥
vijnAnaM sanakamukhoditaM dadhAnaH
svAtmAnaM svayamagamo vanAntasevI 
tattAdRkpRthuvapurIsha satvaraM me
rogaughaM prashamaya vAtagehavAsin 10

विज्ञानं सनकमुखोदितं दधान:
Receiving exalted knowledge from Sage Sanaka himself,
स्वात्मानं स्वयमगमो वनान्तसेवी ।
dwelling within the forest, you realized the knowledge of the self!
तत्तादृक्पृथुवपुरीश सत्वरं मे
O Lord who was thus incarnate in the form of Prithu, quickly for me
रोगौघं प्रशमय वातगेहवासिन् ॥१०॥
my heaps of ailments, pacify them, O One who Resides in the Abode of Wind!
