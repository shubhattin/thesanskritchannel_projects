ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

त्रिंशम् दशकम्
triMsham dashakam

The Thirtieth group of Ten shlokas

वामन अवतार:
vAmana avatAraH

Incarnation as a Dwarf

शक्रेण संयति हतोऽपि बलिर्महात्मा
शुक्रेण जीविततनु: क्रतुवर्धितोष्मा ।
विक्रान्तिमान् भयनिलीनसुरां त्रिलोकीं
चक्रे वशे स तव चक्रमुखादभीत: ॥१॥
shakreNa saMyati hatopi balirmahAtmA
shukreNa jIvitatanuH kratuvardhitoShmA 
vikrAntimAn bhayanilInasurAM trilokIM
chakre vashe sa tava chakramukhAdabhItaH 1

शक्रेण संयति हतोऽपि बलिर्महात्मा
Despite being killed by Indra in battle, Bali, the great being,
शुक्रेण जीविततनु: क्रतुवर्धितोष्मा ।
was revived once again by Shukracharya, and gathered more strength by performing rituals!
विक्रान्तिमान् भयनिलीनसुरां त्रिलोकीं
With great valour, the three worlds with all the suras hiding in fear,
चक्रे वशे स तव चक्रमुखादभीत: ॥१॥
he got it under his control, feeling no fear of repercussion from your chakra!

पुत्रार्तिदर्शनवशाददितिर्विषण्णा
तं काश्यपं निजपतिं शरणं प्रपन्ना ।
त्वत्पूजनं तदुदितं हि पयोव्रताख्यं
सा द्वादशाहमचरत्त्वयि भक्तिपूर्णा ॥२॥
putrArtidarshanavashAdaditirviShaNNA
taM kAshyapaM nijapatiM sharaNaM prapannA 
tvatpUjanaM taduditaM hi payovratAkhyaM
sA dvAdashAhamacharattvayi bhaktipUrNA 2

पुत्रार्तिदर्शनवशाददितिर्विषण्णा
Aditi, crestfallen looking at the suffering of her children, the Devas,
तं काश्यपं निजपतिं शरणं प्रपन्ना ।
sought out her husband, Kashyapa for refuge!
त्वत्पूजनं तदुदितं हि पयोव्रताख्यं
As he spoke to her of a process of your worship, named Payovrata,
सा द्वादशाहमचरत्त्वयि भक्तिपूर्णा ॥२॥
she performed it for twelve days, filled with devotion towards you!

तस्यावधौ त्वयि निलीनमतेरमुष्या:
श्यामश्चतुर्भुजवपु: स्वयमाविरासी: ।
नम्रां च तामिह भवत्तनयो भवेयं
गोप्यं मदीक्षणमिति प्रलपन्नयासी: ॥३॥
tasyAvadhau tvayi nilInamateramuShyAH
shyAmashchaturbhujavapuH svayamAvirAsIH 
namrAM cha tAmiha bhavattanayo bhaveyaM
gopyaM madIkShaNamiti pralapannayAsIH 3

तस्यावधौ त्वयि निलीनमतेरमुष्या:
At the end of that process, in front of her, whose mind was immersed in you,
श्यामश्चतुर्भुजवपु: स्वयमाविरासी: ।
you manifest yourself, in your dark-complexioned, four-armed form!
नम्रां च तामिह भवत्तनयो भवेयं
As she bowed down, to her, "I shall be born as your son!
गोप्यं मदीक्षणमिति प्रलपन्नयासी: ॥३॥
Keeps my words a secret!", speaking thus, you disappeared.

त्वं काश्यपे तपसि सन्निदधत्तदानीं
प्राप्तोऽसि गर्भमदिते: प्रणुतो विधात्रा ।
प्रासूत च प्रकटवैष्णवदिव्यरूपं
सा द्वादशीश्रवणपुण्यदिने भवन्तं ॥४॥
tvaM kAshyape tapasi sannidadhattadAnIM
prAptosi garbhamaditeH praNuto vidhAtrA 
prAsUta cha prakaTavaiShNavadivyarUpaM
sA dvAdashIshravaNapuNyadine bhavantaM 4

त्वं काश्यपे तपसि सन्निदधत्तदानीं
And then, you entered Sage Kashyapa's seed
प्राप्तोऽसि गर्भमदिते: प्रणुतो विधात्रा ।
and reached the womb of Aditi, as Brahma praised your glory!
प्रासूत च प्रकटवैष्णवदिव्यरूपं
She then delivered a divine form, evident with the marks of Vishnu,
सा द्वादशीश्रवणपुण्यदिने भवन्तं ॥४॥
she delivered you, on the auspicious day of Shravana Dvadashi.

पुण्याश्रमं तमभिवर्षति पुष्पवर्षै:
हर्षाकुले सुरगणे कृततूर्यघोषे ।
बध्वाऽञ्जलिं जय जयेति नुत: पितृभ्यां
त्वं तत्क्षणे पटुतमं वटुरूपमाधा: ॥५॥
puNyAshramaM tamabhivarShati puShpavarShaiH
harShAkule suragaNe kRtatUryaghoShe 
badhvAnjaliM jaya jayeti nutaH pitRbhyAM
tvaM tatkShaNe paTutamaM vaTurUpamAdhAH 5

पुण्याश्रमं तमभिवर्षति पुष्पवर्षै:
As that holy ashram was showered with a torrent of flowers,
हर्षाकुले सुरगणे कृततूर्यघोषे ।
as the rejoiced groups of Suras sounded trumpets,
बध्वाऽञ्जलिं जय जयेति नुत: पितृभ्यां
as your parents hailed your victory with folded palms,
त्वं तत्क्षणे पटुतमं वटुरूपमाधा: ॥५॥
at that very moment, you took on the form of a highly accomplished child-brahmachAri!

तावत्प्रजापतिमुखैरुपनीय मौञ्जी-
दण्डाजिनाक्षवलयादिभिरर्च्यमान: ।
देदीप्यमानवपुरीश कृताग्निकार्य:
त्वं प्रास्थिथा बलिगृहं प्रकृताश्वमेधम् ॥६॥
tAvatprajApatimukhairupanIya maunjI-
daNDAjinAkShavalayAdibhirarchyamAnaH 
dedIpyamAnavapurIsha kRtAgnikAryaH
tvaM prAsthithA baligRhaM prakRtAshvamedham 6

तावत्प्रजापतिमुखैरुपनीय मौञ्जी-
And then, Prajapati and others offering you a sacred thread,
दण्डाजिनाक्षवलयादिभिरर्च्यमान: ।
staff, deer-skin mat, rosary beads and such, started worshipping you! 
देदीप्यमानवपुरीश कृताग्निकार्य:
With a glowing form, O Lord! performing the fire ritual,
त्वं प्रास्थिथा बलिगृहं प्रकृताश्वमेधम् ॥६॥
you left for Bali's house, where the Ashvamedha Yajna was being performed!

गात्रेण भाविमहिमोचितगौरवं प्राग्
व्यावृण्वतेव धरणीं चलयन्नयासी: ।
छत्रं परोष्मतिरणार्थमिवादधानो
दण्डं च दानवजनेष्विव सन्निधातुम् ॥७॥
gAtreNa bhAvimahimochitagauravaM prAg
vyAvRNvateva dharaNIM chalayannayAsIH 
ChatraM paroShmatiraNArthamivAdadhAno
daNDaM cha dAnavajaneShviva sannidhAtum 7

गात्रेण भाविमहिमोचितगौरवं प्राग्
With your body, glories that are going to happen yet,
व्यावृण्वतेव धरणीं चलयन्नयासी: ।
as if displaying them beforehand, you walked as the earth trembled,
छत्रं परोष्मतिरणार्थमिवादधानो
holding the umbrella as if to ward off the opponents' heat that you are about to face,
दण्डं च दानवजनेष्विव सन्निधातुम् ॥७॥
and a staff as if to use it against the groups of asuras!

तां नर्मदोत्तरतटे हयमेधशालाम्
आसेदुषि त्वयि रुचा तव रुद्धनेत्रै: ।
भास्वान् किमेष दहनो नु सनत्कुमारो
योगी नु कोऽयमिति शुक्रमुखैश्शशङ्के ॥८॥
tAM narmadottarataTe hayamedhashAlAm
AseduShi tvayi ruchA tava ruddhanetraiH 
bhAsvAn kimeSha dahano nu sanatkumAro
yogI nu koyamiti shukramukhaishshashanke 8

तां नर्मदोत्तरतटे हयमेधशालाम्
On the northern banks of river Narmada, the hall of Ashvamedha Yajna,
आसेदुषि त्वयि रुचा तव रुद्धनेत्रै: ।
as you approached it with your radiance, with their eyes dazzled,
भास्वान् किमेष दहनो नु सनत्कुमारो
"Is this the Sun himself! Or is this fire! May be it is sage Sanatkumara!
योगी नु कोऽयमिति शुक्रमुखैश्शशङ्के ॥८॥
"Who is this Yogi!", thus Shukracharya and others wondered!

आनीतमाशु भृगुभिर्महसाऽभिभूतै:
त्वां रम्यरूपमसुर: पुलकावृताङ्ग: ।
भक्त्या समेत्य सुकृती परिणिज्य पादौ
तत्तोयमन्वधृत मूर्धनि तीर्थतीर्थम् ॥९॥
AnItamAshu bhRgubhirmahasAbhibhUtaiH
tvAM ramyarUpamasuraH pulakAvRtAngaH 
bhaktyA sametya sukRtI pariNijya pAdau
tattoyamanvadhRta mUrdhani tIrthatIrtham 9

आनीतमाशु भृगुभिर्महसाऽभिभूतै:
Highly impressed, as Shukra and others quickly received
त्वां रम्यरूपमसुर: पुलकावृताङ्ग: ।
you, of an exceedingly enchanting form, that asura with an ecstatic body,
भक्त्या समेत्य सुकृती परिणिज्य पादौ
approached with devotion, and having washed your feet,
तत्तोयमन्वधृत मूर्धनि तीर्थतीर्थम् ॥९॥
sprinkled those waters on his head, the most sacred of tIrthas!

प्रह्लादवंशजतया क्रतुभिर्द्विजेषु
विश्वासतो नु तदिदं दितिजोऽपि लेभे ।
यत्ते पदाम्बु गिरिशस्य शिरोभिलाल्यं
स त्वं विभो गुरुपुरालय पालयेथा: ॥१०॥
prahlAdavaMshajatayA kratubhirdvijeShu
vishvAsato nu tadidaM ditijopi lebhe 
yatte padAmbu girishasya shirobhilAlyaM
sa tvaM vibho gurupurAlaya pAlayethAH 10

प्रह्लादवंशजतया क्रतुभिर्द्विजेषु
Due to being born in the lineage of Prahlada, or due to the rituals performed,
विश्वासतो नु तदिदं दितिजोऽपि लेभे ।
or by his faith in dvijas, despite being an asura, he obtained 
यत्ते पदाम्बु गिरिशस्य शिरोभिलाल्यं
the waters from your feet, which are cared for on top of Shiva's head!
स त्वं विभो गुरुपुरालय पालयेथा: ॥१०॥
That You, O Lord! Who Resides in the City of Wind! May you protect me!
