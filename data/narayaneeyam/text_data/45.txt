ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

पञ्चचत्वारिंशम् दशकम्
panchachatvAriMsham dashakam

The Forty-fifth group of Ten shlokas

बाल लीला
bAla lIlA

Plays of Krishna as a Child

अयि सबल मुरारे पाणिजानुप्रचारै:
किमपि भवनभागान् भूषयन्तौ भवन्तौ ।
चलितचरणकञ्जौ मञ्जुमञ्जीरशिञ्जा-
श्रवणकुतुकभाजौ चेरतुश्चारुवेगात् ॥१॥
ayi sabala murAre pANijAnuprachAraiH
kimapi bhavanabhAgAn bhUShayantau bhavantau 
chalitacharaNakanjau manjumanjIrashinjA-
shravaNakutukabhAjau cheratushchAruvegAt 1

अयि सबल मुरारे पाणिजानुप्रचारै:
O Murari! Along with Balarama, crawling with your palms and knees,
किमपि भवनभागान् भूषयन्तौ भवन्तौ ।
as if decorating the entire house, the both of you
चलितचरणकञ्जौ मञ्जुमञ्जीरशिञ्जा-
moving around with you lotus like feet, the pleasant tinkling sounds of your anklets,
श्रवणकुतुकभाजौ चेरतुश्चारुवेगात् ॥१॥
in order to hear it more and more, you both crawled faster and faster!

मृदु मृदु विहसन्तावुन्मिषद्दन्तवन्तौ
वदनपतितकेशौ दृश्यपादाब्जदेशौ ।
भुजगलितकरान्तव्यालगत्कङ्कणाङ्कौ
मतिमहरतमुच्चै: पश्यतां विश्वनॄणाम् ॥२॥
mRdu mRdu vihasantAvunmiShaddantavantau
vadanapatitakeshau dRshyapAdAbjadeshau 
bhujagalitakarAntavyAlagatkankaNAnkau
matimaharatamuchchaiH pashyatAM vishvanRRNAm 2

मृदु मृदु विहसन्तावुन्मिषद्दन्तवन्तौ
With your tender tender smiles revealing baby teeth,
वदनपतितकेशौ दृश्यपादाब्जदेशौ ।
with hair falling on your faces, as the lotus-like soles of your feet were visible,
भुजगलितकरान्तव्यालगत्कङ्कणाङ्कौ
with armlets that slipped from your shoulders all the way down to your palms,
मतिमहरतमुच्चै: पश्यतां विश्वनॄणाम् ॥२॥
the both of you intensely stole the minds of everybody in the world who looked at you!

अनुसरति जनौघे कौतुकव्याकुलाक्षे
किमपि कृतनिनादं व्याहसन्तौ द्रवन्तौ ।
वलितवदनपद्मं पृष्ठतो दत्तदृष्टी
किमिव न विदधाथे कौतुकं वासुदेव ॥३॥
anusarati janaughe kautukavyAkulAkShe
kimapi kRtaninAdaM vyAhasantau dravantau 
valitavadanapadmaM pRShThato dattadRShTI
kimiva na vidadhAthe kautukaM vAsudeva 3

अनुसरति जनौघे कौतुकव्याकुलाक्षे
As groups of people follow you with their eyes wide open in eagerness,
किमपि कृतनिनादं व्याहसन्तौ द्रवन्तौ ।
shrieking in joy as you laugh and run,
वलितवदनपद्मं पृष्ठतो दत्तदृष्टी
and then as you turn your lotus-like bodies around and look back,
किमिव न विदधाथे कौतुकं वासुदेव ॥३॥
O what kind of joyful excitements did you not cause, O Vaasudeva!

द्रुतगतिषु पतन्तावुत्थितौ लिप्तपङ्कौ
दिवि मुनिभिरपङ्कै: सस्मितं वन्द्यमानौ ।
द्रुतमथ जननीभ्यां सानुकम्पं गृहीतौ
मुहुरपि परिरब्धौ द्राग्युवां चुम्बितौ च ॥४॥
drutagatiShu patantAvutthitau liptapankau
divi munibhirapankaiH sasmitaM vandyamAnau 
drutamatha jananIbhyAM sAnukampaM gRhItau
muhurapi parirabdhau drAgyuvAM chumbitau cha 4

द्रुतगतिषु पतन्तावुत्थितौ लिप्तपङ्कौ
Moving quickly, falling, getting back up smeared in mud,
दिवि मुनिभिरपङ्कै: सस्मितं वन्द्यमानौ ।
worshipped by the unblemished sages in the heavens with smiles on their faces,
द्रुतमथ जननीभ्यां सानुकम्पं गृहीतौ
quickly grabbed by your mothers in their compassion,
मुहुरपि परिरब्धौ द्राग्युवां चुम्बितौ च ॥४॥
once again you both were embraced and kissed to be pacified!

स्नुतकुचभरमङ्के धारयन्ती भवन्तं
तरलमति यशोदा स्तन्यदा धन्यधन्या ।
कपटपशुप मध्ये मुग्धहासाङ्कुरं ते
दशनमुकुलहृद्यं वीक्ष्य वक्त्रं जहर्ष ॥५॥
snutakuchabharamanke dhArayantI bhavantaM
taralamati yashodA stanyadA dhanyadhanyA 
kapaTapashupa madhye mugdhahAsAnkuraM te
dashanamukulahRdyaM vIkShya vaktraM jaharSha 5

स्नुतकुचभरमङ्के धारयन्ती भवन्तं
Holding you in her lap, with her full, heavy breasts,
तरलमति यशोदा स्तन्यदा धन्यधन्या ।
the tender-hearted Yashoda, who nursed you is indeed highly fortunate!
कपटपशुप मध्ये मुग्धहासाङ्कुरं ते
O illusory cowherd body, in the middle,
दशनमुकुलहृद्यं वीक्ष्य वक्त्रं जहर्ष ॥५॥
as you have a smile on your face revealing your charming baby teeth, her face was filled with delight!

तदनुचरणचारी दारकैस्साकमारात्
निलयततिषु खेलन् बालचापल्यशाली ।
भवनशुकविडालान् वत्सकांश्चानुधावन्
कथमपि कृतहासैर्गोपकैर्वारितोऽभू: ॥६॥
tadanucharaNachArI dArakaissAkamArAt
nilayatatiShu khelan bAlachApalyashAlI 
bhavanashukaviDAlAn vatsakAMshchAnudhAvan
kathamapi kRtahAsairgopakairvAritobhUH 6

तदनुचरणचारी दारकैस्साकमारात्
And then, when you started walking on your feet, along with your friends,
निलयततिषु खेलन् बालचापल्यशाली ।
roaming around in the neighborhood in your childish wanderings,
भवनशुकविडालान् वत्सकांश्चानुधावन्
chasing pet parrots, cats, and calves, 
कथमपि कृतहासैर्गोपकैर्वारितोऽभू: ॥६॥
somehow by the laughing Gopas you always kept getting caught!

हलधरसहितस्त्वं यत्र यत्रोपयातो
विवशपतितनेत्रास्तत्र तत्रैव गोप्य: ।
विगलितगृहकृत्या विस्मृतापत्यभृत्या
मुरहर मुहुरत्यन्ताकुला नित्यमासन् ॥७॥
haladharasahitastvaM yatra yatropayAto
vivashapatitanetrAstatra tatraiva gopyaH 
vigalitagRhakRtyA vismRtApatyabhRtyA
murahara muhuratyantAkulA nityamAsan 7

हलधरसहितस्त्वं यत्र यत्रोपयातो
Along with Balarama, wherever you went,
विवशपतितनेत्रास्तत्र तत्रैव गोप्य: ।
there always were enchanted Gopis with their eyes set on you,
विगलितगृहकृत्या विस्मृतापत्यभृत्या
neglecting their household chores, forgetting their children and responsibilities!
मुरहर मुहुरत्यन्ताकुला नित्यमासन् ॥७॥
O Slayer of Mura, still, they were always there extremely excited!

प्रतिनवनवनीतं गोपिकादत्तमिच्छन्
कलपदमुपगायन् कोमलं क्वापि नृत्यन् ।
सदययुवतिलोकैरर्पितं सर्पिरश्नन्
क्वचन नवविपक्वं दुग्धमप्यापिबस्त्वम् ॥८॥
pratinavanavanItaM gopikAdattamichChan
kalapadamupagAyan komalaM kvApi nRtyan 
sadayayuvatilokairarpitaM sarpirashnan
kvachana navavipakvaM dugdhamapyApibastvam 8

प्रतिनवनवनीतं गोपिकादत्तमिच्छन्
Desiring the freshly churned butter that was given by the Gopikas,
कलपदमुपगायन् कोमलं क्वापि नृत्यन् ।
singing a sweet song and doing some sort of a dance,
सदययुवतिलोकैरर्पितं सर्पिरश्नन्
eating the butter offered by the young maidens,
क्वचन नवविपक्वं दुग्धमप्यापिबस्त्वम् ॥८॥
and sometimes freshly boiled milk too, you used to drink!

मम खलु बलिगेहे याचनं जातमास्ताम्
इह पुनरबलानामग्रतो नैव कुर्वे ।
इति विहितमति: किं देव सन्त्यज्य याच्ञां
दधिघृतमहरस्त्वं चारुणा चोरणेन ॥९॥
mama khalu baligehe yAchanaM jAtamAstAm
iha punarabalAnAmagrato naiva kurve 
iti vihitamatiH kiM deva santyajya yAchnAM
dadhighRtamaharastvaM chAruNA choraNena 9

मम खलु बलिगेहे याचनं जातमास्ताम्
"Didn't I once upon a time beg at Bali's house!
इह पुनरबलानामग्रतो नैव कुर्वे ।
Here again, with these women, I am not going to beg!"
इति विहितमति: किं देव सन्त्यज्य याच्ञां
deciding thus did you, O Lord, give up begging
दधिघृतमहरस्त्वं चारुणा चोरणेन ॥९॥
and take to the beautiful stealing of curd, butter, and milk!

तव दधिघृतमोषे घोषयोषाजनानाम्
अभजत हृदि रोषो नावकाशं न शोक: ।
हृदयमपि मुषित्वा हर्षसिन्धौ न्यधास्त्वं
स मम शमय रोगान् वातगेहाधिनाथ ॥१०॥
tava dadhighRtamoShe ghoShayoShAjanAnAm
abhajata hRdi roSho nAvakAshaM na shokaH 
hRdayamapi muShitvA harShasindhau nyadhAstvaM
sa mama shamaya rogAn vAtagehAdhinAtha 10

तव दधिघृतमोषे घोषयोषाजनानाम्
As you stole curd and ghee, the womenfolk there
अभजत हृदि रोषो नावकाशं न शोक: ।
neither felt anger, nor despair or grief!
हृदयमपि मुषित्वा हर्षसिन्धौ न्यधास्त्वं
You stole their hearts as well and placed them in the ocean of joy!
स मम शमय रोगान् वातगेहाधिनाथ ॥१०॥
That you, O Lord of the Abode of Wind, pacify my ailments!

शाखाग्रेऽथ विधुं विलोक्य फलमित्यम्बां च तातं मुहु:
संप्रार्थ्याथ तदा तदीयवचसा प्रोत्क्षिप्तबाहौ त्वयि।
चित्रं देव शशी स ते करमगात् किं ब्रूमहे संपत-
ज्ज्योतिर्मण्डलपूरिताखिलवपु: प्रागा विराड्रूपताम् ॥ ११॥
shAkhAgretha vidhuM vilokya phalamityambAM cha tAtaM muhuH
saMprArthyAtha tadA tadIyavachasA protkShiptabAhau tvayi
chitraM deva shashI sa te karamagAt kiM brUmahe saMpata-
jjyotirmaNDalapUritAkhilavapuH prAgA virADrUpatAm  11

शाखाग्रेऽथ विधुं विलोक्य फलमित्य्म्बां च तातं मुहु:
Looking at the moon that appeared at the end of a tree branch, thinking that it was a fruit, you kept asking your mother and father for it,
संप्रार्थ्याथ तदा तदीयवचसा प्रोत्क्षिप्तबाहौ त्वयि।
upon father's words as you flung your arms at the moon, 
चित्रं देव शशी स ते करमगात् किं ब्रूमहे संपत-
O what wonder, he indeed came into your grasp! What to say of your glories!
ज्ज्योतिर्मण्डलपूरिताखिलवपु: प्रागा विराड्रूपताम् ॥ ११॥
O Lord whose entire form was enveloped in that glow, you shined in your cosmic form!

किं किं बतेदमिति संभ्रम भाजमेनं
ब्रह्मार्णवे क्षणममुं परिमज्ज्य तातम् ।
मायां पुनस्तनयमोहमयीं वितन्वन्
आनन्दचिन्मय जगन्मय पाहि रोगात् ॥१२॥
kiM kiM batedamiti saMbhrama bhAjamenaM
brahmArNave kShaNamamuM parimajjya tAtam 
mAyAM punastanayamohamayIM vitanvan
Anandachinmaya jaganmaya pAhi rogAt 12

किं किं बतेदमिति संभ्रम भाजमेनं
As he was utterly bewildered by this wondrous occurrence,
ब्रह्मार्णवे क्षणममुं परिमज्ज्य तातम् ।
immersing your father for a moment in the cosmic ocean,
मायां पुनस्तनयमोहमयीं वितन्वन्
once again you drew him back into the illusion of you being his child!
आनन्दचिन्मय जगन्मय पाहि रोगात् ॥१२॥
O Lord of the nature of Conscious Bliss! O Embodiment of Creation! Protect me from my ailments!
