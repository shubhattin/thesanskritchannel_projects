अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

षट्पञ्चाशम् दशकम्
ShaTpanchAsham dashakam

The Fifty-sixth group of Ten shlokas

कालिय अनुग्रह:
kAliya anugrahaH

Blessing Kaliya

रुचिरकम्पितकुण्डलमण्डल: सुचिरमीश ननर्तिथ पन्नगे ।
अमरताडितदुन्दुभिसुन्दरं वियति गायति दैवतयौवते ॥१॥
ruchirakampitakuNDalamaNDalaH suchiramIsha nanartitha pannage 
amaratADitadundubhisundaraM viyati gAyati daivatayauvate 1

रुचिरकम्पितकुण्डलमण्डल: सुचिरमीश ननर्तिथ पन्नगे ।
As your ear rings fluttered beautifully, O Lord! You gracefully danced on that serpent!
अमरताडितदुन्दुभिसुन्दरं वियति गायति दैवतयौवते ॥१॥
As the Devas gave you the beat of their drums and the celestial maidens gave you a tune!

नमति यद्यदमुष्य शिरो हरे परिविहाय तदुन्नतमुन्नतम् ।
परिमथन् पदपङ्करुहा चिरं व्यहरथा: करतालमनोहरम् ॥२॥
namati yadyadamuShya shiro hare parivihAya tadunnatamunnatam 
parimathan padapankaruhA chiraM vyaharathAH karatAlamanoharam 2

नमति यद्यदमुष्य शिरो हरे परिविहाय तदुन्नतमुन्नतम् ।
Whichever hood bowed down, O Hari, sparing that, whichever hood came up again and again,
परिमथन् पदपङ्करुहा चिरं व्यहरथा: करतालमनोहरम् ॥२॥
trampling on it with youtr lotus-like feet, you danced for long emanating enchanting clapping sounds!

त्वदवभग्नविभुग्नफणागणे गलितशोणितशोणितपाथसि ।
फणिपताववसीदति सन्नतास्तदबलास्तव माधव पादयो: ॥३॥
tvadavabhagnavibhugnaphaNAgaNe galitashoNitashoNitapAthasi 
phaNipatAvavasIdati sannatAstadabalAstava mAdhava pAdayoH 3

त्वदवभग्नविभुग्नफणागणे गलितशोणितशोणितपाथसि ।
From the groups of hoods that have been destroyed by you, as streaks of blood started to pour, making the waters red,
फणिपताववसीदति सन्नतास्तदबलास्तव माधव पादयो: ॥३॥
as the serpent lord lost his strength, his wives approached your feet and bowed down, O Madhava!

अयि पुरैव चिराय परिश्रुतत्वदनुभावविलीनहृदो हि ता: ।
मुनिभिरप्यनवाप्यपथै: स्तवैर्नुनुवुरीश भवन्तमयन्त्रितम् ॥४॥
ayi puraiva chirAya parishrutatvadanubhAvavilInahRdo hi tAH 
munibhirapyanavApyapathaiH stavairnunuvurIsha bhavantamayantritam 4

अयि पुरैव चिराय परिश्रुतत्वदनुभावविलीनहृदो हि ता: ।
O They having already heard of your glories long ago, with their hearts immersed in you,
मुनिभिरप्यनवाप्यपथै: स्तवैर्नुनुवुरीश भवन्तमयन्त्रितम् ॥४॥
started worshipping you, O Lord, with generous words of praise which are impossible for even the sages to compose!

फणिवधूगणभक्तिविलोकनप्रविकसत्करुणाकुलचेतसा ।
फणिपतिर्भवताऽच्युत जीवितस्त्वयि समर्पितमूर्तिरवानमत् ॥५॥
phaNivadhUgaNabhaktivilokanapravikasatkaruNAkulachetasA 
phaNipatirbhavatAchyuta jIvitastvayi samarpitamUrtiravAnamat 5

फणिवधूगणभक्तिविलोकनप्रविकसत्करुणाकुलचेतसा ।
As you saw the devotion of the serpent wives, with compassion blossoming in your mind,
फणिपतिर्भवताऽच्युत जीवितस्त्वयि समर्पितमूर्तिरवानमत् ॥५॥
you let the serpent lord live, O Achyuta, who bowed down to you surrendering himself!

रमणकं व्रज वारिधिमध्यगं फणिरिपुर्न करोति विरोधिताम् ।
इति भवद्वचनान्यतिमानयन् फणिपतिर्निरगादुरगै: समम् ॥६॥
ramaNakaM vraja vAridhimadhyagaM phaNiripurna karoti virodhitAm 
iti bhavadvachanAnyatimAnayan phaNipatirniragAduragaiH samam 6

रमणकं व्रज वारिधिमध्यगं फणिरिपुर्न करोति विरोधिताम् ।
"Go to the Ramanaka island in the middle of the ocean, Garuda will not trouble you there!"
इति भवद्वचनान्यतिमानयन् फणिपतिर्निरगादुरगै: समम् ॥६॥
obeying these words with utmost reverence, the serpent lord left the place along with all the other snakes.

फणिवधूजनदत्तमणिव्रजज्वलितहारदुकूलविभूषित: ।
तटगतै: प्रमदाश्रुविमिश्रितै: समगथा: स्वजनैर्दिवसावधौ ॥७॥
phaNivadhUjanadattamaNivrajajvalitahAradukUlavibhUShitaH 
taTagataiH pramadAshruvimishritaiH samagathAH svajanairdivasAvadhau 7

फणिवधूजनदत्तमणिव्रजज्वलितहारदुकूलविभूषित: ।
Ornamented with a garland of dazzling jewels and resplendent garments offered by the serpent maidens,
तटगतै: प्रमदाश्रुविमिश्रितै: समगथा: स्वजनैर्दिवसावधौ ॥७॥
as dusk approached, you went to the banks to meet your folk waiting with tears in their eyes.

निशि पुनस्तमसा व्रजमन्दिरं व्रजितुमक्षम एव जनोत्करे ।
स्वपति तत्र भवच्चरणाश्रये दवकृशानुररुन्ध समन्तत: ॥८॥
nishi punastamasA vrajamandiraM vrajitumakShama eva janotkare 
svapati tatra bhavachcharaNAshraye davakRshAnurarundha samantataH 8

निशि पुनस्तमसा व्रजमन्दिरं व्रजितुमक्षम एव जनोत्करे ।
As night fell, unable to go back to their houses in that darkness, as the people 
स्वपति तत्र भवच्चरणाश्रये दवकृशानुररुन्ध समन्तत: ॥८॥
slept there itself by the shelter of your feet, a forest fire surrounded you all!

प्रबुधितानथ पालय पालयेत्युदयदार्तरवान् पशुपालकान् ।
अवितुमाशु पपाथ महानलं किमिह चित्रमयं खलु ते मुखम् ॥९॥
prabudhitAnatha pAlaya pAlayetyudayadArtaravAn pashupAlakAn 
avitumAshu papAtha mahAnalaM kimiha chitramayaM khalu te mukham 9

प्रबुधितानथ पालय पालयेत्युदयदार्तरवान् पशुपालकान् ।
Then, as the cowherds having woken up started to cry for help,
अवितुमाशु पपाथ महानलं किमिह चित्रमयं खलु ते मुखम् ॥९॥
to protect them, you effortlessly drank up that fire! What's surprising since the fire element is in fact your very mouth!

शिखिनि वर्णत एव हि पीतता परिलसत्यधुना क्रिययाऽप्यसौ ।
इति नुत: पशुपैर्मुदितैर्विभो हर हरे दुरितै:सह मे गदान् ॥१०॥
shikhini varNata eva hi pItatA parilasatyadhunA kriyayApyasau 
iti nutaH pashupairmuditairvibho hara hare duritaiHsaha me gadAn 10

शिखिनि वर्णत एव हि पीतता परिलसत्यधुना क्रिययाऽप्यसौ ।
"It is from the colour of the fire(pIta:yellow) itself that this activity of pItatA:drinking up comes about!"
इति नुत: पशुपैर्मुदितैर्विभो हर हरे दुरितै:सह मे गदान् ॥१०॥
thus, O Lord, who was worshipped by the elated cowherds, O Hari, along with my sins, remove my ailments as well!
