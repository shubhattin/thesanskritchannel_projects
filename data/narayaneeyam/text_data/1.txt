कायेन वाचा मनसेन्द्रियैर्वा बुद्ध्यात्मना वा प्रकृते: स्वभावात्। 
करोमि यद्यत् सकलं परस्मै नारायणायेति समर्पयामि॥
kAyena vAchA manasendriyairvA buddhyAtmanA vA prakRteH svabhAvAt 
karomi yadyat sakalaM parasmai nArAyaNAyeti samarpayAmi

कायेन वाचा मनसेन्द्रियैर्वा बुद्ध्यात्मना वा प्रकृते: स्वभावात्। 
With my body, my speech, my mind, or my sense organs; through my intellect, my sense of self, or my natural tendencies
करोमि यद्यत् सकलं परस्मै नारायणायेति समर्पयामि॥
whatever actions I perform, all of it is towards the supreme being! By saying 'nArAyaNAya', I submit my offering!

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

प्रथमम् दशकम्

The First group of Ten shlokas

भगवतः स्वरूपं माहात्म्यं च
bhagavataH svarUpam mAhAtmyam cha

The Form of the Divine and its Glory

सान्द्रानन्दावबोधात्मकमनुपमितं कालदेशावधिभ्यां
निर्मुक्तं नित्यमुक्तं निगमशतसहस्रेण निर्भास्यमानम् ।
अस्पष्टं दृष्टमात्रे पुनरुरुपुरुषार्थात्मकं ब्रह्म तत्वं
तत्तावद्भाति साक्षाद् गुरुपवनपुरे हन्त भाग्यं जनानाम् ॥ १ ॥
sAndrAnandAvabodhAtmakamanupamitaM kAladeshAvadhibhyAM
nirmuktaM nityamuktaM nigamashatasahasreNa nirbhAsyamAnam 
aspaShTaM dRShTamAtre punarurupuruShArthAtmakaM brahma tatvaM
tattAvadbhAti sAkShAd gurupavanapure hanta bhAgyaM janAnAm  1 

सान्द्रानन्दावबोधात्मकमनुपमितं कालदेशावधिभ्यां
That which brings about an experience of profound, intense bliss, that which is incomparable,
by the limits of both time and space
निर्मुक्तं नित्यमुक्तं निगमशतसहस्रेण निर्भास्यमानम् ।
that which is unbounded, that which is said to be eternal,
that which is elucidated by hundreds and thousands of realizations  
अस्पष्टं दृष्टमात्रे पुनरुरुपुरुषार्थात्मकं ब्रह्म तत्वं
that which is not obviously apparent,
but which when seen becomes the highest attainment of humans, that principle of the very source of creation,
तत्तावद्भाति साक्षाद् गुरुपवनपुरे हन्त भाग्यं जनानाम् ॥ १ ॥
that shines right in front of our eyes in the city of Guruvayur,
Damn! What great fortune of the people!

एवंदुर्लभ्यवस्तुन्यपि सुलभतया हस्तलब्धे यदन्यत्
तन्वा वाचा धिया वा भजति बत जन: क्षुद्रतैव स्फुटेयम् ।
एते तावद्वयं तु स्थिरतरमनसा विश्वपीड़ापहत्यै
निश्शेषात्मानमेनं गुरुपवनपुराधीशमेवाश्रयाम: ॥ २ ॥z
evaMdurlabhyavastunyapi sulabhatayA hastalabdhe yadanyat
tanvA vAchA dhiyA vA bhajati bata janaH kShudrataiva sphuTeyam 
ete tAvadvayaM tu sthirataramanasA vishvapIDApahatyai
nishsheShAtmAnamenaM gurupavanapurAdhIshamevAshrayAmaH  2 

एवंदुर्लभ्यवस्तुन्यपि सुलभतया हस्तलब्धे यदन्यत्
Like this, when something so hard to obtain is so easily within reach, other stuff
तन्वा वाचा धिया वा भजति बत जन: क्षुद्रतैव स्फुटेयम् ।
is sought after by actions, speech, and mind by people, say, this is so clearly miserable!
एते तावद्वयं तु स्थिरतरमनसा विश्वपीड़ापहत्यै
The others may be so, but we with our unfaltering minds, to rid ourselves of all suffering
निश्शेषात्मानमेनं गुरुपवनपुराधीशमेवाश्रयाम: ॥ २ ॥
in this all-inclusive nature of the Lord of Guruvayur, we seek refuge!

सत्त्वं यत्तत् पराभ्यामपरिकलनतो निर्मलं तेन तावत्
भूतैर्भूतेन्द्रियैस्ते वपुरिति बहुश: श्रूयते व्यासवाक्यम्।
तत् स्वच्छ्त्वाद्यदाच्छादितपरसुखचिद्गर्भनिर्भासरूपं
तस्मिन् धन्या रमन्ते श्रुतिमतिमधुरे सुग्रहे विग्रहे ते ॥ ३ ॥
sattvaM yattat parAbhyAmaparikalanato nirmalaM tena tAvat
bhUtairbhUtendriyaiste vapuriti bahushaH shrUyate vyAsavAkyam
tat svachChtvAdyadAchChAditaparasukhachidgarbhanirbhAsarUpaM
tasmin dhanyA ramante shrutimatimadhure sugrahe vigrahe te 3 

सत्त्वं यत्तत् पराभ्यामपरिकलनतो निर्मलं तेन तावत्
That which is Sattva, which remains unblemished by any other factors, with that
भूतैर्भूतेन्द्रियैस्ते वपुरिति बहुश: श्रूयते व्यासवाक्यम्।
you pervade all beings and their senses as your body! Thus Vyasa's words are heard in multiple ways!
तत् स्वच्छ्त्वाद्यदाच्छादितपरसुखचिद्गर्भनिर्भासरूपं
Enveloped in this pureness - Sattva, and from the womb of the ultimate conscious bliss, your form shines!
तस्मिन् धन्या रमन्ते श्रुतिमतिमधुरे सुग्रहे विग्रहे ते ॥ ३ ॥
And in that rejoice the fortunate ones, in the sweetly heard and thought of, attractive form of yours!

निष्कम्पे नित्यपूर्णे निरवधिपरमानन्दपीयूषरूपे
निर्लीनानेकमुक्तावलिसुभगतमे निर्मलब्रह्मसिन्धौ ।
कल्लोलोल्लासतुल्यं खलु विमलतरं सत्त्वमाहुस्तदात्मा
कस्मान्नो निष्कलस्त्वं सकल इति वचस्त्वत्कलास्वेव भूमन् ॥ ४ ॥
niShkampe nityapUrNe niravadhiparamAnandapIyUSharUpe
nirlInAnekamuktAvalisubhagatame nirmalabrahmasindhau 
kallolollAsatulyaM khalu vimalataraM sattvamAhustadAtmA
kasmAnno niShkalastvaM sakala iti vachastvatkalAsveva bhUman 4 

निष्कम्पे नित्यपूर्णे निरवधिपरमानन्दपीयूषरूपे
In the unwavering, eternally fulfilled, immeasurably blissful, nectarine form,
निर्लीनानेकमुक्तावलिसुभगतमे निर्मलब्रह्मसिन्धौ ।
into which many liberated souls found dissolution, into the ocean of pure universal consciousness,
कल्लोलोल्लासतुल्यं खलु विमलतरं सत्त्वमाहुस्तदात्मा
isn't that said to be of the nature of violent raptures, that pure sense of self!
कस्मान्नो निष्कलस्त्वं सकल इति वचस्त्वत्कलास्वेव भूमन् ॥ ४ ॥
Why then are You not attribute-less! The word Complete is apt only for your attributes, O Abundant One!

निर्व्यापारोऽपि निष्कारणमज भजसे यत्क्रियामीक्षणाख्यां
तेनैवोदेति लीना प्रकृतिरसतिकल्पाऽपि कल्पादिकाले।
तस्या: संशुद्धमंशं कमपि तमतिरोधायकं सत्त्वरूपं
स त्वं धृत्वा दधासि स्वमहिमविभवाकुण्ठ वैकुण्ठ रूपं॥५॥
nirvyApAropi niShkAraNamaja bhajase yatkriyAmIkShaNAkhyAM
tenaivodeti lInA prakRtirasatikalpApi kalpAdikAle
tasyAH saMshuddhamaMshaM kamapi tamatirodhAyakaM sattvarUpaM
sa tvaM dhRtvA dadhAsi svamahimavibhavAkuNTha vaikuNTha rUpaM 5

निर्व्यापारोऽपि निष्कारणमज भजसे यत्क्रियामीक्षणाख्यां
Even if free from worldly transactions and motives, O One Without Birth, the activity that you perform, called being a witness
तेनैवोदेति लीना प्रकृतिरसतिकल्पाऽपि कल्पादिकाले।
it is from that this subtle creation is born, as if without a creator, at the very beginning of Creation!
तस्या: संशुद्धमंशं कमपि तमतिरोधायकं सत्त्वरूपं
Of that creation itself, one pure aspect which does not negate the purity of being
स त्वं धृत्वा दधासि स्वमहिमविभवाकुण्ठ वैकुण्ठ रूपं॥५॥
You assume that form, and conduct your activity, O One Whose Grace is Unsullied, with your flawless form!

तत्ते प्रत्यग्रधाराधरललितकलायावलीकेलिकारं
लावण्यस्यैकसारं सुकृतिजनदृशां पूर्णपुण्यावतारम्।
लक्ष्मीनिश्शङ्कलीलानिलयनममृतस्यन्दसन्दोहमन्त:
सिञ्चत् सञ्चिन्तकानां वपुरनुकलये मारुतागारनाथ ॥६॥
tatte pratyagradhArAdharalalitakalAyAvalIkelikAraM
lAvaNyasyaikasAraM sukRtijanadRshAM pUrNapuNyAvatAram
lakShmInishshankalIlAnilayanamamRtasyandasandohamantaH
sinchat sanchintakAnAM vapuranukalaye mArutAgAranAtha 6

तत्ते प्रत्यग्रधाराधरललितकलायावलीकेलिकारं
That form of yours like a fresh rain-cloud and a series of fresh lotus blossoms, which is playful,
लावण्यस्यैकसारं सुकृतिजनदृशां पूर्णपुण्यावतारम्।
the single-most essence of charm, and for the eyes of the pious, an embodiment of complete virtue,
लक्ष्मीनिश्शङ्कलीलानिलयनममृतस्यन्दसन्दोहमन्त:
the unabashed abode of the play of Lakshmi, the torrential downpour of Amrita within
सिञ्चत् सञ्चिन्तकानां वपुरनुकलये मारुतागारनाथ ॥६॥
that nourishes those who dwell upon it, I meditate upon that form, O Lord of the Residence of Wind!

कष्टा ते सृष्टिचेष्टा बहुतरभवखेदावहा जीवभाजाम्
इत्येवं पूर्वमालोचितमजित मया नैवमद्याभिजाने।
नोचेज्जीवा: कथं वा मधुरतरमिदं त्वद्वपुश्चिद्रसार्द्रं
नेत्रै: श्रोत्रैश्च पीत्वा परमरससुधाम्भोधिपूरे रमेरन्॥७॥
kaShTA te sRShTicheShTA bahutarabhavakhedAvahA jIvabhAjAm
ityevaM pUrvamAlochitamajita mayA naivamadyAbhijAne
nochejjIvAH kathaM vA madhurataramidaM tvadvapushchidrasArdraM
netraiH shrotraishcha pItvA paramarasasudhAmbhodhipUre rameran 7

कष्टा ते सृष्टिचेष्टा बहुतरभवखेदावहा जीवभाजाम्
Your acts of creation are cruel and create all kinds of sensory suffering to living beings...
इत्येवं पूर्वमालोचितमजित मया नैवमद्याभिजाने।
like this, I used to think before, O Undefeated One! But I do not think so now!
नोचेज्जीवा: कथं वा मधुरतरमिदं त्वद्वपुश्चिद्रसार्द्रं
Otherwise, how could living beings ever relish this sweetest form of yours, drenched in the essence of consciousness,
नेत्रै: श्रोत्रैश्च पीत्वा परमरससुधाम्भोधिपूरे रमेरन्॥७॥
drinking it in with their eyes and ears, and rejoice in the ultimate ocean of nectarine bliss! 

नम्राणां सन्निधत्ते सततमपि पुरस्तैरनभ्यर्थितान -
प्यर्थान् कामानजस्रं वितरति परमानन्दसान्द्रां गतिं च।
इत्थं निश्शेषलभ्यो निरवधिकफल: पारिजातो हरे त्वं
क्षुद्रं तं शक्रवाटीद्रुममभिलषति व्यर्थमर्थिव्रजोऽयम्॥८॥
namrANAM sannidhatte satatamapi purastairanabhyarthitAna -
pyarthAn kAmAnajasraM vitarati paramAnandasAndrAM gatiM cha
itthaM nishsheShalabhyo niravadhikaphalaH pArijAto hare tvaM
kShudraM taM shakravATIdrumamabhilaShati vyarthamarthivrajoyam 8

नम्राणां सन्निधत्ते सततमपि पुरस्तैरनभ्यर्थितान -
For those who worship you, you appear ever in front of them, even if they don't ask
प्यर्थान् कामानजस्रं वितरति परमानन्दसान्द्रां गतिं च।
you grant them riches, fulfill all desires and grant them the path to ultimate intense bliss!
इत्थं निश्शेषलभ्यो निरवधिकफल: पारिजातो हरे त्वं
Like this, a completely accessible and boundlessly generous pArijAta tree you are, O Hari!
क्षुद्रं तं शक्रवाटीद्रुममभिलषति व्यर्थमर्थिव्रजोऽयम्॥८॥
That measly tree in Indra's grove(Kalpavriksha), uselessly these desirous people seek!

कारुण्यात्काममन्यं ददति खलु परे स्वात्मदस्त्वं विशेषाद्
ऐश्वर्यादीशतेऽन्ये जगति परजने स्वात्मनोऽपीश्वरस्त्वम्।
त्वय्युच्चैरारमन्ति प्रतिपदमधुरे चेतना: स्फीतभाग्याः
त्वं चात्माराम एवेत्यतुलगुणगणाधार शौरे नमस्ते॥९॥
kAruNyAtkAmamanyaM dadati khalu pare svAtmadastvaM visheShAd
aishvaryAdIshatenye jagati parajane svAtmanopIshvarastvam
tvayyuchchairAramanti pratipadamadhure chetanAH sphItabhAgyAH
tvaM chAtmArAma evetyatulaguNagaNAdhAra shaure namaste 9

कारुण्यात्काममन्यं ददति खलु परे स्वात्मदस्त्वं विशेषाद्
Out of compassion, many objects of desire are granted by other devas, but you are exceptional at granting one's very self!
ऐश्वर्यादीशतेऽन्ये जगति परजने स्वात्मनोऽपीश्वरस्त्वम्।
Other devas rule through their powers over the people of this world, but you rule over the very own sense of self!
त्वय्युच्चैरारमन्ति प्रतिपदमधुरे चेतना: स्फीतभाग्याः
In you they find extreme bliss, in the constant sweetness at every step, those who are truly conscious and highly fortunate,
त्वं चात्माराम एवेत्यतुलगुणगणाधार शौरे नमस्ते॥९॥
knowing that You are the one who revels within yourself! O Abode of Incomparable Qualities, O Valiant One, I bow down to you!

ऐश्वर्यं शङ्करादीश्वरविनियमनं विश्वतेजोहराणां
तेजस्संहारि वीर्यं विमलमपि यशो निस्पृहैश्चोपगीतम्।
अङ्गासङ्गा सदा श्रीरखिलविदसि न क्वापि ते सङ्गवार्ता
तद्वातागारवासिन् मुरहर भगवच्छब्दमुख्याश्रयोऽसि॥१०॥
aishvaryaM shankarAdIshvaraviniyamanaM vishvatejoharANAM
tejassaMhAri vIryaM vimalamapi yasho nispRhaishchopagItam
angAsangA sadA shrIrakhilavidasi na kvApi te sangavArtA
tadvAtAgAravAsin murahara bhagavachChabdamukhyAshrayosi 10

ऐश्वर्यं शङ्करादीश्वरविनियमनं विश्वतेजोहराणां
Rulership of the entire creation, governing the functions of shankara and others, of those who remove brightness from the universe,
तेजस्संहारि वीर्यं विमलमपि यशो निस्पृहैश्चोपगीतम्।
valiance that quells their activity, unblemished glory, all of these qualities of yours are sung by the enlightened ones!
अङ्गासङ्गा सदा श्रीरखिलविदसि न क्वापि ते सङ्गवार्ता
Goddess Shri is always attached with your body, you are all-knowing, and yet there is no trace of attachment within you!
तद्वातागारवासिन् मुरहर भगवच्छब्दमुख्याश्रयोऽसि॥१०॥
O Lord Who Resides in the Abode of the Wind, O Vanquisher of Mura, the very word Bhagavat: The Radiant One mainly takes its definition from you!
