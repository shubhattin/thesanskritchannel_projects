The Thirty-fourth group of Ten shlokas

श्रीमद् रामायणम् हनुमत् समागम पर्यन्तम्
shrImad rAmAyaNam hanumat samAgama paryantam

Ramayana until the meeting with Hanuman

गीर्वाणैरर्थ्यमानो दशमुखनिधनं कोसलेष्वृश्यशृङ्गे
पुत्रीयामिष्टिमिष्ट्वा ददुषि दशरथक्ष्माभृते पायसाग्र्यम् ।
तद्भुक्त्या तत्पुरन्ध्रीष्वपि तिसृषु समं जातगर्भासु जातो
रामस्त्वं लक्ष्मणेन स्वयमथ भरतेनापि शत्रुघ्ननाम्ना ॥१॥
gIrvANairarthyamAno dashamukhanidhanaM kosaleShvRshyashRnge
putrIyAmiShTimiShTvA daduShi dasharathakShmAbhRte pAyasAgryam 
tadbhuktyA tatpurandhrIShvapi tisRShu samaM jAtagarbhAsu jAto
rAmastvaM lakShmaNena svayamatha bharatenApi shatrughnanAmnA 1

गीर्वाणैरर्थ्यमानो दशमुखनिधनं कोसलेष्वृश्यशृङ्गे
Requested by the Devas, for the destruction of the Ten-Headed Ravana,
in the Kingdom of Kosala, as Sage Rishyashringa
पुत्रीयामिष्टिमिष्ट्वा ददुषि दशरथक्ष्माभृते पायसाग्र्यम् ।
performed a Yajna to beget children for King Dasharatha, and offered the resulting Paayasa,
तद्भुक्त्या तत्पुरन्ध्रीष्वपि तिसृषु समं जातगर्भासु जातो
by consuming it, as the three queens had well conceived pregnancies, 
रामस्त्वं लक्ष्मणेन स्वयमथ भरतेनापि शत्रुघ्ननाम्ना ॥१॥
You were born as Rama, along with Lakshmana, Bharata and Shatrughna!

कोदण्डी कौशिकस्य क्रतुवरमवितुं लक्ष्मणेनानुयातो
यातोऽभूस्तातवाचा मुनिकथितमनुद्वन्द्वशान्ताध्वखेद: ।
नॄणां त्राणाय बाणैर्मुनिवचनबलात्ताटकां पाटयित्वा
लब्ध्वास्मादस्त्रजालं मुनिवनमगमो देव सिद्धाश्रमाख्यम् ॥२॥
kodaNDI kaushikasya kratuvaramavituM lakShmaNenAnuyAto
yAtobhUstAtavAchA munikathitamanudvandvashAntAdhvakhedaH 
nRRNAM trANAya bANairmunivachanabalAttATakAM pATayitvA
labdhvAsmAdastrajAlaM munivanamagamo deva siddhAshramAkhyam 2 

कोदण्डी कौशिकस्य क्रतुवरमवितुं लक्ष्मणेनानुयातो
Weilding the kodaNDa bow, you went to protect vishvAmitra's yajna along with Lakshmana,
यातोऽभूस्तातवाचा मुनिकथितमनुद्वन्द्वशान्ताध्वखेद: ।
you went on your father's orders, and received two mantras - bala and atibala, from the sage which remove one's fatigue,
नॄणां त्राणाय बाणैर्मुनिवचनबलात्ताटकां पाटयित्वा
to protect the people, upon the sage's counsel, you slayed tATakA with your arrows,
लब्ध्वास्मादस्त्रजालं मुनिवनमगमो देव सिद्धाश्रमाख्यम् ॥२॥
and having received divine weapons, you went to the sage's hermitage known as siddhAshrama!

मारीचं द्रावयित्वा मखशिरसि शरैरन्यरक्षांसि निघ्नन्
कल्यां कुर्वन्नहल्यां पथि पदरजसा प्राप्य वैदेहगेहम् ।
भिन्दानश्चान्द्रचूडं धनुरवनिसुतामिन्दिरामेव लब्ध्वा
राज्यं प्रातिष्ठथास्त्वं त्रिभिरपि च समं भ्रातृवीरैस्सदारै: ॥३॥
mArIchaM drAvayitvA makhashirasi sharairanyarakShAMsi nighnan
kalyAM kurvannahalyAM pathi padarajasA prApya vaidehageham 
bhindAnashchAndrachUDaM dhanuravanisutAmindirAmeva labdhvA
rAjyaM prAtiShThathAstvaM tribhirapi cha samaM bhrAtRvIraissadAraiH 3

मारीचं द्रावयित्वा मखशिरसि शरैरन्यरक्षांसि निघ्नन्
Driving mArIcha away, protecting the yajna by a shield of arrows, killing the other rAkShasas,
कल्यां कुर्वन्नहल्यां पथि पदरजसा प्राप्य वैदेहगेहम् ।
bestowing wellbeing to ahalyA along the way by the dust of your feet, at King Janaka's residence,
भिन्दानश्चान्द्रचूडं धनुरवनिसुतामिन्दिरामेव लब्ध्वा
shattering Shiva's bow-chandrachUDa, begetting the daughter of earth, Goddess lakShmI herself, devI sItA,
राज्यं प्रातिष्ठथास्त्वं त्रिभिरपि च समं भ्रातृवीरैस्सदारै: ॥३॥
you set out to return to your kingdom along with your three valiant brothers and their wives!

आरुन्धाने रुषान्धे भृगुकुल तिलके संक्रमय्य स्वतेजो
याते यातोऽस्ययोध्यां सुखमिह निवसन् कान्तया कान्तमूर्ते ।
शत्रुघ्नेनैकदाथो गतवति भरते मातुलस्याधिवासं
तातारब्धोऽभिषेकस्तव किल विहत: केकयाधीशपुत्र्या ॥४॥
ArundhAne ruShAndhe bhRgukula tilake saMkramayya svatejo
yAte yAtosyayodhyAM sukhamiha nivasan kAntayA kAntamUrte 
shatrughnenaikadAtho gatavati bharate mAtulasyAdhivAsaM
tAtArabdhobhiShekastava kila vihataH kekayAdhIshaputryA 4

आरुन्धाने रुषान्धे भृगुकुल तिलके संक्रमय्य स्वतेजो
As you were obstructed by the best of the bhRgus, parashurAma who was blinded by rage, and later as he bestowed all his radiance to you
याते यातोऽस्ययोध्यां सुखमिह निवसन् कान्तया कान्तमूर्ते ।
and left, you returned to ayodhyA and lived happily with your wife, O Resplendent One!
शत्रुघ्नेनैकदाथो गतवति भरते मातुलस्याधिवासं
One day as Shatrughna went along with Bharata to his uncle's kingdom,
तातारब्धोऽभिषेकस्तव किल विहत: केकयाधीशपुत्र्या ॥४॥
as your father started the process of your coronation, was it not stopped then by the daughter of the King of Kekaya!

तातोक्त्या यातुकामो वनमनुजवधूसंयुतश्चापधार:
पौरानारुध्य मार्गे गुहनिलयगतस्त्वं जटाचीरधारी।
नावा सन्तीर्य गङ्गामधिपदवि पुनस्तं भरद्वाजमारात्
नत्वा तद्वाक्यहेतोरतिसुखमवसश्चित्रकूटे गिरीन्द्रे ॥५॥
tAtoktyA yAtukAmo vanamanujavadhUsaMyutashchApadhAraH
paurAnArudhya mArge guhanilayagatastvaM jaTAchIradhArI
nAvA santIrya gangAmadhipadavi punastaM bharadvAjamArAt
natvA tadvAkyahetoratisukhamavasashchitrakUTe girIndre 5

तातोक्त्या यातुकामो वनमनुजवधूसंयुतश्चापधार:
Upon father's words, willing to go to the forest accompanied by your younger brother, and wife, carrying a bow,
पौरानारुध्य मार्गे गुहनिलयगतस्त्वं जटाचीरधारी।
stopping the citizens from following you along the way, clad in bark cloth and wearing matted hair, you went to Guha's place!
नावा सन्तीर्य गङ्गामधिपदवि पुनस्तं भरद्वाजमारात्
Crossing the Ganga by boat, visitng Bharadwaja who resided near by, 
नत्वा तद्वाक्यहेतोरतिसुखमवसश्चित्रकूटे गिरीन्द्रे ॥५॥
bowing down to him, and upon instruction, resided very happily in the moutains of Chitrakuta!

श्रुत्वा पुत्रार्तिखिन्नं खलु भरतमुखात् स्वर्गयातं स्वतातं
तप्तो दत्वाऽम्बु तस्मै निदधिथ भरते पादुकां मेदिनीं च
अत्रिं नत्वाऽथ गत्वा वनमतिविपुलं दण्डकं चण्डकायं
हत्वा दैत्यं विराधं सुगतिमकलयश्चारु भो: शारभङ्गीम् ॥६॥
shrutvA putrArtikhinnaM khalu bharatamukhAt svargayAtaM svatAtaM
tapto datvAmbu tasmai nidadhitha bharate pAdukAM medinIM cha
atriM natvAtha gatvA vanamativipulaM daNDakaM chaNDakAyaM
hatvA daityaM virAdhaM sugatimakalayashchAru bhoH shArabhangIm 6

श्रुत्वा पुत्रार्तिखिन्नं खलु भरतमुखात् स्वर्गयातं स्वतातं
Upon hearing from Bharata of the passage of your father, struck by the grief of his son's separation, to heavenly abodes,
तप्तो दत्वाऽम्बु तस्मै निदधिथ भरते पादुकां मेदिनीं च
in grief, performing his rites, you conferred to Bharata your sandals and the kingdom!
अत्रिं नत्वाऽथ गत्वा वनमतिविपुलं दण्डकं चण्डकायं
Paying homage to sage Atri, going to the dense Dandaka forest, and the ferocious bodied
हत्वा दैत्यं विराधं सुगतिमकलयश्चारु भो: शारभङ्गीम् ॥६॥
asura Viradha, slaying him there, you bestowed liberation beautifully to Sage Shaarabhangi!

नत्वाऽगस्त्यं समस्ताशरनिकरसपत्राकृतिं तापसेभ्य:
प्रत्यश्रौषी: प्रियैषी तदनु च मुनिना वैष्णवे दिव्यचापे ।
ब्रह्मास्त्रे चापि दत्ते पथि पितृसुहृदं वीक्ष्य भूयो जटायुं
मोदात् गोदातटान्ते परिरमसि पुरा पञ्चवट्यां वधूट्या ॥७॥
natvAgastyaM samastAsharanikarasapatrAkRtiM tApasebhyaH
pratyashrauShIH priyaiShI tadanu cha muninA vaiShNave divyachApe 
brahmAstre chApi datte pathi pitRsuhRdaM vIkShya bhUyo jaTAyuM
modAt godAtaTAnte pariramasi purA panchavaTyAM vadhUTyA 7

नत्वाऽगस्त्यं समस्ताशरनिकरसपत्राकृतिं तापसेभ्य:
Offering your prayers to Agastya, the destruction of all the Asuras who were troubling the region, to the sages
प्रत्यश्रौषी: प्रियैषी तदनु च मुनिना वैष्णवे दिव्यचापे ।
promising this, you, their well wisher, then as the sage bestowed the divine bow of Vishnu, 
ब्रह्मास्त्रे चापि दत्ते पथि पितृसुहृदं वीक्ष्य भूयो जटायुं
and Brahmastra, receiving them, along the way visiting your father's dear friend Jatayu,
मोदात् गोदातटान्ते परिरमसि पुरा पञ्चवट्यां वधूट्या ॥७॥
in joy, you lived at the banks of river Godavari in Panchavati along with your wife!

प्राप्ताया: शूर्पणख्या मदनचलधृतेरर्थनैर्निस्सहात्मा
तां सौमित्रौ विसृज्य प्रबलतमरुषा तेन निर्लूननासाम् ।
दृष्ट्वैनां रुष्टचित्तं खरमभिपतितं दूषणं च त्रिमूर्धं
व्याहिंसीराशरानप्ययुतसमधिकांस्तत्क्षणादक्षतोष्मा ॥८॥
prAptAyAH shUrpaNakhyA madanachaladhRterarthanairnissahAtmA
tAM saumitrau visRjya prabalatamaruShA tena nirlUnanAsAm 
dRShTvainAM ruShTachittaM kharamabhipatitaM dUShaNaM cha trimUrdhaM
vyAhiMsIrAsharAnapyayutasamadhikAMstatkShaNAdakShatoShmA 8

प्राप्ताया: शूर्पणख्या मदनचलधृतेरर्थनैर्निस्सहात्मा
Approached by Shurpanakha who was overcome by the force of passion, unable to bear her advances,
तां सौमित्रौ विसृज्य प्रबलतमरुषा तेन निर्लूननासाम् ।
diverting her to Lakshmana, who in great anger cut off her nose,
दृष्ट्वैनां रुष्टचित्तं खरमभिपतितं दूषणं च त्रिमूर्धं
angered looking at her, as Khara attacked, and Dushana and Trishiras,
व्याहिंसीराशरानप्ययुतसमधिकांस्तत्क्षणादक्षतोष्मा ॥८॥
you vanquished these asuras and also ten thousand more at that very instance, O One of Undiminishable Energy!

सोदर्याप्रोक्तवार्ताविवशदशमुखादिष्टमारीचमाया-
सारङ्गम् सारसाक्ष्या स्पृहितमनुगत: प्रावधीर्बाणघातम् ।
तन्मायाक्रन्दनिर्यापितभवदनुजां रावणस्तामहार्षीत्
तेनार्तोऽपि त्वमन्त: किमपि मुदमधास्तद्वधोपायलाभात् ॥९॥
sodaryAproktavArtAvivashadashamukhAdiShTamArIchamAyA-
sArangam sArasAkShyA spRhitamanugataH prAvadhIrbANaghAtam 
tanmAyAkrandaniryApitabhavadanujAM rAvaNastAmahArShIt
tenArtopi tvamantaH kimapi mudamadhAstadvadhopAyalAbhAt 9

सोदर्याप्रोक्तवार्ताविवशदशमुखादिष्टमारीचमाया-
Hearing this news from his sister, as the enraged Ravana ordered Maricha to take on an illusory
सारङ्गम् सारसाक्ष्या स्पृहितमनुगत: प्रावधीर्बाणघातम् ।
form of a dear, as Devi Sita desired it, and you followed him, slaying him with an arrow,
तन्मायाक्रन्दनिर्यापितभवदनुजां रावणस्तामहार्षीत्
as she heard his cry, mistaking it for yours, and sent your brother after you, Ravana abducted her!
तेनार्तोऽपि त्वमन्त: किमपि मुदमधास्तद्वधोपायलाभात् ॥९॥
Despite being dejected by this, you were somewhat pleased, now finding the right reason to kill him!

भूयस्तन्वीं विचिन्वन्नहृत दशमुखस्त्वद्वधूं मद्वधेने-
त्युक्त्वा याते जटायौ दिवमथ सुहृद: प्रातनो: प्रेतकार्यम् ।
गृह्णानं तं कबन्धं जघनिथ शबरीं प्रेक्ष्य पम्पातटे त्वं
सम्प्राप्तो वातसूनुं भृशमुदितमना: पाहि वातालयेश ॥१०॥
bhUyastanvIM vichinvannahRta dashamukhastvadvadhUM madvadhene-
tyuktvA yAte jaTAyau divamatha suhRdaH prAtanoH pretakAryam 
gRhNAnaM taM kabandhaM jaghanitha shabarIM prekShya pampAtaTe tvaM
samprApto vAtasUnuM bhRshamuditamanAH pAhi vAtAlayesha 10

भूयस्तन्वीं विचिन्वन्नहृत दशमुखस्त्वद्वधूं मद्वधेने-
And then, searching for your wife, "The Ten-Headed Ravana abducted her by killing me!"
त्युक्त्वा याते जटायौ दिवमथ सुहृद: प्रातनो: प्रेतकार्यम् ।
saying thus, as Jatayu left for the heavens, you as a friend performed his funeral rites!
गृह्णानं तं कबन्धं जघनिथ शबरीं प्रेक्ष्य पम्पातटे त्वं
By slaying Kabandha who caught hold of you, seeing to Shabari's salvation, and at the banks of River Pampa,
सम्प्राप्तो वातसूनुं भृशमुदितमना: पाहि वातालयेश ॥१०॥
meeting the Son of the Wind, Hanuman, the one who was highly pleased, protect me, O Ruler of the Abode of Wind!
