अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अशीतितमम् दशकम्
ashItitamam dashakam

The Eightieth group of Ten shlokas

स्यमन्तक उपाख्यानम्
Syamantaka Upakhyanam

The Story of Syamantaka

सत्राजितस्त्वमथ लुब्धवदर्कलब्धं
दिव्यं स्यमन्तकमणिं भगवन्नयाची: ।
तत्कारणं बहुविधं मम भाति नूनं
तस्यात्मजां त्वयि रतां छलतो विवोढुम् ॥१॥
satrAjitastvamatha lubdhavadarkalabdhaM
divyaM syamantakamaNiM bhagavannayAchIH 
tatkAraNaM bahuvidhaM mama bhAti nUnaM
tasyAtmajAM tvayi ratAM Chalato vivoDhum 1

सत्राजितस्त्वमथ लुब्धवदर्कलब्धं
Then once, from Satrajit, who was like a miser who obtained the very Sun,
दिव्यं स्यमन्तकमणिं भगवन्नयाची: ।
you asked for the divine jewel Syamantaka Mani.
तत्कारणं बहुविधं मम भाति नूनं
I think there might have been many reasons for this!
तस्यात्मजां त्वयि रतां छलतो विवोढुम् ॥१॥
May be one of them was a strategy to marry his daughter, Satyabhama who was in love with you!

अदत्तं तं तुभ्यं मणिवरमनेनाल्पमनसा
प्रसेनस्तद्भ्राता गलभुवि वहन् प्राप मृगयाम् ।
अहन्नेनं सिंहो मणिमहसि मांसभ्रमवशात्
कपीन्द्रस्तं हत्वा मणिमपि च बालाय ददिवान् ॥२॥
adattaM taM tubhyaM maNivaramanenAlpamanasA
prasenastadbhrAtA galabhuvi vahan prApa mRgayAm 
ahannenaM siMho maNimahasi mAMsabhramavashAt
kapIndrastaM hatvA maNimapi cha bAlAya dadivAn 2

अदत्तं तं तुभ्यं मणिवरमनेनाल्पमनसा
As that lowly minded one, did not give the jewel to you,
प्रसेनस्तद्भ्राता गलभुवि वहन् प्राप मृगयाम् ।
his brother Prasena, once, wore it around his neck and went hunting!
अहन्नेनं सिंहो मणिमहसि मांसभ्रमवशात्
A lion killed him thinking that jewel was a huge lump of flesh,
कपीन्द्रस्तं हत्वा मणिमपि च बालाय ददिवान् ॥२॥
and then, Jambavan looking at that jewel, killed the lion and gave it to his daughter! 

शशंसु: सत्राजिद्गिरमनु जनास्त्वां मणिहरं
जनानां पीयूषं भवति गुणिनां दोषकणिका ।
तत: सर्वज्ञोऽपि स्वजनसहितो मार्गणपरो
प्रसेनं तं दृष्ट्वा हरिमपि गतोऽभू: कपिगुहाम् ॥३॥
shashaMsuH satrAjidgiramanu janAstvAM maNiharaM
janAnAM pIyUShaM bhavati guNinAM doShakaNikA 
tataH sarvajnopi svajanasahito mArgaNaparo
prasenaM taM dRShTvA harimapi gatobhUH kapiguhAm 3

शशंसु: सत्राजिद्गिरमनु जनास्त्वां मणिहरं
People then, upon Satrajit's words started suspecting you to be the jewel thief!
जनानां पीयूषं भवति गुणिनां दोषकणिका ।
Even a droplet of fault of the virtuous is like nectar for people to relish!
तत: सर्वज्ञोऽपि स्वजनसहितो मार्गणपर:
Therefore despite knowing everything, you along with your people went in search of that jewel!
प्रसेनं तं दृष्ट्वा हरिमपि गतोऽभू: कपिगुहाम् ॥३॥
Looking at the dead Prasena, and the Lion, you then went to Jambavan's cave!

भवन्तमवितर्कयन्नतिवया: स्वयं जाम्बवान्
मुकुन्दशरणं हि मां क इह रोद्धुमित्यालपन् ।
विभो रघुपते हरे जय जयेत्यलं मुष्टिभि:
चिरं तव समर्चनं व्यधित भक्तचूडामणि: ॥४॥
bhavantamavitarkayannativayAH svayaM jAmbavAn
mukundasharaNaM hi mAM ka iha roddhumityAlapan 
vibho raghupate hare jaya jayetyalaM muShTibhiH
chiraM tava samarchanaM vyadhita bhaktachUDAmaNiH 4

भवन्तमवितर्कयन्नतिवया: स्वयं जाम्बवान्
Jambavan due to his old age, unable to identify you,
मुकुन्दशरणं हि मां क इह रोद्धुमित्यालपन् ।
"Lord Rama is my sole protector! Who is this who dare oppose me!", thus speaking, later,
विभो रघुपते हरे जय जयेत्यलं मुष्टिभि:
"O Vibhu! O Lord of Raghus! O Hari! Hail! Enough with the blows of your fist!" thus
चिरं तव समर्चनं व्यधित भक्तचूडामणि: ॥४॥
that jewel among devotees worshipped you for long!

बुध्वाऽथ तेन दत्तां नवरमणीं वरमणिं च परिगृह्णन् ।
अनुगृह्णन्नमुमागा: सपदि च सत्राजिते मणिं प्रादा: ॥५॥
budhvAtha tena dattAM navaramaNIM varamaNiM cha parigRhNan 
anugRhNannamumAgAH sapadi cha satrAjite maNiM prAdAH 5

बुध्वाऽथ तेन दत्तां नवरमणीं वरमणिं च परिगृह्णन् ।
As he recognized you, then receiving the jewel and his daughter in marriage,
अनुगृह्णन्नमुमागा: सपदि च सत्राजिते मणिं प्रादा: ॥५॥
blessing him, you returned at once and gave the jewel back to Satrajit!

तदनु स खलु व्रीलालोलो विलोलविलोचनां
दुहितरमहो धीमान् भामां गिरैव परार्पिताम् ।
अदित मणिना तुभ्यं लभ्यं समेत्य भवानपि
प्रमुदितमनास्तस्यैवादान्मणिं गहनाशय: ॥६॥
tadanu sa khalu vrIlAlolo vilolavilochanAM
duhitaramaho dhImAn bhAmAM giraiva parArpitAm 
adita maNinA tubhyaM labhyaM sametya bhavAnapi
pramuditamanAstasyaivAdAnmaNiM gahanAshayaH 6

तदनु स खलु व्रीलालोलो विलोलविलोचनां
Didn't he then, overcome with shame, the tremulous-eyed
दुहितरमहो धीमान् भामां गिरैव परार्पिताम् ।
daughter of his, Satyabhama, who was promised to another by word,
अदित मणिना तुभ्यं लभ्यं समेत्य भवानपि
along with the jewel, he offered her to you! With everything that you had received, you too
प्रमुदितमनास्तस्यैवादान्मणिं गहनाशय: ॥६॥
with a joyous mind, presented the jewel to her, with a deeper purpose!

व्रीलाकुलां रमयति त्वयि सत्यभामां
कौन्तेयदाहकथयाथ कुरून् प्रयाते ।
ही गान्दिनेयकृतवर्मगिरा निपात्य
सत्राजितं शतधनुर्मणिमाजहार ॥७॥
vrIlAkulAM ramayati tvayi satyabhAmAM
kaunteyadAhakathayAtha kurUn prayAte 
hI gAndineyakRtavarmagirA nipAtya
satrAjitaM shatadhanurmaNimAjahAra 7

व्रीलाकुलां रमयति त्वयि सत्यभामां
As you were rejoicing with the shy Satyabhama,
कौन्तेयदाहकथयाथ कुरून् प्रयाते ।
upon hearing the news of the Sons of Kunti burning, as you set out to the land of Kurus,
ही गान्दिनेयकृतवर्मगिरा निपात्य
How spiteful that upon the words of Akrura and Kritavarma, killing
सत्राजितं शतधनुर्मणिमाजहार ॥७॥
Satrajit, Shatadhanu stole the jewel!

शोकात् कुरूनुपगतामवलोक्य कान्तां
हत्वा द्रुतं शतधनुं समहर्षयस्ताम् ।
रत्ने सशङ्क इव मैथिलगेहमेत्य
रामो गदां समशिशिक्षत धार्तराष्ट्रम् ॥८॥
shokAt kurUnupagatAmavalokya kAntAM
hatvA drutaM shatadhanuM samaharShayastAm 
ratne sashanka iva maithilagehametya
rAmo gadAM samashishikShata dhArtarAShTram 8

शोकात् कुरूनुपगतामवलोक्य कान्तां
Stricken with grief, as your wife arrived at the land of Kurus, seeing her,
हत्वा द्रुतं शतधनुं समहर्षयस्ताम् ।
slaying Shatadhanu at once, you gladdened her!
रत्ने सशङ्क इव मैथिलगेहमेत्य
As if suspecting the jewel's whereabouts, going to the abode of Mithila,
रामो गदां समशिशिक्षत धार्तराष्ट्रम् ॥८॥
Balarama trained the Son of Duryodhana well in the art of the mace!

अक्रूर एष भगवन् भवदिच्छयैव
सत्राजित: कुचरितस्य युयोज हिंसाम् ।
अक्रूरतो मणिमनाहृतवान् पुनस्त्वं
तस्यैव भूतिमुपधातुमिति ब्रुवन्ति ॥९॥
akrUra eSha bhagavan bhavadichChayaiva
satrAjitaH kucharitasya yuyoja hiMsAm 
akrUrato maNimanAhRtavAn punastvaM
tasyaiva bhUtimupadhAtumiti bruvanti 9

अक्रूर एष भगवन् भवदिच्छयैव
This Akrura, O Lord, by your own will,
सत्राजित: कुचरितस्य युयोज हिंसाम् ।
brought about the death of the evil Satrajit!
अक्रूरतो मणिमनाहृतवान् पुनस्त्वं
You then, did not take the jewel back from Akrura
तस्यैव भूतिमुपधातुमिति ब्रुवन्ति ॥९॥
so that he too may become prosperous, people say!

भक्तस्त्वयि स्थिरतर: स हि गान्दिनेय:	
तस्यैव कापथमति: कथमीश जाता ।
विज्ञानवान् प्रशमवानहमित्युदीर्णं
गर्वं ध्रुवं शमयितुं भवता कृतैव ॥१०॥
bhaktastvayi sthirataraH sa hi gAndineyaH	
tasyaiva kApathamatiH kathamIsha jAtA 
vijnAnavAn prashamavAnahamityudIrNaM
garvaM dhruvaM shamayituM bhavatA kRtaiva 10

भक्तस्त्वयि स्थिरतर: स हि गान्दिनेय:
That son of gAndinI who is firmly devoted to you
तस्यैव कापथमति: कथमीश जाता ।
how did his own mind turn wicked, O Lord!
विज्ञानवान् प्रशमवानहमित्युदीर्णं
"I am knowledgeable and I am in control!", thus as he thought highly of himself
गर्वं ध्रुवं शमयितुं भवता कृतैव ॥१०॥
in great pride, may be to pacify it, this had been done by you alone!

यातं भयेन कृतवर्मयुतं पुनस्तम्
आहूय तद्विनिहितं च मणिं प्रकाश्य ।
तत्रैव सुव्रतधरे विनिधाय तुष्यन्
भामाकुचान्तशयन: पवनेश पाया: ॥११॥
yAtaM bhayena kRtavarmayutaM punastam
AhUya tadvinihitaM cha maNiM prakAshya 
tatraiva suvratadhare vinidhAya tuShyan
bhAmAkuchAntashayanaH pavanesha pAyAH 11

यातं भयेन कृतवर्मयुतं पुनस्तम्
As he fled along with Kritavarma, once again,
आहूय तद्विनिहितं च मणिं प्रकाश्य ।
inviting him back, bringing out the jewel hidden by him,
तत्रैव सुव्रतधरे विनिधाय तुष्यन्
there itself, bestowing it back as he mended his ways, satisfied,
भामाकुचान्तशयन: पवनेश पाया: ॥११॥
O Lord who reclined on the bosom of Satyabhama, O Lord of Wind! May you protect me!
