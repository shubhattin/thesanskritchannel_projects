ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

त्रिचत्वारिंशम् दशकम्
trichatvAriMsham dashakam

The Forty-third group of Ten shlokas

तृणावर्त वध:
tRNAvarta vadhaH

The slaying of Trinavarta

त्वामेकदा गुरुमरुत्पुरनाथ वोढुं
गाढाधिरूढगरिमाणमपारयन्ती ।
माता निधाय शयने किमिदं बतेति
ध्यायन्त्यचेष्टत गृहेषु निविष्टशङ्का ॥१॥
tvAmekadA gurumarutpuranAtha voDhuM
gADhAdhirUDhagarimANamapArayantI 
mAtA nidhAya shayane kimidaM bateti
dhyAyantyacheShTata gRheShu niviShTashankA 1

त्वामेकदा गुरुमरुत्पुरनाथ वोढुं
O Lord of the City of Guruvayur, to carry you,
गाढाधिरूढगरिमाणमपारयन्ती ।
who once grew to an enormous weight, unable to hold you up,
माता निधाय शयने किमिदं बतेति
your mother placed you on a cot, wondering how it could be so,
ध्यायन्त्यचेष्टत गृहेषु निविष्टशङ्का ॥१॥
and thinking thus, she went back to her chores in the house, worrying about you!

तावद्विदूरमुपकर्णितघोरघोष-
व्याजृम्भिपांसुपटलीपरिपूरिताश: ।
वात्यावपुस्स किल दैत्यवरस्तृणाव-
र्ताख्यो जहार जनमानसहारिणं त्वाम् ॥२॥
tAvadvidUramupakarNitaghoraghoSha-
vyAjRmbhipAMsupaTalIparipUritAshaH 
vAtyAvapussa kila daityavarastRNAva-
rtAkhyo jahAra janamAnasahAriNaM tvAm 2

तावद्विदूरमुपकर्णितघोरघोष-
Then, suddenly with a terrible sound that was heard from afar,
व्याजृम्भिपांसुपटलीपरिपूरिताश: ।
filling all the directions in a thicket of dust,
वात्यावपुस्स किल दैत्यवरस्तृणाव-
in the form of a whirl wind, didn't that great asura
र्ताख्यो जहार जनमानसहारिणं त्वाम् ॥२॥
by the name of tRNAvarta, steal away you, who steals the hearts of people!

उद्दामपांसुतिमिराहतदृष्टिपाते
द्रष्टुं किमप्यकुशले पशुपाललोके ।
हा बालकस्य किमिति त्वदुपान्तमाप्ता
माता भवन्तमविलोक्य भृशं रुरोद ॥३॥
uddAmapAMsutimirAhatadRShTipAte
draShTuM kimapyakushale pashupAlaloke 
hA bAlakasya kimiti tvadupAntamAptA
mAtA bhavantamavilokya bhRshaM ruroda 3

उद्दामपांसुतिमिराहतदृष्टिपाते
As one's sight was completely blinded by the darkness due to the raging dust all around,
द्रष्टुं किमप्यकुशले पशुपाललोके ।
when nothing was able to be seen properly in that land of the cowherds,
हा बालकस्य किमिति त्वदुपान्तमाप्ता
"O What happened to the child!" thus approaching you,
माता भवन्तमविलोक्य भृशं रुरोद ॥३॥
and unable to see you there, your mother wailed profusely!

तावत् स दानववरोऽपि च दीनमूर्ति:
भावत्कभारपरिधारणलूनवेग: ।
सङ्कोचमाप तदनु क्षतपांसुघोषे
घोषे व्यतायत भवज्जननीनिनाद: ॥४॥
tAvat sa dAnavavaropi cha dInamUrtiH
bhAvatkabhAraparidhAraNalUnavegaH 
sankochamApa tadanu kShatapAMsughoShe
ghoShe vyatAyata bhavajjananIninAdaH 4

तावत् स दानववरोऽपि च दीनमूर्ति:
Within that time, that great asura too, immensely tired,
भावत्कभारपरिधारणलूनवेग: ।
with his speed diminished under the heaviness of your weight,
सङ्कोचमाप तदनु क्षतपांसुघोषे
shrunk in size, and as the din of dust subsided,
घोषे व्यतायत भवज्जननीनिनाद: ॥४॥
the cries of your mother was now heard all across!

रोदोपकर्णनवशादुपगम्य गेहं
क्रन्दत्सु नन्दमुखगोपकुलेषु दीन: ।
त्वां दानवस्त्वखिलमुक्तिकरं मुमुक्षु:
त्वय्यप्रमुञ्चति पपात वियत्प्रदेशात् ॥५॥
rodopakarNanavashAdupagamya gehaM
krandatsu nandamukhagopakuleShu dInaH 
tvAM dAnavastvakhilamuktikaraM mumukShuH
tvayyapramunchati papAta viyatpradeshAt 5

रोदोपकर्णनवशादुपगम्य गेहं
Hearing the wails of Yashoda, coming back to the house,
क्रन्दत्सु नन्दमुखगोपकुलेषु दीन: ।
as Nanda and the people of Vraja started screaming, the poor
त्वां दानवस्त्वखिलमुक्तिकरं मुमुक्षु:
asura, now wanting to set you free, you who are the one who sets people free,
त्वय्यप्रमुञ्चति पपात वियत्प्रदेशात् ॥५॥
as you did not let go of him, fell to the ground from the skies!

रोदाकुलास्तदनु गोपगणा बहिष्ठ-
पाषाणपृष्ठभुवि देहमतिस्थविष्ठम् ।
प्रैक्षन्त हन्त निपतन्तममुष्य वक्ष-
स्यक्षीणमेव च भवन्तमलं हसन्तम् ॥६॥
rodAkulAstadanu gopagaNA bahiShTha-
pAShANapRShThabhuvi dehamatisthaviShTham 
praikShanta hanta nipatantamamuShya vakSha-
syakShINameva cha bhavantamalaM hasantam 6

रोदाकुलास्तदनु गोपगणा बहिष्ठ-
Exhausted with their crying, the Gopas then, outside
पाषाणपृष्ठभुवि देहमतिस्थविष्ठम् ।
on the stone ground, the terribly huge body
प्रैक्षन्त हन्त निपतन्तममुष्य वक्ष-
which fell, looking at it, in wonder saw on its chest
स्यक्षीणमेव च भवन्तमलं हसन्तम् ॥६॥
you who were smiling playfully, unscathed!

ग्रावप्रपातपरिपिष्टगरिष्ठदेह-
भ्रष्टासुदुष्टदनुजोपरि धृष्टहासम् ।
आघ्नानमम्बुजकरेण भवन्तमेत्य
गोपा दधुर्गिरिवरादिव नीलरत्नम् ॥७॥
grAvaprapAtaparipiShTagariShThadeha-
bhraShTAsuduShTadanujopari dhRShTahAsam 
AghnAnamambujakareNa bhavantametya
gopA dadhurgirivarAdiva nIlaratnam 7

ग्रावप्रपातपरिपिष्टगरिष्ठदेह-
As that huge body was crushed falling on to the stone,
भ्रष्टासुदुष्टदनुजोपरि धृष्टहासम् ।
as you smiled on that wicked asura who was destroyed,
आघ्नानमम्बुजकरेण भवन्तमेत्य
swinging your lotus-like hands at his body, approaching you,
गोपा दधुर्गिरिवरादिव नीलरत्नम् ॥७॥
the gopas picked you up like picking up a small sapphire from a huge mountain!

एकैकमाशु परिगृह्य निकामनन्दन्
नन्दादिगोपपरिरब्धविचुम्बिताङ्गम् ।
आदातुकामपरिशङ्कितगोपनारी-
हस्ताम्बुजप्रपतितं प्रणुमो भवन्तम् ॥८॥
ekaikamAshu parigRhya nikAmanandan
nandAdigopaparirabdhavichumbitAngam 
AdAtukAmaparishankitagopanArI-
hastAmbujaprapatitaM praNumo bhavantam 8

एकैकमाशु परिगृह्य निकामनन्दन्
One by one, as all of them holding you in great joy,
नन्दादिगोपपरिरब्धविचुम्बिताङ्गम् ।
Nanda and other Gopas, embraced you and kissed your limbs,
आदातुकामपरिशङ्कितगोपनारी-
as the Gopikas wished to take you away, but stood hesitant,
हस्ताम्बुजप्रपतितं प्रणुमो भवन्तम् ॥८॥
you who fell into their lotus-like hands, we bow down to you!

भूयोऽपि किन्नु कृणुम: प्रणतार्तिहारी
गोविन्द एव परिपालयतात् सुतं न: ।
इत्यादि मातरपितृप्रमुखैस्तदानीं
सम्प्रार्थितस्त्वदवनाय विभो त्वमेव ॥९॥
bhUyopi kinnu kRNumaH praNatArtihArI
govinda eva paripAlayatAt sutaM naH 
ityAdi mAtarapitRpramukhaistadAnIM
samprArthitastvadavanAya vibho tvameva 9

भूयोऽपि किन्नु कृणुम: प्रणतार्तिहारी
"O What are we to do again and again! The destroyer of devotee's troubles,
गोविन्द एव परिपालयतात् सुतं न: ।
Govinda alone may protect our child!"
इत्यादि मातरपितृप्रमुखैस्तदानीं
with words like these, the mother, father and other elders, then
सम्प्रार्थितस्त्वदवनाय विभो त्वमेव ॥९॥
prayed for your wellbeing to you yourself!

वातात्मकं दनुजमेवमयि प्रधून्वन्
वातोद्भवान् मम गदान् किमु नो धुनोषि ।
किं वा करोमि पुनरप्यनिलालयेश
निश्शेषरोगशमनं मुहुरर्थये त्वाम् ॥१०॥
vAtAtmakaM danujamevamayi pradhUnvan
vAtodbhavAn mama gadAn kimu no dhunoShi 
kiM vA karomi punarapyanilAlayesha
nishsheSharogashamanaM muhurarthaye tvAm 10

वातात्मकं दनुजमेवमयि प्रधून्वन्
Thus slaying that asura of the nature of wind,
वातोद्भवान् मम गदान् किमु नो धुनोषि ।
why wouldn't you destroy my ailments born out of an imbalance of the air element!
किं वा करोमि पुनरप्यनिलालयेश
Once again, What else would I do, O Lord of the Abode of Wind!
निश्शेषरोगशमनं मुहुरर्थये त्वाम् ॥१०॥
I pray to you once more to remove all my ailments!
