अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

द्विसप्ततितमम् दशकम्
dvisaptatitamam dashakam

The Seventy-second group of Ten shlokas

अक्रूर आगमनम्
akrUra Agamanam

Akrura's Arrival

कंसोऽथ नारदगिरा व्रजवासिनं त्वाम्
आकर्ण्य दीर्णहृदय: स हि गान्दिनेयम् ।
आहूय कार्मुकमखच्छलतो भवन्तम्
आनेतुमेनमहिनोदहिनाथशायिन् ॥१॥
kaMsotha nAradagirA vrajavAsinaM tvAm
AkarNya dIrNahRdayaH sa hi gAndineyam 
AhUya kArmukamakhachChalato bhavantam
AnetumenamahinodahinAthashAyin 1

कंसोऽथ नारदगिरा व्रजवासिनं त्वाम्
Then, Kamsa from the words of Sage Narada, that you reside in Vraja,
आकर्ण्य दीर्णहृदय: स हि गान्दिनेयम् ।
upon hearing it, with fear in his heart, akrUra(the son of gAndinI)
आहूय कार्मुकमखच्छलतो भवन्तम्
he summoned, and with a cunning pretext of a festival of yajnas, 
आनेतुमेनमहिनोदहिनाथशायिन् ॥१॥
sent him to bring you, O One who Reclines on the King of Serpents!

अक्रूर एष भवदंघ्रिपरश्चिराय
त्वद्दर्शनाक्षममना: क्षितिपालभीत्या ।
तस्याज्ञयैव पुनरीक्षितुमुद्यतस्त्वाम्
आनन्दभारमतिभूरितरं बभार ॥२॥
akrUra eSha bhavadaMghriparashchirAya
tvaddarshanAkShamamanAH kShitipAlabhItyA 
tasyAjnayaiva punarIkShitumudyatastvAm
AnandabhAramatibhUritaraM babhAra 2

अक्रूर एष भवदंघ्रिपरश्चिराय
This akrUra, who has been a devotee of your feet for long,
त्वद्दर्शनाक्षममना: क्षितिपालभीत्या ।
who always thought himself incapable of your sight out of his fear of the King,
तस्याज्ञयैव पुनरीक्षितुमुद्यतस्त्वाम्
through his order itself, getting an opportunity to see you,
आनन्दभारमतिभूरितरं बभार ॥२॥
was overcome under the weight of immense joy!

सोऽयं रथेन सुकृती भवतो निवासं
गच्छन् मनोरथगणांस्त्वयि धार्यमाणान् ।
आस्वादयन् मुहुरपायभयेन दैवं
सम्प्रार्थयन् पथि न किञ्चिदपि व्यजानात् ॥३॥
soyaM rathena sukRtI bhavato nivAsaM
gachChan manorathagaNAMstvayi dhAryamANAn 
AsvAdayan muhurapAyabhayena daivaM
samprArthayan pathi na kinchidapi vyajAnAt 3

सोऽयं रथेन सुकृती भवतो निवासं
He, setting out on a chariot towards your residence,
गच्छन् मनोरथगणांस्त्वयि धार्यमाणान् ।
holding all his thoughts and desires on you alone
आस्वादयन् मुहुरपायभयेन दैवं
and relishing them, but then again, afraid of facing any calamities,
सम्प्रार्थयन् पथि न किञ्चिदपि व्यजानात् ॥३॥
kept praying to God and observed nothing else along the way!

द्रक्ष्यामि वेदशतगीतगतिं पुमांसं
स्प्रक्ष्यामि किंस्विदपि नाम परिष्वजेयम् ।
किं वक्ष्यते स खलु मां क्वनु वीक्षित: स्यात्
इत्थं निनाय स भवन्मयमेव मार्गम् ॥४॥
drakShyAmi vedashatagItagatiM pumAMsaM
sprakShyAmi kiMsvidapi nAma pariShvajeyam 
kiM vakShyate sa khalu mAM kvanu vIkShitaH syAt
itthaM ninAya sa bhavanmayameva mArgam 4

द्रक्ष्यामि वेदशतगीतगतिं पुमांसं
"I shall see the one who is sung about in the hundreds of hymns of the Vedas!
स्प्रक्ष्यामि किंस्विदपि नाम परिष्वजेयम् ।
I shall at least touch him, even if I don't get an opportunity for a hug!
किं वक्ष्यते स खलु मां क्वनु वीक्षित: स्यात्
What would he say to me! Where would I find him!"
इत्थं निनाय स भवन्मयमेव मार्गम् ॥४॥
thus he was immersed in thoughts of you alone as he made the journey!

भूय: क्रमादभिविशन् भवदंघ्रिपूतं
वृन्दावनं हरविरिञ्चसुराभिवन्द्यम् ।
आनन्दमग्न इव लग्न इव प्रमोहे
किं किं दशान्तरमवाप न पङ्कजाक्ष ॥५॥
bhUyaH kramAdabhivishan bhavadaMghripUtaM
vRndAvanaM haravirinchasurAbhivandyam 
Anandamagna iva lagna iva pramohe
kiM kiM dashAntaramavApa na pankajAkSha 5

भूय: क्रमादभिविशन् भवदंघ्रिपूतं
Then, gradually entering the land that is purified by your feet,
वृन्दावनं हरविरिञ्चसुराभिवन्द्यम् ।
the holy Vrindavana, which is worshipped by the very likes of Shiva, Brahma and others,
आनन्दमग्न इव लग्न इव प्रमोहे
as if drowned in bliss, he became ecstatic!
किं किं दशान्तरमवाप न पङ्कजाक्ष ॥५॥
What kind of blissful states did he then not attain to, O Lotus-eyed One!

पश्यन्नवन्दत भवद्विहृतिस्थलानि
पांसुष्ववेष्टत भवच्चरणाङ्कितेषु ।
किं ब्रूमहे बहुजना हि तदापि जाता
एवं तु भक्तितरला विरला: परात्मन् ॥६॥
pashyannavandata bhavadvihRtisthalAni
pAMsuShvaveShTata bhavachcharaNAnkiteShu 
kiM brUmahe bahujanA hi tadApi jAtA
evaM tu bhaktitaralA viralAH parAtman 6

पश्यन्नवन्दत भवद्विहृतिस्थलानि
Seeing the places that you roam in, he bowed down to them!
पांसुष्ववेष्टत भवच्चरणाङ्कितेषु ।
He revelled in the dust that was in contact with your feet!
किं ब्रूमहे बहुजना हि तदापि जाता
What can we say, even then many people were born,
एवं तु भक्तितरला विरला: परात्मन् ॥६॥
but the one's immersed in devotion were still scarce, O Supreme One!

सायं स गोपभवनानि भवच्चरित्र-
गीतामृतप्रसृतकर्णरसायनानि ।
पश्यन् प्रमोदसरितेव किलोह्यमानो
गच्छन् भवद्भवनसन्निधिमन्वयासीत् ॥७॥
sAyaM sa gopabhavanAni bhavachcharitra-
gItAmRtaprasRtakarNarasAyanAni 
pashyan pramodasariteva kilohyamAno
gachChan bhavadbhavanasannidhimanvayAsIt 7

सायं स गोपभवनानि भवच्चरित्र-
In the evening, at the houses of the Gopas
गीतामृतप्रसृतकर्णरसायनानि ।
where your stories are sung, making them a medicine to the ears which drip nectar,
पश्यन् प्रमोदसरितेव किलोह्यमानो
gazing at them, didn't he, as if floating across a stream of joy,
गच्छन् भवद्भवनसन्निधिमन्वयासीत् ॥७॥
then gradually reached the house where you reside!

तावद्ददर्श पशुदोहविलोकलोलं
भक्तोत्तमागतिमिव प्रतिपालयन्तम् ।
भूमन् भवन्तमयमग्रजवन्तमन्त:
ब्रह्मानुभूतिरससिन्धुमिवोद्वमन्तम् ॥८॥
tAvaddadarsha pashudohavilokalolaM
bhaktottamAgatimiva pratipAlayantam 
bhUman bhavantamayamagrajavantamantaH
brahmAnubhUtirasasindhumivodvamantam 8

तावद्ददर्श पशुदोहविलोकलोलं
Then he saw, you, eagerly looking at cows being milked,
भक्तोत्तमागतिमिव प्रतिपालयन्तम् ।
looking as if you were waiting for the arrival of a dear devotee!
भूमन् भवन्तमयमग्रजवन्तमन्त:
O Abudant One! He saw you and your elder brother, as if in his interiority
ब्रह्मानुभूतिरससिन्धुमिवोद्वमन्तम् ॥८॥
a flood of blissful experience of the divine were gushing forth!

सायन्तनाप्लवविशेषविविक्तगात्रौ
द्वौ पीतनीलरुचिराम्बरलोभनीयौ ।
नातिप्रपञ्चधृतभूषणचारुवेषौ
मन्दस्मितार्द्रवदनौ स युवां ददर्श ॥९॥
sAyantanAplavavisheShaviviktagAtrau
dvau pItanIlaruchirAmbaralobhanIyau 
nAtiprapanchadhRtabhUShaNachAruveShau
mandasmitArdravadanau sa yuvAM dadarsha 9

सायन्तनाप्लवविशेषविविक्तगात्रौ
With both your bodies fresh and clean after your evening bath,
द्वौ पीतनीलरुचिराम्बरलोभनीयौ ।
the both of you, one of a golden hue and the other blue, of exceeding charm,
नातिप्रपञ्चधृतभूषणचारुवेषौ
decorated in minimal and beautiful ornaments,
मन्दस्मितार्द्रवदनौ स युवां ददर्श ॥९॥
with warm gentle smiles on your faces, he saw the both of you!

दूराद्रथात्समवरुह्य नमन्तमेनम्
उत्थाप्य भक्तकुलमौलिमथोपगूहन् ।
हर्षान्मिताक्षरगिरा कुशलानुयोगी
पाणिं प्रगृह्य सबलोऽथ गृहं निनेथ ॥१०॥
dUrAdrathAtsamavaruhya namantamenam
utthApya bhaktakulamaulimathopagUhan 
harShAnmitAkSharagirA kushalAnuyogI
pANiM pragRhya sabalotha gRhaM ninetha 10

दूराद्रथात्समवरुह्य नमन्तमेनम्
From afar, as he descened his chariot and bowed down to you,
उत्थाप्य भक्तकुलमौलिमथोपगूहन् ।
lifting him up, that jewel among devotees, and embracing him,
हर्षान्मिताक्षरगिरा कुशलानुयोगी
with joyful small talk, enquiring of his welfare,
पाणिं प्रगृह्य सबलोऽथ गृहं निनेथ ॥१०॥
holding his hand, along with Balarama you lead him into the house!

नन्देन साकममितादरमर्चयित्वा
तं यादवं तदुदितां निशमय्य वार्ताम् ।
गोपेषु भूपतिनिदेशकथां निवेद्य
नानाकथाभिरिह तेन निशामनैषी: ॥११॥
nandena sAkamamitAdaramarchayitvA
taM yAdavaM taduditAM nishamayya vArtAm 
gopeShu bhUpatinideshakathAM nivedya
nAnAkathAbhiriha tena nishAmanaiShIH 11

नन्देन साकममितादरमर्चयित्वा
Along with Nanda, honouring hi with great regard,
तं यादवं तदुदितां निशमय्य वार्ताम् ।
and the news brought forth by that Yadava, hearing it,
गोपेषु भूपतिनिदेशकथां निवेद्य
conveying the King's words to all the Gopas,
नानाकथाभिरिह तेन निशामनैषी: ॥११॥
in various other kinds of conversations you spent the night with him!

चन्द्रागृहे किमुत चन्द्रभगागृहे नु
राधागृहे नु भवने किमु मैत्रविन्दे ।
धूर्तो विलम्बत इति प्रमदाभिरुच्चै:
आशङ्कितो निशि मरुत्पुरनाथ पाया: ॥१२॥
chandrAgRhe kimuta chandrabhagAgRhe nu
rAdhAgRhe nu bhavane kimu maitravinde 
dhUrto vilambata iti pramadAbhiruchchaiH
Ashankito nishi marutpuranAtha pAyAH 12

चन्द्रागृहे किमुत चन्द्रभगागृहे नु
"May be in Chandra's house, or may be in Chandrabhaga's!
राधागृहे नु भवने किमु मैत्रविन्दे ।
May be in Radha's house, or may be in Mitravinda's!
धूर्तो विलम्बत इति प्रमदाभिरुच्चै:
That where the rascal is getting delayed" thus by the Gopikas you were severely
आशङ्कितो निशि मरुत्पुरनाथ पाया: ॥१२॥
doubted that night, O Lord of the City of Wind! May you protect me!
