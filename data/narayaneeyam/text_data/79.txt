अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकोनाशीतितमम् दशकम्
ekaonAshItitamam dashakam

The Seventy-ninth group of Ten shlokas

रुक्मिणी हरणम्
rukmiNI haraNam

Abducting Rukmini

बलसमेतबलानुगतो भवान् पुरमगाहत भीष्मकमानित: ।
द्विजसुतं त्वदुपागमवादिनं धृतरसा तरसा प्रणनाम सा ।।१॥
balasametabalAnugato bhavAn puramagAhata bhIShmakamAnitaH 
dvijasutaM tvadupAgamavAdinaM dhRtarasA tarasA praNanAma sA 1

बलसमेतबलानुगतो भवान् पुरमगाहत भीष्मकमानित: ।
You went along with Balarama and your forces to the city, and were honoured by King Bhishmaka!
द्विजसुतं त्वदुपागमवादिनं धृतरसा तरसा प्रणनाम सा ।।१॥
The Brahmin boy who conveyed to her of your arrival, she bowed down to him at once!

भुवनकान्तमवेक्ष्य भवद्वपुर्नृपसुतस्य निशम्य च चेष्टितम् ।
विपुलखेदजुषां पुरवासिनां सरुदितैरुदितैरगमन्निशा ॥२॥
bhuvanakAntamavekShya bhavadvapurnRpasutasya nishamya cha cheShTitam 
vipulakhedajuShAM puravAsinAM saruditairuditairagamannishA 2

भुवनकान्तमवेक्ष्य भवद्वपुर्नृपसुतस्य निशम्य च चेष्टितम् ।
Looking at your world-enchanting form, and hearing the deeds of the prince, Rukmi,
विपुलखेदजुषां पुरवासिनां सरुदितैरुदितैरगमन्निशा ॥२॥
as the people of the city spoke to each other in utmost sorrow, with tears in their eyes, night fell!

तदनु वन्दितुमिन्दुमुखी शिवां विहितमङ्गलभूषणभासुरा ।
निरगमत् भवदर्पितजीविता स्वपुरत: पुरत: सुभटावृता ॥३॥
tadanu vanditumindumukhI shivAM vihitamangalabhUShaNabhAsurA 
niragamat bhavadarpitajIvitA svapurataH purataH subhaTAvRtA 3

तदनु वन्दितुमिन्दुमुखी शिवां विहितमङ्गलभूषणभासुरा ।
Then, that moon faced one, adorned in resplendent and auspicious ornaments, in order to worship the Goddess, 
निरगमत् भवदर्पितजीविता स्वपुरत: पुरत: सुभटावृता ॥३॥
that one who had offered her life to you, went to the outskirts of the city, surrounded by her guards.

कुलवधूभिरुपेत्य कुमारिका गिरिसुतां परिपूज्य च सादरम् ।
मुहुरयाचत तत्पदपङ्कजे निपतिता पतितां तव केवलम् ॥४॥
kulavadhUbhirupetya kumArikA girisutAM paripUjya cha sAdaram 
muhurayAchata tatpadapankaje nipatitA patitAM tava kevalam 4

कुलवधूभिरुपेत्य कुमारिका गिरिसुतां परिपूज्य च सादरम् ।
That princess, going along with the noble women, and worshipping Goddess Girija in reverence,
मुहुरयाचत तत्पदपङ्कजे निपतिता पतितां तव केवलम् ॥४॥
prostrated at the Goddess's feet praying again and again that you must be her husband!

समवलोककुतूहलसङ्कुले नृपकुले निभृतं त्वयि च स्थिते ।
नृपसुता निरगाद्गिरिजालयात् सुरुचिरं रुचिरञ्जितदिङ्मुखा ॥५॥
samavalokakutUhalasankule nRpakule nibhRtaM tvayi cha sthite 
nRpasutA niragAdgirijAlayAt suruchiraM ruchiranjitadinmukhA 5

समवलोककुतूहलसङ्कुले नृपकुले निभृतं त्वयि च स्थिते ।
As the groups of kings gathered eager to see Rukmini, as you too stood among them silently,
नृपसुता निरगाद्गिरिजालयात् सुरुचिरं रुचिरञ्जितदिङ्मुखा ॥५॥
the princess stepped out of the temple of Girija, with a face that illuminated all the directions!

भुवनमोहनरूपरुचा तदा विवशिताखिलराजकदम्बया ।
त्वमपि देव कटाक्षविमोक्षणै: प्रमदया मदयाञ्चकृषे मनाक् ॥६॥
bhuvanamohanarUparuchA tadA vivashitAkhilarAjakadambayA 
tvamapi deva kaTAkShavimokShaNaiH pramadayA madayAnchakRShe manAk 6

भुवनमोहनरूपरुचा तदा विवशिताखिलराजकदम्बया ।
Then, with her world-enchanting beauty, she made all the groups of kings lose control!
त्वमपि देव कटाक्षविमोक्षणै: प्रमदया मदयाञ्चकृषे मनाक् ॥६॥
You too, O Lord, with her sidelong glances, were slightly enchanted by her intoxicating beauty!

क्वनु गमिष्यसि चन्द्रमुखीति तां सरसमेत्य करेण हरन् क्षणात् ।
समधिरोप्य रथं त्वमपाहृथा भुवि ततो विततो निनदो द्विषाम् ॥७॥
kvanu gamiShyasi chandramukhIti tAM sarasametya kareNa haran kShaNAt 
samadhiropya rathaM tvamapAhRthA bhuvi tato vitato ninado dviShAm 7

क्वनु गमिष्यसि चन्द्रमुखीति तां सरसमेत्य करेण हरन् क्षणात् ।
Where are you going, O moon-face one! Thus quickly taking hold of her hand, in an instant
समधिरोप्य रथं त्वमपाहृथा भुवि ततो विततो निनदो द्विषाम् ॥७॥
placing her on the chariot, you carried her away, as cries of protest of your enemies started to resound across the land!

क्व नु गत: पशुपाल इति क्रुधा कृतरणा यदुभिश्च जिता नृपा: ।
न तु भवानुदचाल्यत तैरहो पिशुनकै: शुनकैरिव केसरी ॥८॥
kva nu gataH pashupAla iti krudhA kRtaraNA yadubhishcha jitA nRpAH 
na tu bhavAnudachAlyata tairaho pishunakaiH shunakairiva kesarI 8

क्व नु गत: पशुपाल इति क्रुधा कृतरणा यदुभिश्च जिता नृपा: ।
Where did the cowherd go! Thus enraged and going to war, those kings were won over by the Yadavas!
न तु भवानुदचाल्यत तैरहो पिशुनकै: शुनकैरिव केसरी ॥८॥
You were not swayed one bit by them! O Just like a lion wouldn't be by packs of dogs!

तदनु रुक्मिणमागतमाहवे वधमुपेक्ष्य निबध्य विरूपयन् ।
हृतमदं परिमुच्य बलोक्तिभि: पुरमया रमया सह कान्तया ॥९॥
tadanu rukmiNamAgatamAhave vadhamupekShya nibadhya virUpayan 
hRtamadaM parimuchya baloktibhiH puramayA ramayA saha kAntayA 9

तदनु रुक्मिणमागतमाहवे वधमुपेक्ष्य निबध्य विरूपयन् ।
And then, as Rukmi approached you in battle, not killing him, but tying him up and disfiguring him,
हृतमदं परिमुच्य बलोक्तिभि: पुरमया रमया सह कान्तया ॥९॥
taking his pride away, and then releasing him upon Balarama's words, you returned to the city along with your loving wife!

नवसमागमलज्जितमानसां प्रणयकौतुकजृम्भितमन्मथाम् ।
अरमय: खलु नाथ यथासुखं रहसि तां हसितांशुलसन्मुखीम् ॥१०॥
navasamAgamalajjitamAnasAM praNayakautukajRmbhitamanmathAm 
aramayaH khalu nAtha yathAsukhaM rahasi tAM hasitAMshulasanmukhIm 10

नवसमागमलज्जितमानसां प्रणयकौतुकजृम्भितमन्मथाम् ।
The one who was shy of her new union, who was under the eager throes of her passionate love for you,
अरमय: खलु नाथ यथासुखं रहसि तां हसितांशुलसन्मुखीम् ॥१०॥
didn't you rejoice with her O Lord, joyfully and in privacy, with her whose face was radiant with her smiles!

विविधनर्मभिरेवमहर्निशं प्रमदमाकलयन् पुनरेकदा ।
ऋजुमते: किल वक्रगिरा भवान् वरतनोरतनोदतिलोलताम् ॥११॥
vividhanarmabhirevamaharnishaM pramadamAkalayan punarekadA 
RjumateH kila vakragirA bhavAn varatanoratanodatilolatAm 11

विविधनर्मभिरेवमहर्निशं प्रमदमाकलयन् पुनरेकदा ।
Thus, with various kinds of warm gestures, delighting her immensely, then once again,
ऋजुमते: किल वक्रगिरा भवान् वरतनोरतनोदतिलोलताम् ॥११॥
to that straight-minded one, didn't you, with your crafty words agitate the mind of that beautiful one!

तदधिकैरथ लालनकौशलै: प्रणयिनीमधिकं सुखयन्निमाम् ।
अयि मुकुन्द भवच्चरितानि न: प्रगदतां गदतान्तिमपाकुरु ॥१२॥
tadadhikairatha lAlanakaushalaiH praNayinImadhikaM sukhayannimAm 
ayi mukunda bhavachcharitAni naH pragadatAM gadatAntimapAkuru 12

तदधिकैरथ लालनकौशलै: प्रणयिनीमधिकं सुखयन्निमाम् ।
And then again with skilfful words of pacifying and coaxing, highly delighting your beloved, and making her happy,
अयि मुकुन्द भवच्चरितानि न: प्रगदतां गदतान्तिमपाकुरु ॥१२॥
O Mukunda! As we narrate the stories of your deeds, remove the sufferings of our ailments!
