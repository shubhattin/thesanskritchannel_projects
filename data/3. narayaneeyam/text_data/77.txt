अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्तसप्ततितमम् दशकम्
saptasaptatitamam dashakam

The Seventy-seventh group of Ten shlokas

उपश्लोक उत्पत्ति:  जरासन्धादि युद्धं च
upashloka utpattiH jarAsandhAdi yuddhaM cha

Birth of Upashloka and Wars with Jarasandha and Others

सैरन्ध्र्यास्तदनु चिरं स्मरातुराया
यातोऽभू: सुललितमुद्धवेन सार्धम् ।
आवासं त्वदुपगमोत्सवं सदैव
ध्यायन्त्या: प्रतिदिनवाससज्जिकाया: ॥१॥
sairandhryAstadanu chiraM smarAturAyA
yAtobhUH sulalitamuddhavena sArdham 
AvAsaM tvadupagamotsavaM sadaiva
dhyAyantyAH pratidinavAsasajjikAyAH 1

सैरन्ध्र्यास्तदनु चिरं स्मरातुराया
And then, that perfumer, Sairandhri, who was stricken with her love for you,
यातोऽभू: सुललितमुद्धवेन सार्धम् ।
you visited her house along with Uddhava, well adorned!
आवासं त्वदुपगमोत्सवं सदैव
As you arrived at her house that day, as always the festivities of your arrival
ध्यायन्त्या: प्रतिदिनवाससज्जिकाया: ॥१॥
she conducted, thinking of your arrival everyday and making appropriate decorations!

उपगते त्वयि पूर्णमनोरथां प्रमदसम्भ्रमकम्प्रपयोधराम् ।
विविधमाननमादधतीं मुदा रहसि तां रमयाञ्चकृषे सुखम् ॥२॥
upagate tvayi pUrNamanorathAM pramadasambhramakamprapayodharAm 
vividhamAnanamAdadhatIM mudA rahasi tAM ramayAnchakRShe sukham 2

उपगते त्वयि पूर्णमनोरथां प्रमदसम्भ्रमकम्प्रपयोधराम् ।
As you arrived, with her desires fulfilled, and her breasts quivering with excitement,
विविधमाननमादधतीं मुदा रहसि तां रमयाञ्चकृषे सुखम् ॥२॥
as she was honouring you with various offerings, you overjoyed, caused her immense delight in her privacy.

पृष्टा वरं पुनरसाववृणोद्वराकी
भूयस्त्वया सुरतमेव निशान्तरेषु ।
सायुज्यमस्त्विति वदेत् बुध एव कामं
सामीप्यमस्त्वनिशमित्यपि नाब्रवीत् किम् ॥३॥
pRShTA varaM punarasAvavRNodvarAkI
bhUyastvayA suratameva nishAntareShu 
sAyujyamastviti vadet budha eva kAmaM
sAmIpyamastvanishamityapi nAbravIt kim 3

पृष्टा वरं पुनरसाववृणोद्वराकी
As she was asked to make a wish, the poor woman,
भूयस्त्वया सुरतमेव निशान्तरेषु ।
once again asked for union with you alone, in the nights!
सायुज्यमस्त्विति वदेत् बुध एव कामं
Only the wise ones may seek union with you as their desire!
सामीप्यमस्त्वनिशमित्यपि नाब्रवीत् किम् ॥३॥
Who wouldn't just request for some proximity with you always!

ततो भवान् देव निशासु कासुचिन्मृगीदृशं तां निभृतं विनोदयन् ।
अदादुपश्लोक इति श्रुतं सुतं स नारदात् सात्त्वततन्त्रविद्बभौ ॥४॥
tato bhavAn deva nishAsu kAsuchinmRgIdRshaM tAM nibhRtaM vinodayan 
adAdupashloka iti shrutaM sutaM sa nAradAt sAttvatatantravidbabhau 4

ततो भवान् देव निशासु कासुचिन्मृगीदृशं तां निभृतं विनोदयन् ।
Then, O Lord! You, delighting that doe-eyed one immensely during some of those nights,
अदादुपश्लोक इति श्रुतं सुतं स नारदात् सात्त्वततन्त्रविद्बबभौ ॥४॥
gave her a child by the name of Upashloka, who came to be renowned and shined with his learning of Sattvata Tantra from Narada!

अक्रूरमन्दिरमितोऽथ बलोद्धवाभ्याम्
अभ्यर्चितो बहु नुतो मुदितेन तेन ।
एनं विसृज्य विपिनागतपाण्डवेय-
वृत्तं विवेदिथ तथा धृतराष्ट्रचेष्टाम् ॥५॥
akrUramandiramitotha baloddhavAbhyAm
abhyarchito bahu nuto muditena tena 
enaM visRjya vipinAgatapANDaveya-
vRttaM viveditha tathA dhRtarAShTracheShTAm 5

अक्रूरमन्दिरमितोऽथ बलोद्धवाभ्याम्
Then, going to Akrura's house with Balarama and Uddhava,
अभ्यर्चितो बहु नुतो मुदितेन तेन ।
you were worshipped and praised by him who was highly pleased.
एनं विसृज्य विपिनागतपाण्डवेय-
Putting him to the task of the forest-faring Pandavas,
वृत्तं विवेदिथ तथा धृतराष्ट्रचेष्टाम् ॥५॥
you got to know of those entire happenings and the acts of Dhritarashtra!

विघाताज्जामातु: परमसुहृदो भोजनृपते:
जरासन्धे रुन्धत्यनवधिरुषान्धेऽथ मथुराम् ।
रथाद्यैर्द्योर्लब्धै: कतिपयबलस्त्वं बलयुत:
त्रयोविंशत्यक्षौहिणि तदुपनीतं समहृथा: ॥६॥
vighAtAjjAmAtuH paramasuhRdo bhojanRpateH
jarAsandhe rundhatyanavadhiruShAndhetha mathurAm 
rathAdyairdyorlabdhaiH katipayabalastvaM balayutaH
trayoviMshatyakShauhiNi tadupanItaM samahRthAH 6

विघाताज्जामातु: परमसुहृदो भोजनृपते:
Upon the downfall of his dearest frient and son in law, the king of Bhoja,
जरासन्धे रुन्धत्यनवधिरुषान्धेऽथ मथुराम् ।
Jarasandha, blinded with boundless rage, attacked the kingdom of Mathura,
रथाद्यैर्द्योर्लब्धै: कतिपयबलस्त्वं बलयुत:
along with his forces with chariots and everything! You with only a few forces on your side
त्रयोविंशत्यक्षौहिणि तदुपनीतं समहृथा: ॥६॥
destroyed the Twenty-three Akshauhinis of forces brought by him!

बद्धं बलादथ बलेन बलोत्तरं त्वं
भूयो बलोद्यमरसेन मुमोचिथैनम् ।
निश्शेषदिग्जयसमाहृतविश्वसैन्यात्
कोऽन्यस्ततो हि बलपौरुषवांस्तदानीम् ॥७॥
baddhaM balAdatha balena balottaraM tvaM
bhUyo balodyamarasena mumochithainam 
nishsheShadigjayasamAhRtavishvasainyAt
konyastato hi balapauruShavAMstadAnIm 7

बद्धं बलादथ बलेन बलोत्तरं त्वं
As he was tied up by the immensely powerful Balarama, you,
भूयो बलोद्यमरसेन मुमोचिथैनम् ।
once again wanting to quell his power some more, set him free!
निश्शेषदिग्जयसमाहृतविश्वसैन्यात्
That one who had accumulated armies through his victories all over the world,
कोऽन्यस्ततो हि बलपौरुषवांस्तदानीम् ॥७॥
who else was more powerful than him during those times!

भग्न: स लग्नहृदयोऽपि नृपै: प्रणुन्नो
युद्धं त्वया व्यधित षोडशकृत्व एवम् ।
अक्षौहिणी: शिव शिवास्य जघन्थ विष्णो
सम्भूय सैकनवतित्रिशतं तदानीम् ॥८॥
bhagnaH sa lagnahRdayopi nRpaiH praNunno
yuddhaM tvayA vyadhita ShoDashakRtva evam 
akShauhiNIH shiva shivAsya jaghantha viShNo
sambhUya saikanavatitrishataM tadAnIm 8

भग्न: स लग्नहृदयोऽपि नृपै: प्रणुन्नो
Destroyed once, he goaded by other kings, and with his heart's intent,
युद्धं त्वया व्यधित षोडशकृत्व एवम् ।
went to battle with you Sixteen times!
अक्षौहिणी: शिव शिवास्य जघन्थ विष्णो
Shiva! Shiva! Many many Akshauhinis of his armies you destroyed, O Vishnu!
सम्भूय सैकनवतित्रिशतं तदानीम् ॥८॥
All put together, 391 during those times!

अष्टादशेऽस्य समरे समुपेयुषि त्वं
दृष्ट्वा पुरोऽथ यवनं यवनत्रिकोट्या ।
त्वष्ट्रा विधाप्य पुरमाशु पयोधिमध्ये
तत्राऽथ योगबलत: स्वजनाननैषी: ॥९॥
aShTAdashesya samare samupeyuShi tvaM
dRShTvA purotha yavanaM yavanatrikoTyA 
tvaShTrA vidhApya puramAshu payodhimadhye
tatrAtha yogabalataH svajanAnanaiShIH 9

अष्टादशेऽस्य समरे समुपेयुषि त्वं
For the eighteenth time, as he approached you for battle,
दृष्ट्वा पुरोऽथ यवनं यवनत्रिकोट्या ।
looking at the Yavana king in front with three crores of his army,
त्वष्ट्रा विधाप्य पुरमाशु पयोधिमध्ये
making Vishwakarma effortlessly build a city in the middle of the ocean,
तत्राऽथ योगबलत: स्वजनाननैषी: ॥९॥
with your yogic prowess, you placed your own people there!

पद्भ्यां त्वं पद्ममाली चकित इव पुरान्निर्गतो धावमानो
म्लेच्छेशेनानुयातो वधसुकृतविहीनेन शैले न्यलैषी: ।
सुप्तेनांघ्र्याहतेन द्रुतमथ मुचुकुन्देन भस्मीकृतेऽस्मिन्
भूपायास्मै गुहान्ते सुललितवपुषा तस्थिषे भक्तिभाजे ॥१०॥
padbhyAM tvaM padmamAlI chakita iva purAnnirgato dhAvamAno
mlechCheshenAnuyAto vadhasukRtavihInena shaile nyalaiShIH 
suptenAMghryAhatena drutamatha muchukundena bhasmIkRtesmin
bhUpAyAsmai guhAnte sulalitavapuShA tasthiShe bhaktibhAje 10

पद्भ्यां त्वं पद्ममाली चकित इव पुरान्निर्गतो धावमानो
As you started to run away from the city on foot, wearing a garland, pretending to be afraid,
म्लेच्छेशेनानुयातो वधसुकृतविहीनेन शैले न्यलैषी: ।
chased by the king of Mlechchas, who was devoid of any virtues, you lead him towards a mountain!
सुप्तेनांघ्र्याहतेन द्रुतमथ मुचुकुन्देन भस्मीकृतेऽस्मिन्
As his feet struck the sleeping Muchukunda , who then quickly turned him to ash,
भूपायास्मै गुहान्ते सुललितवपुषा तस्थिषे भक्तिभाजे ॥१०॥
for the sake of that devoted king, within that mountain cave, you manifested yourself in your playful form!

ऐक्ष्वाकोऽहं विरक्तोऽस्म्यखिलनृपसुखे त्वत्प्रसादैककाङ्क्षी
हा देवेति स्तुवन्तं वरविततिषु तं निस्पृहं वीक्ष्य हृष्यन् ।
मुक्तेस्तुल्यां च भक्तिं धुतसकलमलां मोक्षमप्याशु दत्वा
कार्यं हिंसाविशुद्ध्यै तप इति च तदा प्रात्थ लोकप्रतीत्यै ॥११॥
aikShvAkohaM viraktosmyakhilanRpasukhe tvatprasAdaikakAnkShI
hA deveti stuvantaM varavitatiShu taM nispRhaM vIkShya hRShyan 
muktestulyAM cha bhaktiM dhutasakalamalAM mokShamapyAshu datvA
kAryaM hiMsAvishuddhyai tapa iti cha tadA prAttha lokapratItyai 11

ऐक्ष्वाकोऽहं विरक्तोऽस्म्यखिलनृपसुखे त्वत्प्रसादैककाङ्क्षी
"I am from the Ikshwaku clan, who is unattached to all the kingly pleasures, who lays waiting for your grace,
हा देवेति स्तुवन्तं वरविततिषु तं निस्पृहं वीक्ष्य हृष्यन् ।
O Lord!" thus as he was praising you in worship, looking at him, who was devoid of any desires in joy, 
मुक्तेस्तुल्यां च भक्तिं धुतसकलमलां मोक्षमप्याशु दत्वा
bestowing upon him devotion, which is equal to liberation, which purifies everything, and finally liberation too,
कार्यं हिंसाविशुद्ध्यै तप इति च तदा प्रात्थ लोकप्रतीत्यै ॥११॥
"To purify the cruelties that you have inflicted, and for the welfare of the world, perform penance!" thus you instructed him.

तदनु मथुरां गत्वा हत्वा चमूं यवनाहृतां
मगधपतिना मार्गे सैन्यै: पुरेव निवारित: ।
चरमविजयं दर्पायास्मै प्रदाय पलायितो
जलधिनगरीं यातो वातालयेश्वर पाहि माम् ॥१२॥
tadanu mathurAM gatvA hatvA chamUM yavanAhRtAM
magadhapatinA mArge sainyaiH pureva nivAritaH 
charamavijayaM darpAyAsmai pradAya palAyito
jaladhinagarIM yAto vAtAlayeshvara pAhi mAm 12

तदनु मथुरां गत्वा हत्वा चमूं यवनाहृतां
And then, going back to Mathura and slaying the army of Yavanas gathered there,
मगधपतिना मार्गे सैन्यै: पुरेव निवारित: ।
blocked by the armies of Jarasandha along the way,
चरमविजयं दर्पायास्मै प्रदाय पलायितो
to his satisfaction, providing him final victory in death, and fleeing,
जलधिनगरीं यातो वातालयेश्वर पाहि माम् ॥१२॥
you went to the city in the ocean, O Lord of the Abode of Wind! Protect me!
