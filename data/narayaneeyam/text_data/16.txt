ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

षोडशम् दशकम्
ShoDasham dashakam

The Sixteenth of first Ten shlokas

नर नारायणयो: अवतार: दक्ष याग: च
nara nArAyaNAyoH avatAraH dakSha yAgaH cha

Incarnation of Nara Narayana and the Yaga of Daksha

दक्षो विरिञ्चतनयोऽथ मनोस्तनूजां
लब्ध्वा प्रसूतिमिह षोडश चाप कन्या: ।
धर्मे त्रयोदश ददौ पितृषु स्वधां च
स्वाहां हविर्भुजि सतीं गिरिशे त्वदंशे ॥१॥
dakSho virinchatanayotha manostanUjAM
labdhvA prasUtimiha ShoDasha chApa kanyAH 
dharme trayodasha dadau pitRShu svadhAM cha
svAhAM havirbhuji satIM girishe tvadaMshe 1

दक्षो विरिञ्चतनयोऽथ मनोस्तनूजां
Daksha, the son of Brahma, the daughter of manu,
लब्ध्वा प्रसूतिमिह षोडश चाप कन्या: ।
prasUti, having gotten her in marriage, bore 16 daughters!
धर्मे त्रयोदश ददौ पितृषु स्वधां च
To Dharma, he gave 13, Svadha to Pitrs,
स्वाहां हविर्भुजि सतीं गिरिशे त्वदंशे ॥१॥
Svaha to Agni, and Sati to Shiva, who is an aspect of your own self!

मूर्तिर्हि धर्मगृहिणी सुषुवे भवन्तं
नारायणं नरसखं महितानुभावम् ।
यज्जन्मनि प्रमुदिता: कृततूर्यघोषा:
पुष्पोत्करान् प्रववृषुर्नुनुवु: सुरौघा: ॥२॥
mUrtirhi dharmagRhiNI suShuve bhavantaM
nArAyaNaM narasakhaM mahitAnubhAvam 
yajjanmani pramuditAH kRtatUryaghoShAH
puShpotkarAn pravavRShurnunuvuH suraughAH 2

मूर्तिर्हि धर्मगृहिणी सुषुवे भवन्तं
Murti, the wife of Dharma, gave birth to You,
नारायणं नरसखं महितानुभावम् ।
as Narayana, the friend of Nara, of great glory!
यज्जन्मनि प्रमुदिता: कृततूर्यघोषा:
At whose birth, with extreme glee, resounding musical instruments, 
पुष्पोत्करान् प्रववृषुर्नुनुवु: सुरौघा: ॥२॥
showering heaps of flowers, and prostrating, the groups of devas celebrated!

दैत्यं सहस्रकवचं कवचै: परीतं
साहस्रवत्सरतपस्समराभिलव्यै: ।
पर्यायनिर्मिततपस्समरौ भवन्तौ
शिष्टैककङ्कटममुं न्यहतां सलीलम् ॥३॥
daityaM sahasrakavachaM kavachaiH parItaM
sAhasravatsaratapassamarAbhilavyaiH 
paryAyanirmitatapassamarau bhavantau
shiShTaikakankaTamamuM nyahatAM salIlam 3

दैत्यं सहस्रकवचं कवचै: परीतं
The asura Sahasrakavacha, who was covered in armours,
साहस्रवत्सरतपस्समराभिलव्यै: ।
which could only be pierced by a penance of a thousand years,
पर्यायनिर्मिततपस्समरौ भवन्तौ
taking turns in fighting and penance, the both of you,
शिष्टैककङ्कटममुं न्यहतां सलीलम् ॥३॥
when he was left but with a single final armour, slayed effortlessly!

अन्वाचरन्नुपदिशन्नपि मोक्षधर्मं
त्वं भ्रातृमान् बदरिकाश्रममध्यवात्सी: ।
शक्रोऽथ ते शमतपोबलनिस्सहात्मा
दिव्याङ्गनापरिवृतं प्रजिघाय मारम् ॥४॥
anvAcharannupadishannapi mokShadharmaM
tvaM bhrAtRmAn badarikAshramamadhyavAtsIH 
shakrotha te shamatapobalanissahAtmA
divyAnganAparivRtaM prajighAya mAram 4

अन्वाचरन्नुपदिशन्नपि मोक्षधर्मं
By practicing and preaching the path of liberation,
त्वं भ्रातृमान् बदरिकाश्रममध्यवात्सी: ।
You, along with your brother, Nara, stayed in Badarikashrama.
शक्रोऽथ ते शमतपोबलनिस्सहात्मा
Then, Indra unable to bear the strength of your penance,
दिव्याङ्गनापरिवृतं प्रजिघाय मारम् ॥४॥
surrounded by celestial maidens, set forth manmatha!

कामो वसन्तमलयानिलबन्धुशाली
कान्ताकटाक्षविशिखैर्विकसद्विलासै: ।
विध्यन्मुहुर्मुहुरकम्पमुदीक्ष्य च त्वां
भीतस्त्वयाऽथ जगदे मृदुहासभाजा ॥५॥
kAmo vasantamalayAnilabandhushAlI
kAntAkaTAkShavishikhairvikasadvilAsaiH 
vidhyanmuhurmuhurakampamudIkShya cha tvAM
bhItastvayAtha jagade mRduhAsabhAjA 5

कामो वसन्तमलयानिलबन्धुशाली
Kama, with pleasant spring breeze by his side,
कान्ताकटाक्षविशिखैर्विकसद्विलासै: ।
with the arrows of sidelong glances of the maidens, and their graceful movements,
विध्यन्मुहुर्मुहुरकम्पमुदीक्ष्य च त्वां
piercing you repeatedly and seeing you unwavering at their advances,
भीतस्त्वयाऽथ जगदे मृदुहासभाजा ॥५॥
frightened, then was spoken to by you thus with a gentle smile!

भीत्याऽलमङ्गज वसन्त सुराङ्गना वो
मन्मानसं त्विह जुषध्वमिति ब्रुवाण: ।
त्वं विस्मयेन परित: स्तुवतामथैषां
प्रादर्शय: स्वपरिचारककातराक्षी: ॥६॥
bhItyAlamangaja vasanta surAnganA vo
manmAnasaM tviha juShadhvamiti bruvANaH 
tvaM vismayena paritaH stuvatAmathaiShAM
prAdarshayaH svaparichArakakAtarAkShIH 6

भीत्याऽलमङ्गज वसन्त सुराङ्गना वो
"Enough with your fear, O Manmatha, Vasanta and Divine Maidens, 
मन्मानसं त्विह जुषध्वमिति ब्रुवाण: ।
here now, you enjoy My mind!" saying thus,
त्वं विस्मयेन परित: स्तुवतामथैषां
as they surrounded you in wonder, and in praise, you
प्रादर्शय: स्वपरिचारककातराक्षी: ॥६॥
displayed to them your own attending maiden of modest glances!

सम्मोहनाय मिलिता मदनादयस्ते
त्वद्दासिकापरिमलै: किल मोहमापु: ।
दत्तां त्वया च जगृहुस्त्रपयैव सर्व-
स्वर्वासिगर्वशमनीं पुनरुर्वशीं ताम् ॥७॥
sammohanAya militA madanAdayaste
tvaddAsikAparimalaiH kila mohamApuH 
dattAM tvayA cha jagRhustrapayaiva sarva-
svarvAsigarvashamanIM punarurvashIM tAm 7

सम्मोहनाय मिलिता मदनादयस्ते
To enamour you, manmatha and others who were gathered there,
त्वद्दासिकापरिमलै: किल मोहमापु: ।
by the fragrance of your maiden, haven't they themselves gotten enamoured!
दत्तां त्वया च जगृहुस्त्रपयैव सर्व-
Accepted the one who was bestowed by you, timidly,
स्वर्वासिगर्वशमनीं पुनरुर्वशीं ताम् ॥७॥
as she quelled the pride of all those heavenly residents, that Urvashi!

दृष्ट्वोर्वशीं तव कथां च निशम्य शक्र:
पर्याकुलोऽजनि भवन्महिमावमर्शात् ।
एवं प्रशान्तरमणीयतरावतारात्
त्वत्तोऽधिको वरद कृष्णतनुस्त्वमेव ॥८॥
dRShTvorvashIM tava kathAM cha nishamya shakraH
paryAkulojani bhavanmahimAvamarshAt 
evaM prashAntaramaNIyatarAvatArAt
tvattodhiko varada kRShNatanustvameva 8

दृष्ट्वोर्वशीं तव कथां च निशम्य शक्र:
Looking at Urvashi and hearing of your story, Indra,
पर्याकुलोऽजनि भवन्महिमावमर्शात् ।
got bewildered reflecting upon your glory!
एवं प्रशान्तरमणीयतरावतारात्
Thus, beyond the pleasant and extremely enchanting form of yours,
त्वत्तोऽधिको वरद कृष्णतनुस्त्वमेव ॥८॥
you are excelled, O Varada! by your own attractive form as Krishna!

दक्षस्तु धातुरतिलालनया रजोऽन्धो
नात्यादृतस्त्वयि च कष्टमशान्तिरासीत् ।
येन व्यरुन्ध स भवत्तनुमेव शर्वं
यज्ञे च वैरपिशुने स्वसुतां व्यमानीत् ॥९॥
dakShastu dhAturatilAlanayA rajondho
nAtyAdRtastvayi cha kaShTamashAntirAsIt 
yena vyarundha sa bhavattanumeva sharvaM
yajne cha vairapishune svasutAM vyamAnIt 9

दक्षस्तु धातुरतिलालनया रजोऽन्धो
Daksha, due to excessive indulgence by brahmA, got blinded by rajo guNa,
नात्यादृतस्त्वयि च कष्टमशान्तिरासीत् ।
and without much regard for you, was struggling with a peace-less mind!
येन व्यरुन्ध स भवत्तनुमेव शर्वं
Due to this he went againt Shiva, who is an aspect of your own form,
यज्ञे च वैरपिशुने स्वसुतां व्यमानीत् ॥९॥
and in the yajna, out of his spite, insulted his own daughter, Sati!

क्रुद्धेशमर्दितमख: स तु कृत्तशीर्षो
देवप्रसादितहरादथ लब्धजीव: ।
त्वत्पूरितक्रतुवर: पुनराप शान्तिं
स त्वं प्रशान्तिकर पाहि मरुत्पुरेश ॥१०॥
kruddheshamarditamakhaH sa tu kRttashIrSho
devaprasAditaharAdatha labdhajIvaH 
tvatpUritakratuvaraH punarApa shAntiM
sa tvaM prashAntikara pAhi marutpuresha 10

क्रुद्धेशमर्दितमख: स तु कृत्तशीर्षो
As Shiva got enraged, his yajna got destroyed and his head lopped off,
देवप्रसादितहरादथ लब्धजीव: ।
and then again as Shiva was pleased by the Devas, he got his life back!
त्वत्पूरितक्रतुवर: पुनराप शान्तिं
With your grace he then completed his yajna, and found peace!
स त्वं प्रशान्तिकर पाहि मरुत्पुरेश ॥१०॥
O Lord who causes profound peace, protect me, O Ruler of the City of Wind!
