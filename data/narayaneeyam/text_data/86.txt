And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

षडशीतितमम् दशकम्

The Eighty-sixth group of Ten shlokas

साल्वादि वध: भारत युद्धं च
sAlvAdi vadhaH bhArata yuddhaM cha

Slaying of Salva and others, The Mahabharata War

साल्वो भैष्मीविवाहे यदुबलविजितश्चन्द्रचूडाद्विमानं
विन्दन् सौभं स मायी त्वयि वसति कुरुंस्त्वत्पुरीमभ्यभाङ्क्षीत् ।
प्रद्युम्नस्तं निरुन्धन्निखिलयदुभटैर्न्यग्रहीदुग्रवीर्यं
तस्यामात्यं द्युमन्तं व्यजनि च समर: सप्तविंशत्यहान्तम् ॥१॥
sAlvo bhaiShmIvivAhe yadubalavijitashchandrachUDAdvimAnaM
vindan saubhaM sa mAyI tvayi vasati kuruMstvatpurImabhyabhAnkShIt 
pradyumnastaM nirundhannikhilayadubhaTairnyagrahIdugravIryaM
tasyAmAtyaM dyumantaM vyajani cha samaraH saptaviMshatyahAntam 1

साल्वो भैष्मीविवाहे यदुबलविजितश्चन्द्रचूडाद्विमानं
Salva, who was defeated by the Yadava armies in Rukmini's marriage, an aerial Vimana, from lord Shiva,
विन्दन् सौभं स मायी त्वयि वसति कुरुंस्त्वत्पुरीमभ्यभाङ्क्षीत् ।
by the name of Saubha, procuring it, that illusory one, as you were in the land of Kurus, attacked your city!
प्रद्युम्नस्तं निरुन्धन्निखिलयदुभटैर्न्यग्रहीदुग्रवीर्यं
Pradyumna, along with all the Yadava soldiers, obstructed him, and the mighty
तस्यामात्यं द्युमन्तं व्यजनि च समर: सप्तविंशत्यहान्तम् ॥१॥
minister of his, named Dyumanta, was slain by them in a battle that lasted for 27 days!

तावत्त्वं रामशाली त्वरितमुपगत: खण्डितप्रायसैन्यं
सौभेशं तं न्यरुन्धा: स च किल गदया शार्ङ्गमभ्रंशयत्ते ।
मायातातं व्यहिंसीदपि तव पुरतस्तत्त्वयापि क्षणार्धं
नाज्ञायीत्याहुरेके तदिदमवमतं व्यास एव न्यषेधीत् ॥२॥
tAvattvaM rAmashAlI tvaritamupagataH khaNDitaprAyasainyaM
saubheshaM taM nyarundhAH sa cha kila gadayA shArngamabhraMshayatte 
mAyAtAtaM vyahiMsIdapi tava puratastattvayApi kShaNArdhaM
nAjnAyItyAhureke tadidamavamataM vyAsa eva nyaShedhIt 2

तावत्त्वं रामशाली त्वरितमुपगत: खण्डितप्रायसैन्यं
Within that time, you, quickly returning with Balarama and almost razing their army to the ground,
सौभेशं तं न्यरुन्धा: स च किल गदया शार्ङ्गमभ्रंशयत्ते ।
went to fight with Salva! Didn't he then strike down your Sharnga with his own mace!
मायातातं व्यहिंसीदपि तव पुरतस्तत्त्वयापि क्षणार्धं
He then also tricked you into believing that he killed your father in front of you, and for half a second
नाज्ञायीत्याहुरेके तदिदमवमतं व्यास एव न्यषेधीत् ॥२॥
you too didn't realize his trickery, some say! But Vyasa himself denied this!

क्षिप्त्वा सौभं गदाचूर्णितमुदकनिधौ मङ्क्षु साल्वेऽपि चक्रे-
णोत्कृत्ते दन्तवक्त्र: प्रसभमभिपतन्नभ्यमुञ्चद्गदां ते ।
कौमोदक्या हतोऽसावपि सुकृतनिधिश्चैद्यवत्प्रापदैक्यं
सर्वेषामेष पूर्वं त्वयि धृतमनसां मोक्षणार्थोऽवतार: ॥३॥
kShiptvA saubhaM gadAchUrNitamudakanidhau mankShu sAlvepi chakre-
NotkRtte dantavaktraH prasabhamabhipatannabhyamunchadgadAM te 
kaumodakyA hatosAvapi sukRtanidhishchaidyavatprApadaikyaM
sarveShAmeSha pUrvaM tvayi dhRtamanasAM mokShaNArthovatAraH 3

क्षिप्त्वा सौभं गदाचूर्णितमुदकनिधौ मङ्क्षु साल्वेऽपि चक्रे-
Crushing Salva with your mace, throwing him off into the ocean, as your chakra then
णोत्कृत्ते दन्तवक्त्र: प्रसभमभिपतन्नभ्यमुञ्चद्गदां ते ।
beheaded him, as Dantavaktra attacked you with his mace, 
कौमोदक्या हतोऽसावपि सुकृतनिधिश्चैद्यवत्प्रापदैक्यं
vanquishing him too with your mace, the Kaumodaki, then he too like Shishupala filled with virtues, attained to unity with you!
सर्वेषामेष पूर्वं त्वयि धृतमनसां मोक्षणार्थोऽवतार: ॥३॥
This incarnation of yours was indeed to grant Moksha to all those who had their minds fixed upon you!

त्वय्यायातेऽथ जाते किल कुरुसदसि द्यूतके संयताया:
क्रन्दन्त्या याज्ञसेन्या: सकरुणमकृथाश्चेलमालामनन्ताम् ।
अन्नान्तप्राप्तशर्वांशजमुनिचकितद्रौपदीचिन्तितोऽथ
प्राप्त: शाकान्नमश्नन् मुनिगणमकृथास्तृप्तिमन्तं वनान्ते ॥४॥
tvayyAyAtetha jAte kila kurusadasi dyUtake saMyatAyAH
krandantyA yAjnasenyAH sakaruNamakRthAshchelamAlAmanantAm 
annAntaprAptasharvAMshajamunichakitadraupadIchintitotha
prAptaH shAkAnnamashnan munigaNamakRthAstRptimantaM vanAnte 4

त्वय्यायातेऽथ जाते किल कुरुसदसि द्यूतके संयताया:
After you left, didn't a game of dice happen in the assembly of the Kurus!
क्रन्दन्त्या याज्ञसेन्या: सकरुणमकृथाश्चेलमालामनन्ताम् ।
As Draupadi cried for help, out of your compassion, you provided an endless stream of drapes!
अन्नान्तप्राप्तशर्वांशजमुनिचकितद्रौपदीचिन्तितोऽथ
Again, as Draupadi was shocked to see Durvasa, arrive as a guest after they finished eating, and thought of you,
प्राप्त: शाकान्नमश्नन् मुनिगणमकृथास्तृप्तिमन्तं वनान्ते ॥४॥
arriving there, eating the left overs, you satiated the hunger of those groups of sages in the forest!

युद्धोद्योगेऽथ मन्त्रे मिलति सति वृत: फल्गुनेन त्वमेक:
कौरव्ये दत्तसैन्य: करिपुरमगमो दौत्यकृत् पाण्डवार्थम् ।
भीष्मद्रोणादिमान्ये तव खलु वचने धिक्कृते कौरवेण
व्यावृण्वन् विश्वरूपं मुनिसदसि पुरीं क्षोभयित्वागतोऽभू: ॥५॥
yuddhodyogetha mantre milati sati vRtaH phalgunena tvamekaH
kauravye dattasainyaH karipuramagamo dautyakRt pANDavArtham 
bhIShmadroNAdimAnye tava khalu vachane dhikkRte kauraveNa
vyAvRNvan vishvarUpaM munisadasi purIM kShobhayitvAgatobhUH 5

युद्धोद्योगेऽथ मन्त्रे मिलति सति वृत: फल्गुनेन त्वमेक:
In the meeting over the preparations of war, you alone were chosen by Arjuna!
कौरव्ये दत्तसैन्य: करिपुरमगमो दौत्यकृत् पाण्डवार्थम् ।
Giving your armies to the Kauravas, you went to Hastinapura as the messenger of Pandavas!
भीष्मद्रोणादिमान्ये तव खलु वचने धिक्कृते कौरवेण
Your words were respected by Bhishma, Drona and others, but weren't they then rejected by the Kauravas! 
व्यावृण्वन् विश्वरूपं मुनिसदसि पुरीं क्षोभयित्वागतोऽभू: ॥५॥
You taking on your cosmic form in that assembly of sages, shaking up the entire city, you went back!

जिष्णोस्त्वं कृष्ण सूत: खलु समरमुखे बन्धुघाते दयालुं
खिन्नं तं वीक्ष्य वीरं किमिदमयि सखे नित्य एकोऽयमात्मा ।
को वध्य: कोऽत्र हन्ता तदिह वधभियं प्रोज्झ्य मय्यर्पितात्मा
धर्म्यं युद्धं चरेति प्रकृतिमनयथा दर्शयन् विश्वरूपम् ॥६॥
jiShNostvaM kRShNa sUtaH khalu samaramukhe bandhughAte dayAluM
khinnaM taM vIkShya vIraM kimidamayi sakhe nitya ekoyamAtmA 
ko vadhyaH kotra hantA tadiha vadhabhiyaM projjhya mayyarpitAtmA
dharmyaM yuddhaM chareti prakRtimanayathA darshayan vishvarUpam 6

जिष्णोस्त्वं कृष्ण सूत: खलु समरमुखे बन्धुघाते दयालुं
O Krishna! Didn't you then become the charioteer for Arjuna in that battle, and when he was overcome with the prospect of killing his relatives,
खिन्नं तं वीक्ष्य वीरं किमिदमयि सखे नित्य एकोऽयमात्मा ।
looking at that valiant one dejected, "O Dear One! What's this! This soul is eternal and one with everything!
को वध्य: कोऽत्र हन्ता तदिह वधभियं प्रोज्झ्य मय्यर्पितात्मा
Who is being killed and who is its killer! Therefore relinquishing your fear, with your self devoted to me,
धर्म्यं युद्धं चरेति प्रकृतिमनयथा दर्शयन् विश्वरूपम् ॥६॥
conduct this war righteously!" thus saying you brought him back to his senses displaying to him your cosmic form!

भक्तोत्तंसेऽथ भीष्मे तव धरणिभरक्षेपकृत्यैकसक्ते
नित्यं नित्यं विभिन्दत्ययुतसमधिकं प्राप्तसादे च पार्थे ।
निश्शस्त्रत्वप्रतिज्ञां विजहदरिवरं धारयन् क्रोधशाली-
वाधावन् प्राञ्जलिं तं नतशिरसमथो वीक्ष्य मोदादपागा: ॥७॥
bhaktottaMsetha bhIShme tava dharaNibharakShepakRtyaikasakte
nityaM nityaM vibhindatyayutasamadhikaM prAptasAde cha pArthe 
nishshastratvapratijnAM vijahadarivaraM dhArayan krodhashAlI-
vAdhAvan prAnjaliM taM natashirasamatho vIkShya modAdapAgAH 7

भक्तोत्तंसेऽथ भीष्मे तव धरणिभरक्षेपकृत्यैकसक्ते
As your exalted devotee, Bhishma, was completely focused on your task of reducing the burden of earth,
नित्यं नित्यं विभिन्दत्ययुतसमधिकं प्राप्तसादे च पार्थे ।
every single day slaying more than ten-thousand people in the war, and as Arjuna got tired resisting him,
निश्शस्त्रत्वप्रतिज्ञां विजहदरिवरं धारयन् क्रोधशाली-
abandoning your vow to not take up arms, wielding your chakra with utmost rage
वाधावन् प्राञ्जलिं तं नतशिरसमथो वीक्ष्य मोदादपागा: ॥७॥
you rushed towards him, and as he at once bowed down with the anjali mudra, you were then pacified!

युद्धे द्रोणस्य हस्तिस्थिररणभगदत्तेरितं वैष्णवास्त्रं
वक्षस्याधत्त चक्रस्थगितरविमहा: प्रार्दयत्सिन्धुराजम् ।
नागास्त्रे कर्णमुक्ते क्षितिमवनमयन् केवलं कृत्तमौलिं
तत्रे त्रापि पार्थं किमिव नहि भवान् पाण्डवानामकार्षीत् ॥८॥
yuddhe droNasya hastisthiraraNabhagadatteritaM vaiShNavAstraM
vakShasyAdhatta chakrasthagitaravimahAH prArdayatsindhurAjam 
nAgAstre karNamukte kShitimavanamayan kevalaM kRttamauliM
tatre trApi pArthaM kimiva nahi bhavAn pANDavAnAmakArShIt 8

युद्धे द्रोणस्य हस्तिस्थिररणभगदत्तेरितं वैष्णवास्त्रं
In the fight with Drona, seated firmly on an elephant, as Bhagadatta employed the Vaishnava astra,
वक्षस्याधत्त चक्रस्थगितरविमहा: प्रार्दयत्सिन्धुराजम् ।
you absorbed it into your chest! You hid the rays of the Sun with your chakra, causing Jayadratha's death!
नागास्त्रे कर्णमुक्ते क्षितिमवनमयन् केवलं कृत्तमौलिं
When Karna let out the Nagastra, you sunk your chariot into the ground letting it just cut out the crown!
तत्रे त्रापि पार्थं किमिव नहि भवान् पाण्डवानामकार्षीत् ॥८॥
In all these situations you saved Arjuna! What all did you not do for the sake of Pandavas!

युद्धादौ तीर्थगामी स खलु हलधरो नैमिशक्षेत्रमृच्छन्
अप्रत्युत्थायिसूतक्षयकृदथ सुतं तत्पदे कल्पयित्वा ।
यज्ञघ्नं वल्कलं पर्वणि परिदलयन् स्नाततीर्थो रणान्ते
सम्प्राप्तो भीमदुर्योधनरणमशमं वीक्ष्य यात: पुरीं ते ॥९॥
yuddhAdau tIrthagAmI sa khalu haladharo naimishakShetramRchChan
apratyutthAyisUtakShayakRdatha sutaM tatpade kalpayitvA 
yajnaghnaM valkalaM parvaNi paridalayan snAtatIrtho raNAnte
samprApto bhImaduryodhanaraNamashamaM vIkShya yAtaH purIM te 9

युद्धादौ तीर्थगामी स खलु हलधरो नैमिशक्षेत्रमृच्छन्
At the start of the battle, Balarama going to the Naimisharanya for pilgrimage,
अप्रत्युत्थायिसूतक्षयकृदथ सुतं तत्पदे कल्पयित्वा ।
killing Suta who dishonoured him by not getting up, and installing his son in his place,
यज्ञघ्नं वल्कलं पर्वणि परिदलयन् स्नाततीर्थो रणान्ते
slaying the asura, Valkala who was destroying the Yajnas on full moon and new moon days, finishing his pilgrimage, at the end of the battle,
सम्प्राप्तो भीमदुर्योधनरणमशमं वीक्ष्य यात: पुरीं ते ॥९॥
arriving there and seeing the fierce battle between Bheema and Duryodhana, went back to your city!

संसुप्तद्रौपदेयक्षपणहतधियं द्रौणिमेत्य त्वदुक्त्या
तन्मुक्तं ब्राह्ममस्त्रं समहृत विजयो मौलिरत्नं च जह्रे ।
उच्छित्यै पाण्डवानां पुनरपि च विशत्युत्तरागर्भमस्त्रे
रक्षन्नङ्गुष्ठमात्र: किल जठरमगाश्चक्रपाणिर्विभो त्वम् ॥१०॥
saMsuptadraupadeyakShapaNahatadhiyaM drauNimetya tvaduktyA
tanmuktaM brAhmamastraM samahRta vijayo mauliratnaM cha jahre 
uchChityai pANDavAnAM punarapi cha vishatyuttarAgarbhamastre
rakShannanguShThamAtraH kila jaTharamagAshchakrapANirvibho tvam 10

संसुप्तद्रौपदेयक्षपणहतधियं द्रौणिमेत्य त्वदुक्त्या
Upon your words, going to the son of Drona, Ashwatthama, who intended to kill the sons of Draupadi as they were asleep,
तन्मुक्तं ब्राह्ममस्त्रं समहृत विजयो मौलिरत्नं च जह्रे ।
retracting the Brahmastra employed by him, Arjuna, took off his crest jewel!
उच्छित्यै पाण्डवानां पुनरपि च विशत्युत्तरागर्भमस्त्रे
To root out the lineage of Pandavas, once again as he employed the astra on Uttara's womb,
रक्षन्नङ्गुष्ठमात्र: किल जठरमगाश्चक्रपाणिर्विभो त्वम् ॥१०॥
with a form of the size of a thumb, didn't you enter her womb with your sudarshana chakra, O Vibhu!

धर्मौघं धर्मसूनोरभिदधदखिलं छन्दमृत्युस्स भीष्म:
त्वां पश्यन् भक्तिभूम्नैव हि सपदि ययौ निष्कलब्रह्मभूयम् ।
संयाज्याथाश्वमेधैस्त्रिभिरतिमहितैर्धर्मजं पूर्णकामं
सम्प्राप्तो द्वरकां त्वं पवनपुरपते पाहि मां सर्वरोगात् ॥११॥
dharmaughaM dharmasUnorabhidadhadakhilaM ChandamRtyussa bhIShmaH
tvAM pashyan bhaktibhUmnaiva hi sapadi yayau niShkalabrahmabhUyam 
saMyAjyAthAshvamedhaistribhiratimahitairdharmajaM pUrNakAmaM
samprApto dvarakAM tvaM pavanapurapate pAhi mAM sarvarogAt 11

धर्मौघं धर्मसूनोरभिदधदखिलं छन्दमृत्युस्स भीष्म:
That Bhishma, who is of wilful mortality, teaching the entire subject of Dharma to Dharmaraja,
त्वां पश्यन् भक्तिभूम्नैव हि सपदि ययौ निष्कलब्रह्मभूयम् ।
gazing upon you with intense devotion, dissolved into the emptiness of creation!
संयाज्याथाश्वमेधैस्त्रिभिरतिमहितैर्धर्मजं पूर्णकामं
Making Yudhishthira perform three great Ashwamedhas fulfilling his desires,
सम्प्राप्तो द्वरकां त्वं पवनपुरपते पाहि मां सर्वरोगात् ॥११॥
you returned to Dwaraka, O Lord of the City of Wind! Protect me from all diseases!
