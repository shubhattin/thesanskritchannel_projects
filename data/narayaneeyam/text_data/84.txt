अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

चतुरशीतितमम् दशकम्
chaturashItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-fourth group of Ten shlokas

समन्तपञ्चक यात्रा बन्धुमित्रादि समागम: च
samantapanchaka yAtrA bandhumitrAdi samAgamaH cha

Journey to Samantha Panchaka, Meeting with Friends and Relatives

क्वचिदथ तपनोपरागकाले पुरि निदधत् कृतवर्मकामसूनू ।
यदुकुलमहिलावृत: सुतीर्थं समुपगतोऽसि समन्तपञ्चकाख्यम् ॥१॥
kvachidatha tapanoparAgakAle puri nidadhat kRtavarmakAmasUnU 
yadukulamahilAvRtaH sutIrthaM samupagatosi samantapanchakAkhyam 1

क्वचिदथ तपनोपरागकाले पुरि निदधत् कृतवर्मकामसूनू ।
Then, once during a solar eclipse, placing Kritavarma and Aniruddha in the city,
यदुकुलमहिलावृत: सुतीर्थं समुपगतोऽसि समन्तपञ्चकाख्यम् ॥१॥
surrounded by the women of Yadukula, you went to a holy site by the name of Samantapanchaka.

बहुतरजनताहिताय तत्र त्वमपि पुनन् विनिमज्य तीर्थतोयम् ।
द्विजगणपरिमुक्तवित्तराशि: सममिलथा: कुरुपाण्डवादिमित्रै: ॥२॥
bahutarajanatAhitAya tatra tvamapi punan vinimajya tIrthatoyam 
dvijagaNaparimuktavittarAshiH samamilathAH kurupANDavAdimitraiH 2

बहुतरजनताहिताय तत्र त्वमपि पुनन् विनिमज्य तीर्थतोयम् ।
For various kinds of well-being of the people, you too performing pujas there and taking a dip in those waters,
द्विजगणपरिमुक्तवित्तराशि: सममिलथा: कुरुपाण्डवादिमित्रै: ॥२॥
offering donatins to the groups of Dvijas, spoke to all the Kaurava and Pandava friends gathered there!

तव खलु दयिताजनै: समेता द्रुपदसुता त्वयि गाढभक्तिभारा ।
तदुदितभवदाहृतिप्रकारै: अतिमुमुदे सममन्यभामिनीभि: ॥३॥
tava khalu dayitAjanaiH sametA drupadasutA tvayi gADhabhaktibhArA 
taduditabhavadAhRtiprakAraiH atimumude samamanyabhAminIbhiH 3

तव खलु दयिताजनै: समेता द्रुपदसुता त्वयि गाढभक्तिभारा ।
Didn't the daughter of Drupada, Draupadi, who had a deep devotion for you, along with your beloved wives, 
तदुदितभवदाहृतिप्रकारै: अतिमुमुदे सममन्यभामिनीभि: ॥३॥
with the stories of various ways in which they married you, was extremely delighted!

तदनु च भगवन् निरीक्ष्य गोपानतिकुतुकादुपगम्य मानयित्वा।
चिरतरविरहातुराङ्गरेखा: पशुपवधू: सरसं त्वमन्वयासी: ॥४॥
tadanu cha bhagavan nirIkShya gopAnatikutukAdupagamya mAnayitvA
chirataravirahAturAngarekhAH pashupavadhUH sarasaM tvamanvayAsIH 4

तदनु च भगवन् निरीक्ष्य गोपानतिकुतुकादुपगम्य मानयित्वा।
And then, O Lord, seeing the Gopas with great joy, approaching them and honouring them,
चिरतरविरहातुराङ्गरेखा: पशुपवधू: सरसं त्वमन्वयासी: ॥४॥
the cowherd women who were emaciated due to their long separation from you, you playfully approached them!

सपदि च भवदीक्षणोत्सवेन प्रमुषितमानहृदां नितम्बिनीनाम् ।
अतिरसपरिमुक्तकञ्चुलीके परिचयहृद्यतरे कुचे न्यलैषी: ॥५॥
sapadi cha bhavadIkShaNotsavena pramuShitamAnahRdAM nitambinInAm 
atirasaparimuktakanchulIke parichayahRdyatare kuche nyalaiShIH 5

सपदि च भवदीक्षणोत्सवेन प्रमुषितमानहृदां नितम्बिनीनाम् ।
Looking at you suddenly in joy, with the sorrows of their hearts wiped away, as the beautiful women,
अतिरसपरिमुक्तकञ्चुलीके परिचयहृद्यतरे कुचे न्यलैषी: ॥५॥
with immense delight had their upper garments slipping, you were immersed again in the familiar sight of their charming bosoms!

रिपुजनकलहै: पुन: पुनर्मे समुपगतैरियती विलम्बनाऽभूत् ।
इति कृतपरिरम्भणेत्वयि द्राक् अतिविवशा खलु राधिका निलिल्ये ॥६॥
ripujanakalahaiH punaH punarme samupagatairiyatI vilambanAbhUt 
iti kRtaparirambhaNetvayi drAk ativivashA khalu rAdhikA nililye 6

रिपुजनकलहै: पुन: पुनर्मे समुपगतैरियती विलम्बनाऽभूत् ।
"Because of the conflicts with my enemies again and again, there has been a delay in meeting you once more!"
इति कृतपरिरम्भणेत्वयि द्राक् अतिविवशा खलु राधिका निलिल्ये ॥६॥
thus saying as you embraced, didn't Radhika at once, beyond her control become immersed in you!

अपगतविरहव्यथास्तदा ता रहसि विधाय ददाथ तत्त्वबोधम् ।
परमसुखचिदात्मकोऽहमात्मेत्युदयतु व: स्फुटमेव चेतसीति ॥७॥
apagatavirahavyathAstadA tA rahasi vidhAya dadAtha tattvabodham 
paramasukhachidAtmakohamAtmetyudayatu vaH sphuTameva chetasIti 7

अपगतविरहव्यथास्तदा ता रहसि विधाय ददाथ तत्त्वबोधम् ।
With their pangs of separation then having been removed, you in secrecy bestowed upon them the knowledge of true realization,
परमसुखचिदात्मकोऽहमात्मेत्युदयतु व: स्फुटमेव चेतसीति ॥७॥
by saying that, "May it dawn in your minds that I am of the nature of the ultimate bliss of consciousness! I am the very self!"

सुखरसपरिमिश्रितो वियोग: किमपि पुराऽभवदुद्धवोपदेशै: ।
समभवदमुत: परं तु तासां परमसुखैक्यमयी भवद्विचिन्ता ॥८॥
sukharasaparimishrito viyogaH kimapi purAbhavaduddhavopadeshaiH 
samabhavadamutaH paraM tu tAsAM paramasukhaikyamayI bhavadvichintA 8

सुखरसपरिमिश्रितो वियोग: किमपि पुराऽभवदुद्धवोपदेशै: ।
A kind of separation mixed with the essence of joy, was experienced by them long before upon Uddhava's preaching.
समभवदमुत: परं तु तासां परमसुखैक्यमयी भवद्विचिन्ता ॥८॥
But now, they now experienced the ultimate bliss of your mere thought!

मुनिवरनिवहैस्तवाथ पित्रा दुरितशमाय शुभानि पृच्छ्यमानै: ।
त्वयि सति किमिदं शुभान्तरैरित्युरुहसितैरपि याजितस्तदाऽसौ ॥९॥
munivaranivahaistavAtha pitrA duritashamAya shubhAni pRchChyamAnaiH 
tvayi sati kimidaM shubhAntarairityuruhasitairapi yAjitastadAsau 9

मुनिवरनिवहैस्तवाथ पित्रा दुरितशमाय शुभानि पृच्छ्यमानै: ।
As the groups of sages there were then inquired by your father regarding any auspicious signs, and pacification of troubles,
त्वयि सति किमिदं शुभान्तरैरित्युरुहसितैरपि याजितस्तदाऽसौ ॥९॥
despite laughing immensely, that when you are present, where is there a chance of anything but auspiciousness, they still made him perform the appropriate rituals.

सुमहति यजने वितायमाने प्रमुदितमित्रजने सहैव गोपा: ।
यदुजनमहितास्त्रिमासमात्रं भवदनुषङ्गरसं पुरेव भेजु : ॥१०॥
sumahati yajane vitAyamAne pramuditamitrajane sahaiva gopAH 
yadujanamahitAstrimAsamAtraM bhavadanuShangarasaM pureva bheju H 10

सुमहति यजने वितायमाने प्रमुदितमित्रजने सहैव गोपा: ।
As that great Yajna was being performed, with the delighted groups of  friends, and all the Gopas,
यदुजनमहितास्त्रिमासमात्रं भवदनुषङ्गरसं पुरेव भेजु : ॥१०॥
the Yadavas celebrated for three months, and experienced the pleasure of your company as they did before!

व्यपगमसमये समेत्य राधां दृढमुपगूह्य निरीक्ष्य वीतखेदाम् ।
प्रमुदितहृदय: पुरं प्रयात: पवनपुरेश्वर पाहि मां गदेभ्य: ॥११॥
vyapagamasamaye sametya rAdhAM dRDhamupagUhya nirIkShya vItakhedAm 
pramuditahRdayaH puraM prayAtaH pavanapureshvara pAhi mAM gadebhyaH 11

व्यपगमसमये समेत्य राधां दृढमुपगूह्य निरीक्ष्य वीतखेदाम् ।
At the time of departure, approaching Radha, taking her in a tight embrace and seeing her now devoid of sorrow,
प्रमुदितहृदय: पुरं प्रयात: पवनपुरेश्वर पाहि मां गदेभ्य: ॥११॥
with a gladdened heart, you returned to your city, O lord of the City of Wind! Protect me from my ailments!
