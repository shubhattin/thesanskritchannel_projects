अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकाशीतितमम् दशकम्
ekAshItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-first group of Ten shlokas

कालिन्दी आदिपरिग्रह: पारिजात हरणं च
kAlindI Adi parigrahaH pArijAta haraNam cha

Marrying Kalindhi and others, and Stealing Parijata

स्निग्धां मुग्धां सततमपि तां लालयन् सत्यभामां
यातो भूय: सह खलु तया याज्ञसेनीविवाहम् ।
पार्थप्रीत्यै पुनरपि मनागास्थितो हस्तिपुर्यां
शक्रप्रस्थं पुरमपि विभो संविधायागतोऽभू: ॥१॥
snigdhAM mugdhAM satatamapi tAM lAlayan satyabhAmAM
yAto bhUyaH saha khalu tayA yAjnasenIvivAham 
pArthaprItyai punarapi manAgAsthito hastipuryAM
shakraprasthaM puramapi vibho saMvidhAyAgatobhUH 1

स्निग्धां मुग्धां सततमपि तां लालयन् सत्यभामां
The loving and beautiful Satyabhama, rejoicing with her for long,
यातो भूय: सह खलु तया याज्ञसेनीविवाहम् ।
didn't you then attend Panchali's marriage along with her!
पार्थप्रीत्यै पुनरपि मनागास्थितो हस्तिपुर्यां
Out of your love for Arjuna, you then stayed back for some more time at Hastinapura,
सशक्रप्रस्थं पुरमपि विभो संविधायागतोऽभू: ॥१॥
and then O Vibhu, you established the city of Indraprastha, and returned!

भद्रां भद्रां भवदवरजां कौरवेणार्थ्यमानां
त्वद्वाचा तामहृत कुहनामस्करी शक्रसूनु: ।
तत्र क्रुद्धं बलमनुनयन् प्रत्यगास्तेन सार्धं
शक्रप्रस्थं प्रियसखमुदे सत्यभामासहाय: ॥२॥
bhadrAM bhadrAM bhavadavarajAM kauraveNArthyamAnAM
tvadvAchA tAmahRta kuhanAmaskarI shakrasUnuH 
tatra kruddhaM balamanunayan pratyagAstena sArdhaM
shakraprasthaM priyasakhamude satyabhAmAsahAyaH 2

भद्रां भद्रां भवदवरजां कौरवेणार्थ्यमानां
The auspicious Subhadra, your younger sister, who was being sought in marriage by the Kauravas,
त्वद्वाचा तामहृत कुहनामस्करी शक्रसूनु: ।
upon your words, the son of Indra, Arjuna, in the form of a fake Sanyasi, took her away!
तत्र क्रुद्धं बलमनुनयन् प्रत्यगास्तेन सार्धं
As Balarama was angered by this, you took him to
शक्रप्रस्थं प्रियसखमुदे सत्यभामासहाय: ॥२॥
Indraprastha, taking Satyabhama along, delighting your dear friend!

तत्र क्रीडन्नपि च यमुनाकूलदृष्टां गृहीत्वा
तां कालिन्दीं नगरमगम: खाण्डवप्रीणिताग्नि: ।
भ्रातृत्रस्तां प्रणयविवशां देव पैतृष्वसेयीं
राज्ञां मध्ये सपदि जहृषे मित्रविन्दामवन्तीम् ॥३॥
tatra krIDannapi cha yamunAkUladRShTAM gRhItvA
tAM kAlindIM nagaramagamaH khANDavaprINitAgniH 
bhrAtRtrastAM praNayavivashAM deva paitRShvaseyIM
rAjnAM madhye sapadi jahRShe mitravindAmavantIm 3

तत्र क्रीडन्नपि च यमुनाकूलदृष्टां गृहीत्वा
Sporting there, and there on the banks of Yamuna, seeing
तां कालिन्दीं नगरमगम: खाण्डवप्रीणिताग्नि: ।
Kalindi, and taking her as your wife, gladdening Agni, by your offering of Khandava forrest, you returned to your city!
भ्रातृत्रस्तां प्रणयविवशां देव पैतृष्वसेयीं
Your aunt's daughter, who was afraid of her brother, and was helplessly in love with you,
राज्ञां मध्ये सपदि जहृषे मित्रविन्दामवन्तीम् ॥३॥
that Mitravinda, you took her as yours at once, amidst the presence of kings!

सत्यां गत्वा पुनरुदवहो नग्नजिन्नन्दनां तां
बध्वा सप्तापि च वृषवरान् सप्तमूर्तिर्निमेषात् ।
भद्रां नाम प्रददुरथ ते देव सन्तर्दनाद्या:
तत्सोदर्या वरद भवत: साऽपि पैतृष्वसेयी ॥४॥
satyAM gatvA punarudavaho nagnajinnandanAM tAM
badhvA saptApi cha vRShavarAn saptamUrtirnimeShAt 
bhadrAM nAma pradaduratha te deva santardanAdyAH
tatsodaryA varada bhavataH sApi paitRShvaseyI 4

सत्यां गत्वा पुनरुदवहो नग्नजिन्नन्दनां तां
Then again, marrying Satya, the daughter of Nagnajit,
बध्वा सप्तापि च वृषवरान् सप्तमूर्तिर्निमेषात् ।
by binding all the Seven mighty bulls, in a minute, by taking on seven forms;
भद्रां नाम प्रददुरथ ते देव सन्तर्दनाद्या:
Being given Bhadra, O Lord, by Santardana and other
तत्सोदर्या वरद भवत: साऽपि पैतृष्वसेयी ॥४॥
brothers of hers, O Bestower of Boons, you took her too, who was your aunt's daughter.

पार्थाद्यैरप्यकृतलवनं तोयमात्राभिलक्ष्यं
लक्षं छित्वा शफरमवृथा लक्ष्मणां मद्रकन्याम् ।
अष्टावेवं तव समभवन् वल्लभास्तत्र मध्ये
शुश्रोथ त्वं सुरपतिगिरा भौमदुश्चेष्टितानि ॥५॥
pArthAdyairapyakRtalavanaM toyamAtrAbhilakShyaM
lakShaM ChitvA shapharamavRthA lakShmaNAM madrakanyAm 
aShTAvevaM tava samabhavan vallabhAstatra madhye
shushrotha tvaM surapatigirA bhaumadushcheShTitAni 5

पार्थाद्यैरप्यकृतलवनं तोयमात्राभिलक्ष्यं
The fish target seen only in it's reflection in the water, which couldn't even by hit by Arjuna and others,
लक्षं छित्वा शफरमवृथा लक्ष्मणां मद्रकन्याम् ।
hitting that target, you took on lakShmaNA, the princess of Madra!
अष्टावेवं तव समभवन् वल्लभास्तत्र मध्ये
Thus, as you had eight wives, within that time,
शुश्रोथ त्वं सुरपतिगिरा भौमदुश्चेष्टितानि ॥५॥
you heard of the misdeeds of Bhauma through Indra's words!

स्मृतायातं पक्षिप्रवरमधिरूढस्त्वमगमो
वहन्नङ्के भामामुपवनमिवारातिभवनम् ।
विभिन्दन् दुर्गाणि त्रुटितपृतनाशोणितरसै:
पुरं तावत् प्राग्ज्योतिषमकुरुथा: शोणितपुरम् ॥६॥
smRtAyAtaM pakShipravaramadhirUDhastvamagamo
vahannanke bhAmAmupavanamivArAtibhavanam 
vibhindan durgANi truTitapRtanAshoNitarasaiH
puraM tAvat prAgjyotiShamakuruthAH shoNitapuram 6

स्मृतायातं पक्षिप्रवरमधिरूढस्त्वमगमो
Arriving upon your remembrance, riding on top of Garuda, you went,
वहन्नङ्के भामामुपवनमिवारातिभवनम् ।
to the enemy's abode, carrying Satyabhama on your lap, as if going to a garden!
विभिन्दन् दुर्गाणि त्रुटितपृतनाशोणितरसै:
Destroying its ramparts, and with the streams of blood of the vanquished armies
पुरं तावत् प्राग्ज्योतिषमकुरुथा: शोणितपुरम् ॥६॥
you made that city of Pragjyotisha Pura, into Shonitapura, the city that bleeds red!

मुरस्त्वां पञ्चास्यो जलधिवनमध्यादुदपतत्
स चक्रे चक्रेण प्रदलितशिरा मङ्क्षु भवता ।
चतुर्दन्तैर्दन्तावलपतिभिरिन्धानसमरं
रथाङ्गेन छित्वा नरकमकरोस्तीर्णनरकम् ॥७॥
murastvAM panchAsyo jaladhivanamadhyAdudapatat
sa chakre chakreNa pradalitashirA mankShu bhavatA 
chaturdantairdantAvalapatibhirindhAnasamaraM
rathAngena ChitvA narakamakarostIrNanarakam 7

मुरस्त्वां पञ्चास्यो जलधिवनमध्यादुदपतत्
The five-faced Mura, pounced upon you, from the vast ocean like forest,
स चक्रे चक्रेण प्रदलितशिरा मङ्क्षु भवता ।
whom you beheaded at once by the throw of your chakra!
चतुर्दन्तैर्दन्तावलपतिभिरिन्धानसमरं
As he was putting up a fight with mighty four-tusked elephants,
रथाङ्गेन छित्वा नरकमकरोस्तीर्णनरकम् ॥७॥
slicing him off with your chakra, you made even that Narakasura, cross over Hell:Naraka!

स्तुतो भूम्या राज्यं सपदि भगदत्तेऽस्य तनये
गजञ्चैकं दत्वा प्रजिघयिथ नागान्निजपुरीम् ।
खलेनाबद्धानां स्वगतमनसां षोडश पुन:
सहस्राणि स्त्रीणामपि च धनराशिं च विपुलं ॥८॥
stuto bhUmyA rAjyaM sapadi bhagadattesya tanaye
gajanchaikaM datvA prajighayitha nAgAnnijapurIm 
khalenAbaddhAnAM svagatamanasAM ShoDasha punaH
sahasrANi strINAmapi cha dhanarAshiM cha vipulaM 8

स्तुतो भूम्या राज्यं सपदि भगदत्तेऽस्य तनये
Having been worshipped by Bhumi, then to Narakasura's son, Bhagadatta,
गजञ्चैकं दत्वा प्रजिघयिथ नागान्निजपुरीम् ।
presenting him with an elephant, you sent the rest to your own city!
खलेनाबद्धानां स्वगतमनसां षोडश पुन:
And then, the ones who were bound by that wicked one, whose minds were fixed on you, those sixteen
सहस्राणि स्त्रीणामपि च धनराशिं च विपुलं ॥८॥
thousand women, and massive amounts of wealth, you sent to Dwaraka too!

भौमापाहृतकुण्डलं तददितेर्दातुं प्रयातो दिवं
शक्राद्यैर्महित: समं दयितया द्युस्त्रीषु दत्तह्रिया ।
हृत्वा कल्पतरुं रुषाभिपतितं जित्वेन्द्रमभ्यागम:
तत्तु श्रीमददोष ईदृश इति व्याख्यातुमेवाकृथा: ॥९॥
bhaumApAhRtakuNDalaM tadaditerdAtuM prayAto divaM
shakrAdyairmahitaH samaM dayitayA dyustrIShu dattahriyA 
hRtvA kalpataruM ruShAbhipatitaM jitvendramabhyAgamaH
tattu shrImadadoSha IdRsha iti vyAkhyAtumevAkRthAH 9

भौमापाहृतकुण्डलं तददितेर्दातुं प्रयातो दिवं
As you went to Swarga to present back the earrings of Aditi, stolen by Narakasura,
शक्राद्यैर्महित: समं दयितया द्युस्त्रीषु दत्तह्रिया ।
as Satyabhama, who accompanied you put the other celestial women there to shame with her beauty,
हृत्वा कल्पतरुं रुषाभिपतितं जित्वेन्द्रमभ्यागम:
taking the Kalpavriksha there, defeating the enraged Indra, you returned!
तत्तु श्रीमददोष ईदृश इति व्याख्यातुमेवाकृथा: ॥९॥
This has definitely been done by you to demonstrate the nature of pride born out of wealth!

कल्पद्रुं सत्यभामाभवनभुवि सृजन् द्व्यष्टसाहस्रयोषा:
स्वीकृत्य प्रत्यगारं विहितबहुवपुर्लालयन् केलिभेदै: ।
आश्चर्यान्नारदालोकितविविधगतिस्तत्र तत्रापि गेहे
भूय: सर्वासु कुर्वन् दश दश तनयान् पाहि वातालयेश ॥१०॥
kalpadruM satyabhAmAbhavanabhuvi sRjan dvyaShTasAhasrayoShAH
svIkRtya pratyagAraM vihitabahuvapurlAlayan kelibhedaiH 
AshcharyAnnAradAlokitavividhagatistatra tatrApi gehe
bhUyaH sarvAsu kurvan dasha dasha tanayAn pAhi vAtAlayesha 10

कल्पद्रुं सत्यभामाभवनभुवि सृजन् द्व्यष्टसाहस्रयोषा:
Planting the Kalpavriksha in Satyabhama's courtyard, the 16000 women,
स्वीकृत्य प्रत्यगारं विहितबहुवपुर्लालयन् केलिभेदै: ।
accepting them as your wives, in each and every house, taking on as many forms, indulging them with various kinds of plays,
आश्चर्यान्नारदालोकितविविधगतिस्तत्र तत्रापि गेहे
seen by Narada in great astonishment in your various different forms in each and every house,
भूय: सर्वासु कुर्वन् दश दश तनयान् पाहि वातालयेश ॥१०॥
then, making ten children each with all of those wives, protect me, O Lord of the Abode of Wind!
