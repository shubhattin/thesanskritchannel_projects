अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

द्व्यशीतितमम् दशकम्
dvyashItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-second group of Ten shlokas

बाणयुद्धम् नृग मोक्ष: च
bANayuddham nRga mokShaH cha

Defeating Banasura and Salvation of Nrga

प्रद्युम्नो रौक्मिणेय: स खलु तव कला शम्बरेणाहृतस्तं
हत्वा रत्या सहाप्तो निजपुरमहरद्रुक्मिकन्यां च धन्याम् ।
तत्पुत्रोऽथानिरुद्धो गुणनिधिरवहद्रोचनां रुक्मिपौत्रीं
तत्रोद्वाहे गतस्त्वं न्यवधि मुसलिना रुक्म्यपि द्यूतवैरात् ॥१॥
pradyumno raukmiNeyaH sa khalu tava kalA shambareNAhRtastaM
hatvA ratyA sahApto nijapuramaharadrukmikanyAM cha dhanyAm 
tatputrothAniruddho guNanidhiravahadrochanAM rukmipautrIM
tatrodvAhe gatastvaM nyavadhi musalinA rukmyapi dyUtavairAt 1

प्रद्युम्नो रौक्मिणेय: स खलु तव कला शम्बरेणाहृतस्तं
Pradyumna, the son of Rukmini, born as an aspect of you, when he was abducted by Shambara,
हत्वा रत्या सहाप्तो निजपुरमहरद्रुक्मिकन्यां च धन्याम् ।
he slayed him and returned to Dwaraka, along with Rati. He then brought the fortunate daughter of Rukmi too in marriage,
तत्पुत्रोऽथानिरुद्धो गुणनिधिरवहद्रोचनां रुक्मिपौत्रीं
and their son, Aniruddha, married the treasure of virtues, Rochana, the grand-daughter of Rukmi.
तत्रोद्वाहे गतस्त्वं न्यवधि मुसलिना रुक्म्यपि द्यूतवैरात् ॥१॥
And in the marriage that you attended, Rukmi was slain by Balarama with his mace over a quarrel in a game of dice!

बाणस्य सा बलिसुतस्य सहस्रबाहो:
माहेश्वरस्य महिता दुहिता किलोषा ।
त्वत्पौत्रमेनमनिरुद्धमदृष्टपूर्वं
स्वप्नेऽनुभूय भगवन् विरहातुराऽभूत् ॥२॥
bANasya sA balisutasya sahasrabAhoH
mAheshvarasya mahitA duhitA kiloShA 
tvatpautramenamaniruddhamadRShTapUrvaM
svapnenubhUya bhagavan virahAturAbhUt 2

बाणस्य सा बलिसुतस्य सहस्रबाहो:
Of Bana, the son of Bali, who had a thousand arms,
माहेश्वरस्य महिता दुहिता किलोषा ।
and who a great devotee of Shiva, wasn't there a daughter named Usha!
त्वत्पौत्रमेनमनिरुद्धमदृष्टपूर्वं
Despite she not having seen your Grand-son Aniruddha before
स्वप्नेऽनुभूय भगवन् विरहातुराऽभूत् ॥२॥
dreaming of him, O Lord, she pined for him eagerly! 

योगिन्यतीव कुशला खलु चित्रलेखा
तस्या: सखी विलिखती तरुणानशेषान् ।
तत्रानिरुद्धमुषया विदितं निशायाम्
आनेष्ट योगबलतो भवतो निकेतात् ॥३॥
yoginyatIva kushalA khalu chitralekhA
tasyAH sakhI vilikhatI taruNAnasheShAn 
tatrAniruddhamuShayA viditaM nishAyAm
AneShTa yogabalato bhavato niketAt 3

योगिन्यतीव कुशला खलु चित्रलेखा
Didn't an extremely skilled Yogini, by the name of Chitralekha,
तस्या: सखी विलिखती तरुणानशेषान् ।
who was Usha's friend, who drew the portraits of many young men,
तत्रानिरुद्धमुषया विदितं निशायाम्
at her place, recognizing Aniruddha's portrait, in the night,
आनेष्ट योगबलतो भवतो निकेतात् ॥३॥
she made her bring him from your residence through her Yogic powers!

कन्यापुरे दयितया सुखमारमन्तं
चैनं कथञ्चन बबन्धुषि शर्वबन्धौ ।
श्रीनारदोक्ततदुदन्तदुरन्तरोषै:
त्वं तस्य शोणितपुरं यदुभिर्न्यरुन्धा: ॥४॥
kanyApure dayitayA sukhamAramantaM
chainaM kathanchana babandhuShi sharvabandhau 
shrInAradoktatadudantadurantaroShaiH
tvaM tasya shoNitapuraM yadubhirnyarundhAH 4

कन्यापुरे दयितया सुखमारमन्तं
As he was rejoicing in bliss in the princess's chambers,
चैनं कथञ्चन बबन्धुषि शर्वबन्धौ ।
as her father, that Shiva's devotee tied him up somehow,
श्रीनारदोक्ततदुदन्तदुरन्तरोषै:
hearing of it from Narada, and extremely angered,
त्वं तस्य शोणितपुरं यदुभिर्न्यरुन्धा: ॥४॥
you attacked his city of Shonitapura with Yadavas!

पुरीपालश्शैलप्रियदुहितृनाथोऽस्य भगवान्
समं भूतव्रातैर्यदुबलमशङ्कं निरुरुधे ।
महाप्राणो बाणो झटिति युयुधानेनयुयुधे
गुह: प्रद्युम्नेन त्वमपि पुरहन्त्रा जघटिषे ॥५॥
purIpAlashshailapriyaduhitRnAthosya bhagavAn
samaM bhUtavrAtairyadubalamashankaM nirurudhe 
mahAprANo bANo jhaTiti yuyudhAnenayuyudhe
guhaH pradyumnena tvamapi purahantrA jaghaTiShe 5

पुरीपालश्शैलप्रियदुहितृनाथोऽस्य भगवान्
The protector of that city, the dearest consort of the mountain's daughter, Lord Shiva,
समं भूतव्रातैर्यदुबलमशङ्कं निरुरुधे ।
along with his mighty Bhuta Ganas, opposed the attack of Yadavas!
महाप्राणो बाणो झटिति युयुधानेनयुयुधे
The valiant Bana quickly took up a fight with Yuyudhana,
गुह: प्रद्युम्नेन त्वमपि पुरहन्त्रा जघटिषे ॥५॥
Guha started fighting with Pradyumna, and you opposed Shiva yourself!

निरुद्धाशेषास्त्रे मुमुहुषि तवास्त्रेण गिरिशे
द्रुता भूता भीता: प्रमथकुलवीरा: प्रमथिता: ।
परास्कन्द्त् स्कन्द: कुसुमशरबाणैश्च सचिव:
स कुम्भाण्डो भाण्डं नवमिव बलेनाशु बिभिदे ॥६॥
niruddhAsheShAstre mumuhuShi tavAstreNa girishe
drutA bhUtA bhItAH pramathakulavIrAH pramathitAH 
parAskandt skandaH kusumasharabANaishcha sachivaH
sa kumbhANDo bhANDaM navamiva balenAshu bibhide 6

निरुद्धाशेषास्त्रे मुमुहुषि तवास्त्रेण गिरिशे
As you blocked all his weapons and as Shiva fainted with one of your astras,
द्रुता भूता भीता: प्रमथकुलवीरा: प्रमथिता: ।
frightened, all the Bhutas fled and the warriors of the Pramatha clan were defeated!
परास्कन्द्त् स्कन्द: कुसुमशरबाणैश्च सचिव:
Their leader Skanda was defeated by the flowery arrows of Pradyumna's bow,
स कुम्भाण्डो भाण्डं नवमिव बलेनाशु बिभिदे ॥६॥
and that Kumbhaanda was effortlessly crushed by Balarama like a fresh pot!

चापानां पञ्चशत्या प्रसभमुपगते छिन्नचापेऽथ बाणे
व्यर्थे याते समेतो ज्वरपतिरशनैरज्वरि त्वज्ज्वरेण ।
ज्ञानी स्तुत्वाऽथ दत्वा तव चरितजुषां विज्वरं स ज्वरोऽगात्
प्रायोऽन्तर्ज्ञानवन्तोऽपि च बहुतमसा रौद्रचेष्टा हि रौद्रा: ॥७॥
chApAnAM panchashatyA prasabhamupagate ChinnachApetha bANe
vyarthe yAte sameto jvarapatirashanairajvari tvajjvareNa 
jnAnI stutvAtha datvA tava charitajuShAM vijvaraM sa jvarogAt
prAyontarjnAnavantopi cha bahutamasA raudracheShTA hi raudrAH 7

चापानां पञ्चशत्या प्रसभमुपगते छिन्नचापेऽथ बाणे
As Bana charged at you by releasing five hundred arrows, but as his bow was shattered,
व्यर्थे याते समेतो ज्वरपतिरशनैरज्वरि त्वज्ज्वरेण ।
returning again in vain, under the heat of his devotion to Shiva, was burnt up by your fire that spread forth!
ज्ञानी स्तुत्वाऽथ दत्वा तव चरितजुषां विज्वरं स ज्वरोऽगात्
His fire of rage, then praising you and withdrawing itself from those who worship you, left!
प्रायोऽन्तर्ज्ञानवन्तोऽपि च बहुतमसा रौद्रचेष्टा हि रौद्रा: ॥७॥
May be, desipe being knowledgeable wihthin, due to the enhanced tamo guna, the devotees of rudra are of wild and cruel deeds!

बाणं नानायुधोग्रं पुनरभिपतितं दर्पदोषाद्वितन्वन्
निर्लूनाशेषदोषं सपदि बुबुधुषा शङ्करेणोपगीत: ।
तद्वाचा शिष्टबाहुद्वितयमुभयतो निर्भयं तत्प्रियं तं
मुक्त्वा तद्दत्तमानो निजपुरमगम: सानिरुद्ध: सहोष: ॥८॥
bANaM nAnAyudhograM punarabhipatitaM darpadoShAdvitanvan
nirlUnAsheShadoShaM sapadi bubudhuShA shankareNopagItaH 
tadvAchA shiShTabAhudvitayamubhayato nirbhayaM tatpriyaM taM
muktvA taddattamAno nijapuramagamaH sAniruddhaH sahoShaH 8

बाणं नानायुधोग्रं पुनरभिपतितं दर्पदोषाद्वितन्वन्
As Bana attacked once again with various fierce weapons, freeing him from the fault of arrogrance,
निर्लूनाशेषदोषं सपदि बुबुधुषा शङ्करेणोपगीत: ।
removing all his vices, at once you made him realize, as Shankara sang your praise!
तद्वाचा शिष्टबाहुद्वितयमुभयतो निर्भयं तत्प्रियं तं
Upon his words, leaving two of his arms unharmed, ridding him of fear, that dear one,
मुक्त्वा तद्दत्तमानो निजपुरमगम: सानिरुद्ध: सहोष: ॥८॥
liberating him, you retuned to your city, along with Aniruddha and Usha offered by him in honour!

मुहुस्तावच्छक्रं वरुणमजयो नन्दहरणे
यमं बालानीतौ दवदहनपानेऽनिलसखम् ।
विधिं वत्सस्तेये गिरिशमिह बाणस्य समरे
विभो विश्वोत्कर्षी तदयमवतारो जयति ते ॥९॥
muhustAvachChakraM varuNamajayo nandaharaNe
yamaM bAlAnItau davadahanapAnenilasakham 
vidhiM vatsasteye girishamiha bANasya samare
vibho vishvotkarShI tadayamavatAro jayati te 9

मुहुस्तावच्छक्रं वरुणमजयो नन्दहरणे
Once again, you defeated Indra. And Varuna when Nanda was abducted!
यमं बालानीतौ दवदहनपानेऽनिलसखम् ।
You defeated Yama when you brought back your Guru's son, and you defeated Agni, the friend of Vayu, when you drank up the forest fire!
विधिं वत्सस्तेये गिरिशमिह बाणस्य समरे
You defeated Brahma when he stole the children and cattle, and you defeated Shiva in Banasura's battle!
विभो विश्वोत्कर्षी तदयमवतारो जयति ते ॥९॥
O Vibhu! Hail to this incarnation of yours which exceeds all the universe!

द्विजरुषा कृकलासवपुर्धरं नृगनृपं त्रिदिवालयमापयन् ।
निजजने द्विजभक्तिमनुत्तमामुपदिशन् पवनेश्वर् पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
dvijaruShA kRkalAsavapurdharaM nRganRpaM tridivAlayamApayan 
nijajane dvijabhaktimanuttamAmupadishan pavaneshvar pAhi mAm 10

द्विजरुषा कृकलासवपुर्धरं नृगनृपं त्रिदिवालयमापयन् ।
Through a Brahmin's curse, King Nrga who was in the form of a chameleon, sending him to the abode of gods,
निजजने द्विजभक्तिमनुत्तमामुपदिशन् पवनेश्वर् पाहि माम् ॥१०॥
instructing your people, to be respectful towards the Dvijas, O Lord of Wind, protect me!
