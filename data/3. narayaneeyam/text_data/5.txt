ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

पञ्चमम् दशकम्
panchamam dashakam

The Fifth group of Ten shlokas

विराट् पुरुष उत्पत्तिः
virAT puruSha utpattiH

Origin of The Body of The Creator

व्यक्ताव्यक्तमिदं न किञ्चिदभवत्प्राक्प्राकृतप्रक्षये
मायायाम् गुणसाम्यरुद्धविकृतौ त्वय्यागतायां लयम् ।
नो मृत्युश्च तदाऽमृतं च समभून्नाह्नो न रात्रे: स्थितिः
तत्रैकस्त्वमशिष्यथा: किल परानन्दप्रकाशात्मना ॥१॥
vyaktAvyaktamidaM na kinchidabhavatprAkprAkRtaprakShaye
mAyAyAm guNasAmyaruddhavikRtau tvayyAgatAyAM layam 
no mRtyushcha tadAmRtaM cha samabhUnnAhno na rAtreH sthitiH
tatraikastvamashiShyathAH kila parAnandaprakAshAtmanA 1

व्यक्ताव्यक्तमिदं न किञ्चिदभवत्प्राक्प्राकृतप्रक्षये
Both physically apparent and inapparent creation that exists right now, none of this exists either before creation or after dissolution.
मायायाम् गुणसाम्यरुद्धविकृतौ त्वय्यागतायां लयम् ।
In the illusory power of creation, the three attributes of Sattva, Rajas, and Tamas were in perfect equilibrium, without any mutations, and were dissolved within You!
नो मृत्युश्च तदाऽमृतं च समभून्नाह्नो न रात्रे: स्थितिः
There was neither death, nor immortality; neither day nor night!
तत्रैकस्त्वमशिष्यथा: किल परानन्दप्रकाशात्मना ॥१॥
There didn't You alone remain, as the supreme bliss of your radiant self!

काल: कर्म गुणाश्च जीवनिवहा विश्वं च कार्यं विभो
चिल्लीलारतिमेयुषि त्वयि तदा निर्लीनतामाययु: ।
तेषां नैव वदन्त्यसत्त्वमयि भो: शक्त्यात्मना तिष्ठतां
नो चेत् किं गगनप्रसूनसदृशां भूयो भवेत्संभव: ॥२॥
kAlaH karma guNAshcha jIvanivahA vishvaM cha kAryaM vibho
chillIlAratimeyuShi tvayi tadA nirlInatAmAyayuH 
teShAM naiva vadantyasattvamayi bhoH shaktyAtmanA tiShThatAM
no chet kiM gaganaprasUnasadRshAM bhUyo bhavetsaMbhavaH 2

काल: कर्म गुणाश्च जीवनिवहा विश्वं च कार्यं विभो
Time, Activity, Attributes, Creatures, Creation, and all else that was manifest, O Vibhu!
चिल्लीलारतिमेयुषि त्वयि तदा निर्लीनतामाययु: ।
In the play of Your own consciousness, then they were all absorbed into!
तेषां नैव वदन्त्यसत्त्वमयि भो: शक्त्यात्मना तिष्ठतां
Yet, their non-existence is not spoken of, O Lord! But only that they existed in their energy forms!
नो चेत् किं गगनप्रसूनसदृशां भूयो भवेत्संभव: ॥२॥
Otherwise, how would they be able to appear again and again as if flowers were blooming across space!

एवं च द्विपरार्धकालविगतावीक्षां सिसृक्षात्मिकां
बिभ्राणे त्वयि चुक्षुभे त्रिभुवनीभावाय माया स्वयम् ।
मायात: खलु कालशक्तिरखिलादृष्टं स्वभावोऽपि च
प्रादुर्भूय गुणान्विकास्य विदधुस्तस्यास्सहायक्रियाम् ॥३॥
evaM cha dviparArdhakAlavigatAvIkShAM sisRkShAtmikAM
bibhrANe tvayi chukShubhe tribhuvanIbhAvAya mAyA svayam
mAyAtaH khalu kAlashaktirakhilAdRShTaM svabhAvopi cha
prAdurbhUya guNAnvikAsya vidadhustasyAssahAyakriyAm 3

एवं च द्विपरार्धकालविगतावीक्षां सिसृक्षात्मिकां
And thus, after the passage of a period of two parArdhas, a glance with an intent to start creation
बिभ्राणे त्वयि चुक्षुभे त्रिभुवनीभावाय माया स्वयम् ।
as you cast that glance, in order to get started with the creation of the three worlds, mAyA-the illusory power of creation herself got activated!
मायात: खलु कालशक्तिरखिलादृष्टं स्वभावोऽपि च
Isn't it from mAyA, that the power of time, and all the invisible causal forces and natural tendencies took birth!
प्रादुर्भूय गुणान्विकास्य विदधुस्तस्यास्सहायक्रियाम् ॥३॥
After coming into existence, they in turn caused the guNas to flourish, helping mAyA further in her activity!

मायासन्निहितोऽप्रविष्टवपुषा साक्षीति गीतो भवान्
भेदैस्तां प्रतिबिंबतो विविशिवान् जीवोऽपि नैवापर: ।
कालादिप्रतिबोधिताऽथ भवता संचोदिता च स्वयं
माया सा खलु बुद्धितत्त्वमसृजद्योऽसौ महानुच्यते ॥४॥
mAyAsannihitopraviShTavapuShA sAkShIti gIto bhavAn
bhedaistAM pratibiMbato vivishivAn jIvopi naivAparaH 
kAlAdipratibodhitAtha bhavatA saMchoditA cha svayaM
mAyA sA khalu buddhitattvamasRjadyosau mahAnuchyate 4

मायासन्निहितोऽप्रविष्टवपुषा साक्षीति गीतो भवान्
Associated along with mAyA, but without yourself getting influenced by it, you are praised as being the Witness!
भेदैस्तां प्रतिबिंबतो विविशिवान् जीवोऽपि नैवापर: ।
In all its varied manifestations, you are reflected entering them as life itself, but nothing different!
कालादिप्रतिबोधिताऽथ भवता संचोदिता च स्वयं
Under the influence of Time and other factors and driven verily by You yourself,
माया सा खलु बुद्धितत्त्वमसृजद्योऽसौ महानुच्यते ॥४॥
mAyA, hasn't she created the principle of intellect, which is now said to be the highest in this creation called as 'mahat-tattva'!

तत्रासौ त्रिगुणात्मकोऽपि च महान् सत्त्वप्रधान: स्वयं
जीवेऽस्मिन् खलु निर्विकल्पमहमित्युद्बोधनिष्पादक: ।
चक्रेऽस्मिन् सविकल्पबोधकमहन्तत्त्वं महान् खल्वसौ
सम्पुष्टं त्रिगुणैस्तमोऽतिबहुलं विष्णो भवत्प्रेरणात् ॥५॥
tatrAsau triguNAtmakopi cha mahAn sattvapradhAnaH svayaM
jIvesmin khalu nirvikalpamahamityudbodhaniShpAdakaH 
chakresmin savikalpabodhakamahantattvaM mahAn khalvasau
sampuShTaM triguNaistamotibahulaM viShNo bhavatpreraNAt 5

तत्रासौ त्रिगुणात्मकोऽपि च महान् सत्त्वप्रधान: स्वयं
And it, despite being of the nature of the three guNas, this mahat is predominant in Sattva, itself
जीवेऽस्मिन् खलु निर्विकल्पमहमित्युद्बोधनिष्पाद्क: ।
doesn't it, in a living being induce the discernment of the sense of 'I' which is indistinct among all creatures!
चक्रेऽस्मिन् सविकल्पबोधकमहन्तत्त्वं महान् खल्वसौ
This in turn is caused to become a distinct sense of individual identity called 'aham-tattva', by the same mahat which indeed being
सम्पुष्टं त्रिगुणैस्तमोऽतिबहुलं विष्णो भवत्प्रेरणात् ॥५॥
pregnant with all the three guNas, but with tamas in larger proportions, O Vishnu! Because of your incitement!

सोऽहं च त्रिगुणक्रमात् त्रिविधतामासाद्य वैकारिको
भूयस्तैजसतामसाविति भवन्नाद्येन सत्त्वात्मना
देवानिन्द्रियमानिनोऽकृत दिशावातार्कपाश्यश्विनो
वह्नीन्द्राच्युतमित्रकान् विधुविधिश्रीरुद्रशारीरकान् ॥६॥
sohaM cha triguNakramAt trividhatAmAsAdya vaikAriko
bhUyastaijasatAmasAviti bhavannAdyena sattvAtmanA
devAnindriyamAninokRta dishAvAtArkapAshyashvino
vahnIndrAchyutamitrakAn vidhuvidhishrIrudrashArIrakAn 6

सोऽहं च त्रिगुणक्रमात् त्रिविधतामासाद्य वैकारिको
That 'aham' again, in the order of the three guNas, becomes mutated as 'vaikArika' 
भूयस्तैजसतामसाविति भवन्नाद्येन सत्त्वात्मना
and then as 'taijasa' and 'tAmasa'! And from the first mutation of sattva,
देवानिन्द्रियमानिनोऽकृत दिशावातार्कपाश्यश्विनो
devas who preside over various aspects of the body are created. Of jnAnendriyas -(organs of knowing) they are dik-devatA(of hearing), vAyu(of touch), sUrya(of sight), varuNa(of taste), and ashwinIs(of smell)
वह्नीन्द्राच्युतमित्रकान् विधुविधिश्रीरुद्रशारीरकान् ॥६॥
Of karmendriyas - (organs of acting) they are agni(of speech), indra(of hands), achyuta(of the legs), mitra(of excretion), and prajApati(of reproduction). Of antaHkaraNa -(Internal faculties) they are vidhu(of manas), vidhi(of buddhi), rudra(of ahamkAra), and kShetrajna(of chitta).

भूमन् मानसबुद्ध्यहंकृतिमिलच्चित्ताख्यवृत्त्यन्वितं
तच्चान्त:करणं विभो तव बलात् सत्त्वांश एवासृजत् ।
जातस्तैजसतो दशेन्द्रियगणस्तत्तामसांशात्पुनः
तन्मात्रं नभसो मरुत्पुरपते शब्दोऽजनि त्वद्बलात् ॥७॥
bhUman mAnasabuddhyahaMkRtimilachchittAkhyavRttyanvitaM
tachchAntaHkaraNaM vibho tava balAt sattvAMsha evAsRjat 
jAtastaijasato dashendriyagaNastattAmasAMshAtpunaH
tanmAtraM nabhaso marutpurapate shabdojani tvadbalAt 7

भूमन् मानसबुद्ध्यहंकृतिमिलच्चित्ताख्यवृत्त्यन्वितं
O Abundant One! manas(mind), buddhi(intellect), ahaMkAra(ego) together with the activity known as chitta,
तच्चान्त:करणं विभो तव बलात् सत्त्वांश एवासृजत् ।
these internal faculties of action, O Vibhu, under your influence, the sAttvic aspect itself has created!
जातस्तैजसतो दशेन्द्रियगणस्तत्तामसांशात्पुनः
From the 'taijasic' aspect are born the Ten indriyas(senses of knowing and acting) and from the 'tAmasic' aspect
तन्मात्रं नभसो मरुत्पुरपते शब्दोऽजनि त्वद्बलात् ॥७॥
the tanmAtra(subtle essence) of Space! O Lord of the City of Wind! Sound was born from your influence!

शब्दाद्व्योम तत: ससर्जिथ विभो स्पर्शं ततो मारुतं
तस्माद्रूपमतो महोऽथ च रसं तोयं च गन्धं महीम् ।
एवं माधव पूर्वपूर्वकलनादाद्याद्यधर्मान्वितं
भूतग्राममिमं त्वमेव भगवन् प्राकाशयस्तामसात् ॥८॥
shabdAdvyoma tataH sasarjitha vibho sparshaM tato mArutaM
tasmAdrUpamato mahotha cha rasaM toyaM cha gandhaM mahIm 
evaM mAdhava pUrvapUrvakalanAdAdyAdyadharmAnvitaM
bhUtagrAmamimaM tvameva bhagavan prAkAshayastAmasAt 8

श्ब्दाद्व्योम तत: ससर्जिथ विभो स्पर्शं ततो मारुतं
From Sound was born Space, and then O Vibhu, you created Touch, then Wind, 
तस्माद्रूपमतो महोऽथ च रसं तोयं च गन्धं महीम् ।
and from that, you created Form, and from that, Fire! From that was created Taste, and then Water, Smell and Earth!
एवं माधव पूर्वपूर्वकलनादाद्याद्यधर्मान्वितं
Thus, O Madhava! Because of this chained relationship, each one containing the attributes of the aspects born before,
भूतग्राममिमं त्वमेव भगवन् प्राकाशयस्तामसात् ॥८॥
this entire assortment of creation, You yourself O Lord! Brought into light from Tamas!

एते भूतगणास्तथेन्द्रियगणा देवाश्च जाता: पृथक्
नो शेकुर्भुवनाण्डनिर्मितिविधौ देवैरमीभिस्तदा ।
त्वं नानाविधसूक्तिभिर्नुतगुणस्तत्त्वान्यमून्याविशन्
चेष्टाशक्तिमुदीर्य तानि घटयन् हैरण्यमण्डं व्यधा: ॥९॥
ete bhUtagaNAstathendriyagaNA devAshcha jAtAH pRthak
no shekurbhuvanANDanirmitividhau devairamIbhistadA 
tvaM nAnAvidhasUktibhirnutaguNastattvAnyamUnyAvishan
cheShTAshaktimudIrya tAni ghaTayan hairaNyamaNDaM vyadhAH 9

एते भूतगणास्तथेन्द्रियगणा देवाश्च जाता: पृथक्
All these clusters of elements, senses, presiding forces which came into existence as different entities 
नो शेकुर्भुवनाण्डनिर्मितिविधौ देवैरमीभिस्तदा ।
were not capable by themselves of creating this cosmic egg! Then, along with these presiding deities
त्वं नानाविधसूक्तिभिर्नुतगुणस्तत्त्वान्यमून्याविशन्
You, whose attributes are praised by various kinds of hymns, entering all these principles
चेष्टाशक्तिमुदीर्य तानि घटयन् हैरण्यमण्डं व्यधा: ॥९॥
by activating their power to act, and making them interact with each other, gave birth to this brilliant egg of creation!

अण्डं तत्खलु पूर्वसृष्टसलिलेऽतिष्ठत् सहस्रं समा:
निर्भिन्दन्नकृथाश्चतुर्दशजगद्रूपं विराडाह्वयम् ।
साहस्रै: करपादमूर्धनिवहैर्निश्शेषजीवात्मको
निर्भातोऽसि मरुत्पुराधिप स मां त्रायस्व सर्वामयात् ॥१०॥
aNDaM tatkhalu pUrvasRShTasaliletiShThat sahasraM samAH
nirbhindannakRthAshchaturdashajagadrUpaM virADAhvayam 
sAhasraiH karapAdamUrdhanivahairnishsheShajIvAtmako
nirbhAtosi marutpurAdhipa sa mAM trAyasva sarvAmayAt 10

अण्डं तत्खलु पूर्वसृष्टसलिलेऽतिष्ठत् सहस्रं समा:
That egg, did it not indeed lie dormant in the previously created cosmic waters for thousands of years!
निर्भिन्दन्नकृथाश्चतुर्दशजगद्रूपं विराडाह्वयम् ।
You cracking it open, gave rise to the Fourteen-fold creation of worlds, known by the name of 'virAT'
साहस्रै: करपादमूर्धनिवहैर्निश्शेषजीवात्मको
With thousands of hands, feet, and heads, as an aspect of all the living beings of creation,
निर्भातोऽसि मरुत्पुराधिप स मां त्रायस्व सर्वामयात् ॥१०॥
You shine forth, O Lord of the City of Wind! May that you protect me from all ailments!
