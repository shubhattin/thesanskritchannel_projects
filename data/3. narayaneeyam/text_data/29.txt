ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकोनत्रिंशम् दशकम्
ekonatriMsham dashakam

The Twenty-ninth group of Ten shlokas

अमृत आहरणम् असुर निग्रह: च
amRta AharaNam asura nigrahaH cha

Receiving Amrita and Controlling Asuras

उद्गच्छतस्तव करादमृतं हरत्सु
दैत्येषु तानशरणाननुनीय देवान् ।
सद्यस्तिरोदधिथ देव भवत्प्रभावाद्
उद्यत्स्वयूथ्यकलहा दितिजा बभूवु: ॥१॥
udgachChatastava karAdamRtaM haratsu
daityeShu tAnasharaNAnanunIya devAn 
sadyastirodadhitha deva bhavatprabhAvAd
udyatsvayUthyakalahA ditijA babhUvuH 1

उद्गच्छतस्तव करादमृतं हरत्सु
As you emerged holding Amrita in your hands, stealing it,
दैत्येषु तानशरणाननुनीय देवान् ।
as the Asuras fled, giving solace to the helpless Devas,
सद्यस्तिरोदधिथ देव भवत्प्रभावाद्
you immediately disappeared from there, O Lord! And with your influence 
उद्यत्स्वयूथ्यकलहा दितिजा बभूवु: ॥१॥
an internal conflict arose within the Asura groups!

श्यामां रुचाऽपि वयसाऽपि तनुं तदानीं
प्राप्तोऽसि तुङ्गकुचमण्डलभंगुरां त्वम् ।
पीयूषकुम्भकलहं परिमुच्य सर्वे
तृष्णाकुला: प्रतिययुस्त्वदुरोजकुम्भे ॥२॥
shyAmAM ruchApi vayasApi tanuM tadAnIM
prAptosi tungakuchamaNDalabhaMgurAM tvam 
pIyUShakumbhakalahaM parimuchya sarve
tRShNAkulAH pratiyayustvadurojakumbhe 2

श्यामां रुचाऽपि वयसाऽपि तनुं तदानीं
Then, a feminine form which is of enchanting beauty and age,
प्राप्तोऽसि तुङ्गकुचमण्डलभंगुरां त्वम् ।
you took on, a form slightly curved under the heaviness of your bosom!
पीयूषकुम्भकलहं परिमुच्य सर्वे
Leaving the conflict over the pot of Amrita, everyone
तृष्णाकुला: प्रतिययुस्त्वदुरोजकुम्भे ॥२॥
overcome with thirst, started to go for your pot-like breasts!

का त्वं मृगाक्षि विभजस्व सुधामिमामि-
त्यारूढरागविवशानभियाचतोऽमून् ।
विश्वस्यते मयि कथं कुलटाऽस्मि दैत्या
इत्यालपन्नपि सुविश्वसितानतानी: ॥३॥
kA tvaM mRgAkShi vibhajasva sudhAmimAmi-
tyArUDharAgavivashAnabhiyAchatomUn 
vishvasyate mayi kathaM kulaTAsmi daityA
ityAlapannapi suvishvasitAnatAnIH 3

का त्वं मृगाक्षि विभजस्व सुधामिमामि-
"Who are you, O doe-eyed one! Distribute this Amrita" thus
त्यारूढरागविवशानभियाचतोऽमून् ।
as they asked, overcome with their infatuation, 
विश्वस्यते मयि कथं कुलटाऽस्मि दैत्या
"Do you all trust me then! I am an unchaste woman though, O Daityas!" 
इत्यालपन्नपि सुविश्वसितानतानी: ॥३॥
cooing thus, you gained all of their trust!

मोदात् सुधाकलशमेषु ददत्सु सा त्वं
दुश्चेष्टितं मम सहध्वमिति ब्रुवाणा ।
पङ्क्तिप्रभेदविनिवेशितदेवदैत्या
लीलाविलासगतिभि: समदा: सुधां ताम् ॥४॥
modAt sudhAkalashameShu dadatsu sA tvaM
dushcheShTitaM mama sahadhvamiti bruvANA 
panktiprabhedaviniveshitadevadaityA
lIlAvilAsagatibhiH samadAH sudhAM tAm 4

मोदात् सुधाकलशमेषु ददत्सु सा त्वं
As they handed over the pot of Amrita to you merrily,
दुश्चेष्टितं मम सहध्वमिति ब्रुवाणा ।
"Excuse my offenses, and bear with me please!" saying thus,
पङ्क्तिप्रभेदविनिवेशितदेवदैत्या
making the Devas and Daityas sit in two separate rows,
लीलाविलासगतिभि: समदा: सुधां ताम् ॥४॥
with charming, playful gait, you started distributing that Amrita!

अस्मास्वियं प्रणयिणीत्यसुरेषु तेषु
जोषं स्थितेष्वथ समाप्य सुधां सुरेषु ।
त्वं भक्तलोकवशगो निजरूपमेत्य
स्वर्भानुमर्धपरिपीतसुधं व्यलावी: ॥५॥
asmAsviyaM praNayiNItyasureShu teShu
joShaM sthiteShvatha samApya sudhAM sureShu 
tvaM bhaktalokavashago nijarUpametya
svarbhAnumardhaparipItasudhaM vyalAvIH 5

अस्मास्वियं प्रणयिणीत्यसुरेषु तेषु
"She is loving towards us!" as the asuras kept thinking thus
जोषं स्थितेष्वथ समाप्य सुधां सुरेषु ।
and waited silently, finishing up all the Amrita to the Suras,
त्वं भक्तलोकवशगो निजरूपमेत्य
you, who are attained by your groups of devotees, assuming your real form,
स्वर्भानुमर्धपरिपीतसुधं व्यलावी: ॥५॥
beheaded Rahu, who drank up half of Amrita sitting on the side of Devas!

त्वत्त: सुधाहरणयोग्यफलं परेषु
दत्वा गते त्वयि सुरै: खलु ते व्यगृह्णन् ।
घोरेऽथ मूर्छति रणे बलिदैत्यमाया-
व्यामोहिते सुरगणे त्वमिहाविरासी: ॥६॥
tvattaH sudhAharaNayogyaphalaM pareShu
datvA gate tvayi suraiH khalu te vyagRhNan 
ghoretha mUrChati raNe balidaityamAyA-
vyAmohite suragaNe tvamihAvirAsIH 6

त्वत्त: सुधाहरणयोग्यफलं परेषु
The result of their efforts, Amrita, taken from them, and to others
दत्वा गते त्वयि सुरै: खलु ते व्यगृह्णन् ।
which was given, they started a fight with the Suras!
घोरेऽथ मूर्छति रणे बलिदैत्यमाया-
In that fierce battle, dizzy under the illusion of the asura, Bali,
व्यामोहिते सुरगणे त्वमिहाविरासी: ॥६॥
as the groups of Suras fainted, you appeared there once again!

त्वं कालनेमिमथ मालिमुखाञ्जघन्थ
शक्रो जघान बलिजम्भवलान् सपाकान् ।
शुष्कार्द्रदुष्करवधे नमुचौ च लूने
फेनेन नारदगिरा न्यरुणो रणं त्वं ॥७॥
tvaM kAlanemimatha mAlimukhAnjaghantha
shakro jaghAna balijambhavalAn sapAkAn 
shuShkArdraduShkaravadhe namuchau cha lUne
phenena nAradagirA nyaruNo raNaM tvaM 7

त्वं कालनेमिमथ मालिमुखाञ्जघन्थ
You then slayed Kalanemi, and Malimukha!
शक्रो जघान बलिजम्भवलान् सपाकान् ।
Indra killed Bali, Jambha, Vala and others!
शुष्कार्द्रदुष्करवधे नमुचौ च लूने
As Namuchi could not be killed by any dry or a wet weapon,
फेनेन नारदगिरा न्यरुणो रणं त्वं ॥७॥
heeding Narada's words, you killed him with foam, and stopped that battle!

योषावपुर्दनुजमोहनमाहितं ते
श्रुत्वा विलोकनकुतूहलवान् महेश: ।
भूतैस्समं गिरिजया च गत: पदं ते
स्तुत्वाऽब्रवीदभिमतं त्वमथो तिरोधा: ॥८॥
yoShAvapurdanujamohanamAhitaM te
shrutvA vilokanakutUhalavAn maheshaH 
bhUtaissamaM girijayA cha gataH padaM te
stutvAbravIdabhimataM tvamatho tirodhAH 8

योषावपुर्दनुजमोहनमाहितं ते
The feminine form of yours which enchanted the groups of Asuras,
श्रुत्वा विलोकनकुतूहलवान् महेश: ।
hearing of it, desirous to see, Lord Shiva
भूतैस्समं गिरिजया च गत: पदं ते
along with his Bhutas and Goddess Parvati, went to your abode!
स्तुत्वाऽब्रवीदभिमतं त्वमथो तिरोधा: ॥८॥
Praising you, as he conveyed his intent, you disappeared from there!

आरामसीमनि च कन्दुकघातलीला-
लोलायमाननयनां कमनीं मनोज्ञाम् ।
त्वामेष वीक्ष्य विगलद्वसनां मनोभू-
वेगादनङ्गरिपुरङ्ग समालिलिङ्ग ॥९॥
ArAmasImani cha kandukaghAtalIlA-
lolAyamAnanayanAM kamanIM manojnAm 
tvAmeSha vIkShya vigaladvasanAM manobhU-
vegAdanangaripuranga samAlilinga 9

आरामसीमनि च कन्दुकघातलीला-
At the resting gardens, resembling the playfulness of striking a ball,
लोलायमाननयनां कमनीं मनोज्ञाम् ।
as you started moving your eyes, the beautiful and enchating
त्वामेष वीक्ष्य विगलद्वसनां मनोभू-
form of yours, whose clothes were slipping, glancing it, under love's
वेगादनङ्गरिपुरङ्ग समालिलिङ्ग ॥९॥
influence, the enemy of manmatha, Shiva, took you in embrace!

भूयोऽपि विद्रुतवतीमुपधाव्य देवो
वीर्यप्रमोक्षविकसत्परमार्थबोध: ।
त्वन्मानितस्तव महत्त्वमुवाच देव्यै
तत्तादृशस्त्वमव वातनिकेतनाथ ॥१०॥
bhUyopi vidrutavatImupadhAvya devo
vIryapramokShavikasatparamArthabodhaH 
tvanmAnitastava mahattvamuvAcha devyai
tattAdRshastvamava vAtaniketanAtha 10

भूयोऽपि विद्रुतवतीमुपधाव्य देवो
Once again, running behind you in that form, that lord
वीर्यप्रमोक्षविकसत्परमार्थबोध: ।
having released his seed, and then realizing the higher truth,
त्वन्मानितस्तव महत्त्वमुवाच देव्यै
then honoured by you, spoke of your grace to Devi Parvati!
तत्तादृशस्त्वमव वातनिकेतनाथ ॥१०॥
O Lord who is of such glory! Protect me, O Lord of the Abode of Wind!
