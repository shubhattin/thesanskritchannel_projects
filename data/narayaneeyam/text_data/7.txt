ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

सप्तमम् दशकम्
saptamam dashakam

The Seventh group of Ten shlokas

ब्रह्मणः तपः वैकुण्ठ दर्शनं च
brahmaNaH tapaH vaikunTha darshanaM cha

Penance of Brahma and the Vision of Vaikuntha

एवं देव चतुर्दशात्मकजगद्रूपेण जात: पुनः
तस्योर्ध्वं खलु सत्यलोकनिलये जातोऽसि धाता स्वयम् ।
यं शंसन्ति हिरण्यगर्भमखिलत्रैलोक्यजीवात्मकं
योऽभूत् स्फीतरजोविकारविकसन्नानासिसृक्षारस: ॥१॥
evaM deva chaturdashAtmakajagadrUpeNa jAtaH punaH
tasyordhvaM khalu satyalokanilaye jAtosi dhAtA svayam 
yaM shaMsanti hiraNyagarbhamakhilatrailokyajIvAtmakaM
yobhUt sphItarajovikAravikasannAnAsisRkShArasaH 1

एवं देव चतुर्दशात्मकजगद्रूपेण जात: पुनः
Thus, O Lord! Once you have manifest yourself as the fourteen-folded form of creation, again
तस्योर्ध्वं खलु सत्यलोकनिलये जातोऽसि धाता स्वयम् ।
at its peak at the abode of satya loka, do you not manifest yourself as brahmA its creator!
यं शंसन्ति हिरण्यगर्भमखिलत्रैलोक्यजीवात्मकं
The one who is praised as the radiant womb of the life of all the three worlds,
योऽभूत् स्फीतरजोविकारविकसन्नानासिसृक्षारस: ॥१॥
the one who is manifest as fullfledged rajo-guNa causing multiple mutations in order to blossom forth various kinds of creation!

सोऽयं विश्वविसर्गदत्तहृदय: सम्पश्यमान: स्वयं
बोधं खल्वनवाप्य विश्वविषयं चिन्ताकुलस्तस्थिवान् ।
तावत्त्वं जगतां पते तप तपेत्येवं हि वैहायसीं
वाणीमेनमशिश्रव: श्रुतिसुखां कुर्वंस्तप:प्रेरणाम् ॥२॥
soyaM vishvavisargadattahRdayaH sampashyamAnaH svayaM
bodhaM khalvanavApya vishvaviShayaM chintAkulastasthivAn 
tAvattvaM jagatAM pate tapa tapetyevaM hi vaihAyasIM
vANImenamashishravaH shrutisukhAM kurvaMstapaHpreraNAm 2

सोऽयं विश्वविसर्गदत्तहृदय: सम्पश्यमान: स्वयं
That one whose heart is set towards the process of creation, witnessing himself,
बोधं खल्वनवाप्य विश्वविषयं चिन्ताकुलस्तस्थिवान् ।
unable to grasp the knowledge of the creation process, remained worried!
तावत्त्वं जगतां पते तप तपेत्येवं हि वैहायसीं
And then suddenly, O Lord of the Worlds, upon hearing the sounds 'tapa' 'tapa' from the sky,
वाणीमेनमशिश्रव: श्रुतिसुखां कुर्वंस्तप:प्रेरणाम् ॥२॥
he then followed this voice, which was pleasing to his ears, which goaded him toward performing penance!

कोऽसौ मामवदत् पुमानिति जलापूर्णे जगन्मण्डले
दिक्षूद्वीक्ष्य किमप्यनीक्षितवता वाक्यार्थमुत्पश्यता ।
दिव्यं वर्षसहस्रमात्ततपसा तेन त्वमाराधितः
तस्मै दर्शितवानसि स्वनिलयं वैकुण्ठमेकाद्भुतम् ॥३॥
kosau mAmavadat pumAniti jalApUrNe jaganmaNDale
dikShUdvIkShya kimapyanIkShitavatA vAkyArthamutpashyatA 
divyaM varShasahasramAttatapasA tena tvamArAdhitaH
tasmai darshitavAnasi svanilayaM vaikuNThamekAdbhutam 3

कोऽसौ मामवदत् पुमानिति जलापूर्णे जगन्मण्डले
"Who is the one who spoke to me!" Thinking thus, among the worlds engulfed in waters
दिक्षूद्वीक्ष्य किमप्यनीक्षितवता वाक्यार्थमुत्पश्यता ।
he let his sights wander, but unable to spot anything, he then understood the essence of that sentence!
दिव्यं वर्षसहस्रमात्ततपसा तेन त्वमाराधितः
For a thousand celestial years, he performed penance, and by him, You were worshipped!
तस्मै दर्शितवानसि स्वनिलयं वैकुण्ठमेकाद्भुतम् ॥३॥
To him, You then granted a vision of your own wonderful abode of vaikunTha!

माया यत्र कदापि नो विकुरुते भाते जगद्भ्यो बहि:
शोकक्रोधविमोहसाध्वसमुखा भावास्तु दूरं गता: ।
सान्द्रानन्दझरी च यत्र परमज्योति:प्रकाशात्मके
तत्ते धाम विभावितं विजयते वैकुण्ठरूपं विभो ॥४॥
mAyA yatra kadApi no vikurute bhAte jagadbhyo bahiH
shokakrodhavimohasAdhvasamukhA bhAvAstu dUraM gatAH
sAndrAnandajharI cha yatra paramajyotiH prakAshAtmake
tatte dhAma vibhAvitaM vijayate vaikuNTharUpaM vibho 4

माया यत्र कदापि नो विकुरुते भाते जगद्भ्यो बहि:
Where mAya never causes any disturbance, the abode which shines beyond all the Fourteen worlds,
शोकक्रोधविमोहसाध्वसमुखा भावास्तु दूरं गता: ।
where grief, rage, delusion, fright, and such negative emotions are left far behind,
सान्द्रानन्दझरी च यत्र परमज्योति:प्रकाशात्मके
which is a torrent of intense bliss, and which is of the radiance of supreme brilliance,
तत्ते धाम विभावितं विजयते वैकुण्ठरूपं विभो ॥४॥
that is your abode that was shown to brahmA, as the form of vaikunTha, O Vibhu!

यस्मिन्नाम चतुर्भुजा हरिमणिश्यामावदातत्विषो
नानाभूषणरत्नदीपितदिशो राजद्विमानालया: ।
भक्तिप्राप्ततथाविधोन्नतपदा दीव्यन्ति दिव्या जनाः
तत्ते धाम निरस्तसर्वशमलं वैकुण्ठरूपं जयेत् ॥५॥
yasminnAma chaturbhujA harimaNishyAmAvadAtatviSho
nAnAbhUShaNaratnadIpitadisho rAjadvimAnAlayAH 
bhaktiprAptatathAvidhonnatapadA dIvyanti divyA janAH
tatte dhAma nirastasarvashamalaM vaikuNTharUpaM jayet 5

यस्मिन्नाम चतुर्भुजा हरिमणिश्यामावदातत्विषो
Where the four-armed beings, who are of deep blue-hued bodies reside,
नानाभूषणरत्नदीपितदिशो राजद्विमानालया: ।
who are ornamented in various jewels that illuminate all directions, who reside in kingly chariots,
भक्तिप्राप्ततथाविधोन्नतपदा दीव्यन्ति दिव्या जनाः
who have attained this stature through devotion and other such exalted means, where such radiant divine beings dwell,
तत्ते धाम निरस्तसर्वशमलं वैकुण्ठरूपं जयेत् ॥५॥
that abode of yours which is rid of all impurities, may that form of vaikunTha be ever victorious!

नानादिव्यवधूजनैरभिवृता विद्युल्लतातुल्यया
विश्वोन्मादनहृद्यगात्रलतया विद्योतिताशान्तरा ।
त्वत्पादांबुजसौरभैककुतुकाल्लक्ष्मी: स्वयं लक्ष्यते
यस्मिन् विस्मयनीयदिव्यविभवं तत्ते पदं देहि मे ॥६॥
nAnAdivyavadhUjanairabhivRtA vidyullatAtulyayA
vishvonmAdanahRdyagAtralatayA vidyotitAshAntarA 
tvatpAdAMbujasaurabhaikakutukAllakShmIH svayaM lakShyate
yasmin vismayanIyadivyavibhavaM tatte padaM dehi me 6

नानादिव्यवधूजनैरभिवृता विद्युल्लतातुल्यया
Inhabited by various kinds of celestial damsels, who resemble streaks of lightning,
विश्वोन्मादनहृद्यगात्रलतया विद्योतिताशान्तरा ।
who enchant the universe with their charming slender forms, that abode which illumines,
त्वत्पादांबुजसौरभैककुतुकाल्लक्ष्मी: स्वयं लक्ष्यते
Due to being attached to the unique fragrance of your lotus feet, where Goddess Lakshmi herself dwells,
यस्मिन् विस्मयनीयदिव्यविभवं तत्ते पदं देहि मे ॥६॥
where awe-inspiring celestial riches reside, grant that abode of yours to me!

तत्रैवं प्रतिदर्शिते निजपदे रत्नासनाध्यासितं
भास्वत्कोटिलसत्किरीटकटकाद्याकल्पदीप्राकृति ।
श्रीवत्साङ्कितमात्तकौस्तुभमणिच्छायारुणं कारणं
विश्वेषां तव रूपमैक्षत विधिस्तत्ते विभो भातु मे ॥७॥
tatraivaM pratidarshite nijapade ratnAsanAdhyAsitaM
bhAsvatkoTilasatkirITakaTakAdyAkalpadIprAkRti 
shrIvatsAnkitamAttakaustubhamaNichChAyAruNaM kAraNaM
vishveShAM tava rUpamaikShata vidhistatte vibho bhAtu me 7

तत्रैवं प्रतिदर्शिते निजपदे रत्नासनाध्यासितं
In that abode of yours which was shown to brahmA, seated on a gem-studded throne,
भास्वत्कोटिलसत्किरीटकटकाद्याकल्पदीप्राकृति ।
shining like a million Suns, ornamented with a crown, bracelets, and other splendid ornaments,
श्रीवत्साङ्कितमात्तकौस्तुभमणिच्छायारुणं कारणं
bearing the mark of shrIvatsa, which is shining red under the glow of the kaustubha jewel, that which is the cause
विश्वेषां तव रूपमैक्षत विधिस्तत्ते विभो भातु मे ॥७॥
of all of creation, that form of yours brahmA saw! O Vibhu, may that be revealed to me!

कालांभोदकलायकोमलरुचीचक्रेण चक्रं दिशाम्
आवृण्वानमुदारमन्दहसितस्यन्दप्रसन्नाननम् ।
राजत्कम्बुगदारिपङ्कजधरश्रीमद्भुजामण्डलं
स्रष्टुस्तुष्टिकरं वपुस्तव विभो मद्रोगमुद्वासयेत् ॥८॥
kAlAMbhodakalAyakomalaruchIchakreNa chakraM dishAm
AvRNvAnamudAramandahasitasyandaprasannAnanam 
rAjatkambugadAripankajadharashrImadbhujAmaNDalaM
sraShTustuShTikaraM vapustava vibho madrogamudvAsayet 8

कालांभोदकलायकोमलरुचीचक्रेण चक्रं दिशाम्
Like dark rain clouds and tender blue water lilies, your aura making all the directions
आवृण्वानमुदारमन्दहसितस्यन्दप्रसन्नाननम् ।
fill up in that light, with a benevolent gentle smile lighting up your pleasant face,
राजत्कम्बुगदारिपङ्कजधरश्रीमद्भुजामण्डलं
resplendent with the conch, mace, discus, and lotus, held in the four auspicious arms,
स्रष्टुस्तुष्टिकरं वपुस्तव विभो मद्रोगमुद्वासयेत् ॥८॥
the creator was content with that form of yours O Vibhu! May it banish my disease!

दृष्ट्वा सम्भृतसम्भ्रम: कमलभूस्त्वत्पादपाथोरुहे
हर्षावेशवशंवदो निपतित: प्रीत्या कृतार्थीभवन् ।
जानास्येव मनीषितं मम विभो ज्ञानं तदापादय
द्वैताद्वैतभवत्स्वरूपपरमित्याचष्ट तं त्वां भजे ॥९॥
dRShTvA sambhRtasambhramaH kamalabhUstvatpAdapAthoruhe
harShAveshavashaMvado nipatitaH prItyA kRtArthIbhavan 
jAnAsyeva manIShitaM mama vibho jnAnaM tadApAdaya
dvaitAdvaitabhavatsvarUpaparamityAchaShTa taM tvAM bhaje 9

दृष्ट्वा सम्भृतसम्भ्रम: कमलभूस्त्वत्पादपाथोरुहे
Upon seeing it, overwhelmed with wonder, the lotus born brahmA took to your divine lotus feet,
हर्षावेशवशंवदो निपतित: प्रीत्या कृतार्थीभवन् ।
thrown under the influence of intense joy, he was filled with love and fulfillment!
जानास्येव मनीषितं मम विभो ज्ञानं तदापादय
"You surely know my mind's desire O Vibhu, grant me that knowledge!"
द्वैताद्वैतभवत्स्वरूपपरमित्याचष्ट तं त्वां भजे ॥९॥
He spoke thus and asked you, "What makes up the forms of both the dual and the non-dual!". To that being, I pray!

आताम्रे चरणे विनम्रमथ तं हस्तेन हस्ते स्पृशन्
बोधस्ते भविता न सर्गविधिभिर्बन्धोऽपि सञ्जायते ।
इत्याभाष्य गिरं प्रतोष्य नितरां तच्चित्तगूढ: स्वयं
सृष्टौ तं समुदैरय: स भगवन्नुल्लासयोल्लाघताम् ॥१०॥
AtAmre charaNe vinamramatha taM hastena haste spRshan
bodhaste bhavitA na sargavidhibhirbandhopi sanjAyate 
ityAbhAShya giraM pratoShya nitarAM tachchittagUDhaH svayaM
sRShTau taM samudairayaH sa bhagavannullAsayollAghatAm 10

आताम्रे चरणे विनम्रमथ तं हस्तेन हस्ते स्पृशन्
As he lay humble at your crimson feet, touching his hands with your own,
बोधस्ते भविता न सर्गविधिभिर्बन्धोऽपि सञ्जायते ।
"You now have that knowledge! You shall also not be bound by the rules of creation"
इत्याभाष्य गिरं प्रतोष्य नितरां तच्चित्तगूढ: स्वयं
Speaking these words, and making him extremely happy, you yourself residing in his mind firmly,
सृष्टौ तं समुदैरय: स भगवन्नुल्लासयोल्लाघताम् ॥१०॥
prompted him in the process of creation. O Radiant Lord! Please hasten my recovery!
