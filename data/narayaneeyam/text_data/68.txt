अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

अष्टषष्टितमम् दशकम्
aShTaShaShTitamam dashakam

The Sixty-eighth group of Ten shlokas

गोपिकानां परम आह्लाद:
gopikAnAM parama AhlAdaH

Ecstacy of Gopikas

तव विलोकनाद्गोपिकाजना: प्रमदसङ्कुला: पङ्कजेक्षण ।
अमृतधारया संप्लुता इव स्तिमिततां दधुस्त्वत्पुरोगता: ॥१॥
tava vilokanAdgopikAjanAH pramadasankulAH pankajekShaNa 
amRtadhArayA saMplutA iva stimitatAM dadhustvatpurogatAH 1

तव विलोकनाद्गोपिकाजना: प्रमदसङ्कुला: पङ्कजेक्षण ।
Upon seeing you, the Gopikas were overcome with joy, O lotus-eyed one!
अमृतधारया संप्लुता इव स्तिमिततां दधुस्त्वत्पुरोगता: ॥१॥
As if they have been drenched in streams of nectar, they became utterly still as you appeared in front!

तदनु काचन त्वत्कराम्बुजं सपदि गृह्णती निर्विशङ्कितम् ।
घनपयोधरे सन्निधाय सा पुलकसंवृता तस्थुषी चिरम् ॥२॥
tadanu kAchana tvatkarAmbujaM sapadi gRhNatI nirvishankitam 
ghanapayodhare sannidhAya sA pulakasaMvRtA tasthuShI chiram 2

तदनु काचन त्वत्कराम्बुजं सपदि गृह्णती निर्विशङ्कितम् ।
Then, one among them, grabbing your hand at once, without hesitation,
घनपयोधरे सन्निधाय सा पुलकसंवृता तस्थुषी चिरम् ॥२॥
placing it on her heavy bosom, immersed in ecstacy, stayed there for long!

तव विभोऽपरा कोमलं भुजं निजगलान्तरे पर्यवेष्टयत् ।
गलसमुद्गतं प्राणमारुतं प्रतिनिरुन्धतीवातिहर्षुला ॥३॥
tava vibhoparA komalaM bhujaM nijagalAntare paryaveShTayat 
galasamudgataM prANamArutaM pratinirundhatIvAtiharShulA 3

तव विभोऽपरा कोमलं भुजं निजगलान्तरे पर्यवेष्टयत् ।
O Vibhu! Another among them took your arms and draped it around her neck,
गलसमुद्गतं प्राणमारुतं प्रतिनिरुन्धतीवातिहर्षुला ॥३॥
as if trying to stop the very life breath coming out of her throat, and felt utter joy!

अपगतत्रपा कापि कामिनी तव मुखाम्बुजात् पूगचर्वितम् ।
प्रतिगृहय्य तद्वक्त्रपङ्कजे निदधती गता पूर्णकामताम् ॥४॥
apagatatrapA kApi kAminI tava mukhAmbujAt pUgacharvitam 
pratigRhayya tadvaktrapankaje nidadhatI gatA pUrNakAmatAm 4

अपगतत्रपा कापि कामिनी तव मुखाम्बुजात् पूगचर्वितम् ।
Setting her shame aside, another lover of yours, the chewed betelnut from your lotus like mouth,
प्रतिगृहय्य तद्वक्त्रपङ्कजे निदधती गता पूर्णकामताम् ॥४॥
taking it into her own lotus-like mouth, fulfilled her desires content!

विकरुणो वने संविहाय मामपगतोऽसि का त्वामिह स्पृशेत् ।
इति सरोषया तावदेकया सजललोचनं वीक्षितो भवान् ॥५॥
vikaruNo vane saMvihAya mAmapagatosi kA tvAmiha spRshet 
iti saroShayA tAvadekayA sajalalochanaM vIkShito bhavAn 5

विकरुणो वने संविहाय मामपगतोऽसि का त्वामिह स्पृशेत् ।
"O Merciless One! Leaving me in this forest, you left! Who is going to touch you now!"
इति सरोषया तावदेकया सजललोचनं वीक्षितो भवान् ॥५॥
thus with anger, one of them looked at you with teary eyes!

इति मुदाऽऽकुलैर्वल्लवीजनै: सममुपागतो यामुने तटे ।
मृदुकुचाम्बरै: कल्पितासने घुसृणभासुरे पर्यशोभथा: ॥६॥
iti mudAkulairvallavIjanaiH samamupAgato yAmune taTe 
mRdukuchAmbaraiH kalpitAsane ghusRNabhAsure paryashobhathAH 6

इति मुदाऽऽकुलैर्वल्लवीजनै: सममुपागतो यामुने तटे ।
Thus as you were surrounded by the overjoyed Gopikas on the banks of Yamuna,
मृदुकुचाम्बरै: कल्पितासने घुसृणभासुरे पर्यशोभथा: ॥६॥
on those soft upper garments laid out on the ground as a carpet, you shined resplendant in a saffron glow!

कतिविधा कृपा केऽपि सर्वतो धृतदयोदया: केचिदाश्रिते ।
कतिचिदीदृशा मादृशेष्वपीत्यभिहितो भवान् वल्लवीजनै: ॥७॥
katividhA kRpA kepi sarvato dhRtadayodayAH kechidAshrite 
katichidIdRshA mAdRsheShvapItyabhihito bhavAn vallavIjanaiH 7

कतिविधा कृपा केऽपि सर्वतो धृतदयोदया: केचिदाश्रिते ।
"How many kinds of compassions! Some have it for all! Some have it towards those who seek it!
कतिचिदीदृशा मादृशेष्वपीत्यभिहितो भवान् वल्लवीजनै: ॥७॥
Yet others are like you! Do you even have compassion towards me!" thus you were spoken to by those Gopikas!

अयि कुमारिका नैव शङ्क्यतां कठिनता मयि प्रेमकातरे ।
मयि तु चेतसो वोऽनुवृत्तये कृतमिदं मयेत्यूचिवान् भवान् ॥८॥
ayi kumArikA naiva shankyatAM kaThinatA mayi premakAtare 
mayi tu chetaso vonuvRttaye kRtamidaM mayetyUchivAn bhavAn 8

अयि कुमारिका नैव शङ्क्यतां कठिनता मयि प्रेमकातरे ।
"O Young Ones! May there be no doubt! My sternness was only born out of love!
मयि तु चेतसो वोऽनुवृत्तये कृतमिदं मयेत्यूचिवान् भवान् ॥८॥
To have your minds firmly placed in me, that had been done by me!" thus you replied back!

अयि निशम्यतां जीववल्लभा: प्रियतमो जनो नेदृशो मम ।
तदिह रम्यतां रम्ययामिनीष्वनुपरोधमित्यालपो विभो ॥९॥
ayi nishamyatAM jIvavallabhAH priyatamo jano nedRsho mama 
tadiha ramyatAM ramyayAminIShvanuparodhamityAlapo vibho 9

अयि निशम्यतां जीववल्लभा: प्रियतमो जनो नेदृशो मम ।
"Hear this, O Beloveds of my Life! There is no one else who loves you like this! 
तदिह रम्यतां रम्ययामिनीष्वनुपरोधमित्यालपो विभो ॥९॥
So rejoice here in these enchanting nights unobstructed!" thus you spoke, O Vibhu!

इति गिराधिकं मोदमेदुरैर्व्रजवधूजनै: साकमारमन् ।
कलितकौतुको रासखेलने गुरुपुरीपते पाहि मां गदात् ॥१०॥
iti girAdhikaM modamedurairvrajavadhUjanaiH sAkamAraman 
kalitakautuko rAsakhelane gurupurIpate pAhi mAM gadAt 10

इति गिराधिकं मोदमेदुरैर्व्रजवधूजनै: साकमारमन् ।
With these words, as they were extremely delighted, rejoicing with those women of Vraja,
कलितकौतुको रासखेलने गुरुपुरीपते पाहि मां गदात् ॥१०॥
O One who had immense eagerness in playing the Raasa Krida! O Lord of the City of Wind! Protect me from disease!
