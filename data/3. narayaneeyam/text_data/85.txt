अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

पञ्चाशीतितमम् दशकम्
panchAshItitamam dashakam

The Eighty-fifth group of Ten shlokas

जरासन्ध वध: शिशुपाल वध: च
jarAsandha vadhaH shishupAla vadhaH cha

Slaying of Jarasandha and Shishupala

ततो मगधभूभृता चिरनिरोधसंक्लेशितं
शताष्टकयुतायुतद्वितयमीश भूमीभृताम् ।
अनाथशरणाय ते कमपि पूरुषं प्राहिणोत्
अयाचत स मागधक्षपणमेव किं भूयसा ॥१॥
tato magadhabhUbhRtA chiranirodhasaMkleshitaM
shatAShTakayutAyutadvitayamIsha bhUmIbhRtAm 
anAthasharaNAya te kamapi pUruShaM prAhiNot
ayAchata sa mAgadhakShapaNameva kiM bhUyasA 1

ततो मगधभूभृता चिरनिरोधसंक्लेशितं
Then, imprisoned by the king of Magadha for long,
शताष्टकयुतायुतद्वितयमीश भूमीभृताम् ।
the group of 20800 kings, O Lord,
अनाथशरणाय ते कमपि पूरुषं प्राहिणोत्
sent someone as a messenger to you, who is the refuge of the hopeless ones,
अयाचत स मागधक्षपणमेव किं भूयसा ॥१॥
who sought that you verily slay that king, what more can I say!

यियासुरभिमागधं तदनु नारदोदीरितात्
युधिष्ठिरमखोद्यमादुभयकार्यपर्याकुल: ।
विरुद्धजयिनोऽध्वरादुभयसिद्धिरित्युद्धवे
शशंसुषि निजै: समं पुरमियेथ यौधिष्ठिरीम् ॥२॥
yiyAsurabhimAgadhaM tadanu nAradodIritAt
yudhiShThiramakhodyamAdubhayakAryaparyAkulaH 
viruddhajayinodhvarAdubhayasiddhirityuddhave
shashaMsuShi nijaiH samaM puramiyetha yaudhiShThirIm 2

यियासुरभिमागधं तदनु नारदोदीरितात्
Ready to fight the king of Magadha, but then, upon Narada's words,
युधिष्ठिरमखोद्यमादुभयकार्यपर्याकुल: ।
learning about Yudhishthira's Yajna, falling into a dilemma,
विरुद्धजयिनोऽध्वरादुभयसिद्धिरित्युद्धवे
as Uddhava advised that Yajnas have the two fold benefit of removing enemies as well,
शशंसुषि निजै: समं पुरमियेथ यौधिष्ठिरीम् ॥२॥
along with your people, you went to the city of Yudhishthira!

अशेषदयितायुते त्वयि समागते धर्मजो
विजित्य सहजैर्महीं भवदपाङ्गसंवर्धितै: ।
श्रियं निरुपमां वहन्नहह भक्तदासायितं
भवन्तमयि मागधे प्रहितवान् सभीमार्जुनम् ॥३॥
asheShadayitAyute tvayi samAgate dharmajo
vijitya sahajairmahIM bhavadapAngasaMvardhitaiH 
shriyaM nirupamAM vahannahaha bhaktadAsAyitaM
bhavantamayi mAgadhe prahitavAn sabhImArjunam 3

अशेषदयितायुते त्वयि समागते धर्मजो
As you arrived with your entire retinue, Dharmaraja,
विजित्य सहजैर्महीं भवदपाङ्गसंवर्धितै: ।
having conquered the earth along with his brothers, enhanced by your side glances,
श्रियं निरुपमां वहन्नहह भक्तदासायितं
enjoying unparalleled riches, that devotee in your service,
भवन्तमयि मागधे प्रहितवान् सभीमार्जुनम् ॥३॥
sent you towards Magadha along with Bhima and Arjuna!

गिरिव्रजपुरं गतास्तदनु देव यूयं त्रयो
ययाच समरोत्सवं द्विजमिषेण तं मागधम् ।
अपूर्णसुकृतं त्वमुं पवनजेन संग्रामयन्
निरीक्ष्य सह जिष्णुना त्वमपि राजयुद्ध्वा स्थित: ॥४॥
girivrajapuraM gatAstadanu deva yUyaM trayo
yayAcha samarotsavaM dvijamiSheNa taM mAgadham 
apUrNasukRtaM tvamuM pavanajena saMgrAmayan
nirIkShya saha jiShNunA tvamapi rAjayuddhvA sthitaH 4

गिरिव्रजपुरं गतास्तदनु देव यूयं त्रयो
O Lord, as you three went to the city of Girivraja,
ययाच समरोत्सवं द्विजमिषेण तं मागधम् ।
and in the disguise of Dvijas, asked that King of Magadha for a wrestling match,
अपूर्णसुकृतं त्वमुं पवनजेन संग्रामयन्
making that wicked one fight with Bhima, the son of wind,
निरीक्ष्य सह जिष्णुना त्वमपि राजयुद्ध्वा स्थित: ॥४॥
you and Arjuna, stayed along the sidelines looking at those kings fight!

अशान्तसमरोद्धतं विटपपाटनासंज्ञया
निपात्य जरस: सुतं पवनजेन निष्पाटितम् ।
विमुच्य नृपतीन् मुदा समनुगृह्य भक्तिं परां
दिदेशिथ गतस्पृहानपि च धर्मगुप्त्यै भुव: ॥५॥
ashAntasamaroddhataM viTapapATanAsaMjnayA
nipAtya jarasaH sutaM pavanajena niShpATitam 
vimuchya nRpatIn mudA samanugRhya bhaktiM parAM
dideshitha gataspRhAnapi cha dharmaguptyai bhuvaH 5

अशान्तसमरोद्धतं विटपपाटनासंज्ञया
To that one who was fighting fiercely, with your gestures of splitting apart a twig,
निपात्य जरस: सुतं पवनजेन निष्पाटितम् ।
you made Bhima throw down Jarasandha and rip him apart!
विमुच्य नृपतीन् मुदा समनुगृह्य भक्तिं परां
Liberating the kings, and blessing them joyfully, supreme devotion
दिदेशिथ गतस्पृहानपि च धर्मगुप्त्यै भुव: ॥५॥
you bestowed unto them, and made them free from compulsions to rule the earth righteously!

प्रचक्रुषि युधिष्ठिरे तदनु राजसूयाध्वरं
प्रसन्नभृतकीभवत्सकलराजकव्याकुलम् ।
त्वमप्ययि जगत्पते द्विजपदावनेजादिकं
चकर्थ किमु कथ्यते नृपवरस्य भाग्योन्नति: ॥६॥
prachakruShi yudhiShThire tadanu rAjasUyAdhvaraM
prasannabhRtakIbhavatsakalarAjakavyAkulam 
tvamapyayi jagatpate dvijapadAvanejAdikaM
chakartha kimu kathyate nRpavarasya bhAgyonnatiH 6

प्रचक्रुषि युधिष्ठिरे तदनु राजसूयाध्वरं
Then, as Yudhishthira was performing the Rajasuya Yajna,
प्रसन्नभृतकीभवत्सकलराजकव्याकुलम् ।
as all the kings gladly served taking up their responsibilites,
त्वमप्ययि जगत्पते द्विजपदावनेजादिकं
you too, O Lord of the World, took up washing the feet of the Dvijas, and other
चकर्थ किमु कथ्यते नृपवरस्य भाग्योन्नति: ॥६॥
such tasks! What can be said about the immense fortune of that great king!

तत: सवनकर्मणि प्रवरमग्र्यपूजाविधिं
विचार्य सहदेववागनुगत: स धर्मात्मज: ।
व्यधत्त भवते मुदा सदसि विश्वभूतात्मने
तदा ससुरमानुषं भुवनमेव तृप्तिं दधौ ॥७॥
tataH savanakarmaNi pravaramagryapUjAvidhiM
vichArya sahadevavAganugataH sa dharmAtmajaH 
vyadhatta bhavate mudA sadasi vishvabhUtAtmane
tadA sasuramAnuShaM bhuvanameva tRptiM dadhau 7

तत: सवनकर्मणि प्रवरमग्र्यपूजाविधिं
Then, in that Savana Karma, during the process of Agra Puja, the first and foremost worship,
विचार्य सहदेववागनुगत: स धर्मात्मज: ।
Dharmaraja, contemplating, and following the words of Sahadeva,
व्यधत्त भवते मुदा सदसि विश्वभूतात्मने
bestowed that honour upon you, the embodiment of all creation, in that court joyfully!
तदा ससुरमानुषं भुवनमेव तृप्तिं दधौ ॥७॥
Then, O Lord, along with the Devas and Manushyas, the entire world became content!

तत: सपदि चेदिपो मुनिनृपेषु तिष्ठत्स्वहो
सभाजयति को जड: पशुपदुर्दुरूटं वटुम् ।
इति त्वयि स दुर्वचोविततिमुद्वमन्नासनात्
उदापतदुदायुध: समपतन्नमुं पाण्डवा: ॥८॥
tataH sapadi chedipo muninRpeShu tiShThatsvaho
sabhAjayati ko jaDaH pashupadurdurUTaM vaTum 
iti tvayi sa durvachovitatimudvamannAsanAt
udApatadudAyudhaH samapatannamuM pANDavAH 8

तत: सपदि चेदिपो मुनिनृपेषु तिष्ठत्स्वहो
Then, the king of Chedi, Shishupala, at once, in the presence of Sages and Kings, "Oh
सभाजयति को जड: पशुपदुर्दुरूटं वटुम् ।
great assembly this is, where a fool is honouring a cowherd rogue!"
इति त्वयि स दुर्वचोविततिमुद्वमन्नासनात्
thus vomiting words of abuse aimed towards you, from his seat
उदापतदुदायुध: समपतन्नमुं पाण्डवा: ॥८॥
he sprang up with his weapon, upon which the Pandavas pounced upon him!

निवार्य निजपक्षगानभिमुखस्यविद्वेषिण:
त्वमेव जहृषे शिरो दनुजदारिणा स्वारिणा ।
जनुस्त्रितयलब्धया सततचिन्तया शुद्धधी:
त्वया स परमेकतामधृत योगिनां दुर्लभाम् ॥९॥
nivArya nijapakShagAnabhimukhasyavidveShiNaH
tvameva jahRShe shiro danujadAriNA svAriNA 
janustritayalabdhayA satatachintayA shuddhadhIH
tvayA sa paramekatAmadhRta yoginAM durlabhAm 9

निवार्य निजपक्षगानभिमुखस्यविद्वेषिण:
Calming down those on your side, that opposer of yours,
त्वमेव जहृषे शिरो दनुजदारिणा स्वारिणा ।
you yourself took his head with your chakra, the vanquisher of asuras!
जनुस्त्रितयलब्धया सततचिन्तया शुद्धधी:
After his three lifetimes as Hiranyakashipu, Ravana and Shishupala, always thinking of you and his mind purified,
त्वया स परमेकतामधृत योगिनां दुर्लभाम् ॥९॥
he finally attained to unity with you, which is hard to obtain even for the yogis!

तत: सुमहिते त्वया क्रतुवरे निरूढे जनो
ययौ जयति धर्मजो जयति कृष्ण इत्यालपन्।
खल: स तु सुयोधनो धुतमनास्सपत्नश्रिया
मयार्पितसभामुखे स्थलजलभ्रमादभ्रमीत् ॥१०॥
tataH sumahite tvayA kratuvare nirUDhe jano
yayau jayati dharmajo jayati kRShNa ityAlapan
khalaH sa tu suyodhano dhutamanAssapatnashriyA
mayArpitasabhAmukhe sthalajalabhramAdabhramIt 10

तत: सुमहिते त्वया क्रतुवरे निरूढे जनो
Then, as that great Yajna was culminated by you, people
ययौ जयति धर्मजो जयति कृष्ण इत्यालपन्।
departed saying, "Hail to Dharmaraja, Hail to Krishna!"
खल: स तु सुयोधनो धुतमनास्सपत्नश्रिया
The wicked Duryodhana on the other hand, jealous of his enemies' prosperity,
मयार्पितसभामुखे स्थलजलभ्रमादभ्रमीत् ॥१०॥
mistook the pool in that great hall built by maya, to be the floor!

तदा हसितमुत्थितं द्रुपदनन्दनाभीमयो:
अपाङ्गकलया विभो किमपि तावदुज्जृम्भयन् ।
धराभरनिराकृतौ सपदि नाम बीजं वपन्
जनार्दन मरुत्पुरीनिलय पाहि मामामयात् ॥११॥
tadA hasitamutthitaM drupadanandanAbhImayoH
apAngakalayA vibho kimapi tAvadujjRmbhayan 
dharAbharanirAkRtau sapadi nAma bIjaM vapan
janArdana marutpurInilaya pAhi mAmAmayAt 11

तदा हसितमुत्थितं द्रुपदनन्दनाभीमयो:
That then, caused Draupadi and Bhima to laugh out aloud,
अपाङ्गकलया विभो किमपि तावदुज्जृम्भयन् ।
O Vibhu, you with your own side glances goaded them to laugh even more,
धराभरनिराकृतौ सपदि नाम बीजं वपन्
sowing the seed then and there to reduce the burden on earth!
जनार्दन मरुत्पुरीनिलय पाहि मामामयात् ॥११॥
O Janardana! O One who Resides in the City of Wind! Protect me from my disease!
