ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

चतुर्दशम् दशकम्
chaturdasham dashakam

The Fourteenth group of Ten shlokas

कपिल अवतार:
kapila avatAraH

Incarnation as Sage Kapila

समनुस्मृततावकाङ्घ्रियुग्म:
स मनु: पङ्कजसम्भवाङ्गजन्मा ।
निजमन्तरमन्तरायहीनं
चरितं ते कथयन् सुखं निनाय ॥१॥
samanusmRtatAvakAnghriyugmaH
sa manuH pankajasambhavAngajanmA 
nijamantaramantarAyahInaM
charitaM te kathayan sukhaM ninAya 1

समनुस्मृततावकाङ्घ्रियुग्म:
Meditating well upon your pair of feet,
स मनु: पङ्कजसम्भवाङ्गजन्मा ।
that svAyambhuva manu, the Son of the lotus-born,
निजमन्तरमन्तरायहीनं
having removed all obstacles from his own interiority,
चरितं ते कथयन् सुखं निनाय ॥१॥
recounting your stories, passed away peacefully!

समये खलु तत्र कर्दमाख्यो
द्रुहिणच्छायभवस्तदीयवाचा ।
धृतसर्गरसो निसर्गरम्यं
भगवंस्त्वामयुतं समा: सिषेवे ॥२॥
samaye khalu tatra kardamAkhyo
druhiNachChAyabhavastadIyavAchA 
dhRtasargaraso nisargaramyaM
bhagavaMstvAmayutaM samAH siSheve 2

समये खलु तत्र कर्दमाख्यो
In time, hasn't there someone by the name of kardama,
द्रुहिणच्छायभवस्तदीयवाचा ।
born from the shadow of brahmA, following his words,
धृतसर्गरसो निसर्गरम्यं
interested in continuing the process of creation, to the charming one beyond creation,
भगवंस्त्वामयुतं समा: सिषेवे ॥२॥
to You O Lord, for a thousand years, he offered his prayers!

गरुडोपरि कालमेघकम्रं
विलसत्केलिसरोजपाणिपद्मम् ।
हसितोल्लसिताननं विभो त्वं
वपुराविष्कुरुषे स्म कर्दमाय ॥३॥
garuDopari kAlameghakamraM
vilasatkelisarojapANipadmam 
hasitollasitAnanaM vibho tvaM
vapurAviShkuruShe sma kardamAya 3

गरुडोपरि कालमेघकम्रं
Riding on garuDa, like a beautiful dark rain cloud,
विलसत्केलिसरोजपाणिपद्मम् ।
holding a resplendent lotus in your playful hand,
हसितोल्लसिताननं विभो त्वं
with a playful smile on your face, O Vibhu! You
वपुराविष्कुरुषे स्म कर्दमाय ॥३॥
had presented your form to kardama!

स्तुवते पुलकावृताय तस्मै
मनुपुत्रीं दयितां नवापि पुत्री: ।
कपिलं च सुतं स्वमेव पश्चात्
स्वगतिं चाप्यनुगृह्य निर्गतोऽभू: ॥४॥
stuvate pulakAvRtAya tasmai
manuputrIM dayitAM navApi putrIH 
kapilaM cha sutaM svameva pashchAt
svagatiM chApyanugRhya nirgatobhUH 4

स्तुवते पुलकावृताय तस्मै
As he sang your praises, filled with glee, to him, 
मनुपुत्रीं दयितां नवापि पुत्री: ।
manu's daughter as a wife, and for them, nine daughters,
कपिलं च सुतं स्वमेव पश्चात्
and later You yourself as his son, kapila,
स्वगतिं चाप्यनुगृह्य निर्गतोऽभू: ॥४॥
and then his own liberation, you granted and returned!

स मनु: शतरूपया महिष्या
गुणवत्या सुतया च देवहूत्या ।
भवदीरितनारदोपदिष्ट:
समगात् कर्दममागतिप्रतीक्षम् ॥५॥
sa manuH shatarUpayA mahiShyA
guNavatyA sutayA cha devahUtyA 
bhavadIritanAradopadiShTaH
samagAt kardamamAgatipratIkSham 5

स मनु: शतरूपया महिष्या
That manu, along with his queen shatarUpa,
गुणवत्या सुतया च देवहूत्या ।
and with his virtuous daughter devahUtI,
भवदीरितनारदोपदिष्ट:
prompted by You, following nArada's advise
समगात् कर्दममागतिप्रतीक्षम् ॥५॥
went to kardama who was awaiting their arrival!

मनुनोपहृतां च देवहूतिं
तरुणीरत्नमवाप्य कर्दमोऽसौ ।
भवदर्चननिवृतोऽपि तस्यां
दृढशुश्रूषणया दधौ प्रसादम् ॥६॥
manunopahRtAM cha devahUtiM
taruNIratnamavApya kardamosau 
bhavadarchananivRtopi tasyAM
dRDhashushrUShaNayA dadhau prasAdam 6

मनुनोपहृतां च देवहूतिं
devahUtI, offered by manu in approval,
तरुणीरत्नमवाप्य कर्दमोऽसौ ।
kardama having begotten a jewel among women, as a wife,
भवदर्चननिवृतोऽपि तस्यां
with your prayers, despite being fully content,
दृढशुश्रूषणया दधौ प्रसादम् ॥६॥
with her dedication towards him was even more pleased!

स पुनस्त्वदुपासनप्रभावाद्
दयिताकामकृते कृते विमाने ।
वनिताकुलसङ्कुलो नवात्मा
व्यहरद्देवपथेषु देवहूत्या ॥७॥
sa punastvadupAsanaprabhAvAd
dayitAkAmakRte kRte vimAne 
vanitAkulasankulo navAtmA
vyaharaddevapatheShu devahUtyA 7

स पुनस्त्वदुपासनप्रभावाद्
He once again, with the effects of your worship,
दयिताकामकृते कृते विमाने ।
to fulfill his wife's desire, in a vimAna arranged
वनिताकुलसङ्कुलो नवात्मा
with groups of attending maidens, as a new being
व्यहरद्देवपथेषु देवहूत्या ॥७॥
sported in the celestial gardens along with devahUtI!

शतवर्षमथ व्यतीत्य सोऽयं
नव कन्या: समवाप्य धन्यरूपा: ।
वनयानसमुद्यतोऽपि कान्ता-
हितकृत्त्वज्जननोत्सुको न्यवात्सीत् ॥८॥
shatavarShamatha vyatItya soyaM
nava kanyAH samavApya dhanyarUpAH 
vanayAnasamudyatopi kAntA-
hitakRttvajjananotsuko nyavAtsIt 8

शतवर्षमथ व्यतीत्य सोऽयं
Thus spending a hundred years, he then
नव कन्या: समवाप्य धन्यरूपा: ।
begetting nine beautiful daughters,
वनयानसमुद्यतोऽपि कान्ता-
as he was getting ready for his vAnaprastha, upon his wife's
हितकृत्त्वज्जननोत्सुको न्यवात्सीत् ॥८॥
request to have You as their son, stayed back.

निजभर्तृगिरा भवन्निषेवा-
निरतायामथ देव देवहूत्याम् ।
कपिलस्त्वमजायथा जनानां
प्रथयिष्यन् परमात्मतत्त्वविद्याम् ॥९॥
nijabhartRgirA bhavanniShevA-
niratAyAmatha deva devahUtyAm 
kapilastvamajAyathA janAnAM
prathayiShyan paramAtmatattvavidyAm 9

निजभर्तृगिरा भवन्निषेवा-
Following her husband's words, in your worship
निरतायामथ देव देवहूत्याम् ।
the one who's constantly involved, O Lord, in devahUtI,
कपिलस्त्वमजायथा जनानां
you were born as kapila, among the people
प्रथयिष्यन् परमात्मतत्त्वविद्याम् ॥९॥
bestowing the systems of knowledge of the supreme sense of self!

वनमेयुषि कर्दमे प्रसन्ने
मतसर्वस्वमुपादिशन् जनन्यै ।
कपिलात्मक वायुमन्दिरेश
त्वरितं त्वं परिपाहि मां गदौघात् ॥१०॥
vanameyuShi kardame prasanne
matasarvasvamupAdishan jananyai 
kapilAtmaka vAyumandiresha
tvaritaM tvaM paripAhi mAM gadaughAt 10

वनमेयुषि कर्दमे प्रसन्ने
As kardama left for the forests happily,
मतसर्वस्वमुपादिशन् जनन्यै ।
teaching the entire subject to his mother,
कपिलात्मक वायुमन्दिरेश
in the form of kapila, O Lord of the Temple of Wind,
त्वरितं त्वं परिपाहि मां गदौघात् ॥१०॥
you quickly protect me from the heaps of diseases!
