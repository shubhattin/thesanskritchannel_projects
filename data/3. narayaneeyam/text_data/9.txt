ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

नवमम् दशकम्
navamam dashakam

The Ninth group of Ten shlokas

ब्रह्मण: तप: भुवन निर्मिति: च
brahmaNaH tapaH bhuvana nirmitiH cha

Penance of Brahma and Creation of the Universe

स्थितस्स कमलोद्भवस्तव हि नाभिपङ्केरुहे
कुत: स्विदिदमम्बुधावुदितमित्यनालोकयन् ।
तदीक्षणकुतूहलात् प्रतिदिशं विवृत्ताननः
चतुर्वदनतामगाद्विकसदष्टदृष्ट्यम्बुजाम् ॥१॥
sthitassa kamalodbhavastava hi nAbhipankeruhe
kutaH svididamambudhAvuditamityanAlokayan 
tadIkShaNakutUhalAt pratidishaM vivRttAnanaH
chaturvadanatAmagAdvikasadaShTadRShTyambujAm 1

स्थितस्स कमलोद्भवस्तव हि नाभिपङ्केरुहे
That lotus born brahmA situated verily in the lotus from your navel,
कुत: स्विदिदमम्बुधावुदितमित्यनालोकयन् ।
"From where has this lotus come forth from this ocean", thinking thus and unable to perceive,
तदीक्षणकुतूहलात् प्रतिदिशं विवृत्ताननः
in his curiosity to see, he turned his head in all directions
चतुर्वदनतामगाद्विकसदष्टदृष्ट्यम्बुजाम् ॥१॥
and thus became endowed with four faces, endowed with eight blooming lotus-like eyes!

महार्णवविघूर्णितं कमलमेव तत्केवलं
विलोक्य तदुपाश्रयं तव तनुं तु नालोकयन् ।
क एष कमलोदरे महति निस्सहायो ह्यहं
कुत: स्विदिदमम्बुजं समजनीति चिन्तामगात् ॥२॥
mahArNavavighUrNitaM kamalameva tatkevalaM
vilokya tadupAshrayaM tava tanuM tu nAlokayan 
ka eSha kamalodare mahati nissahAyo hyahaM
kutaH svididamambujaM samajanIti chintAmagAt 2

महार्णवविघूर्णितं कमलमेव तत्केवलं
Bobbing around in the vast ocean, only that lotus itself
विलोक्य तदुपाश्रयं तव तनुं तु नालोकयन् ।
was visible along with its stem, but unable to see your body,
क एष कमलोदरे महति निस्सहायो ह्यहं
"Who am I, helpless in this huge womb of the lotus!?
कुत: स्विदिदमम्बुजं समजनीति चिन्तामगात् ॥२॥
From where has this lotus been born!?" - thus he started to worry!

अमुष्य हि सरोरुह: किमपि कारणं सम्भवेद्
इति स्म कृतनिश्चयस्स खलु नालरन्ध्राध्वना ।
स्वयोगबलविद्यया समवरूढवान् प्रौढधीः
त्वदीयमतिमोहनं न तु कलेवरं दृष्टवान् ॥३॥
amuShya hi saroruhaH kimapi kAraNaM sambhved
iti sma kRtanishchayassa khalu nAlarandhrAdhvanA 
svayogabalavidyayA samavarUDhavAn prauDhadhIH
tvadIyamatimohanaM na tu kalevaraM dRShTavAn 3

अमुष्य हि सरोरुह: किमपि कारणं सम्भवेद्
"Certainly for this lotus to have been born, they must have been a source!"
इति स्म कृतनिश्चयस्स खलु नालरन्ध्राध्वना ।
Having decided thus, he, through the hollow of the lotus stem,
स्वयोगबलविद्यया समवरूढवान् प्रौढधीः
with his own yogic prowess, and mighty intellect, started descending along it!
त्वदीयमतिमोहनं न तु कलेवरं दृष्टवान् ॥३॥
But he could not perceive your most enchanting form!

तत: सकलनालिकाविवरमार्गगो मार्गयन्
प्रयस्य शतवत्सरं किमपि नैव संदृष्टवान् ।
निवृत्य कमलोदरे सुखनिषण्ण एकाग्रधी:
समाधिबलमादधे भवदनुग्रहैकाग्रही ॥४॥
tataH sakalanAlikAvivaramArgago mArgayan
prayasya shatavatsaraM kimapi naiva saMdRShTavAn 
nivRtya kamalodare sukhaniShaNNa ekAgradhIH
samAdhibalamAdadhe bhavadanugrahaikAgrahI 4

तत: सकलनालिकाविवरमार्गगो मार्गयन्
Then, exploring all the various cavities of that lotus stem,
प्रयस्य शतवत्सरं किमपि नैव संदृष्टवान् ।
striving for a hundred years, he could see absolutely nothing!
निवृत्य कमलोदरे सुखनिषण्ण एकाग्रधी:
He then returned into the lotus, sitting comfortably, made his mind one-pointed!
समाधिबलमादधे भवदनुग्रहैकाग्रही ॥४॥
He resorted to the power of samAdhi, with the sole intention of perceiving your grace!

शतेन परिवत्सरैर्दृढसमाधिबन्धोल्लसत्-
प्रबोधविशदीकृत: स खलु पद्मिनीसम्भव: ।
अदृष्टचरमद्भुतं तव हि रूपमन्तर्दृशा
व्यचष्ट परितुष्टधीर्भुजगभोगभागाश्रयम् ॥५॥
shatena parivatsarairdRDhasamAdhibandhollasat-
prabodhavishadIkRtaH sa khalu padminIsambhavaH 
adRShTacharamadbhutaM tava hi rUpamantardRshA
vyachaShTa parituShTadhIrbhujagabhogabhAgAshrayam 5

शतेन परिवत्सरैर्दृढसमाधिबन्धोल्लसत्-
For a hundred years, having bound himself firmly in samAdhi,
प्रबोधविशदीकृत: स खलु पद्मिनीसम्भव: ।
he then realized the knowledge he sought, the one born in a lotus!
अदृष्टचरमद्भुतं तव हि रूपमन्तर्दृशा
The non-apparent, wondrous form of yours through his inner vision
व्यचष्ट परितुष्टधीर्भुजगभोगभागाश्रयम् ॥५॥
he glimpsed, with a fully content mind, the form that was resting on the body of the great serpent!

किरीटमुकुटोल्लसत्कटकहारकेयूरयुङ्-
मणिस्फुरितमेखलं सुपरिवीतपीताम्बरम् ।
कलायकुसुमप्रभं गलतलोल्लसत्कौस्तुभं
वपुस्तदयि भावये कमलजन्मने दर्शितम् ॥६॥
kirITamukuTollasatkaTakahArakeyUrayun-
maNisphuritamekhalaM suparivItapItAmbaram 
kalAyakusumaprabhaM galatalollasatkaustubhaM
vapustadayi bhAvaye kamalajanmane darshitam 6

किरीटमुकुटोल्लसत्कटकहारकेयूरयुङ्-
With a crown studded with jewels, with bracelets, necklaces, and armlets,
मणिस्फुरितमेखलं सुपरिवीतपीताम्बरम् ।
with a gem-studded waistband, draped in beautiful golden garments,
कलायकुसुमप्रभं गलतलोल्लसत्कौस्तुभं
with the radiance of a blue water-lily, with the kaustubha jewel shining against the neck,
वपुस्तदयि भावये कमलजन्मने दर्शितम् ॥६॥
your form, O Lord! I meditate upon it, which was shown to the lotus-born brahmA!

श्रुतिप्रकरदर्शितप्रचुरवैभव श्रीपते
हरे जय जय प्रभो पदमुपैषि दिष्ट्या दृशो: ।
कुरुष्व धियमाशु मे भुवननिर्मितौ कर्मठाम्
इति द्रुहिणवर्णितस्वगुणबंहिमा पाहि माम् ॥७॥
shrutiprakaradarshitaprachuravaibhava shrIpate
hare jaya jaya prabho padamupaiShi diShTyA dRshoH 
kuruShva dhiyamAshu me bhuvananirmitau karmaThAm
iti druhiNavarNitasvaguNabaMhimA pAhi mAm 7

श्रुतिप्रकरदर्शितप्रचुरवैभव श्रीपते
"O Lord whose Glories are displayed and propagated by the Vedas! O Consort of Lakshmi!
हरे जय जय प्रभो पदमुपैषि दिष्ट्या दृशो: ।
O Hari! Victory be to You, O Lord! You have appeared before my eyes by my fortune!
कुरुष्व धियमाशु मे भुवननिर्मितौ कर्मठाम्
Make my mind effortlessly capable in the process of creation!"
इति द्रुहिणवर्णितस्वगुणबंहिमा पाहि माम् ॥७॥
Thus, O One whose qualities have been described by brahmA, protect me!

लभस्व भुवनत्रयीरचनदक्षतामक्षतां
गृहाण मदनुग्रहं कुरु तपश्च भूयो विधे ।
भवत्वखिलसाधनी मयि च भक्तिरत्युत्कटे-
त्युदीर्य गिरमादधा मुदितचेतसं वेधसम् ॥८॥
labhasva bhuvanatrayIrachanadakShatAmakShatAM
gRhANa madanugrahaM kuru tapashcha bhUyo vidhe 
bhavatvakhilasAdhanI mayi cha bhaktiratyutkaTe-
tyudIrya giramAdadhA muditachetasaM vedhasam 8

लभस्व भुवनत्रयीरचनदक्षतामक्षतां
"May you attain complete expertise in the process of creating the three worlds!
गृहाण मदनुग्रहं कुरु तपश्च भूयो विधे ।
Receive my grace! Perform penance once again, O Creator!
भवत्वखिलसाधनी मयि च भक्तिरत्युत्कटे-
May it be the tool that shall accomplish everything, and an exalted devotion towards me!"
त्युदीर्य गिरमादधा मुदितचेतसं वेधसम् ॥८॥
Speaking these words, you brought about glee to the creator's mind!

शतं कृततपास्तत: स खलु दिव्यसंवत्सरान्
अवाप्य च तपोबलं मतिबलं च पूर्वाधिकम् ।
उदीक्ष्य किल कम्पितं पयसि पङ्कजं वायुना
भवद्बलविजृम्भित: पवनपाथसी पीतवान् ॥९॥
shataM kRtatapAstataH sa khalu divyasaMvatsarAn
avApya cha tapobalaM matibalaM cha pUrvAdhikam 
udIkShya kila kampitaM payasi pankajaM vAyunA
bhavadbalavijRmbhitaH pavanapAthasI pItavAn 9

शतं कृततपास्तत: स खलु दिव्यसंवत्सरान्
Performing penance for a hundred divine years, he then indeed
अवाप्य च तपोबलं मतिबलं च पूर्वाधिकम् ।
attained spiritual and mental prowess more than ever before!
उदीक्ष्य किल कम्पितं पयसि पङ्कजं वायुना
And then looking at the lotus he was seated on swaying in the waters due to wind,
भवद्बलविजृम्भित: पवनपाथसी पीतवान् ॥९॥
empowered by your powers, he drank up both the wind and the waters!

तवैव कृपया पुनस्सरसिजेन तेनैव स:
प्रकल्प्य भुवनत्रयीं प्रववृते प्रजानिर्मितौ ।
तथाविधकृपाभरो गुरुमरुत्पुराधीश्वर
त्वमाशु परिपाहि मां गुरुदयोक्षितैरीक्षितै: ॥१०॥
tavaiva kRpayA punassarasijena tenaiva saH
prakalpya bhuvanatrayIM pravavRte prajAnirmitau 
tathAvidhakRpAbharo gurumarutpurAdhIshvara
tvamAshu paripAhi mAM gurudayokShitairIkShitaiH 10

तवैव कृपया पुनस्सरसिजेन तेनैव स:
By your grace alone, once again, out of that lotus itself, he
प्रकल्प्य भुवनत्रयीं प्रववृते प्रजानिर्मितौ ।
created the three worlds, and engaged himself in the activity of creating living beings.
तथाविधकृपाभरो गुरुमरुत्पुराधीश्वर
You are filled with such great compassion, O Lord of the City of Guru and Wind!
त्वमाशु परिपाहि मां गुरुदयोक्षितैरीक्षितै: ॥१०॥
You effortlessly protect me with your glances overflowing with great mercy!
