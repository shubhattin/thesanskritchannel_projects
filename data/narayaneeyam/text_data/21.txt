ॐ नमो नारायणाय
AUM namo nArAyaNAya

अथ श्रीमन्नारायणीयम्
atha shrImannArAyaNIyam

And now the highly auspicious shlokas of Narayana

एकविंशम् दशकम्
ekaviMsham dashakam

The Twenty-first group of Ten shlokas

नव वर्षाणि सप्त द्वीपादाय:  च
nava varShANi sapta dIpAdayaH cha

Nine Continents, Seven Islands and others...

मध्योद्भवे भुव इलावृतनाम्नि वर्षे
गौरीप्रधानवनिताजनमात्रभाजि ।
शर्वेण मन्त्रनुतिभि: समुपास्यमानं
सङ्कर्षणात्मकमधीश्वर संश्रये त्वाम् ॥१॥
madhyodbhave bhuva ilAvRtanAmni varShe
gaurIpradhAnavanitAjanamAtrabhAji 
sharveNa mantranutibhiH samupAsyamAnaM
sankarShaNAtmakamadhIshvara saMshraye tvAm 1

मध्योद्भवे भुव इलावृतनाम्नि वर्षे
In the middle of the earth, in the land called Ilavrita,
गौरीप्रधानवनिताजनमात्रभाजि ।
with Gauri as their leader, in a place inhabited only by women,
शर्वेण मन्त्रनुतिभि: समुपास्यमानं
by Shiva's feminine form, O One who is worshipped by chants and prayers,
सङ्कर्षणात्मकमधीश्वर संश्रये त्वाम् ॥१॥
O Lord who is manifest there as Sankarshana, I seek refuge in you!

भद्राश्वनामक इलावृतपूर्ववर्षे
भद्रश्रवोभिर्ऋषिभि: परिणूयमानम् ।
कल्पान्तगूढनिगमोद्धरणप्रवीणं
ध्यायामि देव हयशीर्षतनुं भवन्तम् ॥२॥
bhadrAshvanAmaka ilAvRtapUrvavarShe
bhadrashravobhirRShibhiH pariNUyamAnam 
kalpAntagUDhanigamoddharaNapravINaM
dhyAyAmi deva hayashIrShatanuM bhavantam 2

भद्राश्वनामक इलावृतपूर्ववर्षे
In the region named Bhadrashva, which is to the east of Ilavrita,
भद्रश्रवोभि: ऋषिभि: परिणूयमानम् ।
inhabited by sages who are of auspicious realisations,
कल्पान्तगूढनिगमोद्धरणप्रवीणं
the one who is adept at reviving the vedas lost during the end of creation,
ध्यायामि देव हयशीर्षतनुं भवन्तम् ॥२॥
I meditate upon you, O Lord, who is worshipped in the form of Hayagriva!

ध्यायामि दक्षिणगते हरिवर्षवर्षे
प्रह्लादमुख्यपुरुषै: परिषेव्यमाणम् ।
उत्तुङ्गशान्तधवलाकृतिमेकशुद्ध-
ज्ञानप्रदं नरहरिं भगवन् भवन्तम् ॥३॥
dhyAyAmi dakShiNagate harivarShavarShe
prahlAdamukhyapuruShaiH pariShevyamANam 
uttungashAntadhavalAkRtimekashuddha-
jnAnapradaM narahariM bhagavan bhavantam 3

ध्यायामि दक्षिणगते हरिवर्षवर्षे
I meditate, in the land to the south, called Harivarsha,
प्रह्लादमुख्यपुरुषै: परिषेव्यमाणम् ।
upon the one who is worshipped by Prahlada and other great ones,
उत्तुङ्गशान्तधवलाकृतिमेकशुद्ध-
upon the one who is of a tall, white form, who bestows unique, pure
ज्ञानप्रदं नरहरिं भगवन् भवन्तम् ॥३॥
knowledge, upon you, O Lord! in the form of Narahari!

वर्षे प्रतीचि ललितात्मनि केतुमाले
लीलाविशेषललितस्मितशोभनाङ्गम् ।
लक्ष्म्या प्रजापतिसुतैश्च निषेव्यमाणं
तस्या: प्रियाय धृतकामतनुं भजे त्वाम् ॥४॥
varShe pratIchi lalitAtmani ketumAle
lIlAvisheShalalitasmitashobhanAngam 
lakShmyA prajApatisutaishcha niShevyamANaM
tasyAH priyAya dhRtakAmatanuM bhaje tvAm 4

वर्षे प्रतीचि ललितात्मनि केतुमाले
In the land to the west, the beautiful region of Ketumala,
लीलाविशेषललितस्मितशोभनाङ्गम् ।
a resplendent form with an exalted, gentle, playful smile,
लक्ष्म्या प्रजापतिसुतैश्च निषेव्यमाणं
worshipped by Lakshmi and the sons of Prajapati,
तस्या: प्रियाय धृतकामतनुं भजे त्वाम् ॥४॥
in order to please her, I worship you who has taken on the form of Kamadeva!

रम्येप्युदीचि खलु रम्यकनाम्नि वर्षे
तद्वर्षनाथमनुवर्यसपर्यमाणम् ।
भक्तैकवत्सलममत्सरहृत्सु भान्तं
मत्स्याकृतिं भुवननाथ भजे भवन्तम् ॥५॥
ramyepyudIchi khalu ramyakanAmni varShe
tadvarShanAthamanuvaryasaparyamANam 
bhaktaikavatsalamamatsarahRtsu bhAntaM
matsyAkRtiM bhuvananAtha bhaje bhavantam 5

रम्येप्युदीचि खलु रम्यकनाम्नि वर्षे
In the enchanting land to the north, isn't it known by name RamyakaVarsha,
तद्वर्षनाथमनुवर्यसपर्यमाणम् ।
worshipped by the ruler of that land, Manu,
भक्तैकवत्सलममत्सरहृत्सु भान्तं
the one who is completely benevolent to your devotees, who shines in the hearts free from envy,
मत्स्याकृतिं भुवननाथ भजे भवन्तम् ॥५॥
manifest in the form of a Fish, O Lord of Creation, I worship you!

वर्षं हिरण्मयसमाह्वयमौत्तराहम्
आसीनमद्रिधृतिकर्मठकामठाङ्गम् ।
संसेवते पितृगणप्रवरोऽर्यमा यं
तं त्वां भजामि भगवन् परचिन्मयात्मन् ॥६॥
varShaM hiraNmayasamAhvayamauttarAham
AsInamadridhRtikarmaThakAmaThAngam 
saMsevate pitRgaNapravaroryamA yaM
taM tvAM bhajAmi bhagavan parachinmayAtman 6

वर्षं हिरण्मयसमाह्वयमौत्तराहम्
In the region named HiranmayaVarsha, further up north,
आसीनमद्रिधृतिकर्मठकामठाङ्गम् ।
residing there in the form of the tortoise that held the mountain on its back,
संसेवते पितृगणप्रवरोऽर्यमा यं
worshipped by the chief of the groups of Pitrs, Aryama,
तं त्वां भजामि भगवन् परचिन्मयात्मन् ॥६॥
that You, I worship, O Lord! Who is of the Nature of Supreme Consciousness!

किञ्चोत्तरेषु कुरुषु प्रियया धरण्या
संसेवितो महितमन्त्रनुतिप्रभेदै: ।
दंष्ट्राग्रघृष्टघनपृष्ठगरिष्ठवर्ष्मा
त्वं पाहि विज्ञनुत यज्ञवराहमूर्ते ॥७॥
kinchottareShu kuruShu priyayA dharaNyA
saMsevito mahitamantranutiprabhedaiH 
daMShTrAgraghRShTaghanapRShThagariShThavarShmA
tvaM pAhi vijnanuta yajnavarAhamUrte 7

किञ्चोत्तरेषु कुरुषु प्रियया धरण्या
Further up north, in the land of the Kurus, along with your beloved, the earth,
संसेवितो महितमन्त्रनुतिप्रभेदै: ।
worshipped with powerful chants and many prayers,
दंष्ट्राग्रघृष्टघनपृष्ठगरिष्ठवर्ष्मा
with the ends of your tusks touching the sky in your gigantic form,
त्वं पाहि विज्ञनुत यज्ञवराहमूर्ते ॥७॥
you protect me, O One Worshipped by the Wise! O One of the Form of Yajna Varaha!

याम्यां दिशं भजति किंपुरुषाख्यवर्षे
संसेवितो हनुमता दृढभक्तिभाजा ।
सीताभिरामपरमाद्भुतरूपशाली
रामात्मक: परिलसन् परिपाहि विष्णो ॥८॥
yAmyAM dishaM bhajati kiMpuruShAkhyavarShe
saMsevito hanumatA dRDhabhaktibhAjA 
sItAbhirAmaparamAdbhutarUpashAlI
rAmAtmakaH parilasan paripAhi viShNo 8

याम्यां दिशं भजति किंपुरुषाख्यवर्षे
In the land to the south of Ilavrita called as Kimpurusha,
संसेवितो हनुमता दृढभक्तिभाजा ।
worshipped by Hanuman with firm devotion,
सीताभिरामपरमाद्भुतरूपशाली
in an extremely wondrous form that charmed Devi Sita,
रामात्मक: परिलसन् परिपाहि विष्णो ॥८॥
resplendent in the form of Rama, protect me, O Vishnu!

श्रीनारदेन सह भारतखण्डमुख्यैः
त्वं साङ्ख्ययोगनुतिभि: समुपास्यमान: ।
आकल्पकालमिह साधुजनाभिरक्षी
नारायणो नरसख: परिपाहि भूमन् ॥९॥
shrInAradena saha bhAratakhaNDamukhyaiH
tvaM sAnkhyayoganutibhiH samupAsyamAnaH 
AkalpakAlamiha sAdhujanAbhirakShI
nArAyaNo narasakhaH paripAhi bhUman 9

श्रीनारदेन सह भारतखण्डमुख्यैः
Along with the auspicious Narada, with the foremost people of BharataKhanda,
त्वं साङ्ख्ययोगनुतिभि: समुपास्यमान: ।
you, who is worshipped by the prayers of Sankhya and Yoga systems,
आकल्पकालमिह साधुजनाभिरक्षी
who protects the pious until the very end of creation,
नारायणो नरसख: परिपाहि भूमन् ॥९॥
in the form of Narayana, the friend of Nara, protect me, O Abundant One!

प्लाक्षेऽर्करूपमयि शाल्मल इन्दुरूपं
द्वीपे भजन्ति कुशनामनि वह्निरूपम् ।
क्रौञ्चेऽम्बुरूपमथ वायुमयं च शाके
त्वां ब्रह्मरूपमयि पुष्करनाम्नि लोका: ॥१०॥
plAkSherkarUpamayi shAlmala indurUpaM
dvIpe bhajanti kushanAmani vahnirUpam 
kraunchemburUpamatha vAyumayaM cha shAke
tvAM brahmarUpamayi puShkaranAmni lokAH 10

प्लाक्षेऽर्करूपमयि शाल्मल इन्दुरूपं
In the land of Plaksha, in the form of the Sun, and in the land of Shalmala, in the form of the Moon,
द्वीपे भजन्ति कुशनामनि वह्निरूपम् ।
in the island of Kusha, worshipped in the form of Fire,
क्रौञ्चेऽम्बुरूपमथ वायुमयं च शाके
in the land of Krauncha in the form of Water, and in the land of Shaka, in the form of Wind,
त्वां ब्रह्मरूपमयि पुष्करनाम्नि लोका: ॥१०॥
and in the land of Pushkara, you are worshipped in the form of Brahma!

सर्वैर्ध्रुवादिभिरुडुप्रकरैर्ग्रहैश्च
पुच्छादिकेष्ववयवेष्वभिकल्प्यमानै: ।
त्वं शिंशुमारवपुषा महतामुपास्य:
सन्ध्यासु रुन्धि नरकं मम सिन्धुशायिन् ॥११॥
sarvairdhruvAdibhiruDuprakarairgrahaishcha
puchChAdikeShvavayaveShvabhikalpyamAnaiH 
tvaM shiMshumAravapuShA mahatAmupAsyaH
sandhyAsu rundhi narakaM mama sindhushAyin 11

सर्वैर्ध्रुवादिभिरुडुप्रकरैर्ग्रहैश्च
By Dhruva and all kinds of clusters of stars, and grahas,
पुच्छादिकेष्ववयवेष्वभिकल्प्यमानै: ।
visualized as all sorts of limbs from tails to hairs,
त्वं शिंशुमारवपुषा महतामुपास्य:
you, in the form of the great Shimshumara across the sky, are worshipped by the great ones
सन्ध्यासु रुन्धि नरकं मम सिन्धुशायिन् ॥११॥
during the sandhya times! O One Who Reclines in the Ocean, remove my hellish suffering!

पातालमूलभुवि शेषतनुं भवन्तं
लोलैककुण्डलविराजिसहस्रशीर्षम् ।
नीलाम्बरं धृतहलं भुजगाङ्गनाभिः
जुष्टं भजे हर गदान् गुरुगेहनाथ ॥१२॥
pAtAlamUlabhuvi sheShatanuM bhavantaM
lolaikakuNDalavirAjisahasrashIrSham 
nIlAmbaraM dhRtahalaM bhujagAnganAbhiH
juShTaM bhaje hara gadAn gurugehanAtha 12

पातालमूलभुवि शेषतनुं भवन्तं
At the very base of Patala, in the form of Adishesha, you,
लोलैककुण्डलविराजिसहस्रशीर्षम् ।
with thousands of heads, adorned with a single earring,
नीलाम्बरं धृतहलं भुजगाङ्गनाभिः
adorned in blue garments, carrying a plough, with serpent maidens
जुष्टं भजे हर गदान् गुरुगेहनाथ ॥१२॥
accompanying you, I worship! Take away my ailments, O Resident of the Abode of Guru!
